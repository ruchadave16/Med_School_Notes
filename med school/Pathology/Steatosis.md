*Fatty change*

- Accumulation of [[triglyceride]] vacuoles in cell
- Commonly seen in [[liver]] as hepatic steatosis
- Round clear cytoplasmic vacuoles

**Reversible**, seen in [[Alcoholism|alcoholism]] and [[diabetes]]
