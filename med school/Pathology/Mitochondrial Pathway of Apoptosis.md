*[[Apoptosis]] of most pathological and physiological situations*

- [[Cell injury]]: withdrawal of GF, DNA damage, misfolded protein accumulation

[[BH3]] proteins sense injurious stimuli and activate effectors
- **Bak** and **bax** increase mitochondrial membrane permeability
- This allows leakage of [[cytochrome C]] into cytoplasm → triggers [[apoptosis]] by activating [[caspase-9]] → activates [[caspase-3]]

>[!Clinical]
>Implicated in [[neoplasia]]
