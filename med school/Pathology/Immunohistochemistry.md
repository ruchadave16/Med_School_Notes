*”method to visualize specific molecules in a tissue sample”*

Uses [[Antibodies]] that bind to molecule of interest
Antibody is labeled in a way that allows for visualization (direct or indirect)

![[Screenshot 2025-07-12 at 2.32.52 PM.png]]

**Useful**: [[Tumor]]s express specific proteins so rapidly identifying these proteins can help speed diagnosis

Commonly use a brown chromagen → Led to “Brown Wave“ in pathology
- Location of stain must match expected localization of protein

 >[!example] [[Human Chorionic Gonadotrophin]]
 >It’s present in cytoplasm of syncytiotrophoblasts but not in nucleus. If staining is seen in nucleus of cell, need to figure out what went wrong
