*[[Necrosis]] where cellular architecture is preserved for days after death of cells*

- Structural proteins and cellular enzymes are denatured, so cell dies but **does not digest**
- Histology: eosinophilic, anucleate cellular shapes (**”ghost cells”**)
- [[Neutrophils]] recruited to area and these phagocyte dead cells 

>[!Note] Characteristic of [[Infarcts]] in all organs **but brain**
