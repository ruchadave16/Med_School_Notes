If cell is reversibly injured and blood flow restored, might recover
- Might also experience paradoxical response – sustains injury for longer causing cell death

Proposed mechanism
- [[Oxidative Stress|Oxidative stress]] – high ROS
- ↑ Intracellular Ca2+
- [[Acute Inflammation]]
- [[Complement System|Complement]] activation
