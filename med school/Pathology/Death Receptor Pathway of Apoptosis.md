*Cell receptors that trigger [[apoptosis]] under certain conditions*

- Common condition: killing of self-reactive lymphocytes and viral infected cells by cytotoxic T cells

**Receptors**: Type 1 TNF and Fas ([[CD95]]) – members of [[TNF family]]
- Ligands of receptors expressed on cytotoxic [[T Lymphocytes|T cells]]
- Active T cell binds to target cell through FasL-Fas interaction
- **Fas** activated on target cell → assembles proteins into death inducing signaling complex
- [[Caspase-8]] activated → activates [[caspase-3]]
