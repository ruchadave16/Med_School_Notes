*Complete digestion of cells*

- Tissue turns into liquid that is digested and removed by [[Macrophage|macrophages]]
- [[Brain Infarcts]]: tissue completely turned to liquid and leaves behind cavitary space
- [[Bacterial infection]]: rapid inflammatory response and neutrophils release enzymes to digest → result = abscess
