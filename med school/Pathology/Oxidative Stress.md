Free radicals = single unpaired e- (Ex: [[ROS]])
- Increased production due to radiant energy, [[Acute Inflammation]], [[reperfusion injury]] of ischemic tissues
#### Protective Measures
- [[Superoxide dimutase]] (SOD) – converts to hydrogen peroxide
- [[Glutathione peroxidase]] – converts hydrogen peroxide → water
- [[Catalase]] – converts hydrogen peroxide to water
- [[Antioxidants]] (Vitamin E, A, C) – block formation of free radicals or scavenge them
