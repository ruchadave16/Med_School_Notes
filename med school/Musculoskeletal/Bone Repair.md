*Steps to recreate functional [[bone]]*

1) Fracture hematoma
	- 1st week after injury
	- Blood vessels disrupted + [[bone matrix]] damaged
	- Blood clot, damaged matrix removed by [[Macrophage|macrophages]]
	- Granulation tissue moves in

2) Fibrocartilaginous callus
	- Week 2-3
	- Periosteum + endosteum cause ↑↑ [[proliferation]] of osteoprogenitor cells → make [[connective tissue]]

3) Bony callus
	- 1-3 months
	- Made of woven bone
	- Intramembranous and endochondral [[ossification]]

4) Bone remodelling
	- Bony callus reabsorbed and replaced by secondary tissue
