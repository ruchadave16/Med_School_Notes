*Funnel tube in [[femoral triangle]], continuation of transversalis fascia*

>[!Clinical] Common location for [[catheter]] insertion and [[Femoral Nerve|femoral n.]] block

#### Contents
- [[Femoral Artery|Femoral a.]] and v., femoral canal **NOT nerve**
- Femoral canal – empty space in sheath with [[lymph nodes]]
- Femoral ring – proximal opening of femoral canal
- Saphenous opening – superficial opening where [[Great Saphenous Vein|great saphenous v.]] moves

>[!Clinical] [[Femoral hernia]]
>Protrusion of abdominal contents into femoral canal
>- Medial to femoral v, lateral to lacunar ligament
>- More common in females
>- Can cause necrosis of intestines, lacunar ligament may need to be cut
