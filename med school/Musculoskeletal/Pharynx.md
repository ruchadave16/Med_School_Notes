*Superior part of alimentary canal*

- [[Nasopharynx]] – (nasal cavity) has respiratory epithelium
- [[Oropharynx]] – (oral cavity) non-keratinized stratified squamous epithelium
- [[Laryngopharynx]] – (larynx) non-keratinized stratified squamous epithelium

![[Screenshot 2025-10-30 at 9.40.19 PM.png]]

#### Muscles
- Longitudinal pharyngeal m. – elevate larynx, shorten pharynx when speaking or [[swallowing]]
	- Palatopharyngeus m. – in palate, makes posterior pharyngeal arch
	- Salpingopharyngeus m. – auditory tube → palatopharyngeus **([[Vagus Nerve|CN X]] pharyngeal branch)**
	- Stylopharyngeus m. – between superior and middle constrictors **([[Glossopharyngeal Nerve|CN IX]])**

![[Drawing 2025-10-31 13.44.52.excalidraw]]

![[Drawing 2025-10-30 22.55.13.excalidraw]]

- Pharyngeal Constrictors – involuntary for [[swallowing]] **([[Vagus Nerve|CN X]])**
	- Superior – sphenoid bone, lateral walls of [[oropharynx]]
	- Middle – hyoid bone
	- Inferior – thyroid and cricoid cartilage

![[Drawing 2025-10-30 22.52.29.excalidraw]]
#### Innervation
- Motor: Pharyngeal branch of [[Vagus Nerve|vagus n.]] supplies all muscles except stylopharyngeus ([[Glossopharyngeal Nerve|CN IX]]) and tensor veli palatini ([[Mandibular Nerve|CN V3]])

- Sensory: 
	- [[Nasopharynx]]: [[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]] – pharyngeal branch
	- [[Oropharynx]]: [[Glossopharyngeal Nerve|CN IX]] – glossopharyngeal n.
	- [[Laryngopharynx]]: [[Vagus Nerve|CN X]] – pharyngeal branch
#### Vasculature
- Tonsilar a. off of facial a. → [[External Carotid Artery|external carotid a.]]
#### Lymphatics
- [[Nasopharynx]] – retropharyngeal LN → superior deep cervical LN
- [[Oropharynx]] – superior deep cervical LN
- [[Laryngopharynx]] – superior deep cervical LN
