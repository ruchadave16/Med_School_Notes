*Pyramid cavity below orbit apex*

- Connection between [[infratemporal fossa]], middle cranial fossa, nasopharynx, nasal cavity, orbit, oral cavity

![[Screenshot 2025-10-29 at 4.38.38 PM.png]]

#### Contents
- [[Pterygopalatine ganglion]]
- Postsynaptic sympathetic fibers
	- Cervical ganglion → run with [[Internal Carotid Artery|internal carotid a.]], enter pterygopalatine fossa through deep peroneal n., jump onto [[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]] → palatine and nasal mucosa
- [[Maxillary Nerve|Maxillary n.]] (CN V2)
- N. to pterygoid canal 
	- Combination of PS (greater petrosal n. ([[Facial Nerve|CN VII]])) and deep petrosal n. (SY)
- 3rd part of maxillary a. 
	- Enters through pterygomaxillary fissure

![[Pasted image 20251101142016.png]]

#### Openings
- Pterygomaxillary fissure – to [[infratemporal fossa]]
	- [[Maxillary Artery|Maxillary a.]]
	- Posterior superior alveolar n.
- Infraorbital fissure – to orbit
	- Infraorbital n.
	- Zygomatic n. 
	- Infraorbital a/v
	- Inferior ophthalmic v.
- Sphenopalatine foramen – to [[nasal cavity]]
	- Nasopalatine n. 
	- Posterior superior nasal n.
	- Sphenopalatine a/v
- Palatine canal – to oral cavity
	- Greater palatine n.
	- Lesser palatine n.
- Foramen rotundrum – to cranial cavity
	- [[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]]
- Pterygoid canal – to middle cranial fossa
	- N. of pterygoid canal
	- A. of pterygoid canal
- Pharyngeal canal – to [[nasopharynx]]
	- Pharyngeal a/v
