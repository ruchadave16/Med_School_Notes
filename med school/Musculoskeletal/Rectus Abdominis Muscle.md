2 vertical muslces separated by linea alba
- Enclosed by [[rectus sheath]]
- Groups of fibers separated by 3-5 transverse tendinous intersections

Come from pubic symphysis and pubic crest to xiphoid process and costal cartilage 5-7
Action: compress abdominal viscera
- Flex trunk
- Stabilize tilt of pelvis
Innervation: Anterior (ventral) rami of thoraco-abdominal nn. (T6-T12)
