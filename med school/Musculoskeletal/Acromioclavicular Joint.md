*Plane joint between acromion of [[scapula]] and [[clavicle]]*

![[Screenshot 2025-10-11 at 10.54.09 PM.png]]

- Acromioclavicular ligaments
- Coracoclavicular ligaments – conoid and trapezoid
	- Prevents inferior and medial translation
- Coracoacromial ligament – roof of subacromial space

>[!Clinical]
>Shoulder Separation
>- Tear of ligaments connecting acromion to clavicle due to direct blow or fall

#### Subacromial Space
Space above [[glenohumeral joint]] and below acromion
- Roof = acromon and coracoacromial ligament
- Contains subacromial bursa, supraspinatus tendon, long head of biceps tendon
