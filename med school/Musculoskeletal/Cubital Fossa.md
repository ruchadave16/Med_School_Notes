#### Borders
- Superior: Line between medial and lateral epicondyles of [[humerus]]
- Lateral: brachioradialis muslce
- Medial: Pronator teres
- Roof: Skin, fascia, median cubital v., bicipital aponeurosis
- Floor: Brachialis medially, supinator laterally

#### Contents
- Median n. 
- [[Brachial artery]] → ulnar and radial a.
- Tendon of biceps brachii
- Radial n → deep and superficial
- Median cupital v. – between cephalic and basilic v, superficial in fossa

>[!Clinical]
>Brachial pulse – anterior elbow medial to biceps brachii tendon
>Venipuncture commonly at median cupital v. (careful not to hit median n. under)

#### Lymphatics
Cubital LN (anterior to medial epicondyle of [[humerus]], drains to humeral LN)
