#### Muscles
- Quadriceps femoris: 
	- Vastus medialis
		- Attach to linea aspera
	- vastus lateralis
		- Attach to linea aspera
	- Vastus intermedius
	- Rectus femoris – PA: to AIIS
	- DA: tibial tuberosity through quadriceps tendon and [[patellar ligament]]
	- Innervation: [[Femoral Nerve|femoral n.]] ← [[lumbar plexus]]
	- Function: Knee extension
- Iliopsoas
	- Tendon of iliacus + psoas major
	- DA: lesser trochanter
	- Lateral floor of [[femoral triangle]]

>[!Clinical] Tightness can cause anterior pelvic tilt and lower back pain

- Sartorius
	- Superficial muscle which crosses superolateral to inferomedial
	- Attach at ASIS
- Pectineus
	- Medial floor of [[femoral triangle]]
	- Motor: Obturator n. (L2-L4)
