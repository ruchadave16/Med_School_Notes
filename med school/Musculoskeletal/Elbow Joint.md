#### Articulations
- Trochlea → trochlear notch
- Olecranon fossa ([[humerus]]) → olecranon ([[ulna]])
- Coronoid fossa (humerus) → coronoid process (ulna)
- Radial fossa (humerus) → radial head ([[radius]])
- Capitulum (humerus) → radial head (radius)

Joint capsule around these 

**Proximal Radioulnar Joint**
- Head of [[radius]] → radial notch of [[ulna]]
- Annular ligament of radius around it to keep in place

>[!Clinical] [[Nursemaid’s Elbow]]
>Annular ligament interposed between radius and ulna

**Distal Radioulnar Joint**
- Head of ulna → ulnar notch of radius
#### Ligaments
- Radial collateral ligament – lateral epicondyle → annular ligament of radius
- Ulnar collateral ligament – medial epicondyle → ulna

>[!Clinical] Tommy John Surgery
>- Reconstruction of UCL

#### Innervation
- Branches of median, radial, ulnar n. 
#### Vasculature
- Anastomoses of radial and ulnar a. 
#### Muscles
- Flexion: biceps brachii, brachialis, brachioradialis
- Extension: triceps brachii
- Pronation: pronator teres, pronator quadratus
- Supination: supinator, biceps brachii
