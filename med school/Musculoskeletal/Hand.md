#### Fascia
- Superficial fascia
- Deep fascia
	- Palmar carpal ligament
		- Radius → Ulnar
		- Proximal and superficial to flexor retinaculum
		- Deep to plantar aponeurosis
		- Continuous with extensor retinaculum
	- Palmar aponeurosis
		- Triangular fascia at center of hand
	- Flexor retinaculum
		- Covers [[carpal tunnel]]
		- Deep to palmar aponeurosis
		- Attaches to scaphoid/trapezium → hamate hook
		- Between thenar and hypothenar eminences
#### Tendons
- Extensor expansion (dorsal)
	- Continuation of extensor digitorum
	- Lumbrical attaches here
	
>[!Clinical] Damage can happen at different locations here
>

- Flexor (ventral)
	- Tendons of flexor digitorum superficialis split at PIP joint to let flexor digitorum profundus tendon to continue 
	- Synovial sheath – shared between FDS and FDP
	- Fibrous sheath – tunnel for FDS and FDP

>[!Clinical] [[Trigger Finger]]
>Inflammation of sheath causing finger locked in extension
>Common in diabetes mellitus or RA

- Synovial Tendon Sheaths
	- Around all tendons 
	- Reduce friction
	
>[!Clinical]
>[[Tenosynovitis]] – Inflammation of synovial tendon sheaths
#### Compartments
- Hypothenar: muscles
- Thenar: muscles
- Central: flexor tendons (flexor digitorum superficialis, flexor digitorum profundus, flexor pollicis longus) and NAV
- Adductor: muscle
- Interosseous: muscle

>[!Clinical] [[Dupuytren Contracture]]
>Shortening, thickening, and fibrosis of plantar fascia
>- Pulls 4th and 5th fingers into flexion at MCP and PIP joints

#### Muscles
- Thenar muscles – move thumb
	- Adductor pollicis brevis, flexus pollicis brevis, opponens pollicis
	- Innervation: recurrent branch of median n.
	- Deep flexor pollicis brevis = ulnar n. (deep branch)
- Hypothenar muscles – 5th digit
	- Abductor digiti minimi, flexor digiti minimi brevis, opponens digiti minimi
	- Innervation: ulnar n. 
- Adductor pollicis: 
- Lumbrical (4)
	- Attach to flexor digitorum profundus
	- 2 lateral by median n. 
	- 2 medial by ulnar n. 
	- Flexion of MCP, extension of PIP and DIP
- Palmar interossei mm. (3)
	- Adductor of MCP
	- Ulnar n. 
- Dorsal interossei mm (4)
	- Abductor of MCP
	- Ulnar n.

#### Innervation
- Median n. 
	- Motor to 1st and 2nd lumbricals, most thenar m.
	- Sensory to lateral 2/3 anterior hand and 3.5 fingers and distal 1/3 posterior finger (nail)
	- Palmar cutaneous branch – central palm sensory
	- Recurrent branch – motor to all thenar except head of flexor pollicis brevis
	- Common palmar digital n. – divides into 2 proper palmar digital n. 
- Ulnar n. 
	- Superficial branch – common and proper palmar digital nn. to 5th finger and medial half of 4th
		- Only motor to palmaris brevis m. rest sensory
	- Deep branch (motor)
		- 8.5 muscles (hypothenar, 2 lumbricals, Adductor pollicis, FPB)
- Ulnar canal (Guyon’s canal)
	- Floor = hook of hamate and pisiform
	- Roof = pisohamate ligament
	- Compression point of ulnar n. 
- Superficial radial n. 
	- Over snuffbox → dorsal hand

#### Vasculature
- Ulnar a. and radial a. anastomose into superficial and deep palmar arches
- Superficial palmar arch
	- More distal than deep
	- Mainly ulnar a.
	- Forms 3 common palmar digital aa. (metacarpal 2-5)
	- Proper palmar digital a. = medial side of 5th finger
- Deep palmar arch
	- Deep to lumbricals and tendons
	- Mainly radial a.
	- Forms princeps pollicis a. – thumb and radialis indicis a. – lateral index
