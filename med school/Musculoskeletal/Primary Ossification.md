1) Starts with periosteum formation at [[diaphysis]]
	- Ossification starts at center and outsides
	- Mesenchymal cells differentiate into [[osteoblasts]]

2) Bony collar formation
	- Matrix is secreted to form shell enclosing diaphysis
	- Diffusion of nutrients to [[cartilage]]

3) Matrix calcification
	- [[Osteoclasts]] come through bony collar and blood vessels penetrate down, bringing osteoprogenitor cells
	- These become [[osteoblasts]] and make woven [[bone]]
