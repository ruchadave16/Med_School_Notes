Covers pelvic organs, which are subperitoneal

- Inferior = pelvic fascia
- Males: rectovesicle pouch between bladder and rectum
- Females
	- Rectouterine pouch (between uterus and rectum) – location of fluid collection
	- Vesicouterine pouch (between bladder and uterus)
