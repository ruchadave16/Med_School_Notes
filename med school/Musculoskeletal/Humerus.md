*[[Arm]] bone*

**Proximal**
![[Pasted image 20251008232404.png]]
- Head – articulation w glenoid fossa at [[glenohumeral joint]]
- Greater tubercle – lateral process near head
	- DA: supraspinatus, infraspinatus, teres minor
- Lesser tubercle – smaller, anterior process near head
	- DA: subscapularis
- Anatomical neck – indentation between head + tubercles
- Surgical neck – Narrow area distal to tubercles

>[!Clinical]
>Most common location of proximal humerus fracture = surgical neck
>In contact with axillary nerve + posterior humeral circumflex artery – vulnerable to injury

- Intertubercular (bicipital) groove – Between tubercles, tendon of long head of biceps brachii
	- DA: pectoralis major, latissimus dorsi, teres major
- Medial + lateral lip of groove – ridges inferior from tubercles
- Deltoid tuberosity – 1/2 down lateral shaft
	- DA: deltoid
- Radial groove – Posterior shaft w radial n. and deep brachial a. 

**Distal**

![[Pasted image 20251008232640.png]]

- Medial supracondylar ridge
- Lateral supracondylar ridge – superior from epicondyles
	- PA: Brachioradialis, Extensor carpi radialis longus
- Medial epicondyle – medial distal bump at elbow
	- PA: common flexor tendon – pronator teres, flexor carpi radialis, palmaris longus, flexor carpi ulnaris, flexor digitorum superficialis.
- Lateral epicondyle – lateral distal bump at elbow
	- PA: common extensor tendon – Extensor carpi radialis brevis, Extensor digitorum, Extensor digiti minimi, Extensor carpi ulnaris
- Capitulum – Articulate w head of [[radius]]
- Trochlea – Articulate w trochlear notch of [[ulna]]
- Coronoid fossa – coronoid process of [[ulna]]
- Olecranon fossa – olecranon process of [[ulna]] when extended
- Radial fossa – edge of head of [[radius]] when flexed
