*Protrusion of peritoneum / viscera through [[Superficial Inguinal Ring|superficial inguinal ring]]*

86% in males, most can be repaired
- Strangulated hernia means blood supply is compromised → [[Ischemia]]
- Needs surgery

#### Indirect Hernias
- ,Viscera pass through entire [[inguinal canal]] and enter deep ring
- Pass lateral to inferior epigastric a. and v. 
- Because during [[Descent of Testes]], processus vaginalis didn’t fuse
- MAJORITY hernias

#### Direct Hernias
- Bowel passes medial to inferior epigastric and bypasses deep ring
- Exits abdomen through superficial ring lateral to [[spermatic cord]]
- Due to weakness of [[inguinal triangle]]
- 1/3 hernias
