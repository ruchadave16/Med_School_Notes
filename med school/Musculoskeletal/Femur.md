- Head
- Fovea capitis femoris – indentation in head for ligamentum teres femoris
- Neck – 125 degree angle → common location for **femur fracture**
- Greater trochanter – Attachment site of muscles, palpable
- Lesser trochanter – Posterior-medial projection
- Intertrochanteric line – small ridge between greater and lesser (anterior)
- Intertrochanteric crest – ridge between greater and lesser (posterior)
- Gluteal tuberosity – bump between greater trochanter and linea aspera
- Linea aspera – ridge on posterior surface
- Pectineal line – ridge moving down  from lesser trochanter to linea aspera
- Adductor tubercle – proximal to medial condyle
- Popliteal surface – posterior part superior to condyles
- Intercondylar fossa – inferior surface between condyles
- Femoral condyles – articulate with menisci of tibial condyles to make knee joint (medial and lateral) 
- Femoral epicondyles – Bump on outside of condyles
- Angle of inclination = 125

>[!Clinical] If angle is < 120, this is [[coxa vara]]
