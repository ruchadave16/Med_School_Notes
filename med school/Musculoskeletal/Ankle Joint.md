*Between talus and [[tibia]] with fibula as splint*

- Median ligament – deltoid ligament
	- Medial malleolus, talus, sustentaculum tali, navicular
	- Stops eversion of ankle
- Lateral ligaments, stop inversion
	- Anterior talofibular ligament 
		- Lateral malleolus to anterior talus
		- **Most commonly injured in ankle sprain**
	- Calcaneofibular ligament
		- Lateral malleolus to calcaneus
	- Posterior talofibular ligament
		- Lateral malleolus to posterior talus
