Part of pharynx behind [[larynx]]
- Non-keratinized stratified squamous epithelium

**Contents**
- Posterior and lateral walls = middle and inferior pharyngeal constrictor m. 
- Opening: [[epiglottis]]
- Piriform recess: pocket on lateral walls where food can get stuck
- Pharyngeal lymphatic ring: ring of lymphatic tissue made by palatine, pharyngeal and lingual tonsils
