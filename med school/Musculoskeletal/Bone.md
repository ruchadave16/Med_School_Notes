- Periosteum – outer covering of dense connective tissue, innermost layer = osteoprogenitor cells
- Osteoprogenitor cells – spindle shaped cells that come from [[mesenchyme]]
- Endosteum – vascularized tissue lining inner surface of bone marrow cavities (traberculae)
	- Has layer of osteoprogenitor cells that become [[osteoblasts]]
- [[Osteoblasts]] – cells that make bone and secrete [[bone matrix]]
- [[Osteocytes]] – cells that maintain bone, found in spaces (lacunae)
- [[Osteoclasts]] – large, multinuclear cells that reabsorb bones
	- Form seal and secrete collagenases and enzymes into space behind osteoclast (**Howship’s Lacunae**)
