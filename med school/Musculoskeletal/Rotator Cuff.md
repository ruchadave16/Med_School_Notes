*4 scapulohumeral muscles forming rotator cuff around [[glenohumeral joint]]*

SITS
- Supraspinatus
	- Suprascapular n. (C5-6)
	- Abduction of shoulder
	- PA: supraspinous fossa of [[scapula]]
	- DA: greater tubercle of [[humerus]]

![[Screenshot 2025-10-12 at 4.28.25 PM.png]]
- Infraspinatus
	- Suprascapular n. (C5-6)
	- External rotation of shoulder
	- PA: infraspinous fossa of [[scapula]]
	- DA: greater tubercle of [[humerus]]
- Teres Minor
	- Motor: axillary n. (C5-6)
	- External rotation of shoulder
	- DA: greater tubercle of [[humerus]]
- Subscapularis
	- Upper and lower subscapular n. 
	- Internal rotation of shoulder
	- PA: subscapular fossa of [[scapula]]
	- DA: lesser tuberosity of [[humerus]]
