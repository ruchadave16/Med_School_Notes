Anteroposterior compression of [[pelvis]] commonly causes fractures of pubic rami
- Fractures of pelvic ring almost *always* multiple fractures
Lateral compression → acetabula and ilia squeezed and may break

![[Pasted image 20250920145312.png]]

Weak areas:
- Pubic rami
- Acetabula
- Sacro-iliac joints
- Alae of ilium
