L4-S4 ventral rami
- On surface of [[piriformis]]
- Supplies [[pelvis]], [[pelvic floor]], [[perineum]], [[posterior thigh]] , [[foot]]
#### Nerves
- Superior gluteal n. (L4-S1) – exits superior to piriformis m. in greater sciatic foramen
	- Motor to [[gluteal region]] – gluteus minimum/medius and TFL

>[!Clinical]
>Injury due to poor gluteal intramuscular injections
>- Motor: hip abduction weakness
>- Test: have patient stand on one leg and look at level of hip – if opposite hip drops, this is weakened (Trendelenberg sign)
>- Gait: waddling

- Inferior gluteal n. (L5-S2) – exits inferior to piriformis m. in greater sciatic foramen
	- Motor to [[gluteal region]] – gluteus maximus 

>[!Clinical]
>Injury near L5-S1 roots ([[Spondylolysthesis]]), injections, surgery
>- Motor loss of hip extension
>- If gluteus maximus weakened, trunk extends on heal strike because can’t support self
>- Difficulty getting out of chair or climbing

- [[Sciatic Nerve|Sciatic n.]] (L4-S3) – exits inferior to piriformis m. in greater sciatic foramen
	- Between ischial tuberosity and greater trochanter of [[femur]]

>[!Clinical]
>Injury deep to piriformis, poor IM injection, femoral fracture, disc herniation
>- Motor loss: knee flexion, PF and DF, INV and EV
>- Sensory loss: lateral leg, full foot
>- Difficulty standing on toes

- [[Pudendal Nerve|Pudendal n.]] (ventral rami of S2-S4) – exits inferior to piriformis m. in greater sciatic foramen, loops around sacrospinous ligament, re-enters through lesser sciatic foramen
	- Inferior rectal n. – motor to external anal sphincter
	- Perineal n. 
	- Dorsal nerve of penis/clitoris – sensory to penis/clitoris
- N. to levator ani (S3-S4) – pelvic floor muscles
- Posterior femoral cutaneous n. (S1-S3) – sensory, exits inferior to piriformis in greater sciatic foramen w sciatic n.
	- Sensory to [[posterior thigh]], lower buttock, posterior labia/scrotum
