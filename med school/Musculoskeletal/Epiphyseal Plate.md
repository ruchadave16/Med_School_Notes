#### Zones
1) Resting/Reserve – [[hyaline cartilage]]
2) Proliferation – [[chondrocytes]] divide rapidly to lengthen bone
3) Hypertrophy – older chondrocytes enlarge, compressing [[Extracellular Matrix|ECM]]
4) Calcification – calcified [[cartilage]], zone of dead chondrocytes
5) [[Ossification]] – new bone with blood vessels invading 
	- [[Osteoblasts]] replace calcified cartilage with bone
	- [[Osteoclasts]] digest calcified cartilage
