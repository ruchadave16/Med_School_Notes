**Proximal**
- Olecranon process – posterior projection
	- DA: triceps brachii
- Coronoid process – anterior projection
- Trochlear notch – concavity between olecranon + coronoid, articulation w [[humerus]]
- Ulnar tuberosity – distal to coronoid process
	- DA: brachialis 
- Radial notch – lateral notch w head of [[radius]]

**Distal**
- Head – distal part articulating with articular disc of distal radio-ulnar joint
- Ulnar styloid process – attachment for ulnar collateral ligament
