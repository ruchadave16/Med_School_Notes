#### Investing Layer
- Around whole [[neck]]
- Superficial and deep layers enclosing [[sternocleidomastoid]] and [[trapezius]] muscles
- Attaches to skull, cervical vertebra spinous processes superiorly
- Sternum, clavicles, scapula inferiorly

![[Drawing 2025-10-21 00.43.07.excalidraw]]
#### Pretracheal Layer
- Deeper to investing layer
- Superficial with 4 pairs of infrahyoid muscles
- Deep part
	- [[Thyroid gland]]
	- [[Trachea]]
	- [[Esophagus]]
	
![[Pasted image 20251021004517.png]]
#### [[Carotid Sheath]]
- Tube connected to [[cranial cavity]] superiorly and [[mediastinum]] inferiorly
- Connected bilateral by **alar fascia**
- Behind buccopharyngial fascia, in front of prevertebral fascia
#### Prevertebral Fascia
- Around [[vertebral column]]
- Anterior: longus colli and longus capitis
- Lateral: scalene muscles
- Posterior: levator scapulae
- [[Cervical sympathetic trunk]]
