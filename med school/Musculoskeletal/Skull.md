#### Terminology
- Cranial bones – brain and 5 senses
- Facial bones – digestive and respiratory
- Wormian (sutural bones) – irregular bones along [[sutures]]
- Cranial vault – upper part
- Cranial base – inferior part of skull
- Facial skeleton – bones making up face
- Acoustic skeleton – bones making up ear
