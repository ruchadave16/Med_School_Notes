*Dense band made of aponeurosis of external oblique m. of [[Anterior Abdominal Wall|anterior abdominal wall]]*

From [[ASIS]] to pubic tubercule 

![[Screenshot 2025-09-18 at 4.31.48 PM.png]]

- Forms lacunar ligament by attaching lateral to pubic tubercule
	- Medial boundary of [[inguinal canal]]
- Forms pectineal ligament by running along pecten pubis
