#### Ischioanal Fossa
- Wedge shaped with fat – NO peritoneal membrane
- Inferiorly – skin
- Lateral – obturator internus + ischium
- Medial – levator ani, external anal sphincter
- Posterior – sacrotuberous ligament
- Anterior – recesses of ischio-anal fossa
- Contains fat, inferior rectal n, a, v, pudendal canal

#### Pudendal Canal
- In fascia of obturator internus
- Lesser sciatic notch 
- Has internal pudendal a, v, pudendal n.
