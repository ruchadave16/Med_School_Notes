*smooth [[Endoplasmic Reticulum|ER]] of muscles*

Site of $Ca^{2+}$ storage and release for [[excitation-contraction coupling]]

- Forms interconnected meshwork of tubules around myofibrils
- Terminal cisternae is region near [[T Tubules]] which is part of a triad
- Membrane contains $Ca^{2+}$-ATPase (calcium pump) which transfers calcium from intracellular fluid to inside of SR
- Contains $Ca^{2+}$ bound loosely to [[calsequestrin]]
- Contains $Ca^{2+}$ release channel called [[ryanodine receptor]]

##### Method of Release
Membrane depolarization → calcium release from SR → Increase in intracellular calcium → contraction of muscle
