1) Appositional Growth
	- Differentiation in [[perichondrium]] (outer layer)
	- [[Chondroblasts]] lay down matrix on outer edge
2) Interstitial Growth
	- Mitotic division of existing [[chondrocytes]]
	- Internal growth
	- [[Extracellular Matrix|ECM]] secreted into space between cells

#### Process
1) Start with [[mesenchymal cells]] – form chondrogenic layer
2) Differentiate into [[chondroblasts]]
3) Chondroblasts make and secrete [[Extracellular Matrix|ECM]]
4) Surrounded by matrix, chondroblasts = chondrocytes
5) Superficial mesenchyme → [[perichondrium]]
6) Chondrocytes divide and make ground substance and [[collagen]]

#### Regulation
Stimulation
- GH ([[Growth Hormone|somatotropin]]) – division of chondrocytes
- [[Thyroxin]] – [[hypertrophy]] of chondrocytes
- [[Testosterone]]

Inhibited
- [[Corticosteroids]]
- [[Estrogen]]
