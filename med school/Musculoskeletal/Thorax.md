*Thorax: Part of trunk between neck and abdomen containing [[Thoracic cavity]], contents, and [[Thoracic Wall|thoracic wall]]*

[[Thoracic Cavity]]
- [[Pleura|Pleural cavity]] surrounding [[Lungs|lung]]s
- [[Mediastinum]] occupied by visceral structures of thorax not including lungs

[[Thoracic Wall|Thoracic Wall]]
- Thoracic cage: 12 pairs of [[ribs]] and costal cartilages, [[sternum]], 12 thoracic [[vertebrae]], IV discs
