#### Muscles
- Hamstrings (Biceps femoris, semitendinosus, semimebranosus)
	- Start at ischial tuberosity of [[pelvis]]
	- Semimembranosus and semitendinosus insert on medial [[tibia]]
	- Biceps femoris (long) attach to [[fibula]] head, short (adductor tubercle)
	- Innervation: Sciatic n. 
	
![[Drawing 2025-10-06 00.17.01.excalidraw]]

>[!Clinical] Hamstring strain: Tear of tendon from ischial tuberosity 
>- Pain, bruising, hematoma

![[Screenshot 2025-10-02 at 9.20.37 PM.png]]

#### Innervation
- [[Sciatic Nerve|Sciatic n.]] (L4-S3) – motor to posterior thigh
- Posterior femoral cutaneous n. (S1-S3) – sensory to posterior thigh

#### Vasculature
- Branches of deep [[Femoral Artery|femoral a.]] come around here (perforating branches) but no major vessels run here
