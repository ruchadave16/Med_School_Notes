##### Jugular Notch
- Top of manubrium, between clavicles
- At vertebra **T2**
##### Manubrium
- Top of sternum
- Articulations for 1st rib and [[clavicle]]
- [[Aortic arch]] is deep to left border
- Brachiocephalic veins merge to become superior
- [[Vena cava]] deep to right border

##### Sternal Angle (Angle of Louis)
- Between manubrium and sternum body
- At IV disc between T4-T5
- Marks 2nd pair of costal cartilage → *use this as landmark when counting since 1st rib is not palpable easily*
	- 2nd intercostal space right below, marks T2 [[dermatome]]

##### Sternum Body
- T5 - T9 
- Longest part of midline

##### Xiphoid Process
- Inferior part of sternum, T9-T10

>[!Clinical]
>Guide for CPR hand positioning
>To access [[pericardial]] space, needle passed just inferior to xiphoid
