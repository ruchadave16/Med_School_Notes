#### Boundaries
##### Superior Thoracic Apeture
*Called **Thoracic Outlet***
T1 vertebra posteriorly
Medial border of 1st rib and costal cartilage
Superior border of manubrium anteriorly

**Content**
- [[Interscalene triangle]]: [[Subclavian Artery|subclavian a.]] and [[brachial plexus]] trunks
- Subclavian v. 
- [[Trachea]], [[esophagus]], apex of [[lungs]]
- [[Vagus Nerve]], [[Phrenic Nerve]], [[sympathetic trunk]]

>[!Clinical] [[Thoracic Outlet Syndrome]]
>Neurovasculation compression, specifically C8 and T1 spinal nerves, [[Subclavian Artery|subclavian a.]] and v. 
>→ Causes: cervical rib, Pancoast tumor, hypertropy of scalene muscles
>→ Symptoms: medial forearm and hand numbness (compression of nerves), swelling of arm (compression of subclavian vessels), [[Horner’s syndrome]] (compression of sympathetic trunk)
>

##### Inferior Thoracic Apeture
Closed by [[diaphragm]]
T12 vertebra and 11th and 12th pair of rib posteriorly
Costal margin anterolaterally
Xiphoid process anteriorly

#### Internal Features 
##### Muscles of Thoracic Wall
###### Extrinsic Muscles
- Distal attachment, action outside thorax
	- Anterolateral: pectoralis major, pectoralis minor, serratus anterior, subclavius
###### Intrinsic Muscles
- Start and end on thoracic wall, action on thoracic wall
- External and internal intercostal muscle (between [[ribs]]) and subcostal (below ribs - 12)
- Transverseus thoracis m. → Deep to [[sternum]]
- Subcostal, Serratus posterior superior and inferior, levator costarum found on posterior thoracic wall

*Endothoracic Fascia = internal lining of thoracic wall*
##### Joints of Thoracic Cage
- Costovertebral Joint: Head of [[Ribs|rib]] and vertebral body
	- Ribs 2-10 articulate with body of same level or one above (Rib 7 with T7 and T6)
	- Ribs 1, 11, 12 only with 1 vertebral body
- Costotransverse Joint: tubercule of rib with transverse process of same [[Vertebrae|vertebra]]
- Sternocostal Joint: costal cartilage w [[sternum]]
	- Rib 1 → manubrium
	- Rib 2 → sternal angle
	- Rib 2-7 → sternal body

#### Innervation
Neurovascular bundle run in intercostal space inferior to [[Ribs|rib]] and within costal groove
→ Intercostal n.
→ Anterior and posterior intercostal a. and v. 

12 pairs of [[thoracic spinal nerves]] based on respective thoracic level

##### Venous:
Anterior Intercostal Space
- Anterior intercostal vv → musculophrenic OR internal thoracic v. → brachiocephalic v → SVC

Posterior Intercostal Space
- R. posterior intercostal vv. → azygos v. → SVC
- L. posterior intercostal vv. → hemiazygos v. OR accessory hemiazygos v. → azygos v → SVC
