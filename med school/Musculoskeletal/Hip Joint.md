*Ball-and-socket joint ([[Synovial Joints|synovial joint]])*

- Head of [[femur]] attaches to acetabulum of [[pelvis]]
- [[Hyaline Cartilage|Hyaline cartilage]] – articulate cartilage
- **Acetabular labrum** – [[fibrocartilage]] that increases the depth of the acetabulum 
- Joint capsule – acetabular rim → intertrochanteric line 

#### Ligaments
- Iliofemoral ligament – anterior ligament, stops hyperextension
- Pubofemoral – more inferior, stops abduction
- Ischiofemoral – back to anterior femur, stops extension and medial rotation

#### Blood Supply 
- Articular branch of obturator a. → runs in ligament of head of femur
- Medial circumflex femoral a. → supplies femur head and neck
- Lateral circumflex femoral a. → anastomosis with medial circumflex femoral a. 

>[!Clinical] [[Hip Dislocation]] 
>- Usually traumatic, 90% is posterior
>- Can potentially injure [[Sciatic Nerve|sciatic n.]]

>[!Clinical] Femoral neck fracture
>- Common in older patients with osteoporotic bones
>- Loss of blood to femur head (medial circumflex femoral a.) → avascular necrosis
