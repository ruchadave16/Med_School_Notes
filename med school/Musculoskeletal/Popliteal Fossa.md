*Diamond shaped location in back of knee*

#### Borders
- Superior: semimembranosus (medial), biceps femoris (lateral)
- Inferior: gastrocnemius
- Roof: fascia + skin
- Floor: femur + popliteal m. 

#### Contents (superficial → deep)
- Small sephanous v.
- Tibia and common fibular n. 
- [[Popliteal Artery|Popliteal a.]] and v. with branches
- Posterior cutaneous branch of thigh
- Lymph nodes

#### Popliteus Muscle
- For knee flexion
