#### Categories
1) Fibrous (synarthrosis) Joints – Bones bound by fibrous connective tissue
	- [[Sutures]]: Interlocking bone with short fibers, no movement
	- Syndesmosis: Broad sheet or ligament, little/no movement
2) Cartilaginous (amphiarthrosis) Joints – Bones held by [[cartilage]]
	- Symphysis: Bones held by [[fibrocartilage]]
	- Synchondrosis: Bones held by [[hyaline cartilage]]
	- Little movement
3) [[Synovial Joints]] (diarthrosis) – Bones joined with joint cavity of fluid
