Fibrous component encasing [[Rectus Abdominis Muscle|rectus abdominis m.]] and [[Pyramidalis Muscle|pyramidalis m.]], superior and inferior epigastric, lymphatics, distal parts of thoraco-abdominal nerves (T7-T12)
- Linea alba separates bilateral sheaths
- Aponeuroses of 3 flat abdomianl muscles combine to make this

#### Arcuate Line
- Crescent line showing transition between superior 3/4 of posterior rectus sheath (has aponeurotic posterior wall) and inferior 1/4 (only transversalis fascia)
- Inferior epigastric enter below this line
