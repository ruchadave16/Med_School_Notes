#### Boundaries
- Anterior: Border of [[Sternocleidomastoid|SCM]]
- Posterior: Border of trapezius
- Inferior: Middle 3rd of [[clavicle]]
- Apex: Meeting of SCM and trapezius at superior nuchal line of [[occipital bone]]
- Roof: Investing layer of [[Deep Cervical Fascia|deep cervical fascia]]
- Floor: Muscles covered by prevertebral layer of [[Deep Cervical Fascia|deep cervical fascia]]

#### Subdivisions
- [[Omohyoid]] divides these
- [[Occipital Triangle]]
- [[Subclavian Triangle]]
