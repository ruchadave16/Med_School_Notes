S2-S4

Ventral rami → greater sciatic foramen → crosses sacrospinous ligament → perineum through lesser sciatic foramen → pudendal canal

- Inferior rectal n. – external anal sphincter
- Perineal n. – superficial pouch
	- Deep perineal n. – muscle of superficial and deep pouch
	- Superficial perineal n.
- Dorsal n. of penis/clitoris
	- Continues into deep perineal pouch

>[!Clinical]
>Pudendal nerve block – Put into pudendal canal to anesthetize vagina and perineum
>Used during labor
