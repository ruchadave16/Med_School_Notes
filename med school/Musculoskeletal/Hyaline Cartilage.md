*Glass like cartilage*
- Most common, helps with support and flexibility
- Found in [[joints]], [[ribs]], [[larynx]], [[trachea]], and epiphyseal plates of long bones

![[Screenshot 2025-09-29 at 7.39.43 PM.png]]

- Territorial matrix – dark blue because high concentration of [[glycosaminoglycans]]
- Interterritorial matrix – lower GAG concentration so less blue
- [[Calcification]] during [[bone formation]] and with age

![[Screenshot 2025-09-29 at 8.06.24 PM.png]]
#### Matrix
- Type II [[collagen]], [[proteoglycans]], and [[glycoproteins]]
- CANNOT resolve with light microscopy
#### [[Perichondrium]]
- Outer surface of cartilage
- Vascularized dense connective tissue (Type I collagen) ← [[Fibroblasts|fibroblasts]]
- Inner layer = chondrogenic layer – multipotent cells which turn into [[chondroblasts]] for **appositional growth**

>[!Warning] EXCEPTION: No perichondrium in articular cartilage – joints
