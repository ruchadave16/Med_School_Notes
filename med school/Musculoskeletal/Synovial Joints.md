*Adjacent bones held together by joint capsule with outer fibrous and inner serous synovial membrane*

- Cavity is potential space with synovial fluid
- Articular cartilage ([[Hyaline Cartilage]]) – covers surface of bone
- [[Ligaments]] – dense connective tissue providing stability to stop excessive movement

6 main types: 
- Hinge
- Pivot
- Saddle
- Condyloid
- Ball and socket
- Plane

>[!Clinical]
>Sprain – injury of [[ligament]]
>Strain – stretching or tearing of [[Muscle Tissue|muscle]] or [[tendon]]
>[[Arthritis]] – joint inflammation
>[[Osteoarthritis]] – Mechanical wear and tear of joint where [[Hyaline Cartilage|hyaline cartilage]] is destroyed
>[[Tendinopathy]] – Tendon bears more load than it can withstand
