- 2 rows of 4s 

**Proximal** (lateral thumb → medial)
- Scaphoid
- Lunate
- Triquetrum
- Pisiform

**Distal**
- Trapezium
- Trapezoid
- Capitate
- Hamate – hook of hamate

>[!Clinical] Common carpal fractures
>- Scaphoid fracture when falling onto outstretched hands
>	- Can damage radial a., avascular [[necrosis]] of scaphoid
>- Hamate fracture
>	- Can damage ulnar a. and n. 


Scaphoid and capitate have 1 blood vessel so higher chance of avascular necrosis
