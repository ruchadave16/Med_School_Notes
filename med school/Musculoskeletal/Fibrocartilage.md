inc*Strongest [[cartilage]], tough and dense*

- Found in [[intervertebral discs]], [[pubic symphysis]], [[meniscus]]
- **No [[perichondrium]]**
- Round [[chondrocytes]] in rows parallel to Type I collagen
#### Matrix
- Type I AND bundles of II [[collagen]]
