*Bone with multiple attachments but none to axial skeleton*

- Acromion: Prominant ridge
	- PA: middle deltoid, middle trapezius
- Coracoid process: Attachment for 3 muscles, anterior palpation
	- PA: Pectoralis minor, coracobrachialis, biceps brachii short head
- Supraglenoid tubercle
	- PA: biceps brachii (LH)
- Infraglenoid tubercle
	- PA: infraspinatus
- Glenoid cavity – articulation with [[humerus]]
- Superior angle
	- PA: levator scapula
- Inferior angle – inferior tip (T7 vertebral when adducted)
	- PA: teres major
- Lateral border
- Medial border (T3 vertebral when adducted)
	- PA: rhomboids, serratus anterior
- Spine of scapula – horizontal ridge
	- PA: posterior deltoid, lower trapezius
- Supraspinous fossa – above spine of scapula
	- PA: supraspinatus
- Infraspinous fossa – below spine of scapula, posterior
- Subscapular fossa – concave anterior
	- PA: subscapularis

#### Vasculature
Arterial anastamoses to allow for collateral circulation
- 1st part of [[Subclavian Artery|subclavian a.]] → 3rd part of [[Axillary Artery|axillary a.]]

#### Muscles
- Elevation: Levator scapulae, upper trapezius
- Depression: Pectoralis minor, lower trapezius
- Protract: Serratus anterior
- Retract: Rhomboids, middle trapezius
- Upwards rotation: Upper trapezius, lower trapezuis, serratus anterior
- Downwards rotation: Levator scapula, rhomboids
