#### Superficial
- Connect to medial epicondyle by common flexor tendon
- Pronator teres – medial boundary of [[Cubital Fossa|cubital fossa]]
	- Pronation of elbow
- Flexor carpi radialis 
	- Flexion and abduction of wrist
- Palmaris longus – thin tendon superficial attaches to palmar aponeurosis
- Flexor carpi ulnaris
	- Flexion and adduction of wrist
	- DA: hook of hamate

#### Intermediate
- Flexor digitorum superficialis
	- MCP, PIP flexion
	- DA: middle phalanx 2-5
- 2 proximal heads, distal attachment to fingers 2-5
- Median and ulnar n. pass between 2 heads

#### Deep
- Don’t cross elbow
- Flexor digitorum profundus – ulna → distal phalanx 2-5
	- MCP, PIP, DIP flexion
- Flexor pollicis longus – radius → palmar distal phalanx of thumb
	- Thumb movement
- Pronator quadratus – doesn’t cross wrist
	- Pronation of elbow
