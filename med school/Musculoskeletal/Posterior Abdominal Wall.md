#### Muscles
- Quadratus lumborum m. 
	- Lowest rib → [[iliac crest]]
	- Crossed by subcostal n., lumbar plexus anteriorly
- Psoas major m. 
	- Fusiform muscle
	- Pierced by [[lumbar plexus]]
- Iliacus m. 
	- Merges with psoas to make iliopsoas m.

#### Arteries
- Abdominal [[aorta]] – bifurcates into common iliac a. at L4 vertebral
- [[Celiac Trunk|Celiac trunk]], [[Superior Mesenteric Artery|SMA]], [[Inferior Mesenteric Artery|IMA]] – branches of abdominal aorta supplying foregut, midgut, hindgut
- Middle adrenal, renal, gonadal aa. – paired visceral branches
- Paired (somatic) branches
	- Inferior phrenic, subcostal, lumbar
	- Unpaired – median sacral a.

>[!Clinical] [[Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm]]: Local enlargenment of aorta by congenital/acquired weakness of arterial wall
>- 90% mortality if rupture

#### Veins
Drain to [[Inferior Vena Cava|IVC]] direct/indirectly
- Gonadal veins
	- R gonadal v. → IVC
	- L gonadal v. → L renal v
- Adrenal veins
	- R adrenal v. → IVC
	- L adrenal v. → L renal v.
- Inferior phrenic v. 
	- Both to IVC

#### Lymphatics

R and L lumbar lymph nodes (alongside aorta + IVC)
- Posterior abdominal wall directly to here
- Descending colon, pelvis, lower limbs through inferior mesenteric + common iliac lymph nodes → R and L lumbar
- Efferent from lumbar lymph nodes make lumbar lymphatic trunks

GI, liver, spleen, pancreas
- Inferior mesenteric, superior mesenteric, and celiac lymph nodes
- Efferent make intestinal lymphatic trunks

[[Thoracic duct]]
- All inferior to diaphragm (deep) and umbilicus (superficial)
- Cisterna chyli (about T12 vertebral)

>[!Clinical] Since cisterna chyli in upper abdomen near aortic hiatus, clockwise rotation of fluid in abdomen
>- Lymph to RUQ to enter cisterna chyli
>- Intraabdominal infection can cause perihepatic abscess because of rotation
>- Stage IV GI cancers localized to RUQ secondary to rotation

#### Innervation
- Subcostal n. (T12) – Inferolaterally on quadratus lumborum m. 
	- Supplies [[anterior abdominal wall]]
- [[Lumbar plexus]] – Ventral rami L1-L4
	- Ilioinguinal (motor, sensory to scrotum + superior thigh) and iliohypogastric n. (motor, sensory to hypogastric) (from L1)
	- Enter posterior → medial arcuate line
	- Pierce transversus abdominis m. 
- Genitofemoral n. (L1-L2) – pierces psoas m., inferiorly on anterior surface
	- Genital branch enters [[deep inguinal ring]] and supplies [[cremaster m.]], sensory to skin of labia majora + anterolateral scrotum
	- Femoral branch to skin over [[femoral triangle]]
- Lateral femoral cutaneous n. (L2-L3) – Sensory to anterolateral thigh
- Obturator n. (L2-L4)
- [[Femoral Nerve|Femoral n.]](L2-L4)
- Lumbosacral trunk (L4-L5)
