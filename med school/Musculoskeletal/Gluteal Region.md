*Posterior to pelvis and inferior to iliac crests*

#### Landmarks
- Greater trochanter of [[femur]] – lateral border, 1 hand below iliac crest
- Gluteal fold – inferior border
- Intergluteal cleft – division between 2 buttocks
- Posterior superior iliac spine – part of [[pelvis]] marked by dimple in skin
- [[Sacrum]]
- Ischial tuberosity – palpable deep to inferomedial gluteus maximus

![[Pasted image 20251002210738.png]]

#### Muscles
**Superficial**
- Gluteus maximus: hip extensor 
	- Posteriorly attach to sacrum
- Gluteus medius and gluteus minimus
	- , DA = greater trochanter
- Tensor fascia latae (TFL) – Ilium posterior to ASIS through IT band to Gerdy’s tubercle

![[Drawing 2025-10-05 23.59.37.excalidraw]]



**Deep**
- Piriformis 
	- PA: Anteriorly attach to sacrum
	- DA: greater trochanter
- Superior gemellus
- Obturator internus
- Inferior gemellus
- Obturator externus 
- Quadratus femoris

#### Additional Structures
- Sacrospinous ligament
- Sacrotuberous ligament
- Trochanteric bursa – separates gluteus maximus from greater trochanter
- Ischial bursa – separates gluteus maximus from ischial tuberosity 
- Gluteofemoral bursa – separates IT band from vastus lateralis

>[!Clinical] [[Trochanteric bursitis]] – inflammation of trochanteric bursa due to repeated actions 
>- Deep diffuse pain to lateral thigh
>- Tenderness over greater trochanter along IT band

>[!Clinical] Ischial bursitis – Inflammation of bursa between ischial tuberosity and gluteus maximus

#### Innervation
[[Sacral Plexus]]: 
- Superior gluteal n. (L4-S1) → gluteus minimum/medius and TFL **motor**
- Inferior gluteal n. (L5-S2) → gluteus maximus **motor**
- Posterior femoral cutaneous n. (S1-S3) → sensory to posterior thigh and gluteal region
#### Vasculature
- Superior and inferior gluteal a. and v. ← internal iliac a. 

>[!Clinical] Intragluteal Injections
>- Superior lateral quadrant to avoid neurovasculature
>- Medial can damage sciatic n. 

>[!Clinical] [[Piriformis Syndrome]]
>- Sciatic n. irritation due to contraction, hypertrophy, or spasms of piriformis m. 
>- Pain in buttocks, pain radiating down leg
>- Differential Dx: herniated lumbar disc
