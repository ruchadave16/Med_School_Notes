1) Auricle (pinna)
	- Collects sound waves to [[tympanic membrane]]
	- Made of irregular plates of [[elastic cartilage]]
	- Tragus, antitragus, helix, antihelix, lobule

	- *Vasculature*: posterior auricular a. and superficial temporal a. ← [[External Carotid Artery|external carotid a.]]
	- *Sensory Innervation*: Auriculotemporal n. ← [[Mandibular Nerve|CN V3]], (deep) auricular branch of [[Vagus Nerve|CN X]], (deep) auricular branch of [[Facial Nerve|CN VII]], 

2) External Auditory Meatus
	- Brings sound waves to tympanic membrane
	- Stratified squamous epithelium
		- Outer (lateral) 1/3 has [[elastic cartilage]]
			- Hair follicles, sebaceous glands, apocrine sweat glands
		- Medial 2/3 is temporal bone

- *Sensory Innervation*: Auriculotemporal n. ← [[Mandibular Nerve|CN V3]], auricular branch of [[Vagus Nerve|CN X]]

3) [[Tympanic Membrane]]
	- Separates outer and middle ear
