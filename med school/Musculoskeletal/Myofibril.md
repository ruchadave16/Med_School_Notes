##### Thick Filament 
A band at center of [[sacromere]] contains myosin → 6 polypeptide chains with 1 pair of heavy and 2 pairs of light
- Each myosin molecule has 2 heads and 1 tail
- Heads bind [[ATP]] and [[actin]] → cross-bridge formation
- Makes up A-band of [[Sacromere|sacromere]]
- In striated muscle, half heads face one direction and other half the other direction
- Tails towards M-band

##### Thin Filament
Anchored to Z lines
Present in I bands
- Combine with thin filaments in A band
- Contain **[[actin]], [[tropomyosin]], [[troponin]]**


![[Screenshot 2025-08-07 at 5.41.00 PM.png]]

##### Other Proteins:
- Titin:
	- From Z-line towards M-line to center thick filament in middle of 2 Z-lines
	- Prevents excessive stretching by providing passive restoring force
- $\alpha$-Actinin: 
	- Anchors thin actin filaments to Z-line
- Myomesin
	- M-line proteins that hold thick filaments and keep tail ends together
- Desmin
	- Intermediate filament of muscle
	- Surrounds sacromere at Z-line making lattice to attach sacromeres together
- Dystrophin
	- Links cytoskeleton to ECM
