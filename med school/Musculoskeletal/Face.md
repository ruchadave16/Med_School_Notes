#### Vasculature
- Facial a. ← [[External Carotid Artery|external carotid a.]]
	- Deep to [[submandibular gland]]
	- crosses [[mandible]] inferior border
	- Towards medial eye

			- Branches
				- Submental a. – chin
				- Lateral nasal a. – [[nose]]
				- Superior and inferior labial a. – upper and lower [[lips]]
				- Angular a. – medial eye (terminal)
	
	- Transverse facial a. ← superficial temporal a. ← [[External Carotid Artery|external carotid a.]]
		- [[Parotid Gland|Parotid gland]]
	- Supraorbital and supratrochlear a. ← ophthalmic a. ← [[Internal Carotid Artery|internal carotid a.]]
		- Upper eyelid and forehead
	- [[Maxillary Artery|Maxillary a.]] 
		- Deep face and [[pharynx]] and [[nasal cavity]]

VEINS
- Facial v. → drains to [[internal jugular v.]]
- Retromandibular v. → deep areas 
	- Part to external jugular v. 
	- Part to facial v.

- Facial v. anastamose with superior ophthalmic v. = [[cavernous sinus]]

>[!Clinical]
>Danger Triangle of face: Place around nose and upper lip where infection can spread between connection of facial v. and cavernous sinus
