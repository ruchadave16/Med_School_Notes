#### Location
- Supracondylar ridge: Brachioradialis, Extensor carpi radialis longus
- Lateral epicondyle (Lateral → Medial) – Common extensor tendon
	- Abductor pollicis longus (APL), Extensor pollicis brevis (EPB), Extensor pollicis longus (EPL), Extensor indicis (EI)

#### Action
- Extend and abduct/adduct hand
	- Extensor carpi radialis longus (abduction, DA = dorsal 2nd metacarpal), Extensor carpi radialis brevis (abduction, DA = dorsal 3rd metacarpal), Extensor carpi ulnaris (adduction, DA = dorsal 5th metacarpal).
- Extend medial 4 fingers
	- Extensor digitorum (MCP), Extensor indicis, Extensor digiti minimi
- Extend / abduct thumb
	- Abductor pollicis longus (DA = dorsal 1st metacarpal), Extensor pollicis brevis (DA = dorsal proximal thumb phalange, Extensor pollicis longus (DA = dorsal distal thumb phalange)
- Supinator m. 
	- Supination of elbow
- Brachioradialis m. 
	- Flexion of elbow
