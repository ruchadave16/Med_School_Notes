*Fibrous Hinge joint*
- Mandibular condyle articulates with mandibular fossa

**Articular Disc**
- Divides into 2 compartments
- Lateral pterygoid m. and temporal bone attachments

**Movement**
- Closed jaw, disc and mandible condyle is inside temporal bone
- Open jaw, both are under articular tubercle of temporal bone

>[!Clinical]
>TMJ Dislocation
>- Mandibular condyle moves anterior to articular tubercle of temporal bone 
>- Jaw is locked in place
>- Need to depress posterior mandible inferiorly and push posteriorly
