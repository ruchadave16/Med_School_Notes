*Protein fibers within a hydrogel forming cross-linked matrix, form of [[Connective Tissue|connective tissue]]*

- Avascular, nutrients through diffusion
- [[Perichondrium]] – Dense connective tissue around cartilage that is vascularized
#### Types
1) [[Hyaline Cartilage]]
2) [[Elastic Cartilage]]
3) [[Fibrocartilage]]

#### Cells
- [[Chondroblasts]] = immature cartilage cells
	- Formed from multipotent [[mesenchymal]] cell differentiation
	- Can add to cartilage on outside surfaces (appositional growth)
- [[Chondrocytes]] = mature cartilage cells in lacuna of [[Extracellular Matrix|ECM]]
	- Secrete [[Extracellular Matrix|ECM]]
	- Divide within cartilage to expand it through interstitial growth 
- Territorial matrix = area near chondrocytes
- Interterritorial matrix = area between chondrocytes
