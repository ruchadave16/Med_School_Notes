**Anterior**
- Supraorbital foramen
	- Supraorbital n. off of ophthalmic n. ([[Ophthalmic Nerve|CN V1]])
	- Supraorbital a. ← ophthalmic a.
- Zygomaticofacial foramen
	- Zygomaticofacial n. off of maxillary n. ([[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]])
- Infraorbital foramen
	- Infraorbital n. off of maxillary n. ([[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]])
- Mental foramen
	- Mental branch off of mandibular n. ([[Mandibular Nerve|CN V3]])
- Nasolacrimal canal – medial eye → inferior meatus in [[nasal cavity]]
- [[Optic Canal]]
	- [[Optic Nerve|Optic n.]] (CN II)
	- Ophthalmic a. ← [[Internal Carotid Artery|internal carotid a.]]
- Superior orbital fissure
	- Oculomotor n. ([[CN III]]) – superior and inferior branches
	- Trochlear n. ([[CN IV]]) 
	- Ophthalmic n. ([[Ophthalmic Nerve|CN V1]]) 
		- Divides outside into lacrimal n., frontal n, and nasociliary n.
	- Abducens n. ([[CN VI]])
	- Ophthalmic v.  (superior and inferior)
- Inferior orbital fissure
	- [[Maxillary Nerve|Maxillary n.]] (CN V2) – zygomatic and infraorbital n. 
	- Infraorbital vessels
- Zygomaticoorbital fissure
	- Zygomatic branch of [[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]]
- Mandibular foramen
	- Inferior alveolar n. off of mandibular n. ([[Mandibular Nerve|CN V3]])
	- Inferior alveolar vessels

**Lateral**
- Zygomaticotemporal foramen
	- Zygomaticotemporal n. off of maxillary n. ([[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]])
- Sphenopalatine foramen
	- Nasopalatine nerves
	- Sphlenopalatine vessels
- Pterygomaxillary fissure
	- [[Maxillary Artery|Maxillary a.]]
- External acoustic meatus

![[Screenshot 2025-10-29 at 3.22.49 PM.png]]

**Basal**
- Incisive foramen
	- Sphlenopalatine vessels
	- Nasopalatine n. off of maxillary n. ([[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]])
- Greater palatine foramen
	- Greater palatine n. off of maxillary n. ([[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]])
	- Greater palatine vessels
- Lesser palatine foramen
	- Lesser palatine n. off of maxillary n. ([[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]])
	- Lesser palatine vessels
- Foramen rotundum
	- [[Maxillary Nerve|maxillary n.]] (CN V2)
- Foramen ovale
	- [[Mandibular Nerve|mandibular n.]] (CN V3)
- Foramen spinosum
	- Middle meningeal a.
- Carotid canal
	- [[Internal carotid a.]]
	- Internal carotid plexus
- Jugular foramen
	- Internal petrosal sinus  to internal jugular v.
	- Sigmoid sinus to internal jugular v.
	- [[Glossopharyngeal Nerve|Glossopharyngeal n.]] (CN IX)
	- [[Vagus Nerve|Vagus n.]] (CN X)
	- Spinal [[Accessory Nerve|Accessory n.]] (CN XI)
- Hypoglosseal canal
	- [[Hypoglossal nerve]] (CN XII)
- Stylomastoid foramen
	- [[Facial Nerve|Facial n.]] (CN VII)
- Internal acoustic meatus
	- [[Facial Nerve|Facial n.]]  (CN VII)
	- Vestibulocochlear n. ([[Vestibulocochlear Nerve|CN VIII]])
- Foramen magnum
	- [[Medulla oblongata]]
	- [[Vertebral Artery|Vertebral a.]] 
	- Spinal accessory n. root
- Foramen lacerum 
	- Greater petrosal n. passes over this
- Pterygoid canal
	- N. of pterygoid canal → Vidian n.
- Petrotympanic fissure
	- Chorda tympani n. off of [[Facial Nerve|facial n.]] (CN VII)
- Facial canal hiatus
	- Greater petrosal n. off of [[Facial Nerve|facial n.]] (CN VII)
- Cribriform plate of ethmoid bone
	- Olfactory n. ([[Olfactory Nerve|CN I]])
