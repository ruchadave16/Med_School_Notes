#### Muscles
Attach to pubic bone and distally to linea aspera of [[femur]]
Supplied by obturator a. and v. ← internal iliac a.

- Adductor longus
	- Attach to linea aspera

>[!Clinical] [[Rider’s strain]] due to forced abduction while other adductors are contracting on adductor longus

- Adductur brevis
	- Attach to linea aspera
	- Deep to adductor longus and pectineus

![[Pasted image 20251002164744.png]]

- Adductor Magnus
	- Deep to adductor brevis and longus, very large
	- Attach to linea aspera (adductor / anterior)
	- Attach to adductor tubercle
- Gracilis
- Obturator externus
	- Covers obturator foramen

- Motor: obturator n. of [[lumbar plexus]] (L2-L4)
- Sensory: obturator n.
