*Similar to hyaline but with [[elastic fibers]] making it flexible*

Found in [[pinna of ear]], [[auditory tubes]], [[epiglottis]]

- More [[chondrocytes]]
- [[Perichondrium]] present

![[Screenshot 2025-09-29 at 8.10.36 PM.png]]
#### Matrix
- Type II [[collagen]] and [[elastin]]
- [[Tissue Staining|Elastin stain]] to visualize
- [[Extracellular Matrix|ECM]] is much less abundant than hyaline
