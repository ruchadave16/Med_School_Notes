#### Anterior
RUN between body and transverse processes of cervical vertebrae
Act to flex anteriorly

- Longus colli
- Longus capitis

#### Lateral
Roots of [[brachial plexus]] between anterior and middle scalene
- Anterior scalene m: transverse process and 1st rib
- Middle scalene m: transverse process and 1st and 2nd rib
- Posterior scalene m: transverse process and 2nd rib

#### Vasculature
- [[Vertebral Artery|Vertebral a.]]
- Subclavian v. and [[internal jugular v.]]
- [[Venous angle]]
#### Innervation
- [[Glossopharyngeal Nerve|CN IX]], [[Vagus Nerve|CN X]], [[Accessory Nerve|CN XI]] all exit through [[jugular foramen]] anterior to [[internal jugular v.]]
- Posterior to [[Internal Carotid Artery|internal carotid a.]]
- [[Hypoglossal Nerve|CN XII]] through hypoglossal canal
