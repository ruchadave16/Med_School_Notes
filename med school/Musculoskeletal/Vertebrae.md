##### Cervical Vertebrae (C1-C7)
- C1 (atlas) has no body, round in shape, connects with [[skull]] at atlanto-occipital joint
- C2 (axis) joins w atlas at atlanto-axial joint
	- Has **dens** (odontoid process) projecting superiorly to allow head rotation
- Rest all have bifid spinous process (2 part)
- Transverse process has hole (transverse foramen) → creates groove for spinal nerve
- C7 (vertebra prominens) has a long spinous process

- Nuchal ligament (supraspinous)
- Tectorial membrane (Highest part of posterior longitudinal ligament)
##### Thoracic Vertebrae (T1-T12)
- Posterior inferior long spinous process
- Demifacets superior and inferior on body → meet with ribs
- Transverse facet also has flat portion that meets tubercle of ribs

##### Lumbar Vertebrae (L1-L5)
- Short round spinous process
- Heavy vertebral body

##### Sacrum and Coccyx
- Sacrum is wedge-shaped, 5 fused vertebrae (S1-S5)
- **Sacral promontory** is anterior edge of S1 ← easy to find on radiograph
- Coccyx (tailbone) is from 1-4 fused vertebrae
