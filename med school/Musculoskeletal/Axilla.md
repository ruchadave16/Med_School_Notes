##### Boundaries
- Apex – between 1st rib, [[clavicle]], and top edge of subscapularis
- Base – skin + fascia of armpit
- Anterior wall – pectoralis major/minor, clavipectoral fascia
- Posterior wall – [[scapula]] + subscapularis, teres major, latissimus dorsi
- Medial wall – Ribs 1-4, serratus anterior
- Lateral wall – Intertubercular groove of humerus, coracobrachialis, biceps brachii

##### External Features
1) Quadrangular space
	- Borders: Humerus, teres major, teres minor, long head of triceps brachii
	- Contains axillary n. and posterior circumflex humeral a. ← [[Axillary Artery|axillary a.]]
2) Triangular space
	- Borders: teres minor, teres major, long head of triceps brachii
	- Contents: circumflex scapula a. ← subscapular a. ← [[Axillary Artery|axillary a.]]
3) Triangular interval
	- Borders: Humerus, long head of triceps brachii, teres major
	- Contents: Radial n. and deep brachial a.
