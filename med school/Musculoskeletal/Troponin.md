Troponin: regulatory protein that allows cross-bridge formation when binding with $Ca^{2+}$
- Made of 3 globular proteins: 
	- Troponin T – attaches troponin complex to tropomyosin (tropomyosin inhibits)
	- Troponin I – inhibits actin and myosin interaction
	- Troponin C – $Ca^{2+}$ binding protein that allows actin myosin interaction
