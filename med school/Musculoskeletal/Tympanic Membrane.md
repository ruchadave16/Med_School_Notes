*Partition between external acoustic meatus and tympanic cavity*

- Moves in response to air vibrations
- NORMALLY appears pearly white during [[otoscopic exam]]
#### Histology
 3 layers
- Stratified squamous epithelium
- Radial [[collagen]] fibers
- Simple squamous epithelium inside continuous with [[middle ear]] (tympanic cavity)
#### Innervation
- Auriculotemporal n. (sensory to skin anterior) ← [[Mandibular Nerve|CN V3]]
- Tympanic branch of [[Glossopharyngeal Nerve|glossopharyngeal n.]] (CN IX) to internal surface


>[!Clinical] Tympanostomy
>Tubes surgically added through inferior part of membrane to drain fluid from [[middle ear]] to reduce risk of otitis media and reduce pain and pressure
