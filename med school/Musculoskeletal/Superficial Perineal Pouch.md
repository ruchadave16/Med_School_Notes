Inferior to perineal membrane

#### Contents
- Root of penis/clitoris
- Urethra
- Superficial transverse perineal muscles
- Greater vestibular gland (F)
- Vessels + nerves

#### Males
- Muscles
	- Superficial transverse perineal m. – ischial tuberosity → perineal body
	- Bulbospongiosus – around corpus spongiosum
	- Ischiocavernosus – from corpus cavernosum → ischiopubic rami
- Root of penis, spongy urethra
- Internal pudendal a. v/ pudendal n. 

#### Females
- Muscles
	- Superficial transverse perineal m. – ischial tuberosity → perineal body
	- Bulbospongiosus – bulb of vestibule
	- Ischiocavernosis – 2 crura of clitoris
- Bulb of vestibule – erectile tissue surrounded by labia minora
- Greater vestibular glands – Posterior to bulb of vestibule, secrete mucus
