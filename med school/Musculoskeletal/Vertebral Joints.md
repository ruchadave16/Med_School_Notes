##### Zygapophyseal Joint (Facet Joints)
- Synovial joint between superior and inferior articular processes of adjacent [[vertebrae]]
- Gliding movements allowed
	- Cervical: oblique A/P plane
	- Thoracic: coronal plane
	- Lumbar: sagittal plane

##### Intervertebral Discs
- Fibrocartilaginous joints between C2-S1 (23 total)
- Named by vertebra → IV Disc L1-L2
- **Nucleus pulposus**: Inner fluidlike core, gives flexibility
	- Remnant of embryonic notochord
- **Anulus fibrosus**: Outer fibrocartilaginous concentric layers that bind vertebrae together
	- Allows movement – schlerotomes

>[!Clinical]
>[[IV Disc Herniation]]: Extrusion of nucleus pulposus through ruptured anulus fibrosus 
>This is a common cause of back pain, numbness, muscle weakness related to spinal nerve roots
>Material buldges postero-laterally
