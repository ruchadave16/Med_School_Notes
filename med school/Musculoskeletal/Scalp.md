#### Layers
5 layers *Skin, Connective Tissue, Aponeurosis, Loose areolar tissue, [[Pericranium]]*

1) Skin: Thin, with sweat and sebaceous glands and hair follicles
2) Connective Tissue
3) Aponeurosis: 
	- Innervation: [[Facial Nerve|Facial n.]]
4) Loose areolar tissue
	- Allows for free movement
5) Pericranium

#### Nerves
 - 3 divisions of [[Trigeminal Nerve|trigeminal n.]] innervate scalp anterior to external ears
 - Spinal cutaneous (C2-3) innervate posterior portion
