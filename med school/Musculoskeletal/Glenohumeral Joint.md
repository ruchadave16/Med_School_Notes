*[[Humerus]] head connects with glenoid fossa as ball-and-socket joint*

- Glenoid labrum – fibrocartilaginous ring on glenoid
	- Long head of biceps brachii connects to superior part

- Support from [[rotator cuff]], coracohumeral, transverse humeral ligament, and glenuhumeral ligament

	![[Screenshot 2025-10-11 at 10.58.40 PM.png]]

Vasculature: Posterior circumflex humeral a., some suprascapular a. 

>[!Clinical] [[Adhesive Capsulitis]] 
>Frozen shoulder – tightness of capsule between glenoid fossa and humerus causes pain and ↓ ROM


>[!Clinical]
>[[Shoulder Dislocation]]
>- Generally anterior 

#### Muscles
- [[Rotator cuff]] – supraspinatus, infraspinatus, teres minor, subscapularis
- Internal rotation – pectoralis major, subscapularis, latissimus dorsi, teres major, anterior deltoid
- External rotation – infraspinatus, teres minor, posterior deltoid
- Abduction – supraspinatus, deltoid
- Adduction – latissimus dorsi, teres major, pectoralis major, coracobrachialis
- Flexion – anterior deltoid, biceps brachii (LH), pectoralis major, coracobrachialis,
- Extension – latissimus dorsi, teres major, posterior detloid
