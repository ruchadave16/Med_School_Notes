**Proximal**
- Head – articulates w capitulum of [[humerus]] and radial notch of [[ulna]], annular ligament
- Neck 
- Radial tuberosity – DA of biceps brachii tendon

**Distal**
- Articulates with proximal row of [[carpal bones]] (scaphoid, lunate, triquetral)
	- Fossa on distal surface for [[carpal bones]]
- Radial styloid process – distal end
	- DA: brachioradialis
- Listers tubercle – small bump on distal radius

>[!Clinical]
>Fractures of distal radius common when falling and landing on outstretched hand
>[[Colle’s fracture]] – dorsal displacement of distal portion
>[[Smith’s fracture]] – volar (ventral) displacement of distal portion
