*Immovable, fibrous [[Joints|joint]] between bones*

- Coronal suture – frontal and parietal
- Sagittal suture – 2 parietal bones
- Lambdoidal suture – parietal and occipital

![[Drawing 2025-10-29 20.31.48.excalidraw]]
#### Landmarks
- Bregma = junction of sagittal and coronal suture
- Lambda = junction of sagittal and lambdoid
- Vertex = superior part of neurocranium
- Pterion = Location where sutures meet




![[Screenshot 2025-10-29 at 2.18.57 PM.png]]

>[!Clinical]
>Pterion is weak part of [[skull]], overlies middle meningeal a. 
>Fracture here can result in epidural hemorrhage
