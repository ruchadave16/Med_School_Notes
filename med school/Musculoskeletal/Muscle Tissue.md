##### Components:
- Myofilaments: [[actin]] or [[myosin]]
- [[Myofibril|Myofibrils]]: made of [[actin]] and [[myosin]] myofilaments
- [[Myofiber]]: bundles of [[Myofibril|myofibrils]]
- [[Muscle Fascicle]]: bundle of myofibers
- Muscle: bundle of fascicles

##### Characteristics
- Contraction: All muscle cells are elongated with long axis in same direction as contraction
- **Sarcoplasm** = cytoplasm of muscle cells
- [[Sarcoplasmic Reticulum]] = smooth [[Endoplasmic Reticulum|ER]] specially for $Ca^{2+}$ storage
- [[Sarcolemma]] = muscle cell membrane
##### Types of Muscles
1) [[Skeletal Muscle]]
2) [[Cardiac Muscle]]
3) [[Smooth Muscle]]
