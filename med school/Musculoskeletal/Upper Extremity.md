#### Fascia
- Deep fascia = compartments and sometimes attach to bone
	- Pectoral fascia: Pectoralis major, continuous with axillary fascia
	- Clavipectoral fascia: Deep to pectoralis major, invest pectoralis minor
	- Suspensory ligament of axilla: Pulls skin and axillary content up during abduction
	- Axillary fascia: floor of axilla
	- Brachial fascia: around arm, divides into anterior and posterior
	- Antebrachial fascia: Thicker than brachial fascia, around forearm dividing into anterior and posterior
	- Interosseous membrane: Connects [[radius]] and [[ulna]]
- Extensor retinaculum and flexor retinaculum: thickening at wrist
