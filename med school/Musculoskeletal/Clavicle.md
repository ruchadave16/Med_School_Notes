*Rigid support suspending [[scapula]] and free limb away from trunk*
- First bone to ossify through intramembranous [[Ossification|ossification]]
- 2 ends: sternal and acromial

>[!Clinical]
>- One of most commonly fractured bone in body
>- Weakest at junction of middle + distal thirds

- Separates neck and thorax
- Separates deep cervical LN (superior) → axillary LN (inferior)
