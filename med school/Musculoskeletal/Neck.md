#### Superficial Structure
![[Screenshot 2025-10-21 at 12.49.53 AM.png]]

- Anterior jugular v. → external jugular v. 
- Fascial a. and v.

	- [[Lesser occipital n.]] (C2): superior, posterosuperior to auricle
	- [[Great auricular n.]] (C2-3): transverse, angle of mandible + parotid area
	- [[Transverse cervical n.]] (C2-3): transverse, anterior neck
	- [[Supraclavicular n.]] (C3-4): descending

#### Deep Structures
![[Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 3.18.37 PM.png]]

Boundaries
- Inferior: [[thoracic outlet]]
- Lateral: 1st ribs
- Medial: Manubrium
- Posterior: T1 vertebra

Structures
- [[Subclavian Artery|Subclavian a.]] is behind anterior scalene m. 
- Subclavian v. is anterior to anterior scalene m.
- [[Brachial plexus]] between anterior and middle scalene m.
- [[Phrenic Nerve|Phrenic n.]] along anterior scalene surface
- Ascending cervical a. next to [[Phrenic Nerve|phrenic n.]]
- [[Vagus Nerve|Vagus n.]] is medial to phrenic 

- [[Carotid Sheath]]
