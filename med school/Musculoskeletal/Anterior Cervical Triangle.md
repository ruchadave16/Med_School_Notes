#### Boundaries
- Anterior: midline of [[neck]]
- Posterior: [[Sternocleidomastoid|SCM]]
- Superior: [[mandible]]
- Apex: jugular notch of [[sternum]]
- Roof: subcutaneous tissue with platysma
- Floor: [[pharynx]], [[larynx]], [[thyroid gland]]
