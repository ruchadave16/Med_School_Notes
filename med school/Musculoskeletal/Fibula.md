*Lateral non-weight bearing bone that connects to muscles and stabilizes ankle*

![[Screenshot 2025-10-02 at 3.52.17 PM.png]]

- Head: superior part of fibula, palpable below lateral knee
- Neck: deep peroneal (fibular) n. ← common fibular n. wraps around neck just below head
- Body
- Lateral malleolus – lateral ankle bone, palpable

>[!Clinical] [[Pott’s fracture]] – fracture of distal fibula when foot is forced into eversion
