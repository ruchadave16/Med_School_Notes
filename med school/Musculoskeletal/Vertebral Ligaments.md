1) Anterior Longitudinal Ligament
	- Limits extension of [[vertebral column]]
	- Could be damaged in whiplash
2) Posterior Longitudinal Ligament
	- Within vertebral canal
	- Resists hyperflexion, helps prevent herniation of nucleus pulposus
3) Ligamentum Flavum
	- Between lamina and vertebral canal, yellow
	- Limits flexion and twisting, preserves normal curves
4) Interspinosus Ligament
	- Connects spinous processes
5) Supraspinosus Ligament
	- Connects tips of spinous processes (C7 - sacrum)
6) Nuchal Ligament
	- Supraspinosus ligament in cervical portion
