*Bound by [[Sternocleidomastoid|SCM]], trapezius, omohyoid, traversed by [[spinal accessory n.]]*
##### Boundaries
- Anterior:  [[Sternocleidomastoid|SCM]]
- Posterior: Trapezius
- Inferior: Omohyoid
- Apex: Meeting of SCM and trapezius at superior nuchal line of [[occipital bone]]
- Roof: investing layer of [[deep cervical fascia]]
- Floor: Semispinalis capitis, splenius capitis, levator scapulae, posterior, middle and anterior scalene
	- Covered by prevertebral fascia
##### Contents
- Cutaneous nerves of [[cervical plexus]]
	- Lesser occipital
	- Great auricular
	- Transverse cervical
	- Supraclavicular
- Muscle Nerves
	- [[Spinal Accessory N.]] (CN XI) **on floor**
	- Upper trunks of [[brachial plexus]]
		- C5 and C6 (superior trunk)
	- Dorsal scapular n. (C5)
		- Through middle scalene
- Transverse cervical a. and v.
