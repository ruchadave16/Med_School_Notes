*Largest and strongest bone of [[foot]]*

- Sustentaculum tali – shelf-like medial projection, FHL underneath
- Calcaneal tuberosity – posterior structure where calcaneal tendon attaches
- Calcaneal tubercles – bumps on inferior-anterior surface for plantar aponeurosis connection
