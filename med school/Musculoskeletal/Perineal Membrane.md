Triangular shaped inside [[urogenital triangle]]

- Attachments for [[external genitalia]]

2 holes in females, 1 in males
- Urethra + vagina
- Originally just urogenital hiatus

Perineal body – mass along posterior border of perineal membrane, remnant of urorectal septum

2 muscles from ischial tuberosity to perineal body
- Deep transverse perineal m. – deep to perineal membrane
- Superficial transverse perineal m. (in blue) – superficial to perineal membrane
