*Lymph from smaller vessels drains to trunks*

Paired:
- R/L Jugular Trunk – drains head and neck
- R/L Subclavian Trunk – drains upper limb
- R/L Lumbar Trunk – drains lower limb, abdominal wall, pelvic viscera
- R/L Bronchomediastinal Trunk – drains thorax

Unpaired:
- Intestinal Trunk – drains abdominal viscera
