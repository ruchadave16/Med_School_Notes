*Enzyme that breaks down [[fibrin]] into D dimers*

- Generated from circulating plasminogen by tissue plasminogen activator ([[tPA]])
- Patients with low [[tPA]] are at higher risk for **[[thrombosis]]**
	- [[Pulmonary embolism]]
	- [[Myocardial Infarction|MI]]
