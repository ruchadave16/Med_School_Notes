**Active Form**
- Tocopherol
- Tocotrienol
#### Sources
- Meat, eggs
- Oils
- Leafy vegetables
#### Function
- Antioxidant
#### Deficiency
- Muscle weakness
- Demyelination of posterior columns + spinocerebellar tract
#### Toxicity
- ↑ necrotizing enterocolitis in kids
- ↑ Anticoagulatory effect of warfarin
