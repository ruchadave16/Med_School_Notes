*Cells that can self renew and give rise to differentiated cells and tissues*

![[Screenshot 2026-01-31 at 2.52.06 PM.png]]

- Totipotent: can form any differentiated tissue and placental cells
- Pluripotent: can form any differentiated tissue
	- Found in inner mass of blastocyst
	- Called embryonic stem cells
- Multipotent: form multiple types of differentiated tissue but not all
- Adult Stem Cells: limited in terms of tissue they can make
	- Form stem cell niches
#### Cell Division
- Asymmetric – 1 daughter forms mature but other stays stem
- Symmetric – both can self-renew (generally early in embryogenesis)
