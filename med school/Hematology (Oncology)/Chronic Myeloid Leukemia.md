### Etiology
- Frequency: 4500 cases in US, 2400 deaths per year

### Pathophysiology
- 95% patients have Philadelphia Chromosome
	- Reciprocal translocation of Chromosome 9 and 22
	- Fuses ABL tyrosine kinase from Chr 9 to BCR gene on Chr 22
	- BCR-ABL fusion protein made
- This is an active (unregulated) ABL tyrosine kinase that causes ↑↑ cell division, stops DNA repair
	- Always on since BCR is always on promotor
