#### Agents
- [[ECL cells]] in lamina propria of stomach produce it
- [[Mast cells]] release it in response to [[antigens]]
#### Actions
- Draws [[eosinophils]] to site of action
- Binds to H2 receptors in parietal cells to increase HCl secretion
	- Blocked by [[cimetidine]]
	- Decreased by [[somatostatin]] and [[prostaglandins]]
	- Increased by [[gastrin]] and [[Ach]]
- Increases small intestine secretions

- Vasodilation (warmth)
- ↑ permeability venules (swelling)
- Smooth cell contraction (bronchospasm)
