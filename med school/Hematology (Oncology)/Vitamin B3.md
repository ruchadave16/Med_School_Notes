**Active Form**: NADH, NADPH
#### Sources
- Meat (liver)
- Cereals
- Seeds
- Legumes
#### Functions
- Coenzyme for redox reactions
#### Deficiency

**Signs**
- [[Pellagra]]: **Diarrhea, dermatitis (for C3/4 dermatome), dementia**
- Hyperkeratotic patches
- Glossitis
- Loss of consciousness

>[!Note]
>Found in ppl who use corn as main diet
#### Toxicity
- **Flushing**
- Hyperglycemia
- Hyperuricemia
