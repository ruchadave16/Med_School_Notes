*Should NOT be first tool for screening, but can be used to monitor tumor growth or recurrence*

- $\alpha$-FP. – hepatocellular carcinoma, yolk sac tumor, neural tube defects, ataxia-telangiectasia
- [[Human Chorionic Gonadotrophin|hCG]] – hyatidiform mole, choriocarcinomas, testicular cancer
- CEA – colorectal cancer and pancreatic cancers
- PSA – prostate cancer
