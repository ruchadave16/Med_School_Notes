*Too few [[Thrombocytes|platelets]] in circulation*
- <150,000 / microL
#### Causes
- ↓ marrow production – drug, [[HIV]] impact [[megakaryocytes]]
- ↑ destruction of platelets or loss
- Dilutional thrombocytopenia – high fluids given

>[!Note]
>Distinguish by bone marrow biopsy
>- If low or abnormal megakaryocytes, related to ↓ marrow production
>- if normal or increased megakaryocytes, related to hemorrhage, peripheral destruction, or dilution
