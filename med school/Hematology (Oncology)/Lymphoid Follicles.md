Found in cortex of [[lymph nodes]]
- Location of [[B Lymphocytes|B cells]] activation
- Contain follicular dendritic cells
	- Permanent cells in lymph nodes
	- Surface receptors that bind to complement-antigen complex
	- Crosslinking of B cell receptors
	- Very important reserve in [[HIV]]
#### Primary Follicles
- Inactive
- Have follicular dendritic cells and [[B Lymphocytes|B cells]]

#### Secondary Follicles
- “Germinal center”
- B cell growth and differentiation
- Helper T cells nearby can bind and grow
