Binds to [[T Cell Receptors|T cell receptors]] and CD4
- Only expressed on [[Antigen Presenting Cells|antigen presenting cells]]
- Presents exogenous proteins into 15-20 AA peptides

2 chains – alpha and beta
- Binds to invariant chain in ER

**Must merge with acidified [[lysosome]]** where invariant chain is destroyed and then proceeds to surface

#### Steps
1) Endocytosis
2) Proteolysis in acidifying endolysosome
3) Leave ER with invariant chain
4) DM/DO remove and load peptide
