I’M’mediate [[Antibodies|antibody]]

*in [[blood]] and [[lymph]], first to fight infection*

- Binds to [[C1 Protein|C1]] because it has 5 monomers – activate [[complement system]]
- Many Fc portions close together so can fulfill requirements of C1 (binding to 2 Fc close together)
#### Functions
- Activator of [[Complement System|complement system]] – classical pathway
- 10 binding sites (most avidity)
- Prevents pathogen attachment
- Weak opsonization – Cannot bind Fc but can use C3b as opsonin
- Cannot cross placenta
