**Active Forms**
- Retinal
- Retinoic Acid
#### Sources
- Liver, kidney
- Butter, egg yolk, leafy vegetables
#### Function
- Vision (retinal pigments)
- Antioxidant
- Gene transcription
- Cell differentiation and specialization
#### Deficiency
- **Night blindness**
- Xerosis cutis
- Squamous cell [[metaplasia]]
- Keratomalacia
- Immunosuppression
#### Toxicity
- Acute: nausea, vomiting, blurry vision, dizziness
- Chronic: 
	- Alopecia (hair loss)
	- Arthalgias
	- Hepatotoxicity
	- **Pseudotumor cerebri**
- Teratogenicity
