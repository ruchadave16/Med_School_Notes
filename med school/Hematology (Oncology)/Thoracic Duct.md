*Major duct carrying 3/4 of body’s lymph, including left hemithorax*

**Cisterna chyli** → **Left [[venous angle]]**
- Posterior [[mediastinum]] post/right of esophagus
- Azygos on right

At T4, moves from right to left to drain into left venous angle
