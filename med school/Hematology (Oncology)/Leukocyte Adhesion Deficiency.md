### Etiology
- Autosomal recessive condition
### Pathophysiology
- LAD-1
	- [[CD18]] (Integrin) mutation → leukocytes cannot adhere to vessel wall
- LAD-2
	- [[Sialyl Lewis X]] deficiency → no rolling
	- Less severe infections than LAD-1
	- Short stature, face dysmorphia, intellectual delay
- LAD-3
	- [[Kindling-3]] defect → low activation of lymphocytes
	- Bleeding tendencies – low platelet adhesion

### Clinical Features
- **Delayed separation of umbilical cord**
- ↓ wound healing
- Recurrent infections, especially skin
	- **No pus at site of infection**
- Severe [[gingivitis]]
