All’E’rgy – *binds to [[Mast Cells|mast cells]] and [[Eosinophils|eosinophils]]*

- Defense against [[parasites]] that are too large for phagocytosis
- Low concentration in plasma
- **NO complement**
- Allergic reactions **

Forms from [[class switching]] of IgG
- Driven by [[Th2]] cells – **[[IL-4]] is key cytokine**
#### Testing
- Pinprick 
- Intradermal injection
- Positive response = wheal formation
