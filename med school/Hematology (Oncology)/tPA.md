*Thrombolytic agent that converts plasminogen → [[plasmin]], which then breaks down fibrin*

- Most active when bound to [[fibrin]], at sites of [[Coagulation Cascade|coagulation]]
- End result = conversion of fibrin → D dimers

>[!Clinical]
>High D dimers signify high [[thrombosis]] and thrombolysis
