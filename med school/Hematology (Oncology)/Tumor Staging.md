#### Grade
- Degree of differentiation of tumor cells
- Tumor architecture
- Mitotic figures

High grade tumors
- **Enlargened, hyperchromatic nuclei**
- Irregular shapes
- Increase in nuclear:cytoplasmic ratio

#### Stage
- Tumor size
	- T0 = in-situ lesion
	- T1-T4 = increasing size
- Spreading to lymph nodes
	- N0 = lymph nodes clear
	- N1-N3 = increasing number of nodes involved
- Presence of distant metastasis
	- M0 = no metastasis
	- M1 = present metastasis
