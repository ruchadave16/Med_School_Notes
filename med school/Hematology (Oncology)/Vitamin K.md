K1: phytomenadione
K2: menaquinone
#### Source
- Green vegetables
- Made by intestine flora
#### Effect
- [[Coagulation Cascade|Coagulation]] – cofactor for $\gamma$-carboxylation of clotting factors **II, VII, IX, X** and protein C and S in [[liver]]
#### Deficiency
- Coagulopathy
- VKDB

**Signs**
- Bruises, [[purpura]]
- No [[trypsin]] activity
- Fatty stools
- Weight loss even with calorie intake

>[!Info] 
>Babies often have this since they’re born with low vitamin K
#### Toxicity
- Hemolytic anemia
- Hyperbilirubinemia
- Jaundice
