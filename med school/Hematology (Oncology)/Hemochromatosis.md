### Etiology
- Primarily genetic in white population
- Missense [[Mutations|mutation]] in HFE gene (C282Y) or H63D
### Pathophysiology
- Uninhibited iron reabsorption 

### Clinical Features
- Liver
	- Abdominal pain
	- Hepatomegaly
	- **Cirrhosis, jaundice**
- Pancreas
	- Signs of [[Diabetes Mellitus|diabetes mellitus]]
	- ↑ glucose, hyperglycemia
- Skin: hyperpigmentation, **bronze skin**
- 

### Diagnosis
- Labs
	- Liver biopsy will show iron
- Imaging
- Criteria

### Treatment
- Pharmacologic
- Surgical
- Lifestyle

### Complications
- High risk of hepatocellular carcinoma – common cause of death
