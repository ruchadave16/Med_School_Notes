#### Function
- **[[Chronic inflammation]]**
- **Allergic reactions**
- **Parasitic infections**
- Activated by [[IgG]], [[IgA]], [[IgE]], [[IL-5]]
- Phagocytes antigen-antibody complex
- Found in lamina propria of infection site
- **[[Histamine]]** attracts eosinophils to site

>[!Note]
>Increased eosinophil count is characteristic of [[helminth infection]] and [[allergies]]

**Action**
- Discharge contents onto parasites
	- Major basic protein (MBP)
	- Eosinophilic cationic protein (ECP)
	- Eosinophil peroxidase (EPO)
	- Eosinophil derived neurotoxin (EDN)
#### Structure
- **Bi-lobed nucleus**
- Large, eosinophilic granules
