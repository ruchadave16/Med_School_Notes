### Etiology
Genetic: Missense [[Mutations|mutation]] in both alleles of [[beta globin]] gene
- GTG replaces GAG in DNA
- 6th AA glutamate → valine
- Makes **[[Hemoglobin]] S**
### Pathophysiology
- When deoxygenated, V forms hydrophobic bond with other deoxygenated
	- Fibers formed in [[Erythrocytes|erythrocytes]] that distort cells
- Erythrocytes block blood vessels 
### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Hypoxia
- Signs
	- Low hemoglobin
- Variants
