Prevent leakage of lymph 
- [[Tight junctions]] between endothelial cells
- Continuous basal lamina surrounded by smooth muscle cells
- Contain valves to stop back flow

Unite into 2 main channels

#### Histology
Venules and arterioles have **thicker** walls and have [[RBCs]]
Lymphatics have proteinaceous material but no RBCs

>[!Tip] Key
>L = lymphatic vessel, endothelial nuclei marked
>Val = lymphatic valve
>V = venule
>A = arteriole
>Ad = adipocyte
>DCT = dense connective tissue
