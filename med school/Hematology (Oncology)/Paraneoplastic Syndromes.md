*Tumor associated signs and symptoms triggered by abnormal immune response *
- Not characteristic of tissue type of tumor

**Common Signs**
- [[Hypercalcemia]] 
	- Squamous cell carcinoma (lung) 
	- Production of PTH-related protein
- [[Cushing Syndrome]]
	- Small cell carcinoma (lung)
	- ACTH production
- [[SIADH]]
	- Small cell carcinoma (lung)
	- ADH production
- [[Lambert-Eaton Syndrome]]
	- Small cell carcinoma (lung)
	- Auto-antibodies attack pre-synaptic Ca2+ channels → muscle weakness
- Hypertrophic osteoarthropathy (clubbing)
	- Lung cancers
- Venous thrombosis
	- Pancreatic cancer
- Acanthosis nigricans
	- Gastric cancer
