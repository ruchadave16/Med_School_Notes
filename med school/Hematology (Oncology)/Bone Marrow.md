*spongy, soft tissue filling spaces of spongy [[bone]]*

Made of [[stromal cells]] and [[stem cells]]
#### Red Marrow
- Flat bones
- [[Metaphysis]] and [[epiphysis]] of long bones
- [[Hematopoiesis]]
#### Yellow Marrow
- Long bones
- Fatty tissue
- NOT hematopoietic

#### Functions
- [[B Lymphocytes|B cells]] and [[T Lymphocytes|T cells]]
