*Control blood loss by clotting and helping with wound healing*
- Form primary plug for [[hemostasis]]
- Form surface for binding of [[coagulation factors]]
	- Produce [[thromboxane A2]]
#### Structure
Disk shaped, no nucleus
Contain mitochondria, ribosomes, Golgi, and a few peroxisomes
**NOT cells** – membrane bound cytoplasmic segments
#### Development
- Bud off of [[megakaryocytes]] in [[bone marrow]]
- [[Thrombopoietin]] (secreted by liver + kidney) stimulates this
