[[Thoracic Duct]]
- 3/4 of body lymphatic drainage
- Drains: left jugular, left subclavian, left bronchomediastinal, intestinal trunk, R/L lumbar trunks
- R/L lumbar + intestinal merge into **cisterna chyli** at L1/L2
- Empties to venous system at **left [[venous angle]]**
- Anterior to lower thoracic spine from L1/L2 to [[venous angle]]
- Between [[azygos vein]] and [[esophagus]]

Right Lymphatic Duct
- Right jugular trunk, right subclavian, right bronchomediastinal
- 1/4 body [[lymph]]
- Drains into venous system at **right venous angle**
