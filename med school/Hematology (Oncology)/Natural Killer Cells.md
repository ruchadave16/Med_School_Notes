*Recognize antigens on MHC-I and trigger [[Apoptosis|apoptosis]] or cell lysis of presenting cell*
- Lymphocytes but do not mature in thymus
- No memory
- 
#### Function
- Kills [[virus]] cells without stimulation
	- Kill any cells with low [[MHC-I]]
- Produce [[IFN-y]] to activate [[Macrophage|macrophages]]
- Perforins + granzyme
#### Surface Molecules
- [[MHC-I]]
- [[CD16]] 
	- Binds to Fc of [[IgG]] → enhances activity of NK cells
- [[CD56]] (NCAM)
	- Used to identify NK cells
