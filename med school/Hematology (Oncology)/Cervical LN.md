- 2 rings that drain into deep cervical LN (around internal jugular v.) → jugular lymphatic trunk
- No nodes on scalp or face → directly to superficial cervical LN → deep cervical LN
![[Screenshot 2025-10-24 at 5.01.12 PM.png]]

#### Superficial LN
**Outer Ring**
- Occipital LN → Posterior [[scalp]]
	- Located posterolateral base of skull
- Posterior auricular LN → temporal [[scalp]]
	- Located behind ear
- Parotid LN → lateral [[face]], eyelids, middle ear
	- Located in front of ear
	- Drains to deep cervical LN
- Facial (Buccal) LN → anterior [[scalp]] and lateral face
	- Located on cheek
- Submandibular LN → midline of [[face]], medial eyes, [[nose]], upper lip, lateral lower lip
	- Located under [[mandible]]
- Submental LN → chin, central lower lip, floor of mouth, apex of tongue
	- Located under mental prominence
	- Drain to submandibular 
- Superficial cervical LN → superficial neck
	- In neck near external jugular v. 

**Inner Ring**
- Retropharyngeal LN → [[nasopharynx]]
	- In retropharyngeal space
- Prelaryngeal LN → thyroid isthmus, tissue around [[larynx]]
	- At midline over cricothyroid membrane
- Paratracheal LN → inferior and lateral thyroid, trachea
	- Lateral to trachea, below thyroid
- Pretracheal LN → thyroid (midline) and trachea
	- At midline of trachea below thyroid

#### Deep LN
- Run with internal jugular v. in [[carotid sheath]]
- 2 groups divided by omohyoid m. 
	- Superior deep cervical LN
		- Head, posterior [[hard palate]], [[soft palate]], [[paranasal sinuses]], face, larynx, pharynx
		- Jugulo-digastric node: TONSIL drainage, posterior 1/3 of tongue
	- Inferior deep cervical LN
		- All head, chest, lower neck, 
		- Jugulo-omohyoid node: drains tongue
