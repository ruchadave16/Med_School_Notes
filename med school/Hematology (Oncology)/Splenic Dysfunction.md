High risk from **encapsulated organisms**

Loss of marginal zone [[Macrophage|macrophages]] → ↓ [[Phagocytosis]]
Loss of [[opsonization]]
- ↓ IgM and IgG against capsules
- ↓ IgG opsonization
- ↓ complement
- ↓ C3b opsonization

>[!Note] Common Blood Smear Findings
>- Howell Jolly Bodies
>	- RBCs with nuclear remnants that haven’t been cleared by spleen like normal
>- Target cells
>	- Too much surface area = liver disease
>	- Too little volume = hemoglobin diseases
>- [[Thrombocytosis]]
>	- Spleen doesn’t clear platelets
>
>![[Screenshot 2026-01-19 at 1.49.05 AM.png]]

>[!Note]
>[[Strep pneumo]] = common pathogen for [[sepsis]]
>- Death in >50% patients

>[!Note] Common Reasons
>- [[Splenectomy]]
>	- Trauma
>	- ITP
>	- Hereditary spherocytosis
>- [[Functional asplenia]] – [[Sickle Cell Anemia]]
