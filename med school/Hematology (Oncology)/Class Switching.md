Initially [[B Lymphocytes|B cells]] only produce [[IgM]]
- As they mature, **switch class** → CD40L on T helper cells bind to CD40 on B cells to trigger this
	- Activates **AID** (activation-induced cytidine deaminase)
- Rearrangements to make [[IgG]], [[IgE]], [[IgA]]
#### Triggers
- [[Cytokines]]
- [[T Lymphocytes|T cell]] binding
