Secr’A’tory *mucosal surfaces and secretions*

- Monomer in plasma, dimer in secretions
- Crosses endothelial cells through **transcytosis**
- Great for coating mucosal pathogens, NO complement → NO inflammation
- Secreted into milk to protect baby GI

#### Transcytosis
- J chain binds to secretory component to avoid being digested

#### IgA Protease
- Elements that cleave off secretory portion of IgA to allow colonization on mucosal surfaces

>[!Clinical]
>S. pneumonia
>H. influenza
>Neisseria – gonorrhea and meningitis
