*Non-specific immune system*

No memory
intensity the same 
Fast acting

1) Physical barriers 
	- Keratin layer, stratum corneum
	- GI: 
		- [[Enterocytes]] – Zonula occludens
		- [[Paneth cells]] – lysozyme
		- HCl and organic acids
2) [[Inflammatory response]]
	- Phagocytes: [[Macrophage|macrophages]], [[neutrophils]]
	- [[Mast cells]], [[Eosinophils]] [[Natural Killer Cells|NK cells]], [[Basophils]]
	- [[Defensins]], [[Lysozyme]], [[Complement System]], Interferons
#### Recognition
- Identify through “Pathogen-associated molecular patterns (PAMPs)”
	- Found on microbes but not human cells
	- [[Endotoxins]] ([[LPS]])
	- [[Peptidoglycan]] cell wall
	- [[Mannose]] through mannose-binding lectin (MBL) in liver
	- Lipoteichoic acid on gram + bacteria
	- dsRNA
	- Unmethylated DNA
- These attach to “Toll like receptors” (TLRs)
	- Present on macrophages, dendritic cells, mast cells
	- Recognize PAMPs and release [[cytokines]]
