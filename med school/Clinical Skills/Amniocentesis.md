Needle inserted through abdomen into [[amniotic sac]] to get [[amniotic fluid]]

**Week 15-17**

Risk of miscarriage = 0.25-0.5%

![[Screenshot 2025-12-05 at 1.43.02 PM.png]]
#### Indications
- High maternal age (> 35)
- Abnormal maternal serum screening test
- Family history of chromosomal issues
- Neural tube defect / abnormal $\alpha$-fetoprotein
- Mendelian disease
- Abnormal finding in ultrasound
