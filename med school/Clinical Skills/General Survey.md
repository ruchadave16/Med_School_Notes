**General Survey:** 
- External cues
- Apparent State of Health
- Consciousness
- Distress
- Skin color / lesions
- Dress/Grooming/Hygiene

>[!Example] Young-adult patient appearing of average weight and height. She doesn't appear to be in acute distress but seems tired. 

>[!Example] Older middle age patient of average weight and height is sitting on the exam table bent forward holding his head with fingers at temple and nose. Eyes are closed. Appears to be in mild distress.
