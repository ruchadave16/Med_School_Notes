Chief Complaint: What brings them here in their own words in quotes

**HPI**

1) Opening statement: ----- is a ----- y/o patient of Dr. ----- with past history of ______(list important history, chronic conditions, FMH, Social Hx) with ------- hours/days/weeks of ---- (Chief Complaint)
2) OLDCARTSU (Onset (when started), Location, Duration, Character, Alleviating/Aggravating Factors, Radiation, Timing (how often it occurs), Severity, How it affects patient)

3) Pertinent positives and negatives 
4) **Relevant** PMH, FMH, Social Hx 

*If multiple complaints, HIP for each specifically related to each problem addressed. 1 paragraph per problem*

>[!Info] ROS Review

**PMH**
- Childhood diseases/hospitalization
- Adult history (hospitalization, accident, transfusion, toxic exposures)
- Psych (IF RELEVANT)
- OBGYN history (IF RELEVANT) – Last period, age of menarche / menopause, pregnancy (#, deliveries, abortions)

**PSH**
- Surgical History (chronological with date / age)

**Medications**
- What meds? Bullet point list including herbals, treatments, OTC
- Name, dose, route, frequency, reason
- Supplements

**Allergies**
- Allergies (agent, reaction, approximate time, first reaction) OR NKDA (No Known Drug Allergies)

**FMH**
- Social -> Parents, children, siblings, both sets of grandparents

**Social Hx**
- Life Experiences: birthplace, childhood situation, childhood trauma, education, job history, military service, overseas travel
- Current stressors: home situation, marital situation, sexual history, occupation, finances, food security, DO YOU FEEL SAFE, hobbies, religious
- Habits: Tobacco, alcohol, drugs, marijuana, diet / exercise, caffeine, sleep patterns

**Maintenance**
- Immunizations
- Safety issues
- Screening tests
