#### Maternal Serum Screening
- **Low** $\alpha$-fetoprotein = 40% [[Down Syndrome]] case identification
- Higher rate as materal age increases

![[Screenshot 2025-12-05 at 1.28.38 PM.png]]

**Quadruple Screen**
- α-Fetoprotein
- Unconjugated estriol
- Human Chorionic Gonadotropin (HCG)
- Inhibin-A

>[!Info]
>False positive rate = 5%
>Follow up with [[Amniocentesis]] or [[chorionic villus sampling]]

![[Screenshot 2025-12-05 at 1.30.30 PM.png]]

#### Newborn Screening
- [[Phenylketonuria]], [[Galactosemia]], [[Hypothyroidism]], Hearing (60% = genetic)

Heterozygous Carrier Screening for High-Risk Populations
- [[Tay-Sachs]], BRCA1 Mutation – Ashkenazi Jewish
- [[Sickle Cell Anemia]] – African American
- [[Cystic Fibrosis]] – Northern European
- [[Thalassemia]] – Beta (Italian, Greek), Alpha (SE Asian, Chinese)
