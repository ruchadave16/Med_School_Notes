#### PA vs AP

![[Pasted image 20250823174534.png]]

PA
- Patient standing, back to front
- Preferred because of higher quality
- Heart normal size

AP
- Patient lying down
- Lower quality, for patients who cannot stand
- Heart size magnified

#### Reading Method
- Evaluate upper abdomen → thoracic cage → mediastinal → lungs (unilateral) → lungs (bilateral) (ATMLL)
- To locate lung abnormality, need 2 orthogonal views or it must be at border
	- Lingula (left upper above cardiac notch) borders left ventricle of heart
	- Left lower lobe borders left hemidiaphragm
	- Right middle lobe borders right heart border (right atrium)
	- Right lower lobe borders right hemidiaphragm


- For lateral:
	- Anterior heart border = right ventricle (contacts sternum) and posterior heart border = left atrium (contacts esophagus)
