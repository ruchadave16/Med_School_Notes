### Etiology
- Genetic: X linked recessive
	- Mutation of F8 (Factor VIII) gene 
	- Commonly mutations due to recombination between repetitive DNA sequences 
- Risk factors
	- Family history 

### Pathophysiology
- No production of [[Factor VIII]] 

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Newborn with cephalohematoma
- Signs
- Variants

### Diagnosis
Prolong PTT time
- Labs
	- [[Southern Blotting|Southern blot]]
- Imaging
- Criteria

### Treatment
- Pharmacologic
- Surgical
- Lifestyle

### Complications
- Short-term
- Long-term

### High-Yield Associations
- **Most common hereditary disease with life-threatening bleeding**
