**Initiation**
- X chromosomes counted (Both active in zygote)
	- Females = X chromosome mosaics
- One is randomly inactivated

**Expansion**
- Xist gene is at X-inactivation center and makes non-coding XIST RNA
- This RNA coats the entire chromosome and silences most genes
- 15% escapes

**Maintanence**
- [[Barr bodies]]
- DNA Methylation of CpG island
- Histone modifications

>[!Clinical]
>[[Turner Syndrome]]
>- 45XO
>- 0 Barr Bodies
>- Short stature ← haploinsuﬃciency of SHOX gene
>- Neck webbing, broad chest, wide nipples, gonadal dysgenesis
>- Don’t produce enough of the 15% that are normally NOT silenced on the extra X
>
>[[Klinefelter Syndrome]]
>- 47XXY
>- 1 Barr body
>- Hypogonadism at puberty, no secondary sex characteristics
>  
>[[Double Y Syndrome|XYY Syndrome]]
>- 47XYY
>- 0 Barr bodies
>- Normal phenotype
>- 1/2 have language delays, higher ADHD, impulsiveness
