### Etiology
- Genetic:
	- 70% Deletion of 15 (paternal)
	- 25% Uniparental disomy (maternal)

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Significant behavioral problems
- Signs
	- Low birth weight
	- No suckling activity
	- Hypotonia
	- Short growth
	- Excessive eating
- Variants

### Diagnosis
- Labs
	- Chromosome analysis normal
	- _15q11-q13_: FISH (detect deletion), Methylation-specific PCR for silenced allele
- Imaging
- Criteria

### Treatment
- Pharmacologic
- Surgical
- Lifestyle

### Complications
- Obesity, diabetes, cardiac failure
- Max lifespan = 25-30 years
