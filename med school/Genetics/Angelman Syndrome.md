### Etiology
- Genetic
	- 70% Deletion of 15 (maternal)
	- 5% Uniparental disomy (paternal)
	- 10% mutation (UBE3A)

### Clinical Features
- No verbal development
- ↓ motor development
- **Happy**, laughs a lot
- Jerky movements

### Diagnosis
- Labs
	- Abnormal EEG
	- [[Chromosomes]] analysis normal
	- _15q11-q13_: FISH (detect deletion), Methylation-specific PCR for silenced allele
- Imaging

### Complications
- Developmental delay, seizures
- May never walk
