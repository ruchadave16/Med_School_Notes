### Etiology
- Autosomal dominant, haploinsufficient

### Pathophysiology
- Inability to make [[LDL]] receptors or synthesis of defective receptors that cannot bind to LDLs or clatharin-coated pits
- Low ability of cells to take in cholesterol which is normally ingested through endocytosis of LDLs

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
- Signs
	- High [[cholesterol]] in blood → early [[atherosclerosis]]
