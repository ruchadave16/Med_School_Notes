#### Cigarettes
- [[Benzopyrene]] is carcinogenic WHEN oxidized by [[Cytochrome P450s|cytochrome P450]]
- Binds covalently to guanine, interrupts G-C binding
- Distorts [[DNA]] helix
- Fix: [[nucleotide excision repair]] (NER)
#### UV Radiation
- Formation of thymine dimers – cause issues with polymerase: base, insertions, etc
- [[Xeroderma pigmentosum]]: No repair for thymine dimers
	- Fix: [[Nucleotide excision repair]]
