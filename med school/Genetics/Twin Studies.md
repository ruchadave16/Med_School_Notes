Twin frequency = 1/100

#### Monozygotic (MZ)
- Genetically identical
- Difference due to environmental factors
	- Concordance = same trait
		- If 1 sibling has single gene disorder, other will also have
	- Discordance = different trait
		- If 1 sibling has multiple gene disorder, chance of other twin having is present but not 100%
#### Dizygotic (DZ)
- Comparison group
- Environmental exposures similar

>[!Equation] 
>Heritability = 2(cMZ - cDZ)

>[!Info] Takeaways
>If concordance in MZ > DZ, genetic component to disease
>If concordance is NOT 100% in MZ, environmental factor to disease
