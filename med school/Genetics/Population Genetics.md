**Definitions**
- Genetic Drift: Random change in frequency of alleles by chance
	- Can cause loss or increase in allele over time regardless of impact on survival
- Population Bottleneck: Significant reduction in size of population due to some disasters causes gene pool to be limited
#### Gene Pool
All alleles of gene in a population

- p = normal / dominant variant
- q = disease causing / recessive variant
- p + q = 1

#### Hardy-Weinberg Equation

Assumption: only 2 alleles in population

>[!Info] 
>p + q = 1
>$p^2 + q^2 + 2pq = 1$

#### Founder Effect
 - Small founder population can have large change in genes because of small size
