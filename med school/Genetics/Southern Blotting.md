1) Isolate [[DNA]]
2) Digest with restriction endonuclease
	- Cleaves at palindromes 
	- GAATTC ← EcoR1 endonuclease results in 2 “sticky” ends that help with ligation
3) Separate fragments by size and transfer to solid support
4) Hybridize to labeled probe that detects DNA fragment wanted
5) Determine size of fragment
6) Compare across individuals
