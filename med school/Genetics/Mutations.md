Transition – purine for purine, pyr for pyr
Transversion – purine for pyr
Deamination of C – U for C, conversion of G → A
#### Other Alternations
- Frameshift: Insertion of deletion of !3 so shift in reading frame
- Splice Site: Change at boundary of exon and intron
- Trinucleotide Repeat: 3 sequence repeated

>[!Info]
>- Point –1 base change
>- Silent – change to same AA
>- Missense – different AA
>- Nonsense – change to stop codon
>- Insertion
>- Deletion

**Gain of Function** – mutation that adds an activity 
**Loss of Function** – mutation that reduces activity

Dominant negative mutation – Mutation produces protein that interferes with normal behavior of another protein 

Haplo-insufficiency – Gene dosage effect where 1 normal allele isn’t enough to preserve normal function → dominant genetic disorders
