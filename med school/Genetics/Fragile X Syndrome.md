### Etiology
- CGG expansion in 5’ non-coding region of FMR1 gene
	- Normal = 4-39
	- Abnormal = 200-900
- CpG (Cytosine) methylation of promoter silences gene expression
- ONLY through mother (X-linked)
- Semi dominant
- Loss of function

### Pathophysiology
- Without [[folic acid]], X chromosome has single and double stranded breaks

### Clinical Features
- Mental retardation
- Large eyes and jaw
- Macroorchidism in males
