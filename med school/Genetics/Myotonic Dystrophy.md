### Etiology
- Genetic: 
	- 100 CUG repeats in 3’ noncoding region of DMPK (dystrophia myotonica protein kinase) gene
	- DM1 [[Chromosomes|Chromosome]] 19
	- Normal = 5-37
	- Mild = 38-100 (adult onset)
	- Abnormal = >100 (juvenile onset)
- From either parent
- Mutant RNA stays in nucleus and sequesters CUG binding proteins that regulate splicing of pre-mRNAs
### Pathophysiology
- Due to excessive calcium intake so contractions last longer and muscles don’t relax

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Inability to relax muscle at will
	- Onset early adulthood
	- Muscle loss
	- Cardiac arrhythmia
	- Cataracts
	- Frontal baldness
- Signs
- Variants
