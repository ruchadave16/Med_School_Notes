*Laser based lab test that allows for quantification of optical parameters on cell-by-cell basis*
#### Fluorescence in-site Hybridization
- Detect certain DNA based on hybridization of fluorescent dye labelled DNA probe to complementary sequence
- Samples denatured
- Labelled probe added
- Anneals to complementary sequence
- Excess washed off
