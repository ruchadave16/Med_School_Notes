#### Abnormalities
- [[Down Syndrome|Trisomy 21]]
- [[Edwards Syndrome]]
- [[Patau Syndrome|Trisomy 13]]
- [[Klinefelter Syndrome]]
- [[Double Y Syndrome|XYY Syndrome]]
- [[Turner Syndrome]]
- [[Triple X Syndrome]]
- Robertsonian Translocation: 2 long arms fuse together from different chromosomes
	- Most common translocation
	- 1 / 1000 babies
- Reciprocal Translocation
	- 2 non-homologous chromosomes exchange spot
- Nondisjunction
	- Don’t separate during [[Meiosis|meiosis]] so abnormal # chromosomes

Uniparental Isodisomy
- Lethal Trisomy → recessive can be expressed when 2 disease transmitted by 1 parent only
