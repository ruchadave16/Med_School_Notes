##### Cell Division
Karyokinesis ([[nucleus]] division)
Cytokinesis (cell division)
	
- Prophase:
	- Nucleolus disappear
	- [[Chromosomes]] disperse
	- Envelope fragments
-  Metaphase
	- 3rd check point
	- Chromosomes to equatorial plane
- Anaphase
	- Sister chromatids separate
- Telophase
	- Nuclear envelope reforms
	- Chromosomes condense

Controlled by [[cdk]] (cyclin-dependent kinases) and associated cyclins

>[!Clinical Considerations]
>
>
>Karyotype: look at ploidy for genetic issues, look at mitotic rates for cancer
>
>[[p53]]: tumor suppressor gene that protects genome against toxins
>	- Induces apoptosis when genotoxic stress
>	- transcription factor that controls activation of proapoptotic genes
