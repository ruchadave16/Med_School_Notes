Semi-conservative – 1 original strand, 1 new strand
- 5’ → 3’
- Multiple sites of origin
- Proteins
	- Topoisomerase – Remove supercoiling ← inhibited by [[etoposide]]
		- Compacts to fit into cell
		- Stability
		- Stops enzyme reactions
		- Controls gene expression
		- Forms chromatid, chromatin
	- Helicases – Unwinding enzyme, dependent on ATP
	- SSBs – stabilize single stranded [[DNA]]

>[!Info] **[[Prokaryotic Replication]]**
#### Requirements
- Nucleotides (dNTPs)
- Complementing primer providing 3’-OH
- DNA template
#### Steps
- During S phase of [[Cell Cycle|cell cycle]]

![[Screenshot 2025-12-03 at 10.38.38 PM.png]]

Helicase unwinds
SSB proteins stabilize
Primase add RNA primer and Poly A extends primer
Proofreading + extension + topoisomerase to stop supercoiling
Primer removal 
Gap filling – Poly D
Ligase to form phosphodiester bonds

1) DNA Poly $\alpha$ extends RNA primer 
2) Poly $\delta$ – lagging strand, fills gaps after removal of primer
3) Poly $\epsilon$ – leading strand, helps with [[DNA repair]]
4) Poly $\gamma$ – replication in [[mitochondria]]
