*3 [[nucleotides]] from 5’ → 3’*

- Non overlapping, no skips
- Universal
- 3rd position wobble – multiple codons can be 1 [[Amino Acids|amino acid]]
- 1 [[tRNA]] can recognize multiple codons
- 20 amino acids – 64 codons
	- **UAA, UGA, UAG = non-coding**

>[!Info]
>In [[mitochondria]],
>- UGA = tryptophan, not stop
>- AUA = methionine, not isoleucine
>- CUA = threonine, not leucine

>[!Clinical]
>[[Hemochromatosis]]


>[!Clinical] Trinucleotide Repeat Expansions
>[[Huntingtons Disease]]
>[[Fragile X Syndrome]]
>[[Myotonic Dystrophy]]
>- Onset early adulthood
>- 100 CUG repeats in 3’ noncoding region of DMPK (dystrophia myotonica protein kinase) gene
