*$\alpha1$ agonist*
- Can activate $\beta$ receptors at very high dose
#### Action
- Activates $\alpha1$ receptor on vascular smooth muscle
- Peripheral vascular resistance ↑ 
- ↑ BP
#### Uses
- Manage severe hypotension
- Mydriatic (pupil dilation)
- Nasal decongestant
