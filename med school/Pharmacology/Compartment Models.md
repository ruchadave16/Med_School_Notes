**Central Compartment**: plasma, serum, and well-perfused tissues like the liver and kidneys

**Peripheral Compartment**: less-perfused tissues

![[Screenshot 2025-12-03 at 4.01.23 PM.png]]

#### Example: IV Administration
*One Compartment*
- Distribution Phase: Immediately
- Elimination Phase: [[Volume of Distribution|Vd]] = Dose / Co (initial plasma concentration)
	- 1st order elimination: k (elimination constant) = CL / [[Volume of Distribution|Vd]]

#### Example: Oral Administration
- Cmax = peak concentration of drug
- Immediate Release = 1st order absorption
- Extended Release = 0th order absorption
