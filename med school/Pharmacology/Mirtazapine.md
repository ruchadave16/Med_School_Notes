#### Action
- [[Alpha Antagonists|Alpha antagonist]] ($\alpha2$ antagonist) – increases release of [[Norepinephrine|norepinephrine]] and [[serotonin]]
- 5-HT2 and 5-HT3 antagonist – ↓ anxiety and GI effects of serotonin
- [[H1 antagonist]] – sedative and weight gain
#### Uses
- [[Major Depressive Disorder|Depression]], when sedation and appetite stimulation wanted
