Antidote for [[Cholinergic Poisoning|organophosphate poisoning]], nicotinic antagonist
#### Action
- Oxime Group (=NOH)
	- High affinity for P in phosphorylated enzyme
	- Competes with -OH of serine to remove phosphate and regenerate active
- Doesn’t work if enzyme gone through *aging*
- Positively charged, **DOES NOT** pass BBB
	- Can reverse peripheral effects but not CNS
