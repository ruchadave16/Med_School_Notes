Short acting Depolarizing [[Neuromuscular Junction Blocking Drugs|neuromuscular junction blocking drug]] → Depolarizing means it is an AGONIST
#### Action
- Nicotinic receptor agonist
#### Uses
- Muscular block in surgery
	- Phase I = nonreversable, depolarizing
	- Phase II = reversible by atropine
#### Administration

#### Dosing
- Genetic variants can cause differences
- Rapid hydrolysis by [[BuChE]]
#### Adverse Effects
- [[Malignant Hyperthermia]]
- Arrhythmias
- Hyperkalemia
- ↑ intraocular pressure
- Postoperative muscle pain
#### Contraindication
