*[[Drug]] category that can activate a system*

- Full agonist – produces maximum response
- Partial agonist – smaller effect due to low efficacy
- Inverse agonist – binds to receptors to reduce constitutive activity (activity without anything bound to receptor) → ↓ efficacy
	- Bind to same site as agonist BUT act as antagonist

>[!Warning] If full agonist is present, partial agonist can act as a competitive [[Inhibitors|inhibitor]]
