**Enzyme**: [[UDP-glucuronosyltransferases]] (UGTs)
- Expressed in [[liver]] and [[Digestive Tract|digestive tract]]
**Products**: glucuronides
**Cofactor**: UDP-glucuronic acid

Add glucuronic acid from cofactor UDP-glucuronic acid to substrate with –OH, -COOH, -SH,
-NH2

- Forms products more soluble in water (hydrophilic) for renal excretion and higher molecular weight for biliary excretion
- Products sensitive to β-glucuronidase cleavage
	- β-glucuronidase made by intestinal flora – high concentration in [[large intestine]]
	- Substrate can be reabsorbed in colon for cycling (**enterohepatic recycling**)

>[!Clinical]
>[[Gilberts Syndrome]] – benign, 10% of population
>- High bilirubin circulation
>- $UGT1A1*28$ causes reduced expression of UGT1A1
>	- **Primary cause of [[neonatal jaundice]]
