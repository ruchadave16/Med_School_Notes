#### Schedule I
- No use in US
- High risk of abuse
- Heroin, LSD, ecstasy, marijuana (Schedule III at some state levels)
#### Schedule II
- High risk of abuse
- Medical use
- [[Opioids]], Stimulants
#### Schedule III
- Moderate abuse risk
- Lower risk than I or II
- Codeine, ketamine, anabolic steroids
#### Schedule IV
- Low risk of abuse
- Limited dependance risk
- Benzodiazepines, Z-drugs, Tramadol, Carisoprodol
#### Schedule V
- Lowest risk
- Codeine cough syrups, Pregabalin, Atropine
