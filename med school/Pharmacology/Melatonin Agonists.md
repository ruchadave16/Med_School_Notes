Example: [[Ramelteon]] and [[Tasimelteon]]
#### Action
- Agonist of MT1 and MT2 receptors ← **suprachiasmatic nucleus** of hypothalamus
	- MT1 = sleep onset by reducing time till falling asleep
	- MT2 = circadian rhythm regulation
#### Effects
- Reduces latency of persistent sleep
- Doesn’t alter structure of sleep
- No rebound insomnia
- No withdrawal effects
#### Benefits
- Safe – no GABA action so no day sedation, no cognition or psychomotor impairment, no abuse
- No respiratory depression
