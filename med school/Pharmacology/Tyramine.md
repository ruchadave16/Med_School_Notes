Formed from [[Phenylalanine and Tyrosine Metabolism|tyrosine metabolism]]
- Normally metabolized by [[MAO]]
- Found in fermented food (cheese, pickled fish, salami)
#### Action (Same as [[Amphetamine]])
- Resemble [[Norepinephrine|norepinephrine]], enter terminal through *Uptake 1* transporters
- Enter vesicles through VMAT, displacing NE
- Norepinephrine → cytoplasm
	- Degraded by [[MAO]]
	- Released to synaptic cleft
- Calcium is NOT required to release this
