**Enzyme**: [[Glutathione-S-transferases]] (GSTs)

- Conjugate drug with [[glutathione]] to help with detox
- [[Glutathione]] can be either oxidized (GSSG) or reduced (GSH)
- Reduction in GSH can make cells more susceptible to [[oxidative damage]]
	- [[Acetaminophen]] overdose significantly reduces GSH

**CANNOT** be reversed by deconjugating enzyme
