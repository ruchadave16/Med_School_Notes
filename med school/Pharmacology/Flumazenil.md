#### Action
- Competitive antagonist to BDZ binding site of [[GABA A Receptors|GABA A receptor]]
- Does not affect function in absence of benzodiazepine or Z drug
- Does NOT reverse CNS effects of other sedatives, ethanol, opioids, or general anesthetics
#### Uses
- [[Benzodiazephines|Benzodiazephine]] overdose
- Reversal of benzodiazephine sedation
#### Administration

#### Dosing
- Short half life (~1 hr) so need repeated doses or continuous injection
#### Adverse Effects
- Common
	- Agitation
	- Confusion
	- Dizziness
	- Nausea
- Serious risk
	- Abstinence syndrome in BDZ dependence, causing seizures, high anxiety
#### Contraindication
