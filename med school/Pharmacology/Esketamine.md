#### Action
- Blocks [[NMDA Receptors]] on GABAergic interneurons
- Disinhibition of glutamatergic neurons → ↑ [[glutamate]] release → activates [[AMPA Receptors|AMPA receptor]] and stimulates BDNF production

Can also affect
- [[Opioid receptors]]
- Monoaminergic receptors
- Cholinergic receptors
- Voltage gated Ca2+ channels
#### Uses
- Treatment resistant [[Major Depressive Disorder|depression]]
#### Administration
- Nasal spray 
- Regulated under Risk Evaluation and Mitigation Strategy (REMS) 
#### Dosing
#### Adverse Effects
- ↑ BP
- Disrupts sleep + concentration
- Abuse potential
#### Contraindication
