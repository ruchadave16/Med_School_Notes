[[Alpha Antagonists|Alpha antagonists]] (competitive, nonselective)
#### Action
- ↓ peripheral resistance because block alpha in blood vessels
- Tachycardia, arrhythmias, myocardial ischemia → nonselective nature
- Blocks serotonin receptors
- Activates H1, H2, and muscarinic receptors
#### Uses
- [[Pheochromocytoma]]
- [[Hypertensive Emergency]]** MAKE SURE to give before any beta blockers
