*Theoretical value representing ratio of total [[drug]] amount in body to plasma concentration*

- ↑ Vd = ↑ tissue distribution
- Close to plasma volume means drug confined to plasma or bound to plasma proteins
- Vd ~0.2 L/kg (V of [[ECF]]) seen in polar drugs that don’t pass [[blood brain barrier]]

>[!Equation]
>$V_d = \frac{V Body}{[Plasma]}$
