*Reduction in response to drug after repeated use*

1) Innate Tolerance
	- Genetically lack sensitivity

2) Acquired Tolerance
	- Pharmacokinetic
		- Change in drug metabolism / distribution 
		- Ex: Chronic alcohol use causes alcohol dehydrogenase → increased metabolism
	- Pharmacodynamic
		- Adaptive change in receptor / signaling system
		- Ex: Downregulation of receptors
	- Learned Tolerance
		- Behavioral like learning to walk straight in intoxication
		- Conditioned (Pavlovian)

3) Acute Tolerance
	- Developed over short time during continuous use

4) Cross-Tolerance
	- Tolerance to 1 drug causes same in other
