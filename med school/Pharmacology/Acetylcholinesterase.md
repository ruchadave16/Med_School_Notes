*Breaks down [[Ach]] in synaptic cleft into acetic acid and choline*

Type of [[Cholinesterases|Cholinesterase]]
- High turnover rate

**Mechanism**
- Class: Serine hydrolases
- Active Site: 
	- Anionic site – [[glutamate]] which binds to choline group of Ach
	- Catalytic site – histidine and serine residues where rxn happens

1) [[Ach]] binds to active site of AchE
2) Acetyl Transfer
	- Acetyl moved to serine -OH, forming acetylated enzyme and releasing free choline
3) Hydrolysis
	- Acetylated enzyme hydrolyzed reforming enzyme + acetic acid
