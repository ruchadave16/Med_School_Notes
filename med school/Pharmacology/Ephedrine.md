*Mixed-action sympathomimetic drug*
- Resistant to COMT metabolism 
#### Action
- ↑ release of NE from [[Sympathetic Nervous System|sympathetic]] neurons
- Activates $\alpha$ and $\beta$ [[Adrenergic Receptors|adrenergic receptors]]
- Cardiac stimulant and bronchodilator
- Mild CNS stimulation
#### Uses
- Nasal decongestion
- Traditional therapy use but BANNED in 2004
- **[[Pseudoephedrine]]** is much more common OTC decongestant
#### Administration
- Effective orally
