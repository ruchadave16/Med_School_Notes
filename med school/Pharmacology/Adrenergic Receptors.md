Original:
- Alpha: [[Epinephrine]] ≥ [[Norepinephrine]] > > [[Isoproterenol]]
- Beta: [[Isoproterenol]] > [[Epinephrine]] ≥ [[Norepinephrine]]

**Current System**
- $\alpha1$: a1A, a1B, a1D
	- Vascular smooth muscles – contraction
	- Pupillary dilator – contraction to dilate 
	- Pilomotor smooth muscle – hair erect
	- Prostate $\alpha1A$ – contraction
	- Heart – ↑ contraction force
- $\alpha2$: a2A, a2B, a2C
	- Platelet – aggregation
	- Adrenergic and cholinergic terminals – stop transmitter release
	- Some vascular smooth muscle – contraction
	- Adipocytes – stop lipolysis
- $\beta1$
	- Heart – ↑ force and rate of contraction
	- Juxtaglomerular cells – ↑ renin release
- $\beta2$
	- Respiratory, uterine, vascular smooth muscle – relax
	- Skeletal muscle – tremor
	- Liver – glycogenolysis, [[gluconeogenesis]]
- $\beta3$
	- Adipocytes – lipolysis
	- Detrusor – relaxation
