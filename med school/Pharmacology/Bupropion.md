- Metabolism by [[CYP2B6]]
#### Action
- Increases [[Norepinephrine|norepinephrine]] release and lesser extent [[dopamine]] release
- NO effect on serotonin
#### Uses
- [[Major Depressive Disorder|Depression]], including [[seasonal affective disorder]]
- Weight management (combine with [[naltrexone]])
- [[Smoking Cessation]] – lowers urge to smoke
#### Administration

#### Dosing
#### Benefit
- No sexual dysfunction
#### Adverse Effects
- Agitation, irritation, anorexia
- Risk of seizures at high doses
#### Contraindication
- Patients on drugs that inhibit CYP2B6
	- Paroxetine, Sertraline
- [[Bulimia Nervosa]]
