Ex: [[Suvorexant]], [[lemborexant]]
- Metabolized by [[CYP3A4]]
	- Inhibitors can increase plasma levels
	- Inducers can reduce effectiveness
#### Action
- DORAs (dual orexin receptor antagonists) blocking OX1 and OX2 receptors → reduces wakefulness, 
	- OX1 antagonism = reduces arousal
	- OX2 antagonism = regulates sleep onset and sleep maintenance
#### Uses
- Insomnia with difficulty in sleep onset and maintanence

#### Dosing

#### Advantage
- Non-GABA so lower risk of dependence
- Minimal impact on respiration
#### Adverse Effects
- Daytime drowsiness
- Sleep paralysis
- Unusual sleep behaviors (rare)
#### Contraindication
- [[Narcolepsy]] or other impaired wake-sleep regulation
