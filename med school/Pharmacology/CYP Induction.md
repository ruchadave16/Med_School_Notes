- Drugs can increase CYP enzyme levels by ↑ synthesis or ↓ degradation
- Induction = higher metabolism of substrate = ↓ pharmacological action OR worsening of toxicity
#### Increased Synthesis
- [[Aryl hydrocarbon receptor]] (AhR) in cytoplasm can bind to **pollutants** or [[drugs]]
	- ↑ expression of [[CYP1A1]] or [[CYP1A2]]
- Nuclear receptor
	- Constitutive androstane receptor (CAR)
		- Inverse agonists: clotrimazole, meclizine (INHIBIT gene activation)
		- Agonists: phenobarbital, some pesticides and steroids (ACTIVATE gene activation)
	- Peroxisome proliferator receptor (PPAR)
	- Pregnane X receptor (PXR, or SXR)
		- Ligand binds and PXR forms heterodimer with RXR receptor
		- Heterodimer binds to elements in promoter to induce expression 
		- Ligands: rifampicin, nifedipine, mevastatin, ritonavir (chronic use), paclitaxel, hyperphorin
