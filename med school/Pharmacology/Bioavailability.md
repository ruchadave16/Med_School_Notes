*Bioavailability (F) = amount of administered dose that reaches circulation*

- IV: F = 1 (100%)
- Oral: F < 1 – [[First Pass Effect|first pass effect]]

>[!Info]
>GENERALLY: 
>- Absorption Rate:  IV > IM > SC > Oral
>- Effect Duration: Oral > SC > IM > IV

#### AUC

![[Screenshot 2025-12-03 at 4.05.15 PM.png]]

- Absolute bioavailability = AUC (extravascular) / AUC (IV) * 100%
- Relative bioavailability = $\frac{AUC_A*D_B}{AUC_B * D_A}$
