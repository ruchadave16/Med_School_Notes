#### Repeat Administration
- Drugs accumulate until steady state – 4-5 half lives

$C_{SS} = \frac{F * Dose}{CL * \tau}$

$LD = \frac{C_{SS} * Vd}{F}$ (Loading Dose initially given to get target concentration quickly)

$MD = \frac{C_{SS}*CL}{F}$ (Maintenance Dose needed to maintain steady state)
