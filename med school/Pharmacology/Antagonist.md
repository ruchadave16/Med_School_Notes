#### Receptor Antagonist
- Bind to receptors but do not activate them
- Stop agonist from activating them
- Competitive  
	- Attach to same binding site
	- Need to increase concentration of agonist
- Noncompetitive
	- Bind to allosteric site
- Irreversible
	- Binds to same site as agonist and cannot be removed
#### Chemical Antagonist
- Chemical rxn where one drug reduces concentration of antoher
#### Pharmacokinetic Antagonist
- Antagonist reduces concentration of active drug at site by changing absorption, metabolism
#### Physiological Antagonist
- Drugs bind to different receptors causing opposing effect on same tissue
