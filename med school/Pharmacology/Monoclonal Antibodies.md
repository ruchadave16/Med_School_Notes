*Engineered proteins ([[Antibodies]]) that bind to specific targets, used clinically*

**Structure**
- Fab fragments – antibody-binding region
- Full length antibodies – intact immunoglobulins
- Modified antibodies

**Mechanism**
- Block signaling pathways
- Increase immune-mediated destruction
#### Production
![[Screenshot 2025-12-13 at 11.28.50 PM.png]]
Hybridoma Based 
- Mice immunized with antigen to make  B cells that produce antibodies
- B cells fused with myaloma cells to make hybridoma
	- Grown in test tube
	- Non-fused cells destroyed using antitumor drugs

#### Types
- Murine (-omab)
	- Mouse sequence derived
	- Immune response in humans because foreign
- Chimeric (-ximab)
	- Combine murine Fab and human Fc to increase compatibility
- Humanized (-zumab)
	- Human antibody + murine hypervariable region
- Fully Human (-umab)
	- Transgenic mice / phage display
	- Long half life
	- Must be given parentally (degrade in GI)
