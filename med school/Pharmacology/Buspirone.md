*Slow onset [[anxiolytic]]*
- No impact on GABA
- [[CYP3A4]]
#### Action
- Partial agonist of [[5 HT1A]] → serotonin signal, reduces anxiety
- [[D2 receptors]]
#### Uses
- [[General Anxiety Disorder]]

>[!Warning] Gradual onset so NOT for acute
#### Administration

#### Dosing
- Takes 1-2 weeks to get effects
#### Advantages
- No drowsiness, no psychomotor impairment, no euphoria
- No anticonvulsant or muscle relaxant
- No CNS depression when combined with alcohol
#### Adverse Effects

#### Contraindication
- Interaction with CYP3A4 inhibitors or inducers
