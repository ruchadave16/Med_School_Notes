- 7 families (5-HT1 → 5-HT7)
	- Metabotropic (GPCR) majority
	- 5-HT3 = ionotropic

**Inhibitory**
- 5-HT1 (Gi)
	- Opens K+, closes Ca2+ channels

**Excitatory**
- [[5-HT2]] (Gq) → increase intracellular Ca2+
- [[5-HT3]] (ion) → Na+ and Ca2+ influx
	- Mediates [[vomiting reflex]]
- 5-HT4 (Gs) → ↑ cAMP
