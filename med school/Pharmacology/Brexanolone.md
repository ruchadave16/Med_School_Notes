- Naturally occurring progesterone metabolite
- Rapid onset (<60 hrs)
#### Action
- Binds to steroid site on [[GABA A Receptors|GABA A receptor]]
- Increases Cl- influx, rapid anxiolytic, sedative, anticonvulsant effects
#### Uses
- Postpartum Depression
#### Administration
- REMS protocol
#### Dosing

#### Adverse Effects
- Sedation + dizziness
- Risk of renal toxicity
#### Contraindication
