1) [[Acetylcholinesterase|AchE]] 
	- Found in cell membranes at cholinergic synapse, in CSF, and on RBCs
	- Specific to [[Ach]] and close esters
2) Butyrylcholinesterase
	- In plasma, liver, brain, GI, skin
	- Not on cholinergic synapses
