*Alkaloid isolated from leaves of coco plant*
#### Action
- Blocks norepinephrine transporters 
	- Arousal, insomnia, tachycardia, HTN, arrythmias
- Blocks dopamine transporters 
	- Mood elevation, euphoria
- Blocks serotonin transporters 
	- ↓ appetite, hyperthermia, hallucinations
- Inhibits voltage gated Na channels
	- Local anesthetic
#### Dosing
- Injected or through mucosal membranes
#### Adverse Effects
- Neuro:
	- ↑ risk of intracranial hemorrhage, ischemic stroke, seizures
	- Psychosis
- CV:
	- HTN, tachycardia, MI, arrhythmias
	- Aortic dissection
- Systemic
	- Hyperthermia
- Teratogenic effect during pregnancy

>[!Warning] SIGNS
>- Agitation, **HTN**, **tachycardia**, chest pain, mydriasis, septal damage
#### Contraindication

#### Withdrawal
- Mild, just some cravings, fatigue, mood swings, sensation of insects crawling (formication)
- Hypersomnia
- Severe depression
#### Treatment
- Psychosocial therapy
- [[Benzodiazephines]]
