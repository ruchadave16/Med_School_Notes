*Block [[Nicotinic Receptors|nicotinic receptors]]* in autonomic ganglion through either depolarizing or non-depolarizing method

#### Effects
- CNS
	- [[Mecamylamine]] passes [[blood brain barrier]] to cause sedation, tremor, involuntary movements, mental disturbances
- Eye
	- Cycloplegia, loss of accomodation
	- Mydriasis
- Cardiovascular
	- Hypotension
- GI
	- ↓ motility, constipation
	- Reduced sedation
- Urinary
	- Hesitancy – difficulty initiating
