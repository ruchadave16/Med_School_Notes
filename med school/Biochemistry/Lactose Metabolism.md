#### Synthesis
- Made in [[Mammary Glands|mammary glands]] in [[Golgi Apparatus|golgi apparatus]]
- Facilitated by UDP-galactose:glucose galactosyltransferase

#### Catabolism
- Broken down by B-galactosidase ([[lactase]]) in mucosal cells of [[Small Intestine|small intestine]]

>[!Clinical] [[Lactose Intolerance]]
>- Absence fo B-glaactosidase (lactase)
>- Eating dairy makes GI problems
>- Bloating, diarrhea, gas, nausea, abdominal pain
>- Solution: Lactase supplement or avoid lactose
