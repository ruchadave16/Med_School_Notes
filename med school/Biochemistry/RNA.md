*Single stranded [[nucleic acid]] where thymine subbed for uracil and deoxyribose for ribose*

**Categories**
- [[mRNA]]
- [[tRNA]]
- [[rRNA]]
- snRNA – nuclear processes
- snoRNA – process and modify rRNA
- scaRNA – modify snRNA and snoRNA
- miRNA – regulate gene expression
- siRNA – turn off gene expression

**Characteristics**
- -OH at 2’ end of ribose 
- Can function as [[Enzymes]]
	- Ribozymes – precursor for rRNA
	- Ribonucleases – cleave RNA molecules
	- Peptidyltransferase – part of [[Translation|protein translation]]
#### RNA Capping
- Cap added by RNA Polymerase III at 5’ in eukaryotes (m7G)
	- Needed for stability of mRNA molecule
- Methyl transfered from [[SAM]] using energy from GTP
#### PolyA Tail
- PolyA tail added (AMPs) to make mature mRNA
- Can be a sign for degradation in prokaryotes
