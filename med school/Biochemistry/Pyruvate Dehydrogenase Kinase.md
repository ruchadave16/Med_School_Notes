*Deactivates [[Pyruvate Dehydrogenase Complex|pyruvate dehydrogenase complex]] when activated*

**Inhibitors**
- [[Pyruvate]] – if high, we want it to be brought in and not linger

**Activators**
- ATP, [[Acetyl CoA]], NADH
- If these are high, do not need more pyruvate to get more energy
