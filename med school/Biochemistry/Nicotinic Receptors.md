*Type of ion-channel receptors that bind to [[Ach]]*

2 Ach bind to receptor
- Conformational change
- Na+ in, K+ out of cell
- Muscle contraction

![[Screenshot 2025-12-02 at 3.16.44 PM.png]]

**Types**
- Nm receptors: [[Skeletal Muscle|skeletal muscle]] for muscle contraction
	- Can cause fasciculations (initial disorganized twitches)

- Nn receptors: autonomic ganglion and adrenal glands to transmit nerve signals
	- Presynaptic in CNS to release other transmitters, can also be postsynaptic
	- Causes both sympathetic (cardiovascular) and parasympathetic (GI and urinary) discharge 
	- $\alpha4\beta2$ most prevalent in brain – [[nicotine]] addiction

>[!Clinical]
>[[Myasthenia Gravis]] – Ach receptors endocytosed so lower levels
