Nucleosides that form [[Hydrogen Bonds|hydrogen bond]] with complimentary single strand to form double stranded DNA

![[Screenshot 2025-11-24 at 1.02.27 AM.png]]

- Chargaff Rule: [[purines]] = [[pyrimidines]]

>[!Info]
>**G-C** stronger than A-T because 3 H-bond vs 2

**Eukaryote**
- [[Chromosomes]] have dsDNA 1 strand connected to non-histone and histone molecules to make [[chromatin]]
	- Autosomes = [[chromosomes]] that are not sex-linked

**Prokaryote**
- Circular dsDNA with non-histone proteins to make nucleoid

>[!Clinical]
>[[Dactinomycin]] – cytotoxic by **intercalating** into narrow groove of helix
>- Interferes with DNA synthesis
>  
>  [[Daunorubicin]] and [[doxorubicin]] – intercalate between bases of DNA
>- Interfere with [[Topoisomerase II]], stopping replication
>  
>  [[Cisplatin]] – binds to DNA tightly
>- Structural malfunction
#### Melting Temperature
- If more GC content, harder to break so higher melting point
