### Etiology
- Genetic (autosomal recessive)

### Pathophysiology
- Loss of PAH for [[Phenylalanine and Tyrosine Metabolism|phenylalanine metabolism]] (converts to tyrosine) causes buildup of phenylalanine in blood
	- Buildup of phenyl pyruvic acid, which is toxic to fetus
	- Melanin synthesis requires PAH so light skin

### Clinical Features
Presentation
- Musty odor on skin
- Epilepsy
- Light skin 
- Microcephaly
- Organ damage
- Mental retardation
- Unusual posture
### Diagnosis
- Labs
- Imaging
- Criteria

### Treatment
- Decrease phenylalanine in diet and monitor
- Pregnant women must limit intake

### Complications
- Maternal PKU – SEVERELY compromised pregnancy
