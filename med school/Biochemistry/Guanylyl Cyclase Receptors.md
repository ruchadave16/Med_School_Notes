*Membrane bound receptors that convert GTP → cGMP*

- Directly make cGMP when ligand binds
- ↑ cGMP activates protein kinase G → phosphorylates target proteins
- Nitric oxide is commonly cited for using this method

![[Screenshot 2025-12-10 at 4.35.36 PM.png]]

>[!Warning]
>cGMP degraded by phosphodiesterases

>[!Clinical]
>cGMP elevating drugs used to treat multiple diseases like [[angina pectoris]], [[heart failure]], and [[erectile dysfunction]]
