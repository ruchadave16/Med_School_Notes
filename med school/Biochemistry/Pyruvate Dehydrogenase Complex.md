*Group of enzymes that catalyze condensation rxn joining [[pyruvate]] with [[acetyl CoA]]*

- Coenzyme A is carrier for 2 C brought by pyruvate
- PDHC adds acetyl (C1 and C2) to acetylCoA
- [[Citrate synthase]] moves acetyl to oxaloacetate to make [[citrate]]

#### Regulation
- [[Pyruvate dehydrogenase kinase]] – deactivates PDHC when it is activated
- [[Pyruvate dehydrogenase phosphatase]] – activates PDHC by dephosphorylating the complex
