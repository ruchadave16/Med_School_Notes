- Glycine conjugates with succinyl-CoA
	- Makes d-aminolevulinic acid (ALA)
	- In [[mitochondria]]
	- **[[Heme]] inhibits ALAS1**
- 2 ALA combined together in cytosol to make porphobilinogen
	- d-aminolevulinic acid dehydratase
	- Inhibited by lead
- Converted to protoporphyrin IX
- In mitochondria, ferrochelatase adds Fe2+ to make heme
	- Inhibited by lead
