### Pathophysiology
**Stages**
1) [[Hyperuricemia]] – high [[uric acid]] in blood due to overproduction or low excretion
2) [[Acute Gout]] – deposition of monosodium urate crystals (MSU) in joints causing inflammation
3) [[Chronic Gout]] – repeat gout attacks damage joints and can cause gouty arthritis
