6 member [[Pyrimidines|pyrimidine]] ring connected to 5 member [[imidizole]] ring

Nucleobases
- Adenine, Guanine

![[Screenshot 2025-11-24 at 12.59.09 AM.png]]

Breakdown product
- [[Hypoxanthine]], Xanthine, [[Uric Acid]]
#### Synthesis
1) IMP from ribose 5 phosphate
2) AMP and GMP from IMP

**Substrates**
- [[Amino Acids]]: glutamine, asparate, glycine
- CO2
- N10-Formyl-tetrahydrofolate from [[folate cycle]]

**Steps**
![](http://127.0.0.1:50856/984466e6d6a7a7eae6c67d21e4b36fb2.jpg)

![[Screenshot 2025-11-23 at 11.50.24 PM.png]]
1) Dephosphorylation of 5-phosphoribosyl-1-pyrophosphate (PRPP) → 5-phosphoribosylamine (5PRA) (**Enzyme = GPAT**)
	- Activation: cytosolic phosphate
	- Inhibition: AMP, GMP
	
2) Nucleobase synthesis on PRPP
	- Formyltransferases inhibited by methotrexate and other folates
	- These can reduce purine synthesis and slow replication

3) Intermediate = IMP (can make AMP and GMP)
	- Adenylosuccinate synthetase and adenylosuccinate lyase make AMP
		- AMP inhibits adenylosuccinate synthetase
		- GMP inhibits IMP dehydrogenase
	-  IMP dehydrogenase + GMP synthetase → GMP

#### Purine Salvage Pathway

![[Screenshot 2025-11-24 at 12.09.55 AM.png]]

- Interchange of AMP and GMP through IMP
- HGPRT converts guanine to GMP or IMP and [[hypoxanthine]] to IMP or GMP
#### Purine Degradation
- [[Hypoxanthine]] is substrate for xanthine oxidase, oxidized twice to make [[uric acid]]
