>[!Clinical]
>5-Fluorouracil used for chemotherapy
>- Blocks synthesis of thymidine triphosphate, no DNA can be made
#### Synthesis

![](http://127.0.0.1:50856/40e22e7b7cbc56af51ef580ad9d87df2.jpg)


**Substrates**
- CO2
- [[Amino Acids]]: asparate and glutamine

**Steps**
1) Carbamoyl phosphate synthetase II makes carbamoyl phosphate from ATP, CO2, and glutamine → eventually makes orotate
2) Orotate to ribose 5’phosphate to make OMP and decarboxylated to make UMP
3) Cytosine triphosphate (CTP) and deoxyribothymidine monophosphate (dTMP) = initial products
	- Some turned to CDP to make dCDP
