### Etiology
- Primary causes
- Risk factors

### Pathophysiology
- Deficiency of [[GALT]] → buildup of galactose-1-phosphate
- Accumulation of Galactose-1-Phosphate → liver toxicity

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Cataracts from galactitol buildup
	- Liver damage
- Signs
	- Rapid weight loss even after being fed
	- Lethargy
- Variants

### Diagnosis
- Labs
	- Elevated galactose
- Imaging
- Criteria

### Treatment

1)  Switch onto non-galactose diet 
2) High galactose in blood after breaks do not cause an aversion to galactose foods, so need to become familiar with food (build up immunity)
3) Emmentaler, Gruyere, and Tilsiter cheeses are safe — fermentation eliminates galactose



### Complications
- Death in 3 days from sepsis from E.Coli
