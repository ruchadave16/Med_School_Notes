*Proteins modified by glycoside with a few monosaccharide units (oligosaccharide) made of hexoses*

- [[Glycosylation]] of protein means they can function as cell recognition sites and structure of ECM
- Form through N or O bonds with AA
	- O-glycosidic link: serine or threonine
	- N-glycosidic link: asparagine
