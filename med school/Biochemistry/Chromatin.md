*[[DNA]] combined with [[histones]] makes chromatin, a compact DNA-protein complex*

- Euchromatin: fine, light, actively [[Transcription|transcribed]], located medially
- Heterochromatin: dense, inactive, randomly located

- Highest compaction during [[metaphase]] → makes chromatids

#### Nucleosomes
- Repeating unit of chromatin
- **8 histone** proteins around 147 pairs of [[DNA]]
