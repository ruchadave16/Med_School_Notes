*Genetic information is stored in these.*

- [[DNA]]
- [[RNA]]

- Rungs made of nitrogenous base ([[Nucleotides|nucleotide]])
	- DNA: Thymine, cytosine, adenine, and guanine
	- RNA: Uracil, cytosine, adenine, guanine
- Base bound to sugar (called nucleoside)
	- DNA: Deoxyribose (-H on 2’ C)
	- RNA: Ribose (-OH on 2’ C)
- Each base selective for counterpart (connect by [[Hydrogen Bonds|hydrogen bond]])
	- Cytosine –2– thymine
	- Guanine –3– adenine
