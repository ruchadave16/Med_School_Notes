### Etiology
- Genetic condition 

### Pathophysiology
- Deficiency in methionine synthase OR deficiency in cystathionine-b-synthase
- Loss of these causes buildup of homocysteine and methionine
- Presents with [[homocysteinemia]]

- Cardiovascular Disease
	- Inflammatory effect of Hcy on arterial walls
- 
### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Ocular defect – lens subluxation (lens moves in eye freely)
	- Pectus excavatum (depressed sternum)
	- Tall and thin body
- Signs
- Variants

### Treatment
- Pharmacologic

- Surgical
