JAKs = Janus kinases
STATs = Signal transducers and activators of [[transcription]]

- Used by [[cytokines]] and [[Growth Hormone|growth hormones]]

Cytokine binding → receptor dimerization → JAK recruted
- Auto phosphorylation of JAK and tyrosine receptor
- STATS have SH2 domain that binds to phosphotyrosines of JAK
- JAK phosphorylates STATs
- STAT dissociates and dimerize with other STATs
- Move to nucleus for gene expression
