- Occurs in [[liver]] cytosol
- Building block = [[acetyl CoA]]
- First makes palmitic acid, then all other [[fatty acids]] made by modifying this

![](http://127.0.0.1:50856/ad2729aecb1958f43aa4f15be8b16774.jpg)
#### Step 1
**RATE LIMITING STEP = conversion of acetyl CoA → [[malonyl CoA]]**

- Catalyzed by acetyl-CoA carboxylase
	- [[Citrate]] activates by dephosphorylating
	- Palmitol-CoA inhibits
- [[Insulin]] causes synthesis of the enzyme
- [[AMPK]] phosphorylates the enzyme to deactivate it
#### Step 2
- Fatty acid synthase adds 2 C from [[malonyl CoA]] → FA chain to make palminate
	- Fatty acid synthase made by insulin presence
- After each unit is added, 2 reduction rxn that use [[NADPH]] (from [[pentose phosphate pathway]] and from malate → pyruvate conversion by malic enzyme)
- Oxidation back is PREVENTED by [[malonyl-CoA]]
	- Enzyme that moves long FA into mitochondria INHIBITED by [[malonyl-CoA]]
#### Step 3
- Activation to CoA derivative → palminate can be elongated OR desaturated to make FA
- Elongation: palmityl-CoA (16) → stearyl-CoA (18)
	- [[Malonyl CoA]] donates 2C, NADPH is reductant
- Desaturation: Double bond between 9 and 10
	- Fatty acyl desaturases add *cis* double bond using $O_2$, NADH, Cytochrome B5, and FAD-linked reductase
