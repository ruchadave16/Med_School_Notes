**No oxygen to tissue**
- Most common cause = [[ischemia]] from arterial obstruction
#### Impact on [[Oxidative Phosphorylation|ETC]]
 - NO O2 at Complex IV → whole chain falls apart
- Proton gradient goes away
	- Cell runs out of ATP and dies
- NADH accumulation in [[mitochondria]]
- NADH inhibits citric acid cycle which builds up [[lactate]] in cell
