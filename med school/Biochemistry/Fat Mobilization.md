[[Triacylglycerol|TAG]] stored in white [[adipose]] as droplets, very little in [[liver]] (moved to peripheral as VLDL)

- Lipases breakdown TAG to release [[fatty acids]]
- Adipose triglyceride lipase (ATGL) – TAG → diacylglycerol (DAG) and fatty acid
- Hormone-sensitive ligase – Diacylglycerol → monoacylglycerol (MAG) and fatty acid
	- Activated by [[epinephrine]] → increases cAMP, activates protein kinase A which phosphorylates HSL
	- Inactivated by [[insulin]] → converts to dephosphorylated
- MAG lipase – monoacylglycerol → [[glycerol]] and fatty acid
	- Glycerol → [[liver]] where its converted to glycerol-3-phoshphate
