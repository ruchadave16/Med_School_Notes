#### Nucleobase
- Either [[Purines|purine]] or [[Pyrimidines|pyrimidine]]
#### [[Ribose]] Sugar
- Comes from ribose 5’ phosphate from [[Pentose Phosphate Pathway|PPP]] but can also be reduced to 2-deoxyribose
	- Deoxyribose in [[DNA]]
	- Ribose in [[RNA]]
#### Phosphate Backbone
- 1, 2, or 3 phosphates joined through [[phosphodiester bonds]]
- Reactive = triphosphates
	- [[ATP]], [[GTP]] – energy
	- [[UTP]] – [[Galactose Metabolism|galactose metabolism]]
- Diphosphates are converted back to triphosphates
	- ADP → ATP
