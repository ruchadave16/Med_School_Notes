#### Structure
- Branched polysaccharide with units of [[glucose]] in $\alpha$ (1-4) or $\alpha$ (1-6) glycosidic bonds
- Core = [[glycogenin]]

![[Screenshot 2025-11-19 at 11.04.31 PM.png]]
#### Function
- Store [[glucose]]
- Found in [[liver]] and [[skeletal muscle]]
	- Liver – maintain blood glucose
	- Muscle – ATP synthesis during ↑ muscle contraction
