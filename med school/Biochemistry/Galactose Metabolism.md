- Phosphorylation by [[galactokinase]] → galactose-1-phosphate
	- If insufficient, galactose enters polyol pathway → galactitol
- Galactose-1-phosphate cycles with uridyldiphosphate-galactose epimerase (GALE) and Gal-1-P uridyltransferase (GALT) → glucose-1-phosphate → glucose-6-phosphate
- This goes into [[glycolysis]]
