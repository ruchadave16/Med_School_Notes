In [[mitochondria]]

1) [[Fatty acids]] brought into cells by fatty acid binding proteins
2) Fatty acyl-CoA synthesized
3) Acyl group → mitochondrial matrix bound to [[carnitine]] and fatty acyl-CoA remade in matrix
4) $\beta$-oxidation spiral → cleavage into 2 acetyl CoA units
	- FADH2
	- NADH
	- Acetyl CoA
		- Can also be made into ketone bodies in liver
	- Oxidation of these can make ATP
#### Steps
- Unsaturated fatty acids need to change cis to trans double bond OR have it reduced
- Odd-length remove 2 until last spiral when 5 left
	- Here acetyl CoA and 3C (propionyl CoA) made → succinyl CoA

>[!Warning]
>[[Medium chain acyl CoA dehydrogenase deficiency]] (MCAD) = most common

>[!Info] Yield
>7 spirals of 1 palmitoyl-CoA (16C)
>- 7 NADH
>- 7 FADH2
>- 8 acetyl CoA

#### Regulation
- ↑ Free fatty acids induced $\beta$-oxidation
- [[Malonyl CoA]] inhibits movement of fatty acids to mitochondria
