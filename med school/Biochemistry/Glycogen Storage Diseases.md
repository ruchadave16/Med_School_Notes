Type 1 [[Von Gierke Disease]]
- Glucose phosphatase deficiency so glucose cannot be released from liver
- VERY low fasting blood sugar
- ↑ glycogen in liver
- Elevated lactase
- Hepatomegaly
 
Type III [[Cori Disease]]
- Deficiency of debrancher enzyme
- Normal lactate but hypoglycemia and poorly structured glycogen

 Type IV [[Anderson Disease]]
- Absence of debrancher enzyme
- Fatal in 5 yrs because of cirrhosis
- Poorly structured glycogen and hepatic accumulation
