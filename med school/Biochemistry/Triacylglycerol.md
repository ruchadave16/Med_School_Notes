*[[Fatty acids]] are converted to TAGs to be packaged in [[liver]] into VLDL for secretion OR stored in [[adipose]]*

- 90% of dietary lipids = TAGs
- Glycerol-3-phosphate required (made in liver and adipose)
- Liver makes it from [[glycerol]] and [[glucose]]
	- Packaged with [[cholesterol]], proteins, and phospholipids to make [[VLDL]]
- Adipose has no glycerol kinase so it makes from glucose-derived glycolysis intermediates
