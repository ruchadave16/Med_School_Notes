*Brings [[Amino Acids|amino acids]] to [[Ribosome|ribosomes]] to make peptide chains*

![[Screenshot 2025-11-24 at 1.10.21 AM.png]]

3 modifications 
- Dihydrouridine
- Ribothymidine
- Pseudouridine
#### Synthesis
Made by RNA Polymerase III – recognizes split-promoter in [[nucleolus]]

- Nucleotidyltransferase adds CCA to 3’ end of tRNA where AA binds
- Uracil methylated by [[SAM]] → Thymine 
	- Double bond in U reduced to make dihydrouracil
	- Uracil rotated → pseudouridine
	- Adenosine → hypoxanthine, which attaches to ribose to form inosine (anticodon)
