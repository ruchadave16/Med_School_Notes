### Etiology
- African and Mediterranean descent
- [[Mutations|Mutation]] of G6PD gene → deficiency of G6P dehydrogenase

**Triggers**
- Fava beans
- [[Aspirin]], quinone and quinoline drugs, antimalarials
- [[Methylene blue]]
### Pathophysiology
- No G6P dehydrogenase means ↓ cytosolic [[NADPH]]
	- Elevated [[ROS|ROS]] because can’t reduce GSSG back to [[Glutathione|GSH]] (glutathione reductase needs NADPH)
- [[Erythrocytes]] very sensitive to NADPH depletion and go under [[hemolysis]] due to [[oxidative stress]]
