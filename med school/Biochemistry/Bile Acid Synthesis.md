[[Cholesterol]] → Primary Bile Acids by [[hepatocytes]] in [[liver]] → conjugated to amino acid to make bile salt → excreted to gut → bacteria there deconjugate and create secondary bile acids → circulated back to livers at [[ileum]] 

1) 7$\alpha$-hydroxylase catalyzes 1st step **RATE limiting** – negative feedback if bile acid ↑ 
	- About 50% ionized, present as salt

2) Conjugation with glycine or taurine (highly ionized)
	- Forms stored in [[gallbladder]]

3) In intestine, deconjugation forms secondary
