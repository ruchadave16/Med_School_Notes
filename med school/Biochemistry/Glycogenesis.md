- Glucose-6-phosphate (G6P) from [[glycolysis]]→ G1P by phosphoglucomutase
	- Phosphate from 6 → 1 C
- Phosphate displaced by uridine triphosphate (UTP) using UDP-glucose transferase
- Glycogen synthase adds glucose-1-UTP to glycogen through **1-4 glycosidic bond**
- **Branching Enzyme** adds UDP-glucose through 1,6 glycosidic bond every 8-14 units

>[!Clinical]
>Type 0 – [[Glycogen Synthase Deficiency]] – large swings in blood sugar since glucose can’t be stored as glycogen

#### Regulation
- [[Insulin]] promotes by increasing glycogen synthase activity and inhibiting glycogen phosphorylase

- *Muscles don’t have [[glucagon receptors]], no glucose-6-phosphate*
