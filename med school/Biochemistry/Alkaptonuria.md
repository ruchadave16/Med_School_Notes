### Etiology
- Genetic

### Pathophysiology
- Homogentisic acid oxidase is defective (for [[Phenylalanine and Tyrosine Metabolism|tyrosine metabolism]])
- Cannot breakdown phenylalanine or tyrosine
- Accumulation of homogentisic acid
### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
- Signs
	- Urine and tears can darken when exposed to air **
	- Pigment deposits in skin
	- Deposits at joints causing artheritis
- Variants

### Diagnosis
- Labs
- Imaging
- Criteria

### Treatment
- Pharmacologic
- Surgical
- Lifestyle

### Complications
- Short-term
- Long-term

### High-Yield Associations
- “Black urine disease”
