Heterogeneous, water-insoluble molecules that provide energy to body

1) [[Fatty Acids]]
2) [[Triacylglycerol]]
3) [[Steroid Hormones]]
4) [[Sphingoglycolipid]]
5) [[Phospholipids]]
#### Digestion
- Initiated by lingual and gastric lipases
- Lipid emulsification in duodenum by bile salts and peristalsis
- Digestion in small intestine by pancreatic enzymes
	- Pancreatic lipase – removes FA from positions 1 and 3 of TAG
	- Colipase – binds to lipase, anchors it to lipid-aqueous surface
	- Phospholipase A2 – cleaves at position 2 of phospholipid
	- Cholesteryl esterase – hydrolyzes cholesterol esters

>[!Warning]
>Regulation: [[Cholecystokinin|CCK]] promotes release of pancreatic enzymes
>- [[Secretin]] makes optimal environment
