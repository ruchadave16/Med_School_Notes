*Breakdown of [[glucose]] to [[pyruvate]]*
- Can receive [[fructose]] or [[galactose]]
- Can also create [[glycogen]]
- Connected to [[pentose phosphate pathway]]

![[Screenshot 2025-11-19 at 8.06.11 PM.png]]

**Inputs**
- Investment: 1 glucose, 2 ATP, 2 NAD+
- Payoff: 4 ATP, 2 NADH, 2 [[pyruvate]]
	- NADH comes from redox reaction of NAD+ by GAPDH
#### Regulation
- [[Hexokinase]]
- [[Phosphofructokinase 1]]
- [[Pyruvate Kinase]]

>[!Clinical]
>1) [[Pyruvate Kinase Deficiency]]
>	- Rare disorder (3.5-8.5 every million)
>	- North America and Europe
>	- Associated with hemolytic anemia
>	- RBC only uses glycolysis, cannot maintain Na/K pump and shrivel up
>	- Short lifespan of RBC
