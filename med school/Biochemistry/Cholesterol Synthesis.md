**Precursor** = [[Acetyl CoA]]
- Requires [[NADPH]] and energy

**RATE Limiting Step**: HMG CoA reductase catalyzed step that converts HMG CoA → mevalonate
#### Regulation
Mainly through [[HMG CoA Reductase]]
- Transcriptional Regulation
	- Low cholesterol moves regulatory complex from ER to Golgi
	- Proteolytic factor → nucleus, works as [[transcription factor]]
	- Binds to sterol regulatory element upstream of HMG CoA Reductase gene
	- ↑ [[transcription]] of HMG CoA Reductase

- Proteolytic degradation of enzyme
	- Sterol-sensing regions on membrane domains of HMG CoA Reductase
	- High cholesterol causes [[oligomerization]] of enzyme → proteolytic cleavage

- Covalent Modification
	- AMPK phosphorylates enzyme to inhibit it
	- ↑ [[glucagon]] and [[epinephrine]] increase phosphorylation (inhibition)

>[!Clinical]
>[[Statins]] target HMG CoA Reductase
