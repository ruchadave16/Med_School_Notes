- Fluidity of [[cell membrane]]
- Precursor for making [[steroid hormones]]
#### Sources
- [[Cholesterol synthesis]] from all tissues, mainly [[liver]], intestines, adrenal, and reproductive
- Dietary intake
	- [[Small intestine]] through simple diffusion, regulated by transporters on [[enterocytes]]

>[!Warning]
>Direct correlation between amount of cholesterol and [[coronary heart disease]]
#### Elimination
- Secreted in [[bile]] for elimination
- Converted to [[bile salts]] and bile acids
