*Ceramine-derived [[lipids]] with a sphingosine head connected*

- Found on outside surface of [[cell membrane]] and act as signal

![[Screenshot 2025-11-22 at 6.00.17 PM.png]]

#### Categories
- Neutral: Headgroup = neutral
- Acidic: Headgroup negatively charged at physiological pH, from N-acetylneuraminic acid (NANA)
- Sulfatides: Negatively charged, found in brain and kidneys
- Gangliosides: Found in brain
#### Degradation
- Consumed by [[Phagocytosis]]
- Glycan broken down to sphingolipid

>[!Clinical] [[Ganglioside GM1]] Degradation

>[!Clinical]
>[[Lysosome]] storage issues due to missing hydrolases in steps for degradation
>- Most important = myelin and ganglioside metabolism
