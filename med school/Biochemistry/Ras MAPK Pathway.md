**Phosphotyrosine** = binding site for proteins with SH2

[[Grb2]] has SH2 protein → phosphotyrosine and SH3 domain →  **proline-rich** area of SOS (guanine nucleotide exchange factor) on [[RTKs|RTK]]

- SOS activates GTP/GDP exchange on Ras
- Ras-GTP binds Raf
- Phosphorylation cascade → ↑ [[transcription]] of genes for cell survival
- Ras hydrolyses GTP → GDP so signal STOPS
