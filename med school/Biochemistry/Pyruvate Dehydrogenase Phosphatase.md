*Activates [[Pyruvate Dehydrogenase Complex|pyruvate dehydrogenase complex]] when activated*

**Inhibitors**
- NADH, [[Acetyl CoA]] – if high, we want to deactivate phosphatase because we don’t need more energy

**Activators**
- $Ca^{2+}$ – if high, we want to maximize rate of pyruvate into citric acid cycle
