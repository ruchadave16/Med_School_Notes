#### Fed State
- Initially blood sugar ↑↑ 
- [[Insulin]] release from pancreas (insulin spike)
- Uptake of [[glucose]] within 4 hrs (glucagon drop → [[glycogenesis]])

#### Fasted State
- [[Glycogenolysis]] peaks at 6 hrs and decays over day

#### Starving
- [[Gluconeogenesis]] because no glycogen
