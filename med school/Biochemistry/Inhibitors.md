*Compounds that impede [[Enzymes|enzyme]] activity*
- Impact on [[Lineweaver-Burk Plots]] is of importance
#### Competitive
- Bind to **active site** and compete with substrate
- $K_m$ ↑ but $V_{max}$ stays same

![[Screenshot 2025-11-18 at 11.03.04 PM.png]]

>[!Example]
>[[Alcohol dehydrogenase]] normal substrate = ethanol
>- Ethylene glycol and methanol are also substrates
>- Affinity to ethanol >> ethylene glycol
>- [[Ethylene glycol poisoning]] can be treated with ethanol
#### Noncompetitive
- Binds to **allosteric site** and doesn’t compete
- $V_{max}$ ↓ but $K_m$ stays same

![[Screenshot 2025-11-18 at 11.07.45 PM.png]]

>[!Example]
>[[Statins]]
#### Uncompetitive
- Bind to ES complex to prevent release of product
- $K_m$ and $V_{max}$ both ↓ 

![[Screenshot 2025-11-18 at 11.09.21 PM.png]]
