### Etiology
- Autosomal recessive disease due to [[Mutations|mutation]] of gene for [[Cystic Fibrosis Transmembrane Regulator]]
	- Could in some cases be de novo
- Most common mutation = deletion of F508
### Pathophysiology
- Chloride secretion lowers, depletion of water from cell surface
- Thick mucus created that clogs pancreatic ducts and stops pancreatic enzymes from reaching intestine
	- Low fat absorption

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Progressive degenerative pulmonary disease
	- [[Exocrine Pancreas|Exocrine pancreas]] deficiency
	- Obstructive Azoospermia
- Signs
	- Elevated sweat chloride
	- Growth failure  
- Variants
	- 6 different CFTR mutations
