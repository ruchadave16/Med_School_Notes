*Inability to remove [[bilirubin]] from blood*

- Generally because glucuronidate bilirubin cannot make bilirubin water-soluble 
#### Adults
- Damage to liver, cirrhosis, hepatic injury causes
#### Neonatal 
- Liver can’t handle breakdown of erythrocytes
- Bilirubin is not glucuronidated and accumulates
