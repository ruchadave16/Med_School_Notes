- Glucose phosphorylase make G1P from glycogen
	- Pi and [[Vitamin B6]] REQUIRED as COFACTORS
- Phosphoglucomutase converts G1P → G6P
	- [[Glycolysis]] or [[pentose phosphate pathway]]
- Glucose phosphatase removes phosphate from G6P making glucose
#### Regulation
- [[Glucagon]] inhibits by inactivating glycogen synthase and activation glycogen phosphorylase
- [[Epinephrine]] (acts like glucagon) 
- *In muscles: $Ca^{2+}*$
	- Activates glycogen phosphorylase kinase → activates glycogen phosphorylase
- *In muscles: AMP*
