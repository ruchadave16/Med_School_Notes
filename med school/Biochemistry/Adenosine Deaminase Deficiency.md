### Etiology
- Associated with [[severe combined immunodeficiency disease]] (SCID)
### Pathophysiology
- No adenosine deaminase → adenosine accumulation
- High phosphorylation to dATP
- Ribonucleotide reductase inhibited by high dATP so other nucleotides CANNOT be made
	- Cells can’t make DNA
- Immune cells cannot proliferate
