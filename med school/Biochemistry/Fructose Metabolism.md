- Comes from [[sucrose]]
	- Sucrase in intestine breaks into fructose and glucose
- Metabolized by [[liver]]
	- Fructose → fructose-1-phosphate (F1P) by fructokinase OR hexokinase (Uses ATP)
	- Aldolase B cleaves F1P into glyceraldehyde (GA) and dihydroxyacetonephosphate (DHAP)
	- Triose kinase phosphorylates GA → glyceraldehide-3-phosphate (GAP) 
	- This enters [[glycolysis]]

*Fructose BYPASSES [[Phosphofructokinase 1|PFK-1]], which is rate limiting step*
- DOES NOT depend on insulin

>[!Clinical]
>1) [[Essential Fructosuria]]
>	- Deficiency of fructokinase
>	- Buildup of fructose in blood
>	- Fructose found in urine (+ sugars in urine, - glucose oxidase test)
>
>2) [[Hereditary Fructose Intolerance]]
