*Catalyze transfer of molecular group from 1 substrate to another*

- Aminotransferase – Amine group from donor to acceptor instead of ketone
- Kinase – Phosphate from ATP to acceptor 

>[!Example] 
>- [[Hexokinase]]
>- [[Tyrosine Kinase]] – specifically phosphorylate tyrosine
