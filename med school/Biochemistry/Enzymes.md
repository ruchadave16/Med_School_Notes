*Proteins that catalyze reaction using active site*
- Enzymes requiring [[cofactors]] = apoenzyme when absent and holoenzyme when present
- Coenzymes = non-metal substances that bind to active site
- Prosthetic group = covalently attached substance that is deep in enzyme
#### Structure
- Active site with **binding motif** and internal **catalytic site**
- Lock and key vs induced fit models

#### Enzyme Classes
1) [[Oxidoreductase]] – oxidize one to reduce another
2) [[Transferases|Transferase]] – group from 1 to another
3) [[Hydrolase]] – break bond with water
4) [[Isomerase]] – rearrange without adding or removing atoms
5) [[Lyase]] – break bond through elimination rxn 
6) [[Ligase]] – join 2 molecules without making double bond
