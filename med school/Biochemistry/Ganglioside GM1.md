*Found in [[brain]] in high quantity, regulates lipid rafts and maintains plasticity of synapse*

#### Degradation
1) Terminal galactose cleaved to give GM2
2) GalNAc cleaved to give GM3
3) NANA removed to make Lac-Cer
4) Galactose removed to give Glc-Cer
5) Glucose removed to make [[ceramide]]


>[!Clinical]
>[[GM1 Gangliosidosis]]
>- Loss of beta-galactosidase
>- No cleaving of terminal galactose on GM1 (step 1)
>- Type 1 = infancy, Type 2 = adolescence
>- No treatments
>
