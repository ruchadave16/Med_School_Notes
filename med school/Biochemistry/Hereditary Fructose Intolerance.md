### Etiology
- Primary causes
- Risk factors

### Pathophysiology
- Normal if no [[fructose]] eaten
- No Aldolase-B activity so F1P trapped in blood
- [[Fructose metabolism]] in liver → drain for hepatic ATP

### Clinical Features
- Seen during weaning from breastfeeding onto formula containing fructose and sucrose
- GI discomfort initially
	- Labs show acute liver failure
### Diagnosis
- Labs
	- Seen on standard newborn screening panel

### Treatment
- Pharmacologic
- Surgical
- Lifestyle
	- Avoid fructose, sucrose, sorbitol

### Complications
- If not diagnosed, can cause chronic liver injury, death, enlarged liver
