*Oxidizes [[NADH]] to reduce [[ubiquinone]]*

4 parts
- N module (NADH oxidation)
- Q module (ubiquinone reduction)
- Pp and Pd (proton channels)

P modules have [[flavin mononucleotide]] (FMN) – oxidizer for NADH → NAD+ and H-
- Iron sulfur complexes bring electrons from FMNH2 through P module → ubiquinone reduction site at Q where ubiquinol is made
- This movement changes Pp and Pd, and **4 H+** pumped out
