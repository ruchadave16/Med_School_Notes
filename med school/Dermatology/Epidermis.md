##### Layers
Thick skin has 5 layers, thin has only 4 

Mnemonic: Come Lets Get Sun Burned

1) Stratum Corneum
	- Dead cells, no [[Nucleus|nucleus]]
	- Only has amorphous, fibrillar proteins with plasma membrane surrounded by [[lipid]]-rich layer
	- Protein: [[keratin]] tonofibrils massed with filaggrin
2) Stratum Lucidum:
	- Only in thick skin
	- Thin, flat layer of eosinophilic [[Keratinocytes]] held together by [[desmosomes]] lacking nuclei + organelles
3) Stratum Granulosum
	- Terminal stage of [[keratinization]]
	- Keratohyaline granules with [[filaggrin]]
4) Stratum Spinosum
	- Polyhedral keratinocytes with central nuclei and prominent nucleoli
	- Connections through [[desmosomes]]
5) Stratum Basale
	- 1 layer between dermis and epidermis
	- Mitotically active cells 
	- [[Hemidesmosomes]] connected to [[basal lamina]] and [[desmosomes]] connect to epidermal cells

>[[Bullous Pemphigoid]] vs [[Pemphigus Vulgaris]]
