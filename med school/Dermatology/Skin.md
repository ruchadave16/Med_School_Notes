##### Functions
1) Protection: Barrier against injury and maintains [[Homeostasis|homeostasis]]
2) Sensation through receptors
3) Thermal regulation: Insulation and regulating hair loss
4) Secretion
5) Metabolism
	- Synthesis of [[Vitamin D3]] through UV light for calcium metabolism
	- Energy storage through [[Adipose|fat]]
	- Excess electrolyte removal 
6) Support appendages
