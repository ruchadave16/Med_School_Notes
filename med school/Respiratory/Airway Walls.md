#### Histology
**4 layers** (some might have more, thickness decreases till alveoli)
*Structure change → function change*

1) [[Respiratory Epithelium]]
2) [[Respiratory Lamina Propria]]
3) [[Smooth Muscle]]/[[Cartilage]]
	- SM encircles lumen from trachea to bronchioles
	- Contraction + relaxation of SM determines airway diameter 
	- [[Cartilage]] – structural support
	- **Disappears at bronchioles**
4) [[Adventitia]]
	- Amorphous connective tissue on outside of muscle/cartilage
	- Collagen + elastin
