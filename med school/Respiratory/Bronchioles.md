Conduct air, important for [[bronchoconstriction]] and [[bronchidilation]]

**NO cartilage** and prominent [[Smooth Muscle|smooth muscle]] circle
##### Conducting Bronchioles
Branch from segmental [[bronchi]] 
- No glands 
- Epithelium from simple ciliated columnar to simple ciliated cuboidal going down
- Few to no goblet cells
- [[Club cells]] but hard to distinguish
##### Terminal Bronchioles
- Conduct air to lungs, have exocrine [[club cells]] 
- Incomplete and thinner layers of smooth muscle
- **No cartilage**
- **No goblet cells**
- Simple cuboidal epithelium
