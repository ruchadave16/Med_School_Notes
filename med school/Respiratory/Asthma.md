##### Definition
[[Chronic inflammation]] of [[bronchioles]] tree in lungs

##### Etiology
- Primary causes
- Risk factors

##### Pathophysiology
- Bronchioles become hypersensitive because of allergic reactions 
- [[Mast Cells|Mast cells]] degranulation triggered by dust, dander, pollen
- Increased, sudden constrictions of [[Smooth Muscle|smooth muscle]] → [[bronchospasms]] 
- Relevant pathways (e.g. immune, hormonal)

##### Clinical Features
- Difficulty breathing → mild to severe
- Signs
- Variants

- Test

- Criteria

##### Treatment
- [[Epinephrine]] during asthma attacks → relax muscle and increase diameter by stimulating [[Sympathetic Nervous System|sympathetic]] nervous system
- Surgical
- Lifestyl.
