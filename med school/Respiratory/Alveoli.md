*Site of all gas exchange*

- NO smooth muscle
- Walls of [[Elastic Fibers|elastic fiber]] and [[Reticular Fibers|reticular fiber]]
- Surrounded by capillaries

3 Layers
1) Capillary endothelium
2) Dual fused [[basement membrane]]
3) [[Type I Pneumocytes|Type 1 pneumocytes]]
