*Tetrametric globular protein made of 2 heterodimers each made of alpha and beta chain*
- Chains held together by hydrophobic bond
- Heterodimers held by ionic and hydrogen bonds

Heme molecule in center that binds to $O_2$
- When deoxygenated, molecules are NOT planar (**Tense State**)
- When oxygenated, planar (**Relaxed State**)
