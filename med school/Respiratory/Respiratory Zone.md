*MUST have [[Alveoli]] to be part of this zone*

1) Respiratory bronchioles – some gas exchange
	- Off of terminal [[bronchioles]]
	- Simple cuboidal epithelium **interrupted by alveoli**
	- [[Cilia]] decrease till gone
	- Some smooth muscle fibers around alveolar openings
	
![[Screenshot 2025-08-16 at 8.26.17 PM.png]]

2) [[Alveolar Ducts]] – Conduct air with much gas exchange
	- Non-ciliated simple cuboidal epithelium between MANY [[Alveoli]]
	- Knobs of [[Smooth Muscle|smooth muscle]] into lumen where alveoli walls meet 
	
![[Screenshot 2025-08-16 at 8.27.51 PM.png]]

3) Alveolar Sacs: Conducts air with much gas exchange
	- Knobs almost obliterated, no smooth muscle

![[Screenshot 2025-08-16 at 8.28.45 PM.png]]
