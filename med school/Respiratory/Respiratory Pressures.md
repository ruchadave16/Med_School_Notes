Lungs are trying to pull inwards, chest wall is trying to expand
- Creates negative pressure in intrapleural space

$\Delta$ in size of [[thorax]] → $\Delta$ in size of [[lungs]] → $\Delta$ in intrapulmonary pressure (intra-alveolar pressure)
- Based on Boyle’s law which dictates [[inspiration]] and [[expiration]]
- These change alveolar pressure so air can come in and out
