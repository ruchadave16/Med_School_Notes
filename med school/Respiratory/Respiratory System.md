*System for gas exchange of oxygen and release of carbon dioxide between air and blood*
- Ventilation: movement of air between [[upper respiratory tract]] and [[lower respiratory tract]]
- Oxygenation: deoxygenated blood from heart to pulm capillaries, where gas is exchanged and then oxygenated blood to heart
- Respiration: Ventilation + Oxygenation
##### Structural Division
[[Upper Respiratory Tract]]: [[nasal cavity]], [[paranasal sinuses]], [[pharynx]], [[larynx]]
[[Lower Respiratory Tract]]: [[Trachea]], [[bronchi]], [[bronchioles]], [[alveoli]]

![[Screenshot 2025-08-16 at 5.17.10 PM.png]]
##### Functional Divisions
1) [[Conducting Zone]]: conducts gas, NO gas exchange
	- [[Nasal cavity]] → terminal [[bronchioles]]
	- Warms, humidifies, and filters air
	- Carries air to and from sites of exchange WITHOUT collapsing

2) [[Respiratory Zone]]: gas exchange
	- Respiratory [[bronchioles]] → [[alveolar ducts]] → alveolar sacs → [[alveoli]]
	- Lung barenchyma

>[!Warning] Respiratory failure is leading cause of perinatal mortality

System – nonfunctional before birth so delayed development
- At birth, [[alveoli]] have amniotic fluid 
- Replaced with air by mouth and nose (during delivery, pressure on thorax) and pulmonary capillaries + lymphatics (reabsorption)
