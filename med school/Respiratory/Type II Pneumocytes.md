Scattered around [[Alveoli|alveolus]], more cuboidal
Bound to [[Type I pneumocytes]] by desmosomes + tight junctions
Many [[mitochondria]]

Function: Produce [[pulmonary surfactant]]

Regeneration: Death of alveolar cells stimulates mitotic activity in type II which differentiate into Type I or II

>[!Clinical] [[Infant Respiratory Distress Syndrome]] → leading cause of death in premature babies because of incomplete differentiation of Type II pneumocytes
