*Conduct air deeper to lungs*

![[Drawing 2025-11-08 13.57.09.excalidraw]]

1) Primary bronchi: [[trachea]] divides into 2 
	- Location: **carina** → hilum
	- Similar to trachea histologically but smaller diameter
	- Cartilage rings + [[Smooth Muscle|smooth muscle]] that encircle bronchi 
	- [[Respiratory Epithelium|Respiratory epithelium]]

>[!Clinical] Aspirated foreign bodies
>Right primary is wider and straighter, foreign bodies usually lodge on **right side**

![[Pasted image 20250818235645.png]]

ALL OTHERS: 
- Lumen diameter increases
- Spiral bands of smooth muscle around bronchi
- Mixed glands with ducts opening into bronchi lumen
- Pulmonary arteries and veins adjacent
- Bronchi become smaller

2) Secondary (lobar) bronchi
	- Cartilage = irregular [[hyaline cartilage]] plates
	- 1 secondary bronchus per lobe named by lobe (3 for right lung, 2 for left)

3) Tertiary (segmental) bronchi
	- Irregular cartilage plates
	- Each serves 1 **bronchopulmonary segment**

>[!Clinical] We can remove 1 segment without disrupting others
