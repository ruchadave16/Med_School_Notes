#### External Anatomy
1) Lobes
	- Right lung: 3 lobes (superior, middle, inferior)
	- Left lung: 2 lobes (superior, inferior)

2) Fissures (between lobes)
	- Oblique Fissure
		- Right lung – separates inferior from superior + middle
		- Left lung – separates inferior from superior
		- Located posteriorly (R4 obliquely to R6 anteriorly)
	- Horizontal Fissure
		- ONLY RIGHT LUNG – separates middle + superior 
		- Located from midaxillary line along 4th rib anteriorly

**LEFT ONLY**
- Cardiac notch: indentation in anterior where heart is
- Lingula: ‘tongue-like’ projection defining inferior end of cardiac notch

![[Pasted image 20250819000457.png]]

3) Surfaces:
	- Costal – peripheral near [[ribs]]
	- Diaphragmatic – adjacent to [[diaphragm]]
	- Mediastinal – medial

4) Hilum – medial concavity where structures enter + exit (root of the lung)

5) Root of the Lung – structures passing in hilum
	- [[Bronchi]]
	- [[Pulmonary artery]] – low oxygen blood (blue)
	- [[Pulmonary vein]] – high oxygen blood (red)
	- Bronchial artery + vein
	- Nerves + lymphatics (aRALS – artery, on right is anterior, on left is superior to bronchi)
		- In right lung, pulmonary artery ANTERIOR to bronchus
		- In left lung, pulmonary artery SUPERIOR to bronchus

![[Pasted image 20250819000713.png]]

6) Bronchopulmonary Segments
	- Segment of lobe self sufficient, separated by [[connective tissue]]
		- Contains 1 lobule, 1 tertiary (segmental) bronchus, 1 tertiary pulmonary artery
	- Right has 10 segments, left has 8

INFERIOR LIMIT: MCL – rib 6, MAL – rib 8, scapular line – rib 10

#### Blood Supply
Pulmonary Vessels (oxygen exchange)
Bronchial Vessels (support lung tissue) – left from thoracic aorta, right from aorta + intercostal arteries

>[!Clinical] [[Pulmonary Embolism]]
>

#### Innervation
[[Sympathetic Nervous System|Sympathetic]]
- [[Sympathetic trunk]] T1-T4 → [[bronchodilation]] → larger airway → breath in

[[Parasympathetic Nervous System|Parasympathetic]]
- [[Vagus Nerve]] → [[bronchoconstriction]] → smaller airway → breath less

[[Visceral Afferent Fibers|Visceral afferent]] (sensory)
- Reflexes on vagus n.
	- Cough, stretch, Hering-Breuer, chemoreceptors, pressor receptors
- Pain fibers – Lung insensitive to pain

>[!Clinical] [[Pancoast Tumor]]

#### Lymphatics

>[!Info] Pulmonary nodes (secondary [[bronchi]]) → bronchopulmonary (hilar) nodes (at hilum) → inferior tracheobronchial nodes → superior tracheobronchial nodes → paratracheal nodes → R/L bronchomediastinal trunks

- Inferior tracheobronchial nodes (Carinal)
	- Left: lymph from inferior left lobe
	- Right: lymph from inferior right lobe
- Superior tracheobronchial nodes
	- Left: lymph from left superior lobe
	- Right: lymph from right superior and middle, right inferior tracheobronchial nodes, most form left trachebronchial nodes

>[!Clinical] [[Bronchogenic carcinoma]] in LEFT INFERIOR will metastasize to RIGHT side nodes
