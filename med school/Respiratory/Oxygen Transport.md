Minimal as dissolved oxygen, mainly oxygenated or oxyhemoglobin

*[[Hemoglobin]] binds to up to 4 $O_2$ molecules through cooperative binding*

![[Pasted image 20250818220626.png]]

		Red: Loading region (hemoglobin binds tightly to O2)
		Blue: Unloading region (decrease in saturation)

Curve can also shift to change affinity of Hb – Right shift causes it to release more easily and left makes it hold more tightly
##### Right Shift
- Increase in $P_{CO2}$
- Decrease in pH
- ↑ Temperature
- ↑ 2,3-DPG (during low $O_2$ conditions)
##### Left Shift
Opposites of above + [[CO poisoning]]
