#### Cartilages
- 9 cartilages, located C3-6 vertebral levels

1) Thyroid Cartilage
	- Largest, superior border at C4 vertebra
	- Laminae – bilateral plates anterior side, fused
	- Laryngeal prominence – (adam’s apple), midline protrusion that protects vocal folds
	- Superior thyroid notch –  V shaped thickening of laminae
	- Superior horns – posterior border of laminae 
	- Inferior horns – articulate with cricoid cartilage
	- Thyrohyoid membrane – connects to hyoid bone

2) Cricoid cartilage
	- Complete ring around airway
	- Superior border at C6
	- Cricothyroid ligament 
	- Cricotracheal ligament 

>[!Clinical] Cricothyroctomy
>- Emergency to open airway if no intubation
>- Needle inserted through cricothyroid ligament

3) [[Epiglottis]]
4) Arytenoid Cartilage
	- 3 sided cartilage that attaches to cricoid
	- Crico-arytenoid joints
#### Inside of Larynx
- Laryngeal cavity – inlet → inferior border of cricoid cartilage
- Vestibule – space between inlet and vestibular folds
- Vocal apparatus
- Laryngeal vestibule 
- Infraglottic cavity

#### Vocal Apparatus
- Widest part of larynx 
- Bilateral symmetric pairs of mucous folds
	- **Vestibular Fold** (False Vocal Cords)
		- Thyroid cartilage above vocal ligament → arytenoid cartilage
		- Immovable 
		- [[Respiratory Epithelium]]
		- NOT part of vocalization
		- Mucous and serous glands in [[Respiratory Lamina Propria|lamina propria]]
	- **Vocal Fold** (True Vocal Cords)
		- Angle of thyroid cartilage to vocal processes of arytenoid cartilage
		- Makes sounds 
		- Stratified squamous epithelium
	- Vocal Ligament 
		- Dense regular connective tissue that pulls on vocal cords
	- Vocalis Muscle
		- [[Skeletal Muscle]] under vocal cords that controls tension

#### Innervation
- Superior laryngeal n. ← [[Vagus Nerve|CN X]]
	- Internal branch – sensory to internal laryngeal cavity above vocal cords
	- External branch – supplies cricothyroid m.
- Inferior laryngeal n. ← [[Vagus Nerve|CN X]] ([[Recurrent Laryngeal Nerve|recurrent laryngeal n.]])
	- All other laryngeal m.

#### Vasculature
- Branches of superior and inferior thyroid a. 
- Superior laryngeal ← superior thyroid ← [[External Carotid Artery|external carotid a.]]
- Inferior laryngeal ← inferior thyroid ← thyrocervical trunk ← [[Subclavian Artery|subclavian a.]]
