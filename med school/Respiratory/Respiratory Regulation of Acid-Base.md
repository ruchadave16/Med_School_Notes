↑ in ventilation → ↓ $P_{CO2}$ → ↓ $HCO_3$ → less acid, higher pH ([[alkalosis]])
↓ in ventilation → ↑ $P_{CO2}$ → ↑ $HCO_3$ → more acid, lower pH ([[acidosis]])

#### Feedback Regulation
↓ pH → ventilation increases → ↓ $P_{CO2}$ → ↓ $HCO_3$  → less acid, so pH increases
↑  pH → ventilation decreases → ↑ $P_{CO2}$ → ↑ $HCO_3$  → more acid, so pH decreases
