*CO2 retention due to hypoventilation causes ↓ pH*

**Causes**: trauma to respiratory control center, pulm. obstruction, respiratory muscle weakness, overdose

- [[Buffers]]: [[Intracellular Buffers|Intracellular buffering]]
- [[Respiratory Regulation of Acid-Base|Lungs]] (minutes): CANNOT compensate because cause
- [[Renal Regulation of Acid-Base|Kidneys]] (hours/days): ONLY method
	- ↑ secretion of $H^+$ and ↑ formation of $HCO_3^-$ 
	- More acidic urine
	- pH ↑ brought back to normal
