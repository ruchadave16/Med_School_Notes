- **Respiratory diverticulum** (lung bud) comes off of ventral wall of foregut on 4th week
- 2 **Tracheoesophageal ridges** bilaterally during elongation
- Grow inwards to meet and form tracheoesophageal septum
	- Forms laryngotracheal tube
	- Cranial part = larynx + trachea
	- Caudal part = bronchi + lungs
	
![[Screenshot 2025-08-23 at 4.52.55 PM.png]]

>[!Clinical] [[Congenital Tracheoesophageal Fistula]]: Incomplete fusion of ridges
