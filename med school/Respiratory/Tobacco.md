**#1 preventable death in US**
- 400,000 deaths a year
- Survival reduced by 7.5 years with continued tobacco use
#### Diseases
- [[Lung Cancer]]
	- ↑ mucus → [[bronchitis]]
	- ↑ inflammation eventually causing [[metaplasia]]
	- Especially benzopyrene can directly cause 
- Bladder cancer, esophagus cancer, pancreas cancer, kidney cancer, colon cancer, uterine cervix cancer
- [[Atherosclerosis]]
	- [[peripheral artery disease]]
	- [[coronary heart disease]]
	- [[stroke]]
- [[COPD]]
	- [[Emphysema]]
	- Chronic bronchitis
	- [[Asthma]]
#### Developmental
- Maternal smoking increases risk for:
	- spontaneous abortion
	- preterm birth
	- growth retardation
