#### Large Airway Development
- Week 5 – Lung bud caudal ends bifurcate and grow into splanchnic [[mesoderm]]
- Week 5-7 – Buds split to form bronchi
- Tube lined by **endodermal** cells 
- Splanchnic [[mesoderm]] turns into cartilage, smooth muscle, and connective tissue of bronchi, trachea, and lungs

>[!Clinical]
>[[Laryngomalacia]] – malformation of [[cartilage]] in [[larynx]] → airway closes when supine
>[[Tracheomalacia]] – malformation of cartilage in [[trachea]]
