##### Histology
- [[Nasal Cavity]] = paired chambers separate by septum 
	- Bone + cartilage lined w [[Respiratory Epithelium|respiratory epithelium]]
	- [[Mucous gland]]s + venous sinuses in [[Respiratory Lamina Propria|lamina propria]]
	- [[Venous Plexus]] = in [[Respiratory Lamina Propria|lamina propria]] of septum 
		- Soft tissue where blood vessels swell, blocking air to nostril at time 
		- Prevent overdrying of epithelium

![[Screenshot 2025-08-16 at 5.46.04 PM.png]]

- [[Olfactory Epithelium]] = roof of each fossa, close to brain
	- Thick pseudostratified columnar epithelium
	- [[Olfactory receptor cells]] (bipolar neurons)
	- **NO goblet cells** but has [[olfactory glands]] in [[Respiratory Lamina Propria|lamina propria]] to keep moist
- [[Paranasal Sinuses]] = air-filled spaces connected to nasal cavity, lined with [[respiratory epithelium]] 
- [[Pharynx]] 
	- [[Nasopharynx]] – has respiratory epithelium
	- [[Oropharynx]] – non-keratinized stratified squamous epithelium
	- [[Laryngopharynx]] – non-keratinized stratified squamous epithelium
- [[Larynx]] 
- [[Trachea]] 
- [[Bronchi]] (ALL contain cartilage)
- [[Bronchioles]] (ALL have NO cartilage and prominent smooth muscle circle)
