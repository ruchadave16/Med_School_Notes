### Definition
**Leading** cause of death in premature babies

### Etiology
- Premature babies
- Gestational diabetes
- C-section delivery

### Pathophysiology
- Incomplete differentiation of [[Type II Pneumocytes|type II pneumocytes]] → lack of [[Pulmonary Surfactant|pulmonary surfactant]] → ↑ surface tension → alveolar collapse (atelectasis)
- Difficulty expanding [[alveoli]] → lower SA for gas exchange and low $O_2$ saturation

### Clinical Features
- Respiratory distress
- Signs
- Variants

### Diagnosis
##### Imaging
- Ground glass appearance on X-Ray
 
![[Pasted image 20250823173216.png]]

### Treatment
- [[Glucocorticoid]] treatment for at-risk pregnancies increases lung development
- Premature babies given artificial surfactant
- Mechanical ventilation before 26 weeks 
	- Can damage alveolar lining causing [[bronchopulmonary dysplasia]] or [[intraventricular hemorrhage]]

- Short-term
- Long-term
