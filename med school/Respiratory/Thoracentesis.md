*Procedure to access [[Pleura|pleural cavity]] through needle in intercostal space*

**Purpose**: sample pleural fluid or drain if symptomatic

##### Procedure
- Insert needle superior to rib **(AVOID VAN)**
- High enough to avoid collateral branches
- Pass through intercostal muscles and costal parietal pleura into pleural cavity
- Generally 9th intercostal space in midaxillary line during expiration
- Angle upwards to **avoid penetrating deep side of recess**

>[!Warning]
>Don’t put in midclavicular line because it’s too hard to get to 
>NOT near paravertebral (erector spinae)
>Go more close to midaxillary line **
>- Chance of damaging lungs in 7th intercostal space
>- Nerve might be a bit lower than expected so palpate 9th rib and go right above the bone of the 9th rib
