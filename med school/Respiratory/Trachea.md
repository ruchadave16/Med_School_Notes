16-20 C-shaped [[cartilage]]nous rings
- **C6 → T4** vertebral levels
- **Carina** = ridge of cartilage between left and right [[bronchi]]
	- Located at T4 (sternal angle)
	- Posterior to [[ascending aorta]], anteromedial to [[thoracic aorta]]

>[!Clinical] Carina is landmark during [[bronchoscopy]] and condition can diagnose respiratory disease
##### Epithelium
Pseudostratified columnar with [[Cilia|cilia]] and [[goblet cells]]
[[Respiratory lamina propria]] with seromucous glands that drain into lumen of trachea
C-shaped ([[hyaline cartilage]])
- Face [[esophagus]] → Lets them flex when swallowing food
- Ends joined by 
	- Smooth muscle ([[trachealis muscle]]) which constricts lumen and increases airflow 
	- Fibroelastic ligament which prevents overstretching
