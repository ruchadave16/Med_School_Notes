##### Pathophysiology
- Degradation of [[elastase]] and other proteins in alveolar wall due to enzymes from [[Alveolar Macrophages|macrophages]] and [[neutrophils]]
- Protease activity causes loss of [[alveoli]], alveolar capillaries, and parts distal to terminal bronchiole → loss of gas exchange area
- Loss of elastic tissue + recoil → lungs and chest walls to expand 
- Airways narrow


##### Clinical Features
- Difficulty exhaling
- Pursed lip breathing → increases airway pressure + opens airways
