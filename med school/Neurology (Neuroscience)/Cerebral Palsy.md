### Etiology
- Primary causes
	- Damage to brain before, during, or right after birth
	- Hypoxic or ischemic injury
- Risk factors

### Pathophysiology
- Nonprogressive neurological disorder
- Brain cell death → disruption of white matter tracts
- Abnormal myelination

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Motor impairments ([[spasticity]], [[dyskinesia]], [[ataxia]])
	- Issues with mobility, fine motor, speech
	- Delayed **developmental milestones**
	- Abnormal muscle tone
	- Feeding difficulties
	- Seizures
- Signs
- Variants

### Diagnosis
- Imaging
	- MRI
- Developmental assessments
- Genetic testing

### Treatment
- Physical therapy
- Occupational therapy
- Speech + language therapy

- Medications
	- Antispastic ([[baclofen]]), Antiepileptics
- Orthopedic surgery for joint deformities

### Complications
- Short-term
- Long-term
