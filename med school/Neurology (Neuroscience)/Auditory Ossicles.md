*Mechanically brings sound from external to inner ear*

- Malleus (hammer) – connects to [[tympanic membrane]]  
	- Tendon of tensor tympani m. inserts here
	- Chorda tympani n. crosses medial surface
- Incus (anvil) – middle bone
- Stapes (stirrup) – medial bone connecting to oval window

#### Muscles
- Tensor tympani m. 
	- From cartilaginous part of [[Pharyngotympanic Tube|pharyngotympanic tube]], inserts on malleus and pulls it to tense [[tympanic membrane]]
	- Innervation: [[Mandibular Nerve|CN V3]]
- Stapedius m.
	- Pulls stapes posteriorly
	- Innervation: Nerve from [[Facial Nerve|CN VII]]
