Lateral to [[Vertebral Column|vertebral column]] from skull to sacrum
- Paravertebral ganglia linked together

>[!Clinical] [[Pancoast Tumor|Pancoast tumor]] compresses [[stellate ganglion]] (anterior to C6-7) which shows as [[Horner’s Syndrome]]

**[[Cervical Sympathetic Trunk]]**

**Thoracic Viscera Sympathetic Innervation**
- Preganglionic bodies in T1-T5 spinal cord
- [[Heart]]: Postganglionic in **cervical ganglia**
- [[Lungs]]: Postganglionic in T1-T5

Function: ↑ heart rate, ↑ contractility, [[bronchodilation]]

>[!Clinical] [[Asthma]] inhalers like sympathetic neurotransmitters in bronchi

[[Autonomic Plexus]] → sympathetic + parasympathetic fibers

[[Splanchnic nerves]] – off of sympathetic trunk
- Preganglionic innervated abdominal viscera
- Medially from sympathetic trunk to be anterior of esophagus

[[Visceral Afferent Fibers|Visceral afferent]] for pain in sympathetic *retrogradely*
