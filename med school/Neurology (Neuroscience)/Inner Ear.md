- At oval window, energy of vibration of ossicles converted to sonar waves in fluid
- Contains vestibulocochlear organ
	- [[Cochlea]] – hearing
	- [[Vestibule]] – balance
	
![[Drawing 2025-10-31 19.51.04.excalidraw]]
#### Components
1) Bony Labyrinth
	- Fluid filled cavity within petrous part of temporal bone
	- [[Vestibule]] – central cavity with **saccule** and **utricle**
		- Linear movement
		- Vestibular n. 
	- 3 Semi-circular Canals – with [[semicircular ducts]]
		- Rotational movement
		- Vestibular n. 
	- [[Cochlea]] – snail-like cavity around modiolus with [[cochlear duct]]
		- Sound
		- Cochlear n. 

2) Membranous Labyrinth
	- Fluid sac within bony labyrinth

3) Labyrinth Fluids
	- Perilymph
		- Inside bony labyrinth
		- Similar composition to extracellular fluid – high Na, low K, minimal protein
	- Endolymph
		- Inside membranous labyrinth
		- Similar to intracellular fluid – high K, low Na
		- Made by stria vascularis
