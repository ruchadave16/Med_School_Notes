*Coverings of brain right under calvarium*

1) [[Dura Mater]]
2) Arachnoid Mater
	- Has granulations of villi
	- Subarachnoid space has [[CSF]] and major blood vessels
3) Pia Mater
	- Connects to brain parenchyma through glial end feet
