- Mu ($\mu$) – affinity for endorphins
	- Mediate euphoria, respiratory distress
- Delta $\delta$ – affinity for enkephalins
	- Analgesia and emotional states
- Kappa $\kappa$ – affinity for dynorphins
	- Analgesia but can cause dysphoria
#### Mechanism
[[G Protein Coupled Receptors|GPCR]] coupled to Gi
- Inhibition of adenylyl cyclase → ↓ cAMP
- Closure of volt gated Ca2+ channels → low neurotransmitter release
- Opening of K+ channels – hyperpolarization
