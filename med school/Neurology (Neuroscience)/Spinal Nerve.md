Initially named by relation to [[Spinal Cord|vertebral column]]

- 8 [[cervical nerves]] (C1-C8)
	- **Exit above the [[Spinal Cord|vertebra]] with the same number
	- Rest 3 types exit below
	- C2-C4 innervate back of head generally
	- C5-C8 innervate upper limb
- 12 [[Thoracic Spinal Nerves|thoracic nerves]](T1-T12)
	- T1-T12 innervate trunk + abdominal
- 5 [[lumbar nerves]] (L1-L5)
	- L1-L5 innervate portion of back, portion of lower limb
- 5 [[sacral nerves]] (S1-S5)
	- S1-S5 innervate [[perineum]], portion of lower limb

>[!Clinical] Damage to spinal nerve at [[Spinal Cord|spinal cord]] leads to loss of function on a **[[myotome]]** and sensory loss on a **[[Dermatome]]**

![[Screenshot 2025-07-17 at 7.39.16 PM.png]]

- [[Myotome]] and [[Dermatome]] come from same [[Somites|somite]] (section)

>[!Clinical]
>[[Stenosis]] can compress [[spinal nerve]]
