*Air filled extension of nasal cavity*

![[Pasted image 20251101142546.png]]

1) Frontal Sinuses
	- In frontal bone
	- Drain to semilunar hiatus of middle nasal meatus
	- Innervation = [[Ophthalmic Nerve|CN V1]] – supraorbital n
2) Ethmoidal cells
	- Anterior: Drain to semilunar hiatus of middle nasal meatus
	- Middle: Drain to middle meatus 
	- Posterior: Drain to superior meatus
	- Innervation = [[Ophthalmic Nerve|CN V1]] anterior and posterior ethmoidal branches of nasociliary n.
3) Sphenoidal Sinuses
	- In sphenoid bone
	- Open into sphenoethmoidal recess
	- Innervation = posterior ethmoidal a. ← ophthalmic a. and n. ([[Ophthalmic Nerve|CN V1]])
4) Maxillary sinuses
	- Drain to semilunar hiatus of middle nasal meatus
	- Innervation = [[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]] branches
	- Alveolar n. through here

>[!Clinical]
>Most commonly infected = maxillary sinus
>- Injury due to blowout fracture of orbit
>- Dental removal of maxillary molar teeth can injure floor of sinus
