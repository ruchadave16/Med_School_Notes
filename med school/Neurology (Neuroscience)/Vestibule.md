*Collects information on balance, head position, equilibrium (positional)*

Components
- Utricle 
- Saccule

Each has a [[macula]] (sensory component)
- Epithelium with [[hair cells]] with stereocilia and kinocilium
- Otoliths – crystals of calcium carbonate

**Detects linear movement**
#### Innervation
- Vestibular division of [[Vestibulocochlear Nerve|vestibulocochlear n.]] (CN VIII)
	- Hair cells send afferent fibers, sensory signal
