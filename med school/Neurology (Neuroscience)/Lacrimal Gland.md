*Secretes lacrimal fluid (aqueous part of [[Tear Film]])*

#### Divisions
Superior orbital and inferior palpebral portion ← tendon of levator palpebrae superioris separates the two
#### Drainage
- Lacrimal canaliculi: median angle of eye, drained to nasolacrimal duct
- [[Nasolacrimal duct]]: brings to inferior nasal meatus of nasal cavity
#### Nerves
- Sensory: Lacrimal n. ← [[Ophthalmic Nerve|CN V1]]
- Presynaptic parasympathetic: [[Facial Nerve|facial n.]] (CN VII) by greater petrosal n. → nerve of pterygoid canal → [[pterygopalatine ganglion]] 
	- Mixed fibers: zygomatic n. ([[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]]) → lacrimal n. ([[Ophthalmic Nerve|CN V1]])
- Postsynaptic sympathetic: superior cervical ganglion of [[Cervical Sympathetic Trunk|cervical sympathetic trunk]] →  internal carotid plexus and deep petrosal n.
