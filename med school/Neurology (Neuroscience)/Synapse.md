Electrical: 
- [[gap junction]]s – ionic signals cross directly
- Rare in adult CNS
- Prominent during development and pathological conditions

Chemical:
- Typical synapse
	- Presynaptic and postsynaptic membranes
	- Synaptic vesicle – [[Neurotransmitters|neurotransmitter]]
	- Synaptic cleft
	- Excitatory / Inhibitory signals
