*Transmembrane protein encoded by ABCB1 gene*

- Mediates efflux of xenobiotics and drugs
- Important for drug resistance –  pumps agents out of tumor cells

#### Inhibition
- Increased CNS drug levels
- **Amiodarone, [[erythromycin]], diltiazem, verapamil, itraconazole, ritonavir, quinidine**

#### Induction
- Activation of PXR induces P-gp expression
- [[Rifampin]], [[St Johns Wort]]
