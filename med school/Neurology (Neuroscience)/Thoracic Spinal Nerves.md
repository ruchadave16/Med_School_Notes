T1 - T12 into 2 parts:
→ Exit below thoracic [[vertebra]] of same number 

##### Anterior (Ventral) Rami 
T1-T11 form **Intercostal nerves** that run in intercostal space with same number
- Motor: Intercostal muscle at same level
	- T7-T11 exit deep to costal margin and innervate [[abdominal wall]] muscles
- Sensory: wall and skin at that level
	- **T2**: sternal angle
	- **T4**: nipple
	- **T10**: umbilicus

T12 (**subcostal nerve**) → runs below rib 12

##### Posterior (Dorsal) Rami
Supply joints, deep back muscles, skin along spine
