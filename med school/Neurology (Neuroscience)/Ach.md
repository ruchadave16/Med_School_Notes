*Excitatory and inhibitory [[Neurotransmitters|neurotransmitter]] in [[CNS]]*
- Cholinergic neurons originate in nuclei of forebrain
- Diffuse to cortex, hippocampus, etc
#### Receptors
- [[Nicotinic Receptors]]: ionotropic, ion channels
	- Presynaptic in CNS
	- Facilitate release of other neurotransmitters
- [[Muscarinic Receptors]] (GPCR)
	- M1 (Gq) = slow excitation
	- M2 (Gi) = slow inhibition

>[!Clinical]
>[[Alzheimers Disease|Alzheimers]] decline associated with loss of cholinergic neurons
>- Therapy = [[Acetylcholinesterase|AchE]] inhibitors
