*3 ducts of membranous labyrinth in semicircular canals*

- Ampulla = wide part of semicircular duct, has 1 crista ampullaris
#### Crista Ampullaris
Raised epithelium perpendicular to duct
- Has hair cells and support cells – stereocilia in **cupula** with NO otoliths
- Branch of [[Facial Nerve|CN VII]] underneath
- Head rotation makes endolymph move
#### Innervation
- [[Vestibulocochlear Nerve|CN VIII]]
	- Afferent fibers
