- Proteins that are resistant to degradation and aggregate into insoluble fibrils
- Transmissible between hosts
#### PrP (Prion protein)
PrP is found in mammal brain, agent for transmissible spongiform encephalopathies
- Form 1: $PrP^c$  – normal folded
- Form 2: $PrP^{Sc}$ – pathogenic form

>[!Warning] $PrP^{Sc}$ can convert normal to pathogenic through contact
>- Accumulates into plaques that are fatal
