*Cells that produce myelin sheath*

- Myelinated nerve fibers
	- Multiple wrap
	- Sheath on single axonal segment (1 axon per schwann cell)
- Unmyelinated nerve fibers
	- Schwann cells help sustain axons
	- Single wrap – many axons in 1 schwann cell

>[!Example] C-Fibers bunched together by Schwann cell (Remak Bundle)

- Nodes of Ranvier = junction between 2 adjacent cells devoid of myelin
	- Internodes 
	- Mediate [[saltatory conduction]]
- Schmidt-Lanterman cleft – collections of cytoplasm within myelin layers
