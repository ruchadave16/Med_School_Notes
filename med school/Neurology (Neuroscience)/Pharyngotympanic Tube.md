Bony walls near [[middle ear]]
- [[Elastic Cartilage|Elastic cartilage]] with [[mucous glands]]
- Ciliated pseudostratified columnar epithelium with [[goblet cells]]

Must be opened by levator vali palatini and tensor vali palatini m. 
#### Innervation
- [[Glossopharyngeal Nerve|CN IX]] innervates mucosa
