*”network of [[Nerve|nerves]]”*

Can be somatic or autonomic

##### Somatic Plexuses
- Formed from **ventral rami** of [[Spinal Nerve|spinal nerves]]
- Cervical plexus (C1 - C4) – Innervates neck and posterior head
- [[Brachial plexus]] (C5 - T1) – Innervates upper limb
- [[Lumbar plexus]] (L1 - L4) – Innervates parts of abdominal wall and part of lower limb
- [[Sacral plexus]] (L4 - S4) – Innervates parts of lower limb and gluteal region
- **No plexus between T2 - T12** → These emerge between [[ribs]] (intercostal)

##### Autonomic Plexuses 
- Form from [[Sympathetic Nervous System]] fibers, [[Parasympathetic Nervous System]] fibers, and visceral sensory neurons
