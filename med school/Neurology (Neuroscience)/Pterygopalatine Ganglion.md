Synapse of [[Parasympathetic Nervous System|parasympathetic]] fibers of greater petrosal n. off of ([[Facial Nerve|CN VII]])

Postganglionic PS fibers → nose glands, pharynx glands, palate glands on [[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]] fibers
- [[Lacrimal gland]] fibers leave on V2 and jump onto [[Ophthalmic Nerve|CN V1]] (lacrimal n.)


>[!Clinical]
>Block applied for chronic facial pain to this ganglion
>- Through pterygomaxillary fissure or trans-nasal
