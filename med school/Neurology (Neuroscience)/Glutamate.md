*Primary excitatory [[Neurotransmitters|neurotransmitter]] in [[CNS]]*

- Ionotropic Receptors (directly control ion channels)
	- [[AMPA Receptors]] (only in CNS)
	- [[KA Receptors]]
	- [[NMDA Receptors]]

- Metabotropic Receptors ([[G Protein Coupled Receptors|GPCR]])  (mGluR receptors)
	- Group I (Gq coupled)
		- Postsynaptic
		- Excitatory, phospholipase C → IP3 → Ca2+ release
	- Group II and III (Gi coupled)
		- Presynaptic terminal
		- Inhibitory autoreceptor

>[!Warning]
>Excitotoxicity: high glutamate can cause excitatory neuronal death
