*Ionotropic [[glutamate]] receptors*

- Only in [[CNS]]
- Permeable to Ca2+, Na+, and K+
- REQUIREMENTS
	- Glutamate binding
	- [[Glycine]] binding (co-agonist)
	- Depolarization of membrane to remove Mg2+ block
#### Antagonists
- [[Memantine]] (used for [[Alzheimers Disease|Alzheimers]])
- [[Ketamine]] (for anesthesia and depression)
