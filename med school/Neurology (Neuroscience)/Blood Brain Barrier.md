*Separates blood from CNS extracellular fluid*
- Formed by capillary endothelial cells with [[tight junctions]]
- [[Astrocytes]] end feet

**Regions Excluded**
- Area Postrema
	- Part of [[vomiting center]], detects toxins through chemoreceptor trigger zone
- Regions that Secrete Neurohormones
	- Hypothalamus
	- Pineal Gland
#### Drug Permeability
- Must be **highly lipophilic** or use transporters, cannot be substrate of [[P glycoprotein]]

>[!Example]
>- First Generation Antihistamines
>	- Lipophilic: pass and cause drowsiness
>	- 2nd gen are less and cause minimal effects
>- Glucose and essential AA
>	- Cross using transporters
>- [[L DOPA]]
>	- Precursor to dopamine, crosses using AA transporter
