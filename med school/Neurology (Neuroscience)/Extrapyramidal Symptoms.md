*Motor disturbances caused by D2 blockage*

- Dystonia: Muscle spasms (1-5 days onset)
- Akathisia: Restlessness, cannot sit still (onset 5-60 days)
- Parkinsonism: Bradykinesia, rigidity, tremor, posture instability (onset 5-30 days)
- [[Neuroleptic Malignant Syndrome]] (onset weeks - months)
- [[Perioral Tremor]] (onset months to years)
- [[Tardive Dyskinesia]]: Involuntary face and limb movements (onset months to years)
