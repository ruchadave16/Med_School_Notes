*Air filled space connected to [[nasopharynx]] through [[Pharyngotympanic Tube|pharyngotympanic tube]]*

LINING: mucous membrane, simple cuboid epithelium
#### Function
Mechanical transmission of sound from outer to inner ear

Works as impedance matching system
- Ratio of high SA of tympanic membrane → small oval window
- Mechanical advantage by lever system of ossicles
#### Structure
- [[Auditory ossicles]]
- Stapedius and tensor tympani m. 
- Tympanic plexus
	- SY from internal carotid plexus and tympanic branch of [[Glossopharyngeal Nerve|CN IX]] (sensory from middle ear)
- Chorda tympani n. (branch of [[Facial Nerve|CN VII]])
	- Passes neck of malleus
	- Special sensory (taste) from anterior 2/3 of tongue (retrograde)
	- Preganglionic PS to sublingual and submandibular glands
- [[Pharyngotympanic tube]] to [[nasopharynx]]
