### Etiology
- Primary causes
- Risk factors

### Pathophysiology
- Dysfunction of cortico-striatal-thalamo-cortical circuits.

### Clinical Features
- Motor and vocal tics (multiple motor and at least 1 vocal)

### Diagnosis
- Labs
- Imaging
- Criteria
	- Occur several times a day for 1+ year
	- Onset before 18 years
#### Common Confusions
- Must exclude substance abuse, other medical conditions ([[Huntingtons Disease]], encephalitis)
### Treatment
- Habit reversal therapy

### Complications
- Short-term
- Long-term

### High-Yield Associations
- Classic buzzwords
- Boards clues
