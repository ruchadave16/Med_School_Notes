*Part of membranous labyrinth running through [[cochlea]]*

- Filled with **endolymph** (high K, low Na)
#### Boundaries
- Vestibular Membrane
	- Upper boundary
- Stria vascularis
	- Lateral border, makes endolymph
- Basilar Membrane
	- Lower border, [[Organ of Corti]] sits here
