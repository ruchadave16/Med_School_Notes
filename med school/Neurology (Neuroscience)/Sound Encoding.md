#### Frequency Encoding
- Basilar Membrane length and tension depends on location in cochlea
- Base is stiffer and shorter (high frequency vibration)
- Apex is thinner, more flexible (low frequency vibration)

Neurotonic map made based on what location neurons are at 
- Waves reach location on membrane and then energy dissipated → scala vestibula and out to middle ear through round window

#### Loudness Encoding
- Loud = ↑ energy = ↑ amplitude 
- Higher frequency action potentials AND multiple hair cells recruited

>[!Clinical]
>Hair cells become damaged if loud energy exposure often
>[[Presbycusis]] = hearing loss with age of high frequency
