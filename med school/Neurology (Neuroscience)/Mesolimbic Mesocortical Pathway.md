#### Anatomy
- Mesolimbic: links ventral tegmental area (VTA) to limbic
	- Regulates motivation, reward
	- Overactivity associated with [[Schizophrenia|schizophrenia]] positive symptoms
	- Target of [[antipsychotics]] that block D2 receptors
	- Implicated in [[addiction]]
- Mesocortical: links VTA to prefrontal cortex
	- Cognition and executive function
	- Dysfunction associated with negative symptoms in [[schizophrenia]]
