[[Mesolimbic System]] of [[dopamine]] is main target of drugs
#### Anatomy
- **Ventral Tegmental Area** (VTA)
	- In midbrain, origin
	- Dopaminergic neurons start here
- **Nucleus Accumbens**
	- Target of VTA projections
- **Projections to the Prefrontal Cortex**
	- Cognitive behavior + decision making
#### Activation
- Drugs increase [[dopamine]] levels in nucleus accumbens → artificial euphoria
