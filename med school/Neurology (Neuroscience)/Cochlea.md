**Modiolus**
- Spongy [[bone]] core surrounded by [[cochlear duct]]
	- Contains spiral ganglion with sensory cell bodies of cochlear nerve

**Helicotrema**
- Hollow apex of cochlea continuous with scala vestibuli

**Spiral Laminae**
- Thin bone ridges coming out from modiolus 

3 Components:
1) Scala vestibuli
2) [[Cochlear Duct|Scala medius]]
3) Scala tympani
