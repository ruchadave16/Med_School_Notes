Comes off of [[Vagus Nerve|vagus n.]]
- Right under r. [[Subclavian Artery|subclavian a.]]
- Left under aortic arch near ligamentum arteriosum

Ascend lateral to trachea and esophagus to innervate larynx

Controls muscles for vocalization

>[!Clinical] Damage to this nerve causes hoarseness by surgery or compression due to tumor
