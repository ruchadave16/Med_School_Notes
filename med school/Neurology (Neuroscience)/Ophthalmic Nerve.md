*Cutaneous innervation to upper face, branch of [[Trigeminal Nerve|trigeminal n.]]*

Exits from superior orbital fissure to divide into lacrimal, frontal, and nasociliary n.

1) Lacrimal n.  – lateral upper [[Eyelids|eyelid]]
	- Gets postganglioning parasympathetic from [[Facial Nerve|facial n.]] through zygomatic n. of [[Maxillary Nerve|maxillary n.]] (CN V2)
2) Supraorbital n. – middle upper [[Eyelids|eyelid]] and anterolateral forehead
	- Exits supraorbital fossa
	- Branches go to frontal sinus
3) Supratrochlear n. – medial upper [[Eyelids|eyelid]]
4) Infratrochlear n. – corner of [[eye]], lacrimal sac, nose bridge
5) External nasal n. – nose + tip
6) Nasociliary n. 
	- Anterior and posterior ethmoidal branches → anterosuperior nasal mucosa and ethmoidal cells
		- Posterior also to sphenoidal sinuses
7) Frontal n.
