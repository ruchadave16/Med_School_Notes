### Brain
- 2 layers: external and internal periosteal 
- Pain fibers

>[!Clinical] Pain of dura mater seen as headache from cutaneous or mucosal regions

##### Vasculature
- Middle meningeal a. ← [[Maxillary Artery|maxillary a.]] 
- Anterior meningeal a. ← ophthalmic a. ← [[Internal Carotid Artery|internal carotid a.]]
- Posterior meningeal a. ← occipital a. ← [[External Carotid Artery|external carotid a.]] and [[vertebral a.]] ← [[Subclavian Artery|subclavian a.]]
