*L2-L4*

Location: In [[femoral triangle]], lateral to [[Femoral Artery|femoral a.]]
- Passes under inguinal ligament, outside [[femoral sheath]]
Motor: [[anterior thigh]]
Sensory: [[anterior thigh]] and [[medial thigh]]

Terminates as saphenous n. → sensory to medial [[leg]]

>[!Clinical]
>Damage to nerve at femoral triangle → loss of knee extension and ↓ patellar reflex, loss of sensation in anterior and medial thigh and medial leg
