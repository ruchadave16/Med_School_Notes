*Superior to C1 vertebral level*

3 cervical sympathetic ganglion

1) Inferior cervical sympathetic ganglion
	- Fuses with 1st thoracic ganglion forming [[stellate ganglion]]
	- C7 vertebral level
	- Postsynaptic fibers with C7-C8 anterior rami (brachial plexus)
2) Middle cervical ganglion
	- Cricoid cartilage
	- C6 vertebral level
	- Postsynaptic fibers with C5-6 anterior rami
3) Superior cervical ganglion
	- C1-2 vertebral level
	- Postsynaptic fibers form internal carotid sympathetic plexus

>[!Clinical]
>Any damage = [[Horner’s Syndrome]]
