*Internal meningeal layer of dura mater pulls away from external periosteal layer to form reflections (infoldings)*

1) Falx cerebri
	- Cerebral fissure separating right and left cerebral hemispheres
2) Tentorium cerebelli
	- Fissure separating occipital lobe from cerebellum
3) Falx cerebelli
	- Vertical fissure under tentorium cerebelli in posterior part
	- Separates cerebellar hemispheres slightly
4) Diaphragma sellae
	- Circular sheet between clinoid processes
	- Covers [[pituitary gland]]
