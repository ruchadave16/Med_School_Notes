*[[Neurotransmitters|Neurotransmitter]] for motor control, reward, mood regulation, endocrine*

- Works on D1-D5 receptors
	- D1-like (D1, D5) = stimulate adenylyl cyclase, increase cAMP
	- D2-like (D2, D3, D4) = inhibit adenylyl cyclase, decrease cAMP
#### Major Pathways of Synthesis
1) [[Nitrostriatal Pathway]]
	- Motor control
	- Dopamine antagonism causes **[[Extrapyramidal Symptoms|extrapyramidal side effects]]**
2) [[Mesolimbic Mesocortical Pathway]]
	- Behavior + emotion
	- Dopamine antagonism reduces + symptoms, can worsen - symptoms
3) [[Tuberoinfundibular Pathway]]
	- Prolactin secretion
	- Dopamine antagonism causes [[hyperprolactinemia]]
