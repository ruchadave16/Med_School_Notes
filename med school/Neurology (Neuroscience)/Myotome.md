*“group of muscles with common action”*

Split into: 
- Dorsal epimeres:
	- Give rise to **epaxial** muscles – erector spinae
	- Innervated by [[dorsal rami]] 
- Ventral hypomeres
	- Give rise to **hypaxial** muscles – body wall + limbs
	- Innervated by [[ventral rami]]
