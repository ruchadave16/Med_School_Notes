*Organ for hearing and balance*

![[Screenshot 2025-10-28 at 3.56.55 PM.png]]

1) [[Inner Ear]]
2) [[Middle Ear]]
	- [[Tympanic Membrane]] separates middle and external ear
	- [[Pharyngotympanic tube]] connects middle ear to [[nasopharynx]]
3) [[External Ear]]

>[!Clinical]
>- [[Vertigo]]: False body rotation feeling
>- [[Meniere Disease]] – episodes of vertigo with hearing loss and tinnitus
>	- High pressure in membranous labyrinth makes it rupture
>	- Endolymph into perilymph
>- Motion Sickness – Overstimulation of vestibule because changes in acceleration
>- [[Tinnitus]]
>	- Sounds heard even without stimuli
>	- Ringing, clicking in ear
>- Hearing Loss
>	- Sensorineural: Damage to inner ear or CN VIII
>	- Conductive: Sounds don’t reach inner ear (earwax, fluid, tympanic membrane damage)
>	- Presbycusis: Age related
>- Cochlear Implant
