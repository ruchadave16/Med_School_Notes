*Found in [[smooth muscle]], [[heart]], [[glands]]*

[[G Protein Coupled Receptors|GPCR]]
- M1: brain and stomach to ↑ gastric acid secretion
	- Gq → IP3 and DAG → ↑ Ca2+
- M2: (SA and AV node) heart to ↓ heart rate and reduce contraction force
	- Relaxes GI smooth muscle
	- Relaxes [[Urinary Bladder|bladder]] sphincter
	- SA: ↓ HR
	- Atrial muscle: ↓ contraction
	- AV: ↓ conduction velocity
	- Gi → ↓ cAMP, K+ efflux
- M3: 
	- Bronchoconstriction
	- ↑ secretions (salivary, gastric, pancreatic, lacrimal, sweat, nasopharyngeal, tracheobronchial mucus)
	- Receptors in endothelial cells (smooth muscle) activated by external muscarinic agents → Produce [[nitric oxide]] → smooth muscle causing relaxation and vasodilation
	- In GI causes smooth muscle contraction directly
	- Contraction of [[detrusor muscle]]
	- Gq → IP3 and DAG → ↑ Ca2+
- M4: CNS to modulate [[neurotransmission]]
	- Gi → ↓ cAMP
- M5: CNS for dopamine pathway and vascular effect
	- Gq → IP3 and DAG

**Other impacts**
- [[Miosis]] (pupil constriction) and no accomodation because [[Ciliary Body|ciliary body]] muscles constrict
