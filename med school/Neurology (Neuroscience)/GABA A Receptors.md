#### Structure
- Ligand gated Cl- channel
- Binding of [[GABA]] opens, Cl- influx, hyperpolarization
- Multiple binding sites for modulation
	- [[Benzodiazephines]] increase frequency of opening of channel
	- Bind to site that is not GABA binding site
	- Do NOT activate GABA receptor but enhance GABA effects allosterically 

>[!Clinical]
>[[Alcohol]] enhances GABA A causing sedation 
>Chronic alcohol use causes downregulation of receptors → withdrawal symptoms
