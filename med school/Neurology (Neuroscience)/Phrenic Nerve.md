**Course**
- Anterior to anterior scalene muscle in neck
- In lateral margin of [[pericardium]] along with pericardiocophrenic a/v
- Anterior to root of lung
- Ends at [[diaphragm]]

**Function**
- Motor + sensory to [[diaphragm]]
- Sensory to [[pericardium]]
- Sensory to mediastinal + diaphragmatic pleura

>[!Clinical] Referred pain
> Irritation to pericardium, diaphragm, or parts of pleura cause pain to C3, C4, C5 [[dermatome]]
