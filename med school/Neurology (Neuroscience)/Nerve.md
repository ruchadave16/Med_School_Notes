Multiple axons wrapped in [[Connective Tissue]]
- Vary in diameter and myelination so different speeds
- Connective tissue holds nerve together

Both somatic and autonomic fibers 
Can be motor (efferent), sensory (afferent), or both

>[!Clinical] De-innervation can cause loss of function or sensation

1) [[Cranial Nerve]]s
	- Exit [[brain]] and exit skull through foramina (small openings)
	- **12 pairs** 
2) [[Spinal Nerve]]s
	- **31 pairs** 
	- Each exit [[Spinal Cord|vertebral column]] through **intervertebral foramen**
