1) Pressure waves through external auditory canal
2) Waves vibrate [[tympanic membrane]]
3) Vibrations brought to ossicles through oval window
4) Oval window moves fluid of [[inner ear]]
5) Fluid through scala vestibuli
6) Fluid vibrates vestibular membrane → scala media
7) Basilar membrane vibrates
8) Auditory hair cells move relative to tectorial membrane
9) Cilia bends causing ↑ conductance of K into cell (depolarization)
10) Voltage-gated Ca channels open
11) [[Glutamate]] released onto post-synaptic cochlear nerve fibers
