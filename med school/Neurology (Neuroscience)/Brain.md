##### Subdivisions 
- [[Cerebral Cortex]]
	- Peripheral gray matter, central white matter
	- 6 layers
	- Function: Integration of afferent and efferent information
- [[Cerebellar Cortex]]
	- 3 layers:
		- [[Molecular Layer]]
		- [[Purkinjie Layer]]
		- [[Granular Layer]]
	- Function: Coordinate balance and muscle tone
