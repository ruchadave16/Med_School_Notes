**Function**: [[Homeostasis|homeostasis]]

Preganglionic cell body in [[brainstem]] or [[Spinal Cord|spinal cord]] S2-S4
- [[Craniosacral]] division

Preganglionic fibers
- [[CN III]], [[Facial Nerve|CN VII]], [[Glossopharyngeal Nerve|CN IX]]: face innervation
- [[Vagus Nerve|CN X]]: [[thoracoabdominal viscera]] innervation
- [[Pelvic splanchnic nerve]] (S2-S4): innervation of [[pelvic viscera]] 

Ganglion:
- 4 ganglia in head: [[ciliary ganglion]], [[pterygopalatine ganglion]], [[submandibular ganglion]], [[otic ganglion]]
- In organ for thoracic, abdominal, pelvic

Postganglionic fibers
- Release [[Ach]], which binds to [[muscarinic receptors]] on target organs
