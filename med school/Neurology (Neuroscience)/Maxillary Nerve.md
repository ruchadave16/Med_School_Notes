*Branch of [[trigeminal nerve]]*

**Location**
- Exits brain and gives off Meningeal branch (sensory from dura mater of middle cranial fossa) 
- Exits skull through foramen rotundrum → [[pterygopalatine fossa]]

![[Screenshot 2025-10-29 at 9.57.52 PM.png]]


![[Screenshot 2025-10-29 at 4.26.48 PM.png]]

**Function**
- Sensory information from nasal and oral cavities, orbit, [[pharynx]], brain
- Parasympathetic and sympathetic come on and off but DON’T originate on CN V
#### Branches
- Meningeal branch comes
- 6 branches within [[pterygopalatine fossa]]
	- Nasopalatine n. – internal nasal septum, through incisive foramen to anterior [[hard palate]]
		- Sensory
		- PSY fibers run on here
		- SY fibers also jump onto here
	- Greater palatine n. – through greater palatine foramen → posterior nose and [[hard palate]]
	- Lesser palatine n. – through lesser palatine foramen → [[soft palate]]
	- Pharyngeal n. – [[nasopharynx]]
	- Nasal n. – lateral nasal wall
![[Drawing 2025-10-29 22.11.05.excalidraw]]
	- Posterior superior alveolar n. – upper molars

![[Pasted image 20251029220910.png]]

- Zygomatic n. (Cutaneous face)
	- Communicating branches to lacrimal n. ([[Ophthalmic Nerve|CN V1]])
		- Postganglionic parasympathetic from [[Facial Nerve|facial n.]]
	- Zygomaticotemporal n. – anterior temporal fossa
		- Exits zygomaticotemporal foramen
	- Zygomaticofacial n. – zygomatic bone
		- Exits zygomaticofacial foramen

![[Drawing 2025-10-29 22.06.17.excalidraw]]

3) Infraorbital n. – cutaneous to lateral nose, below eye, upper lip
	- Exits infraorbital foramen
	- Branches = middle and anterior superior alveolar n. within infraorbital canal)
		- Innervate maxillary sinus and maxillary teeth

![[Drawing 2025-10-29 22.01.15.excalidraw]]

>[!Clinical]
>Tooth pain seen with [[maxillary sinusitis]]
