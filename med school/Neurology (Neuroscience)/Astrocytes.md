Supports cells and forms end feet on [[Blood Vessels]]
Expresses [[glial fibrillary acid protein]] (GFAP)

##### Histology
Larger cells with elongated, irregular nuclei
- More open [[chromatin]] (euchromatin) compared to oligodendrocytes
- More prominent cytoplasm that is stained 

##### Function
- Regulate ionic environment 
- Contribute to [[blood brain barrier]]
- Remove excess neurotransmitters
- Form [[glial scar tissue]] after injury
