3 layers around:

1) Epineurium:
	- Dense, irregular [[Connective Tissue|connective tissue]]
	- Surrounds whole nerve
	- Contains multiple nerve fascicles
	- Merges with soft tissue
	- Location of large blood vessels
2) Perineurium
	- Loose [[Connective Tissue|connective tissue]]
	- Surrounds each fascicle individually
	- Contains multiple nerve fibers
	- Critical for nerve function (part of [[blood-nerve barrier]])
3) Endoneurium
	- Loose [[Connective Tissue|connective tissue]], surrounds axons and [[Schwann Cells|Schwann cell]]s
	- Contains [[Fibroblasts]] and capillaries
