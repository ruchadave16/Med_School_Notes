*Carries unconscious sensation and triggers reflexes*

Visceral sensation is poorly localized but somatic is very sharp

1-neuron pathway to spinal cord or brainstem
- Cell body in [[dorsal root]] ganglion or ganglia from cranial nerves

**Visceral pain** – travels retrogradely on [[sympathetic fibers]]
**Visceral reflex** – travels retrogradely on [[parasympathetic fibers]]
**Visceral sensation** – travels retrogradely on [[parasympathetic fibers]]
