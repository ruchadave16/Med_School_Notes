- Choanae – posterior opening to [[nasopharynx]]
- Respiratory area – inferior 2/3 
	- Warms and moistens air before it enters [[respiratory system]]
- Olfactory area – superior 1/3
	- Pierced by [[Olfactory Nerve|CN I]]
#### Bones
- Ethmoid: roof, septum, lateral wall
- Sphenoid: posterior part
- Frontal: roof
- Vomer: inferior septum
- Nasal: bridge of nose (anterior roof)
- Maxilla: floor, lateral walls
- Palatine: floor and septum
- Lacrimal: lateral wall

#### Lateral Wall
- Nasal conchae (superior, middle, inferior) – projections that increase SA for heat exchange
	- Middle and superior are from ethmoid
	- Inferior concha is its own bone
- Nasal meatuses – space under concha that gets drainage from [[paranasal sinuses]]
	- Superior meatus – opening for posterior ethmoid air cell and sphenopalatine foramen
	- Middle meatus – has slit called hiatus semilunaris (opening of 3 sinus)
		- Anterior ethmoidal air cell, frontal sinus, maxillary sinus
	- Inferior meatus
		- Nasolacrimal duct opening
- Sphenoethmoid recess
	- Supero-posterior to superior meatus
		- Opening of sphenoidal sinus

#### Vasculature
- Anterior and posterior ethmoidal a ← Ophthalmic a. ← [[Internal Carotid Artery|internal carotid a.]]
- Sphenopalatine a. and greater palatine a. ← [[Maxillary Artery|maxillary a.]] 
- Superior labial a. and lateral nasal a. ← facial a. ← [[External Carotid Artery|external carotid a.]]

- Submucosal venous plexus

Woodruff area: inferior, posterior, lateral
- Anastamose with sphenopalatine and pharyngeal a. 

>[!Clinical]
>Most severe, nontraumatic bleeds in Woodruff area

Kiesselbach’s Area: anterior, inferior part of nasal septum
- Arterial plexus from anterior ethmoid, greater palatine, sphenopalatine, and superior labial
- 90% nosebleeds happen here
