- Inspiratory center – controls rhythm of breathing but activity changes by chemoreceptors
- Expiratory center – inactive during restful breathing
- Apneustic center – excites inspiratory center and prolongs respiration
- Pneomotaxic center – turns off inspiration and limits tidal volume
- [[Cerebral cortex]] – overrides above by voluntary hyperventilation and hypoventilation
