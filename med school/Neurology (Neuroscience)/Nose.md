*Smell, respiration, filtration of dust, humidification of air, and secretions through [[paranasal sinuses]] and nasolacrimal ducts*

#### External Nose
- Upper 1/3 = bone (2 nasal bones, maxillary bones, and fontal bone)
- Inferior 2/3 = [[cartilage]]
- Nares = openings (nostrils)
- Vestibule of nose = interior – highly vascularized epithelium with hair

#### Nasal Septum
- Divides chamber into 2 caviities
- Perpendicular plate of ethmoid bone = superior part
- Vomer = postero-inferior part
- Septal cartilage

#### Innervation
- Sensation: (MUCOSA)
	- Posterior inferior: [[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]] nasopalatine n. (septum) and branches of greater palatine nerve (lateral wall)
	- Anterosuperior: [[Ophthalmic Nerve|CN V1]] through anterior and posterior ethmoidal branches of nasociliary n.

- SY to smooth muscle
	- Superior cervical ganglion through deep petrosal n. (postganglionic)
	- Join with greater petrosal n. to become n. to pterygoid canal
	- Fibers onto [[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]] branches

- PS to mucosal glands
	- Postganglionic body in pterygopalatine ganglion
	- Axons run on nasopalatine n. [[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]]

- [[Olfactory Nerve|CN I]] (special sense)
	- From cells in olfactory epithelium in superior nasal cavity
	- Through cribriform plate
	- End at olfactory bulb
