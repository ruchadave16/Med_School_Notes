Part of pharynx behind nasal cavity
- Has [[respiratory epithelium]]

**Contents**
- Pharyngeal tonsil (adenoid)
- Opening to [[Pharyngotympanic Tube|auditory tube]]: connects pharynx to [[middle ear]]
	- Need to contract tensor vali palatini m. to open
- Torus tubarius
	- Fold over levator veli palati m. 
	- Elevates [[soft palate]]
	- Innervation: [[Vagus Nerve|Vagus]]
- Salpingopharyngeal fold
	- Over salpingopharyngeus m. 
	- Auditory tube → palatopharyngeus m.
	- Raises [[pharynx]] and [[larynx]] when [[swallowing]]
- Tensor veli palatini
	- Innervation: [[Maxillary Nerve|maxillary n.]] (CN V3) – pharyngeal branch
