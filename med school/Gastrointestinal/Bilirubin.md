*Substance that gives bile yellow-green color*

Byproduct of [[hemoglobin metabolism]] by [[reticuloendothelial system]] where RBCs are broken down
- Attaches to [[albumin]] to travel to [[liver]]
- Liver removes bilirubin from albumin and conjugates it by **uridine diphosphate glucuronyl transferase**

Some converted to [[urobilinogen]] by gut bacteria and reabsorbed
Some converted to others that make color of feces

>[!Clinical] [[Jaundice]]
>yellowing of skin, sclera, mucosal surfaces by accumulation of bilirubin
>Due to blockage of bile system
