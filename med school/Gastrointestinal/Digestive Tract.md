#### Functions
- Protection – epithelial lining
- Digestion
- Secretory – facilitate transport and degrade food
- Absorb nutrients
- Immune
- Mobility

#### Arterial Supply
**3 branches of aorta**
1) [[Celiac trunk]]
	- Supplies [[foregut]] 
2) [[Superior Mesenteric Artery|Superior mesenteric a.]]
	- Supplies [[midgut]]
3) [[Inferior Mesenteric Artery|inferior mesenteric a.]] 
	- Supplies [[hindgut]]

**3 pairs of L/R supply other organs**
- [[kidneys]], [[suprarenals]], [[gonads]]

#### General Histology
1) Mucosa
	- [[Epithelial Tissue|Epithelium]]
	- [[Basement Membrane]]
	- Lamina Propria = Loose irregular CT
	- Muscularis mucosa = [[smooth muscle]] separating mucosa from smooth mucosa

2) Submucosa
	- Dense irregular CT with larger blood + lymphatic vessels
	- **[[Submucosal Plexus]]**

3) Muscularis Propria
	- Thick [[smooth muscle]] 
		- Inner circular layer
		- Outer longitudinal layer
		- *** [[Stomach]] has 3 layers
	- [[Myenteric plexus]] 
		- Between inner and outer muscles
		- [[Autonomic Nervous System|Autonomic]] neurons in small ganglia
		- Makes [[enteric nervous system]]

4) Serosa / Adventitia
	- Loose connective tissue
	- Simple squamous mesothelium
	- Continous w [[Mesentery Formation|mesenteries]]
	- *** Exception – replaced w adventitia where GI not in cavity but directly to adjacent structure
