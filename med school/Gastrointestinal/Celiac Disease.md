### Definition
Immune reaction to eating gluten in [[small intestine]]

### Etiology
- Primary causes
- Risk factors

### Pathophysiology
- Eventually damage to small intestine lining preventing it from absorbing nutrients
- Reduced capacity because of lack of villi and microvilli

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Diarrhea
	- Fatigue
	- Weight loss
	- Bloating
	- Anemia
- Signs
- Variants

### Diagnosis
- Labs
	- Biopsy specimen with complete loss of villi (flat mucosal surface)
- Imaging
- Criteria

### Treatment
- Pharmacologic
- Surgical
- Lifestyle

### Complications
- Short-term
- Long-term
	- Malnutrition
