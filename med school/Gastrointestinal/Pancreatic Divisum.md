### Definition
In 10% of embryos, ventral and dorsal (ducts) DON’T fuse

### Etiology
- Primary causes
- Risk factors

### Pathophysiology
- In embryo, most pancreas drained through dorsal duct (minor papilla)
- In adults, opposite and most drained through ventral duct (major papilla)
- If division remains, adults follow embryo pattern and drain through dorsal

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Some may have abdominal pain, vomiting, nausea
	- Acute + chronic pancreatitis
- Signs
- Variants
