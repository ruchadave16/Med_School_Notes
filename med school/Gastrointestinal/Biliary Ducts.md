Cystic duct – [[bile]] to and from [[gallbladder]]
Common hepatic duct – Junction of R and L hepatic ducts
Common bile duct – junction of common hepatic duct + cystic duct
- Brings bile to [[duodenum]]

Hepatopancreatic ampulla (of Vater) – union of common bile duct + pancreatic duct (enters duodenum and controlled by sphincter)

- Greater duodenal papilla – Projection into duodenum showing border of foregut + midgut
- Minor duodenal papilla – Opening of accessory pancreatic duct into descending 2nd section of duodenum

>[!Clinical] 
>Minor duodenal papilla can be associated with recurrent [[pancreatitis]] if patent
>
>[[Gallstones]] – Hard deposits of bile in gallbladder that can cause pain, [[jaundice]], 
or infection
