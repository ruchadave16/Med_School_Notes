### Definition
Failure of recanalization of duodenum during [[Duodenum Embryology|duodenum development]]

### Etiology
- Primary causes
- Risk factors
	- Common in individuals with [[Down Syndrome]] (30% of atresia have Down Syndrome)

### Pathophysiology
- Mechanism of disease
- Relevant pathways (e.g. immune, hormonal)

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- 85% infants have constriction distal to ampulla of Vater so **bilious vomit**
- Signs
	- Can increase amniotic fluid in pregnancy because fetus cannot swallow
- Variants

### Diagnosis
- Labs
- Imaging
	- Double-bubble sign (Dilation of fundus and proximal duodenum with constriction in between due to gas)
