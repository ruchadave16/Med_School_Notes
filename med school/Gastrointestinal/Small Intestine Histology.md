1) Mucosa
	- Plicae Circulares = permanent circular folds covered with villi
	- Villi – fingerlike extensions with central lacteal (open lymphatic vessel) + capillary bed
	- Epithelium – simple columnar w [[microvilli]] + goblet cells
		- Intestinal glands between villi
	- Columnar cells = [[enterocytes]] – absorptive tall columnar w oval nuclei (basal), surface has microvilli
	- Goblet cells = throughout enterocytes, ↑ as we get to colon – secrete mucous 
	- [[Paneth cells]] = basal part (exocrine glands) w large, eosinophilic glands that release lysozyme + defensins
	- [[M cells]] = epithelial in [[ileum]] that overly [[Peyer's Patches|Peyer’s patches]] w invaginations for lymphocytes + [[Langerhans Cells|dendritic cells]]
	- [[DNES cells]] – [[I cells]] (CCK), [[Mo cells]] (motilin), [[S cells]] (secretin)
