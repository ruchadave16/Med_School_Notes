*Lubricates food with mucus to help movement, initial digestion, buffering of foods*

#### Formation
Secreted by [[acinar cells]] (isotonic to [[plasma]])
[[Ductal cells]] modify it
- Absorb $Na^+$ and $Cl^-$
- Secrete $K^+$ and $HCO_3^-$
- Absorption of solutes

>[!Note] Higher flow means ductal cells CANNOT modify saliva (closer to plasma)

**Final Saliva**
- [[Tonicity|Hypotonic]]
- High $[K^+]$ and $[HCO_3^-]$

#### Functions
**$\alpha$-amylase** ([[ptyalin]])
- Digests [[starch]]
- pH optimal 7, denatured at pH 4
	- Inactive in [[stomach]]
	- Up to 75% of starch digestion due to this
	- No defect if lack of salivary amylase – pancreas can compensate

**Lingual lipase**
- Starts digestion of lipids
- Not affected by surface-active detergents
- acidic pH optimum, active in stomach + intestine

###### Antibacterial Components
- [[Lysozyme]]
	- Attacks bacterial cell walls
	- Prevents overgrowth of oral microbial infection
	- Prevents overgrowth of oral fungi ([[thrush]])
- [[Lactoferrin]]
	- Chelates iron, stops growth of organisms that need it
- IgA binding protein
	- Forms [[IgA]] which is active against viruses and bacteria
