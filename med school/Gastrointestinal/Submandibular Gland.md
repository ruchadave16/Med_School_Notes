Mixed serous/mucous
- Serous main – round nuclei with basophilic cytoplasm
- Mucous – nuclei at base with paler cytoplasm

Secrete [[amylase]] and [[lysozyme]]

![[Pasted image 20251108140724.png]]

- Lower edge of mandible
- Submandibular duct: between mylohyoid and hyoglossus m., runs above lingual n. and opens beside frenulum of tongue at **sublingual caruncles**
#### Innervation
- Parasympathetic: Presynaptic from [[Facial Nerve|CN VII]] → Chorda tympani n. → lingual n. [[Mandibular Nerve|CN V3]] and synapse on submandibular ganglion
- SYM: Superior cervical ganglion
