Forms from 2 buds off of foregut [[endoderm]]
- Ventral pancreatic bud
	- Off of hepatic diverticulum
	- Ends posteriorly because of stomach rotation + migration of liver
	- Forms main [[pancreatic duct]], [[uncinate process]], and part of pancreatic head
- Dorsal pancreatic bud
	- Forms accessory pancreatic duct, head, body, and tail of pancreas

Week 7-8, ventral bud merges with dorsal
Week 10 – [[insulin production]] starts
Week 15 – [[glucagon production]] starts

>[!Clinical]
>
>[[Annular Pancreas]]
>[[Pancreatic Divisum]] – Ventral and dorsal don’t fuse (mainly no symptoms but can cause chronic abdominal pain or pancreatitis)
