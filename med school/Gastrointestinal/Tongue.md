*Thick, muscular organ covered with mucous membrane containing taste buds in papillae*

Contains papillae, lymph nodes [[lingual tonsils]], NO submucosa
Core of [[Skeletal Muscle|skeletal muscle]], serous, and mucous glands
#### Papillae

Elevations of oral [[Epithelial Tissue|epithelium]] and lamina propria
- Filiform – most common, keratinized, NO [[taste buds]]
- Fungiform – taste bunds on apical
- Foliate – poor development in humans
- Circumvallate – Surrounded by trench, margin lined by [[taste buds]]
	- Has [[serous glands]] of von Ebner – secrete [[lipase]]
- Taste buds – 50-100 cells, onion-like structure

#### Parts
- Body = anterior 2/3
- Roof = posterior 1/3, part of anterior [[oropharynx]]
- Terminal sulcus = V shaped groove that divides into anterior 2/3 and posterior 1/3

**Dorsum**
- Midline groove – divides anterior into left and right
- Foramen Cecum – midline pit, remnant of thyroglossal duct
- [[Taste buds]] on lingual papillae
- Lingual tonsils

![[Pasted image 20251101150334.png]]

**Inferior**
- Frenulum – midline fold on floor of mouth 
- Sublingual caruncle – opening of [[Submandibular Gland|submandibular glands]]

#### Muscles
Extrinsic change position, intrinsic change shape
- Intrinsic muscles – no bony attachment
- Extrinsic
	- Genioglossus
		- Fan shaped, most of tongue
		- Depresses tongue, moves side to side
		- [[Hypoglossal Nerve|CN XII]]
	- Hyoglossus
		- Thin quadrilateral
		- Depresses tongue and shortens
		- [[Hypoglossal Nerve|CN XII]]
	- Styloglossus
		- Short triangular muscle
		- Retrudes tongue and curs its side
		- [[Hypoglossal Nerve|CN XII]]
	- Palatoglossus
		- Crescent shaped
		- Elevates posterior tongue, depresses [[soft palate]]
		- [[Vagus Nerve|CN X]] ← pharyngeal branch
![[Pasted image 20251101150540.png]]
#### Innervation
- Taste: 
	- anterior 2/3 = [[Facial Nerve|facial n.]] (CN VII) through chorda tympani n. (this joins lingual n.)
	- posterior 1/3 = [[Glossopharyngeal Nerve|glossopharyngeal n.]] (CN IX)
- Motor (muscles): 
	- [[Hypoglossal Nerve|hypoglossal n.]] (CN XII)
	- EXCEPTION: palatoglossus = [[Vagus Nerve|CN X]] pharyngeal branch
- Sensation (touch and temperature)
	- Anterior 2/3 = [[Mandibular Nerve|CN V3]] → lingual n.
	- Posterior 1/3 = [[Glossopharyngeal Nerve|CN IX]]

#### Vasculature
- Lingual a. ← [[External Carotid Artery|external carotid a.]]
#### Lymphatics
- Anterior tip → submental LN
- Lateral anterior 2/3 → submandibular LN
- Midline anterior 2/3 → inferior deep cervical LN
- Posterior 1/3 → superior deep cervical LN
