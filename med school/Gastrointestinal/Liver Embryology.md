4th week
- Hepatic diverticulum (liver bud) develops off of [[foregut]] into septum transversum
- Continued growth causes ventral [[Mesentery Formation|mesentery]] to form [[falciform ligament]] and lesser omentum to connect liver → stomach
- Foregut → hepatic diverticulum connection forms [[Biliary Ducts|common bile duct]] (found in lesser omentum)

Falciform ligament – contains left umbilical v. (turns into ligamentum teres)

>[!Clinical] [[Biliary Atresia]] – obliteration of extrahepatic and/or intrahepatic ducts 
>- Can cause jaundice, dark urine, clay-colored stool
