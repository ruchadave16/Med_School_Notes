Almost completely [[serous gland]]
Source of salivary [[amylase]] to hydrolyze ingested carbs

![[Pasted image 20251108140716.png]]

- Pierced by CN VII BUT NOT INNERVATED by facial n.
- Parotid duct: superifical to masseter m, pierces buccinator m. to drain saliva 
#### Vasculature
- Transverse facial a. ← superficial temporal a. ← [[External Carotid Artery|external carotid a.]]

#### Innervation
- PSY: preganglionic parasympathetic in [[Glossopharyngeal Nerve|CN IX]] → tympanic n. → lesser petrosal n. 
	- Synapse in otic ganglion
	- Postganglionic → auricotemporal n. ← [[Mandibular Nerve|CN V3]]
	- This secretes saliva
- SYM: postganglionic sympathetic from superior cervical ganglion → periarterial nerve plexuses of middle meiningeal a.
