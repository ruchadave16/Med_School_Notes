*Secreted by G cells in mucosa of antrum of stomach* 

- Bind to CCK-B in parietal cells of stomach
- Reduced secretion by [[somatostatin]] 

#### Actions
- Regulates [[gastrocolic reflex]] – efferent
- Activates HCl release from [[ECL cells]] in stomach
- Increases small intestine hormone secretions
- Increases gastrin mobility
