*Stores [[Gastrointestinal/Bile]] made by [[liver]] and releases to [[duodenum]]*

- **Intraperitoneal**
- In fossa on visceral surface of liver

##### Blood Supply
- Cystic a. ← right hepatic a. ← proper hepatic a. ← hepatic common a. ← [[Celiac Trunk|celiac trunk]]
- Cystohepatic triangle between cystic duct, common hepatic duct, underside of liver (contains cystic a.)

![[Pasted image 20250905234432.png]]

##### Innervation
- Parasympathetic to contract (controlled by [[Cholecystokinin|CCK]])
##### Histology
Mucosa
- Simple columnar epithelium of [[cholangiocytes]] w short [[microvilli]]
- Lamina Propria – fenestered capillaries, ↑ lymphocytes + plasma cells, sometimes mucin-secreting glands
Mucous folds **
NO muscularis mucosae or submucosa
Muscularis externa
- Thin w [[smooth muscle]] in many orientations
Adventitia
- Dense connective w visceral peritoneum (mesothelium + loose connective tissue)
- Also has elastic + adipocytes
