Blood supply to [[foregut]] 

Arises from abdominal aorta at **vertebral T12**


1) Left gastric a. 
	- Along lesser curvature of stomach in lesser omentum
	- Anastamose with R gastric a. 
	- Branch: **Esophageal a.**

2) Splenic a. 
	- Runs along superior part of [[pancreas]] into splenorenal ligament
	- Branch: 
		- **L gastroepiploic a.** – along greature curvature of stomach
		- **Short gastric a.** – supplies fundus of stomach
		- **Pancreatic a.**

3) Common hepatic a. 
	- Supplies liver, gallbladder, proximal duodenum, stomach + pancreas
	- Branches:
		- **Hepatic proper a.**
			- **R gastric a.** – along lesser curvature of stomach to anastamose with L gastric a. 
			- **R and L hepatic a.** – supply L and R lobes of liver
			- **Cystic a.** – branch of R hepatic to gallbladder
		- **Gastroduodenal a.**
			- **R gastroepiploic a.**  – along greater curvature
			- **Superior pancreaticoduodenal a.** – supplies head pancreas + proximal duodenum. Anastamose with inferior from [[Superior Mesenteric Artery|SMA]]
