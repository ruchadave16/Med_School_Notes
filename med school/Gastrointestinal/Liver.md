##### Boundaries
Mainly in RUQ
Connected to diaphragm by **coronary ligaments**
##### External Features
Lobes: Right (large, has [[gallbladder]]) and Left (all anterior) – covered by dense irregular CT (Glisson’s capsule)
Quadrate (bound by gall bladder + ligamentum teres) and caudate (bound by IVC and ligamentum venosum) (posterior) 

![[Pasted image 20250905232926.png]]

##### Internal Features
8 segments surgically removable served by branch of [[portal triad]]
- Ligamentum teres – remainder of umbilical vein
- Ligamentum venosum – remainder of ductus venosus

**Portal System**
Blood enters inferior liver through hepatic portal v. and proper hepatic a.  → liver sinusoids → empty to IVC 
##### Blood Supply
Right lobe – R hepatic a. 
Left lobe– L hepatic a. 

Both come off of proper hepatic a. ← common hepatic a. ← [[celiac trunk]]
