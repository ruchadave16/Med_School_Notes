### Definition
Splitting of belly due to failure of somatopleure closure

### Etiology
- Primary causes
- Risk factors
	- Maternal smoking
	- Illicit drugs
	- Alcohol
	- Young age

### Pathophysiology
- Somatopleure doesn’t close during [[embryo folding]]
- Intestinal loop herniates into [[amniotic cavity]]
- No covering for peritoneal sac, paraumbilical (usually right of umbilicus)
- Could be due to involution of right umbilical v.
- Relevant pathways (e.g. immune, hormonal)

- Symptoms
- Signs
- Variants

### Diagnosis
- Labs
	- Elevated $\alpha$-fetoprotein concentration in maternal serum
- Imaging
- Criteria

### Treatment
- Pharmacologic

- Long-term
