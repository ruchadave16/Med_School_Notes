Comes from abdominal aorta at **L1 vertebral level**
- Posterior to pancreas
- Anterior to horizontal part of duodenum
- Descends to RLQ


![[Screenshot 2025-09-06 at 12.31.21 AM.png]]

- **Inferior pancreaticoduodenal a.** – anastomose w superior (from celiac trunk) supplying duodenum + head pancreas
- **Intestinal aa.** – supply jejunum + ileum
- **Ileocolic a.** – ascending colon
- **Right colic a.** – ascending colon
- **Middle colic a.** – right colic flexure
- **Marginal a. (of Drummond)** – anastomose w L colic aa. (of [[Inferior Mesenteric Artery|inferior mesenteric a.]])

>[!Clinical] SMA Syndrome ([[Wilkie’s Syndrome]])
>3rd part of duodenum compressed because of change of angle between SMA and aorta
>- ↓ mesenteric fat 

>[!Clinical] [[Nutcracker Syndrome]]
>Compression of L renal vein between SMA and aorta
>- Limited venous drainage from kidney
>- Left flank pain, renal hypertension, hematuria (blood in urine), L sided varicocele
