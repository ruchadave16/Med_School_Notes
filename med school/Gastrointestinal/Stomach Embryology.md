Week 4-6 of development

- Stomach rotates 90$\degree$ clockwise on longitudinal and antero-posterior axis pushing spleen to left
- Left Vagus – anteriorly and Right Vagus – posteriorly
- Growth more on left side to form great curvature
	- Dorsal mesogastrium pulled to left as a result
	- Creates omental bursa (lesser sac) posterior to stomach

>[!Clinical] [[Hypertrophic Plyoric Stenosis]] – stenosis of stomach to small intestine opening
