#### Metabolic Functions
[[Carbohydrates]]
- [[Glycogenesis]] = creation of glycogen as storage for glucose
- [[Gluconeogenesis]] = creation of glucose from non-carb sources

Proteins
- Makes non-essential amino acids
- Makes all plasma proteins
	- [[Albumin]]
	- Clotting factors
- Turns ammonia to urea (excreted in urine)

[[Lipids]]
- [[Beta Oxidation|Fatty acid oxidation]]
- [[Cholesterol synthesis]], phospholipid synthosis, lipoprotein synthesis
- [[Bile Acid Synthesis]]

- [[Complement System|Complement]]
#### Detoxification Functions
**First pass metabolism**
- Toxins modified by [[hepatocytes]]

**[[Drug Metabolism]]**
*Phase I Biotransformation*
- P-450 cytochromes
- Oxygen added to substrate → more polar

*Phase II Biotransformation*
- Conjugation with other compounds makes hydrophilic
