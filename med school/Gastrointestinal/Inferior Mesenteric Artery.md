Anterior surface of abdominal aorta at vertebral level **L3** and descends retroperitoneally towards LLQ

- **L colic a.** – descending colon, anastomose with middle colic a. ← SMA to make marginal a.
- **Sigmoid a.** – descending + sigmoid colon
- **Superior rectal a.** – terminal branch for proximal 1/3 of rectum
