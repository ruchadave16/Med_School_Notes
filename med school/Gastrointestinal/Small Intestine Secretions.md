**[[Hormones]]**
- Enteroendocrine cells produce same hormones as in stomach
- [[Motilin]] – from Mo cells in duodenum and jejunum
	- Stimulates gastric + intestinal motility
- [[Cholecystokinin|CCK]] – from I cells in duodenum and jejunum
	- Stimulates gallbladder contraction
	- Pancreatic enzyme secretion
	- Pancreatic bicarbonate secretion
	- Pancreatic growth
	- Inhibits gastric emptying
- [[Gastric Inhibitory Peptide]] – from K cells in duodenum and jejunum
	- Increases [[insulin]] release
	- Inhibits gastric acid secretion
- [[Secretin]] – from S cells in duodenum
	- Increases pancreatic enzyme secretion
	- Pancreatic bicarbonate ion secretion
	- Pancreatic growth
	- Inhibits gastric acid secretion

**Digestive Secretions**
- [[Enterocytes]] digest food products by secreting these
	- [[Enterokinase]] – proteins
	- Disaccharidases – carbs
	- Lipases – [[lipids]]
- ↑ Secretion
	- Gastrin, histamine, prostaglandins
	- Parasympathetic (ACh, VIP)
	- Distention of gut
- ↓ Secretion
	- [[Somatostatin]]
	- Sympathetic ([[norepinephrine]])
