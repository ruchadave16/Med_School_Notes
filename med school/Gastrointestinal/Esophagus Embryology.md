[[Lung Buds Formation|Respiratory diverticulum]] develops in 4th week off of ventral wall of foregut (esophagus)
Tracheoesophageal septum separates airway from esophagus

>[!Clinical] 
>[[Congenital Tracheoesophageal Fistula]], atresia
>[[Sliding hiatal hernia]] – Gastroesophageal junction displaced upwards and cardiac stomach/esophagus in thoracic cavity
>- Happens when infant laid down
>- “Hourglass stomach”
> 
[[Paraesophageal Hiatal Hernia]] – Weak spot (fundus) herniates into thorax near esophagus but GEJ and esophagus stay there
