*Secreted by S cells in mucosa of duodenum*

Releases in reaction to increase of $H^+$ in duodenum

- Reduces constriction of stomach
- Stimulates secretion of bicarbonate water by pancreatic ductal epithelium 
- Stimulates secretion of bicarbonate into bile through liver
