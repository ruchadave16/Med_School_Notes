### Definition
Ring of [[pancreas]]

### Etiology
- Primary causes
- Risk factors

### Pathophysiology
- Poor migration of ventral bud during [[Pancreas Embryology|pancreas development]]
- Forms ring around duodenum causing [[duodenum stenosis]]
- Relevant pathways (e.g. immune, hormonal)

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Duodenum stenosis
	- CAN BE asymptomatic
- Signs
	- Infants intolerant to oral feeding with nonbilious vomit
- Variants
