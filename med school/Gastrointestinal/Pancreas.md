Functions
- [[Endocrine]] gland 
- [[Exocrine]] gland – send enzymes to [[duodenum]] for digestion + alkaline juices to neutralize acidic chyme
##### Boundaries
- **Retroperitoneal**
- L1-L2 vertebral bodies on posterior wall
##### Contents
###### [[Exocrine Pancreas]]

###### [[Endocrine Pancreas]]
##### External Features
- Head – Lodged into C curve of [[duodenum]], overlies IVC
- Neck – overlies superior mesenteric vessels + portal v.
- Body – overlies aorta, L2 vertebra, splenic v.
- Tail – anterior to left kidney, in splenorenal ligament towards hilum of spleen
##### Blood Supply
- Branches off of splenic a. ← [[celiac trunk]]
- Head also gets blood from superior ← gastroduodenal a. ← hepatic common a. ← celiac trunk AND inferior pancreaticoduodenal a. ← [[Superior Mesenteric Artery|SMA]]
##### Innervation
- Parasympathetic
##### Lymphatic Drainage
- Follow blood vessels to celiac nodes / superior mesenteric nodes
