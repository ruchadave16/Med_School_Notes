- Elevates during respiration
- Folds down during swallowing

- Aryepiglottic fold – connects base to arytenoid cartilage
- Piriform recess – folds on two sides where superior laryngeal n. (inferior branch) runs deep to mucosa
#### Histology
- Upper region: non-keratinized stratified squamous epithelium
- Transitions to pseudostratified [[Respiratory Epithelium|respiratory epithelium]]
- Core = elastic [[cartilage]]
