>[!Info] Flow in [[liver]]
>Blood: [[Portal triad]] → central vein
>Bile: Central vein → portal triad

#### Cells
- [[Hepatocytes]]
	- Large cuboidal/polyhedral cells with large central round nuclei
	- Have eosinophilic cytoplasm w mitochondria
	- Organized into plates separated by venous sinusoids
	- Reticulin – [[Collagen]] III – runs along plates of hepatocytes
- Vascular Sinusoids
	- Discontinuous capillaries w holes letting plasma into perisinusoidal space (space of Disse)
	- Hepatic stellate cells (Ito cells)
		- Stores Vitamin A + fat-soluble (ADEK)
		- ECM produced
	- Kupfferl Cells – [[Macrophage|macrophages]]

**Bile Canaliculi**
- Smallest branches of biliary tree where hepatocytes secrete bile
- Loop around 4 sides of hepatocyte
- *Canals of Hering* partially lined w hepatocytes and partially w cholangiocytes
	
>[!Info] Sinusoids → “Holes” in endothelium → “Holes” in discontinuous lamina → Perisinusoidal Space → Microvilli

**Zones**
1) Close to short axis – blood supply from portal vein and hepatic artery
	- 1st cells to get oxygen, nutrients, toxins
	- *First to die* from toxins
	- *Last to die* from ischemia – circulation problems
	- ↑ aerobic metabolism, protein synthesis
2) In middle, no boundaries
3) Furthest from short axis, closest to terminal hepatic v. (central v.)
	- Central part of traditional hexagon
	- *First to die* from ischemia – circulation
	- First to show fat accumulation
	- *Last to die* from toxin – exceptions do exist
	- ↑ [[glycolysis]], lipid formation,
