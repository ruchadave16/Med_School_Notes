Mixes food with [[saliva]]
- Lubricate + digest

Reduce size of food particles
- Minimize damage
#### Control
- Voluntary and involuntary
- Chewing is regulated by mechanoreceptors in mouth giving info to [[brainstem]]
#### Muscles
- Masseter
	- PA: Zygomatic arch, DA: mandible angle
	- Elevates jaw

>[!Clinical] [[Bruxism]] = [[hypertrophy]] of masseter due to grinding

- Temporalis
	- PA: Temporal Fossa, DA: coronoid process of mandible
	- Elevates jaw
- Medial pterygoid
	- Depresses jaw, unilaterally adducts jaw (contralateral)
- Lateral pterygoid
	- Elevates jaw
	- Unilaterally adducts jaw (contralateral)
