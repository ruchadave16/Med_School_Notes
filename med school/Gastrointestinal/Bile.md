*Substance created by [[liver]] for lipid digestion*

50% bile salts
- Amphipathic – hydrophobic end and hydrophilic end
	→ helps make micelles to transport digested lipids in aqueous solutions
40% phospholipids
4% [[cholesterol]] (to eliminate from body)
2% bile pigments

>[!Info] [[Bilirubin]] gives color to bile
#### [[Bile Acid Synthesis]]
#### Secretion of Bile Salts
Transferred to [[gallbladder]] through bile ducts
- Ions and waters pumped into bile to dilute
- [[Secretin]] increases this secretion – liver adds sodium + bicarbonate to bile
- [[Somatostatin]] decreases this secretion

Storage in [[gallbladder]]
- Fills while [[Sphincter of Oddi]] is closed
- Epithelial cells of gallbladder reabsorb water and ions to ↑ concentration

**Ejection of bile**
- Stimulus: [[Cholecystokinin|CCK]] from I cells
	- Due to [[Fatty Acids|fatty acid]] + amino acids in gut after meal
- Contracts [[gallbladder]]
- Relaxes sphincter of Oddi
- Release in *spurts*
