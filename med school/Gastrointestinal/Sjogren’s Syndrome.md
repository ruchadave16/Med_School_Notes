### Definition
[[Immune System]] disorder showing as dry eyes and dry mouth

### Etiology
- Accompanied by other immune disorders
	- [[Systemic Lupus Erythematosus|Lupus]] or [[rheumatoid arthritis]]


### Pathophysiology
- Mucous membranes 


### Clinical Features
- Dry [[Eye|eyes]] – burning, itching, gritty
- Dry mouth – mouth like its full of cotton

### Diagnosis
- Labs
- Imaging
- Criteria

### Treatment
- Pharmacologic
- Surgical
- Lifestyle

### Complications
- Dental cavities – saliva protects teeth from bacteria
- **Yeast infections** – oral [[thrush]] because of lack of [[lysozyme]]
- Vision problems
