Anorectal junction → anus

Divided by pectinate line

UPPER
- Anal columns – longitudinal folds with branches of superior rectal vessels
- Anal valves – Inferior end of columns that form pectinate line
- Anal sinuses – Blind space between columns with glands releasing mucous
- Internal anal sphincter – Smooth muscle (sympathetic)

LOWER
- External anal sphincter – Skeletal muscle ← inferior rectal n ← [[Pudendal Nerve|pudendal n.]] (S2-S4)
