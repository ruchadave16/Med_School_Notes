1) Mucosa
	- Simple columnar epithelium
		- Gastric pits – invaginations of epithelium into lamina propria (surface mucous cells)
		- Isthmus / neck – Mucous cells
		- Glands – Chief + G cells
	- Cells: 
		- Surface mucous
			- Line lumen + gastric pits
			- Secrete mucous layer w bicarbonate to protect
		- Mucous neck
			- Apical secretory granules
			- Less alkaline secretions
		- [[Parietal Cell|Parietal cells]]
		- [[Chief cells]]
		- [[G cells]]
	- Lamina Propria
		- [[D cells]] → create [[somatostatin]]
		- [[ECL cells]] → create [[histamine]]

>[!Info] Distinct Feature
>- Cardiac region
>	- Deep gastric pits
>	- Cardiac glands
>	- Limited parietal + chief cells
>	- Mainly surface + neck mucous cells
>- Fundus + Body
>	- Gastric glands to make gastric juice
>	- Short pits
>	- Mainly parietal in neck + glands
>	- ↑↑ chief cells in glands
>- Pyloric
>	- Mainly mucous cells
>	- Pyloric glands – tubular w long pits
>	- ↓ parietal + chief
>	- ↑ G and D cells

2) Submucosa
	- Mucosa + submucosa make **rugae** – folds 

3) Muscularis Externa
	- **3 layers** ← UNIQUE for stomach, rest GI = 2 layers
	- Innermost oblique, Inner circular, outer longitudinal
	- Inner circular makes pyloric sphincter
