*Secretion of [[saliva]] in oral cavity, hypotonic*

Types
- [[Parotid Gland]]
- [[Submandibular Gland]]
- [[Sublingual Gland]]
- Minor Glands
	- Non-encapsulated, usually mucous EXCEPT von Ebner on [[tongue]]
	- Lymphatic aggregates ([[IgA]] secretion)
#### Organization
- Capsule – dense irregular [[Connective Tissue|connective tissue]]
- Septa – divides gland into lobes + lobules
- Nerves, blood vessels, ducts in septa
##### Cell Arrangement
Arrangement in acinar groups – spherical mass of epithelial cells connected by junctional complexes with lumen in middle
- Serous cells
	- High staining
	- Polarized epithelial cells (pyramid shape)
	- Broad base on [[basal lamina]], tip with small, irregular [[microvilli]] facing lumen
	- Secrete enzymes
- Mucous cells
	- Pale staining
	- Oval nuclei at base
	- Cuboidal/columnar 
	- Have [[glycoproteins]]
- Myoepithelial cells
	- Between basal lamina + basal plasma membrane
- Lamina propria
	- Has [[plasma cells]]
##### Duct Organization

>[!Info] Order: secretory end pieces → intercalated ducts → striated ducts → interlobular (excretory) ducts

![[Pasted image 20250901164144.png]]

1) Striated Ducts
	- Radial striations
	- Folds in basal surface to ↑ SA 
	- ↑ [[mitochondria]] for active transport
	- High density ion pumps
2) Interlobular Ducts (Excretory)
	- In connective tissue septae separating lobules
	- Varied epithelia
	- Distal parts can be stratified with mucous-secreting and then stratified squamous w keratinization as they approach oral cavity
#### Regulation of Saliva Production
Neuron Based
- Dual [[Sympathetic Nervous System|sympathetic]] and [[Parasympathetic Nervous System|parasympathetic]]
	- **Parasympathetic dominates**
	- [[Ach]] increases secretion of [[saliva]] 
	
>[!Success] Side Effects
>[[Atropine]] is parasympathetic [[antagonist]] that blocks [[muscarinic receptors]] Ach 

![[Screenshot 2025-08-30 at 1.00.49 PM.png]]

Hormone Based
- [[Aldosterone]] ↑ $Na^+$ absorption and $K+$ secretion by [[duct cells]]
- Changes composition of saliva – NOT flow

>[!Clinical]
>[[Sjogren’s Syndrome]] – immune disorder giving dry eyes and dry mouth
