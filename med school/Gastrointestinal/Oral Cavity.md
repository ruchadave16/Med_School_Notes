*Mouth and structures – tongue, teeth, salivary glands, tonsils*
#### Function
- Receive food
- Begin chewing (mechanical digestion)
- Protective barrier
#### Structure
Stratified squamous [[Epithelial Tissue|epithelium]]
- Non-keratinized = [[soft palate]], lips, cheeks, floor of mouth
- Keratinized = gingiva, [[hard palate]]

Lamina propria
- Papillae like [[dermis]]

Submucosa
- Visible with lining oral mucosa
#### Components
Oral Vestibule – space between teeth and gums and lips and cheeks
Oral Cavity Proper – space between maxillary and mandibular dental arches
Palate – [[Hard Palate]] and [[Soft Palate]]
[[Teeth]]
[[Tongue]]
[[Salivary Gland|Salivary glands]]

### Innervation
Sensory
- Floor = branches of [[Mandibular Nerve|CN V3]]
- Roof = branches of [[Maxillary Nerve|CN V2]]
