Runs along medial part of hepatoduodenal ligament

Hepatic portal vein (posterior), common bile duct (anterior R), proper hepatic a. (anterior L)
- Vein brings nutrient rich blood to liver (from combination of superior mesenteric v. and splenic v.)

>[!Clinical] [[Hepatic Portal Hypertension]]


Portal vein – largest, least round, floppiest
- Flat endothelial cells

Hepatic artery
- Smaller, round, muscular cells

Bile duct
- Small, round with cuboidal epithelial cells
