*Lining of tissue with minimal blood flow that compartmentalizes abdominal cavity*

- Parietal peritoneum – lines inner [[Anterior Abdominal Wall|anterior abdominal wall]]
- Visceral peritoneum – covers organs in abdominal cavity

Both are continuous and have **peritoneal cavity** in between
#### Ligaments
- Gastrophrenic lig – stomach to inferior diaphragm
- Gastrosplenic lig. – stomach to hilum of spleen (dorsal mesogastrium)
- Splenorenal lig. – spleen to kidney (dorsal mesogastrium)
- Falciform lig. – anterior abdominal wall to anterior liver (ventral mesentery)
- Ligamentum teres hepatis – in margin of falciform ligament, remnant of umb. vein
- Coronary lig. – on superior part of liver, turn into triangular ligaments
