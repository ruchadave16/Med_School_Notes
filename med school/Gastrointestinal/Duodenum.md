- Proximal duodenum = [[foregut]]
- Rest = [[midgut]]

Mainly **retroperitoneal**
Fixed by parietal peritoneum to posterior wall by mesoduodenum
#### Components

![[Screenshot 2025-09-05 at 11.11.43 PM.png]]
1) Superior (A + B)
	- Anterolateral to body of L1 vertebra
2) Descending (C)
	- Curves around pancreas head
	- Recieves Ampulla of Vater which opens onto major duodenal papilla
3) Horizontal (D) 
	- Over IVC, aorta, L3 vertebra and under superior mesenteric vessels
4) Ascending (E)
	- Superior along left aorta to reach inferior pancreas border
	- Held by suspensory ligament (of Treitz)
#### Blood Supply
Arterial
- Superior pancreaticoduodenal a. – off of gastroduodenal a. ← hepatic common a. ← [[celiac trunk]]
	- Supplies proximal to major duodenal papilla
- Inferior pancreaticoduodenal a. – off of superior mesenteric a. 
	- Supplies distal to major duodenal papilla
#### Lymphatics
- Lymph nodes drain to superior mesenteric lymph nodes or celiac lymph nodes

>[!Clinical] Because shared lymph with [[pancreas]] head, cancers of duodenum and head of pancreas resected with pancreaticoduodenectomy ([[Whipple procedure]])
