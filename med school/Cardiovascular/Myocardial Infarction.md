##### Definition
Heart attack because of lack of [[blood]] and oxygen to heart

##### Etiology
- Blocked [[Coronary Arteries|coronary artery]] → [[Coagulative Necrosis|coagulative necrosis]]
- Risk factors
	- low [[tPA]] (increased chance of thrombosis of coronary artery because ↓ plasmin)

##### Pathophysiology
- Mechanism of disease
- Relevant pathways (e.g. immune, hormonal)

##### Clinical Features
- Chest pain, referred pain to shoulder, mid-thoracic back, arm
- Diaphoresis, nausea, vomiting, SOB, fatigue
