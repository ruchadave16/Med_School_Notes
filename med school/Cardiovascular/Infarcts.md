*Region of tissue that has died most commonly due to [[ischemia]] or [[hypoxia]]*

Can be “white” (no hemorrhage ← heart, spleen, kidney) or “red” (hemorrhage)
Wedge shaped 
Septic – infarct covered in abscess

Histology most often is [[Coagulative Necrosis|coagulative necrosis]]
- Arterial occlusion = **most common cause for tissue infarction**
- Venous occlusion more commonly cause [[congestion]] but can also cause infarct

>[!Warning]
>Exception = [[brain infarct]]: [[Liquefactive Necrosis|liquefactive necrosis]]
