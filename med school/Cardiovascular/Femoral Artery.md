Starts under [[Inguinal Ligament|inguinal ligament] → through [[femoral triangle]] → adductor hiatus (becomes [[Popliteal Artery|popliteal a.]])

Supplies [[anterior thigh]] and [[medial thigh]]
#### Branches
- Deep femoral a. (profunda femoris a.) – down medial side of femur, supplies posterior 
	- Medial circumflex femoral a. – around iliopsoas, supplies neck and head of [[femur]]
	- Lateral circumflex femoral a. – 3 branches to supply hip, lateral thigh, knee
	- Perforating aa. (4) – through adductor magnus to [[posterior thigh]]

>[!Clinical]
>Compression of femoral artery to stop lower extremity bleeding
>
>Fracture to distal femur and poor retraction during knee replacement can damage femoral a.
