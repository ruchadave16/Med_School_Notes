Continuation of [[Femoral Artery|femoral a.]] after adductor hiatus, pressed up against knee joint with vein superficial
- 5 anastamotic **genicular** branches for knee joint

Divides
- Anterior tibial a. 
	- In gap superior to interosseous membrane in anterior
	- Changes to dorsalis pedis a. at malleolar line

>[!Clinical] Dorsalis pedis a. runs lateral to extensor hallucis longus on dorsum of foot 

- Posterior tibial a.
	- In posterior leg deep to soleus
	- Runs with tibial n. 
	- Divides into **medial and lateral plantar a/v**
	- **Fibular a.** → between tibialis posterior and flexor hallucis longus to supply lateral muscles

>[!Clinical] Posterior tibial a. crosses ankle behind medial malleolus


>[!Clinical]
>- Locked knees can pop this artery
