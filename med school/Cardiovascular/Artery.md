##### Types of Arteries
1) Large Elastic (Conducting)
	- Many layers to minimize variability in pressure
	- Proximal
2) Medium Muscular (Distributing)
	- Smooth muscle fibers
	- Can decrease diameter (vasoconstrict) to regulate flow
3) Small Arteries
