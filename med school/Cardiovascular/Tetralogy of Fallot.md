4 heart defects, **blue/gray skin when crying or feeding**

1) Narrowing of pulmonary valve ([[pulmonary valve stenosis]]) reduces flow of blood to lungs

2) [[Ventricular Septal Defect]] causes mixing of oxygen rich from LV and poor from RV

3) [[Right Ventrical Hypertrophy]] due to increased blood from LV to RV

4) [[Aorta]] shifted to right to be above ventricular septal defect
