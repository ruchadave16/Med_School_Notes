*Branch of [[aorta]] (L) or brachiocephalic trunk (R)*

- Ends at lateral border of 1st rib
- 3 parts defined by anterior scalene (1st before, 2nd deep, 3rd distal)

Branches
1st part: 
- [[Vertebral Artery|Vertebral a.]]
- Thyrocervical trunk
	- Suprascapular a. 
		- Over transverse scapular ligament
		- Supplies supraspinatus and infraspinatus
	- Transverse cervical a. 
		- Superficial cervical a. 
		- Dorsal cervical a. – medial border of [[scapula]]
	- Inferior thyroid a. 
		- Ascending cervical a. 
		- Inferior laryngeal a. 
- Internal thoracic a. 
	- Supplies medial [[Breast|breast]]
	- Anterior intercostal a. branches to supply intercostal spaces
	- Pericardiacophrenic a. supplies [[pericardium]] and [[diaphragm]]
	- Divides at costal margin into medial **superior epigastric a.** and **musculophrenic a.**

2nd
- Costocervical trunk

3rd: 
- Dorsal scapular a. → [[rhomboids]] and [[levator scapulae]]
