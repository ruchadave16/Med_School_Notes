Posterior division
- Superior gluteal a. – exits greater sciatic foramen above piriformis → upper gluteal region
- Iliolumbar a. – ascends to supply psoas major, quadratus lumborum, iliacus
- Lateral sacral a. — [[sacrum]], sacral nerve roots, meninges, adjacent muscles

Anterior division
- Umbilical a. – superior bladder (superior vesical a.), distal part = median umbilical ligament
- Obturator a. – through obturator foramen → [[medial thigh]]
	- Send artery through ligamentum teres into [[femur]] head

>[!Clinical]
>[[Legg-Calve Perthes Disease]] – childhood condition where poor blood supply to head of [[femur]] = poor formation of it
>

- Inferior vesical a. – inferior [[Urinary Bladder|bladder]] (in men), [[prostate]], [[Seminal Gland|seminal vesicle]]
- Vaginal a. – inferior bladder + [[vagina]] (in women)
- Uterine a. – [[uterus]], uterine tube, superior vagina (in women)
	- Travels in [[cardinal ligament]]
- Middle rectal a. – middle [[rectum]]
- Internal pudendal a. – [[perineum]]
	- Leaves through greater sciatic foramen → around sacrospinous ligament → in lesser sciatic foramen → ischioanal fossa → pudendal canal
	- Supplies anus (inferior rectal a.), [[superficial perineal pouch]] (perineal a.) (vestibule of vagina + bulb of penis), clitoris/penis (dorsal a. of penis)
- Inferior gluteal a. – exits greater sciatic foramen below piriformis → gluteus maximus + [[hip joint]]
