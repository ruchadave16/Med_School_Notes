*Continuation of axillary a. from inferior border of teres major*

Branches
- Deep brachial (profunda brachii) a. – with radial n. in radial groove of [[humerus]]
	- Ends to peri-articular anastamoses around elbow
- Superior and inferior collateral a. – add to anastamoses of elbow

Terminal branches
- Divide into ulnar and radial a.
