Drain to coronary sinus → [[Right Atrium|RA]]
- Posterior side of heart under [[Left Atrium|LA]]

**Great cardiac v.** → Drains LCA area
- Anterior IV groove, wraps around in coronary sulcus → coronary sinus

**Small cardiac v.** → Right border of heart, in R. coronary sulcus → coronary sinus
- *Can be absent*

**Middle cardiac v.** → Posterior IV groove → coronary sinus

**Left posterior ventricular v.** → Posterior ventricular wall → coronary sinus

**Left marginal (obtuse) v.** → Left border of heart → great cardiac v.

**Anterior cardiac vv.** → Drains R. ventricular wall → [[Right Atrium|RA]]
