Blood enters from [[SVC]], [[IVC]], and coronary sinus
- **Sinus venarum** – internal posterior wall with openings for vessels
- Pectinate muscles – area around auricle
	- Auricle = ear like appendage of anterior surface of atrium
- Crista terminalis – ridge between rough and smooth surfaces
- [[SA node]]: junction of SVC and crista terminalis
