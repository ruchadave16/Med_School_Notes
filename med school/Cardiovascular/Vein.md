##### Characteristics 
- **Low pressure** → generally need valves to facilitate blood flow
- Thinner walls 
- No pulse, no spurt blood

##### Valves
Flaps of [[endothelium]] with sinuses that fill from above
- Reverse flow of blood
- Require contraction of [[skeletal muscle]]
- Some veins are valveless
