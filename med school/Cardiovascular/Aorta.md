*Major [[artery]] out of [[heart]]*

Ascending aorta – first part, R/L [[Coronary Arteries|coronary aa.]] branch from here
Aortic arch – in superior [[mediastinum]], 3 branches
- Brachoicephalic trunk → R. [[Subclavian Artery|subclavian a.]] and R. [[Common Carotid Artery|common carotid a.]]
- L. [[Common Carotid Artery|common carotid a.]] 
- L. [[Subclavian Artery|subclavian a.]] 
	- Vertebral a. supplies brain
	- **Internal thoracic a.** deep to costal cartilage  Used for [[Coronary Bypass Surgery]] 
		- Anterior intercostal a. branches to supply intercostal spaces
		- Pericardiacophrenic a. supplies [[pericardium]] and [[diaphragm]]
		- Divides at costal margin into medial **superior epigastric a.** and **musculophrenic a.**

#### Thoracic Aorta 
- Supplies thorax and viscera
- **Posterior intercostal aa.** supplies intercostal spaces
	- [[Anastomose]]s with anterior intercostal a.
- **Bronchial aa.** supplies [[Lungs|lung]] parenchyma
- **Esophageal aa.** supplys [[esophagus]]
