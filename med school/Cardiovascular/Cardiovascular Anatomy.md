#### Functions
- Transportation + Distribution of oxygen and nutrients and excretory materials
- Regulation by carrying hormones
- Protection through clotting and WBCs

![[Pasted image 20250824134409.png]]

##### [[Heart]]
Double pump located in [[thorax]] that moves blood throughout circulatory system

##### [[Blood Vessels]] 

1) [[Artery]]: [[Blood]] away from heart
2) Arteriole
3) Capillary: Site of exchange of gas and nutrients
	- small endothelial tubes network
4) Venule
5) [[Vein]]: Blood towards the heart

[[Lymphatics|Lymphatic system]] – Removes interstitial fluid from tissues and returns to veins near heart

#### Circulation
Systemic – oxygenated blood in arteries, deoxygenated in veins, **high pressure**
Pulmonary – deoxygenated blood in arteries, oxygenated in veins **low pressure**
