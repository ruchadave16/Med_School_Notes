*4 chamber pump that alternates between contractions (systole) and relaxations (diastole)*
##### Boundaries
- Middle [[mediastinum]], inferior to sternal angle
	- Obliquely, rotated on z-axis
	- 2nd and 5th rib
	- 2/3 to left to midsternal line
	- Between [[sternum]] and thoracic [[vertebrae]]
- Rests on [[diaphragm]]
- Surrounded by double-walled sac ([[pericardium]])
![[Screenshot 2025-08-21 at 3.30.19 PM.png]]

**Surfaces**
- Base – top more posterior facing
	- Location of vessels leaving
	- Left atrium
	- Against [[esophagus]]
- Apex – inferiorly and to left
	- Part of left ventricle
	- 5th intercostal
	- Near mid clavicular
- Sternocostal (anterior) – Right ventricle
- Diaphragmatic (inferior) – Mainly left ventricle
- Left pulmonary – Left ventricle, makes impression on left [[Lungs|lung]]
- Right pulmonary – Right atrium between [[SVC]] and [[IVC]]

![[Screenshot 2025-08-21 at 3.50.34 PM.png]]

**2D Borders**
- Superior: atria/great vessels
- Diaphragmatic (inferior): right ventricle 
- Right border (convex): right atrium, some SVC + IVC
- Left border: left ventricle, some left auricle, pulmonary artery

##### External Features
Heart Wall Layers

1) [[Epicardium]] (visceral serous pericardium) – coronary vessels deep to epicardium
2) [[Myocardium]] – [[Cardiac Muscle]] and [[Connective Tissue|connective tissue]] for contraction
3) [[Endocardium]] – lines chambers, continuous with blood vessel lining
	- Made of endothelium, [[basal lamina]], loose [[Connective Tissue|connective tissue]] 
	
![[Screenshot 2025-08-21 at 3.47.29 PM.png]]

##### Blood Supply
Follows [[Vascular Circuit|vascular circuit]], heart itself gets blood from [[Coronary Arteries|coronary aa.]]
##### Innervation
[[Cardiac Plexus]], sympathetic and parasympathetic

Sympathetic
- Preganglionic T1-T5 spinal cord – travel into [[sympathetic trunk]]
- Postganglionic superior, middle, and inferior cervical ganglia
- ↑ heart rate, ↑ contraction, vasodilation of coronary aa.

Parasympathetic
- Preganglionic in brain by [[vagus nerve]]
- Postganglionic near/within heart
- ↓ heart rate

>[!Clinical] [[Ischemia]] stimulates pain fibers. Visceral afferents run with sympathetic (T1-T4) → Pain on those [[Dermatome|dermatomes]]
