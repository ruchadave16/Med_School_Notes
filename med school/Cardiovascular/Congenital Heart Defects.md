*Defects in heart baby is born with*

#### Shunt Defects

L → R (non-cyanotic) shunts: [[Atrial Septal Defect]], [[Ventricular Septal Defect]], [[Patent Ductus Arteriosus]]
- Cyanosis may occur later

R → L (cyanotic) shunts 
- [[Persistent Truncus Arteriosus]], [[Triscuspid Atresia]], [[Tetralogy of Fallot]], [[TAPVR]], [[Great Artery Transposition]]
- URGENT SURGERY

Obstructions: 
- Coarctation, valvular stenoses
