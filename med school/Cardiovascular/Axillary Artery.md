*Lateral border of 1st rib → distal border of teres major*

3 parts based on pectoralis major
1) 1 vessel branch – proximal to pectoralis major
	- Superior thoracic a. – 1st 2 ICS, pectoralis major, [[sternoclavicular joint]]
2) 2 vessels branch – posterior to pectoralis major
	- Thoracoacromial a. with 4 branches (acromial, clavicular, pectoral, deltoid)
	- Lateral thoracic a. (travels with long thoracic n.)
		- Supplies lateral [[breast]], serratus anterior
3) 3 vessels branch – distal to pectoralis minor
	- Subscapular a. – largest diameter
		- Circumflex scapular a. – around [[scapula]] → teres major and subscapularis
		- Thoracodorsal a. – latissimus dorsi
	- Anterior circumflex humeral a. – anteriorly around surgical neck [[humerus]]
	- Posterior circumflex humeral a. 
		- Through quadrangular space w axillary n. around surgical neck of [[humerus]]
		- Supplies [[Glenohumeral Joint|shoulder joint]]
