Unpaired vein on **right** starting in posterior abdominal region where ascending lumbar and subcostal v. join

- In thorax located anterior-laterally to vertebral column, right of thoracic duct
- Arcs anteriorly to drain into [[SVC]]
- **R. posterior intercostal vv.** – drain into azygos v. 
- **Accessory hemiazygos v.** – on left, middle drains 1/3 of L. posterior intercostal v. 
- **Hemiazygos v.** – left side, drains lower 1/3 of L. posterior intercostal v.
- **Esophageal v., R. bronchial v.** drain into azygos system

>[!Clinical] Makes collateral pathway for blood in case of obstructions of SVC or IVC
