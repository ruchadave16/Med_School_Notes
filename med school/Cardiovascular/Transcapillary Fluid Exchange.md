**[[Hydrostatic Pressure]]** and **[[Osmosis|Osmotic pressure]]** determine fluid movement

>[!Tip]
>Fluid movement $J_v = K_f ((P_c - P_i) - (\pi_c - \pi_i))$
>Where P = hydrostatic pressure, $\pi$ = [[Osmosis|osmotic pressure]], c = capillary, i = interstitial, $K_f$ = water permeability coefficient

If net pressure > 0, filtration of fluid out of capillary (generally at arterial end)
If net pressure < 0, absorption of fluid into capillary (generally at venous end)

*P ↓ from atrial to venous end*
