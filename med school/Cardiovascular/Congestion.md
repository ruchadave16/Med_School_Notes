*↑ blood volume in tissues*

- Passive process due to ↓ venous outflow
- Tissue appears **blue** ([[cyanosis]]) because high deoxygenated blood
- Commonly congestion can cause [[edema]]

>[!Example]
>[[Chronic Pulmonary Congestion]] – associated with buildup of [[Heart Failure Cells|heart failure cells]] (hemosiderin-laden macrophages)
>Chronic Passive Liver Congestion – upstream consequence of CHF → death of hepatocytes → nutmeg look to liver
