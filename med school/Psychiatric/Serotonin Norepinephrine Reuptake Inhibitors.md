[[Duloxetine]], [[Venlafaxine]], Desvenlafaxine (active metabolite), Levomilnacipran
#### Action
- Inhibit reuptake of serotonin and norepinephrine by blocking NET and SERT
- Higher affinity for SERT than NET
- No impact on other receptors
#### Uses
- [[Major Depressive Disorder|Depression]]
- [[Pain Disorders]]
	- Neuropathic pain (e.g., diabetic peripheral neuropathy)
	- Fibromyalgia
	- Chronic musculoskeletal pain
- [[General Anxiety Disorder]]
- Stress Urinary Incontinence
- Menopause

#### Dosing
#### Benefits
- Better tolerated than TCAs
#### Adverse Effects
- Nausea (most common)
- Constipation
- Insomnia
- Headaches
- Sexual dysfunction
