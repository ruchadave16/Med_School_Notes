#### Goals
- Reduce cravings
- Inhibit reinforcing effect of smoking
#### Therapies
- Nicotine Replacement Therapies
	- Substitution therapy reduces withdrawal
	- Oral (gums, lozenges), nazal sprays, transdermal patches
	- Efficacy: *2x chance of abstinence at 6 months compared to placebo*
	- Adverse Effects: Insomnia, dizziness, headache
- [[Bupropion]]
	- [[Antidepressants|Antidepressant]] that inhibits norepinephrine and dopamine reuptake
	- Can act as nicotine receptor antagonist
	- Metabolism = [[CYP2B6]] 
		- Inter-individual variability = efficacy impact
	- Adverse Effects: Insomnia, dry mouth, rarely seizures
- [[Varencline]]
	- Partial agonist of $\alpha4\beta2$
		- Maintains baseline dopamine but no withdrawal symptoms
	- Can act as full agonist for $\alpha7$
	- **Adverse Effects**
		- Nausea (20-25%), sleep disturbance, depression
		- Take after meal with full glass of water, do not discontinue
