**Anxiety** = feelings of tension, worried thoughts, and physical changes
- Often start in preschool (phobia, separation anxiety), grade (generalized anxiety, OCD), adolescence (social phobia, panic disorder), adults (think medical issues)

*Pathological:* 
- Fear out of proportion to threat
- Response beyond existence of threat
- Generalized to other situations
- Social functioning impaired
- Maladaptive
- Comorbid substance use + depression
#### Categories
- [[General Anxiety Disorder]]: persistent worry about aspects of daily life
- [[PTSD]]: anxiety after traumatic event
- [[OCD]]: intrusive thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors (compulsions)
- [[Panic Disorder]]: repeated, sudden episodes of high fear and physical symptoms
- [[Social Anxiety Disorder]]: intense fear of social situations and being judged by others
- [[Phobias]]: fear and avoidance of specific objects
- [[Separation Anxiety Disorder]]
#### Treatment
- First Line
	- [[Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors|SSRIs]], [[Serotonin Norepinephrine Reuptake Inhibitors|SNRIs]], [[Buspirone]] for chronic treatment
		- Slow onset (1-4 weeks)
		- Good for long term NOT for acute
- Benzodiazepine
#### Commonly Confused Disorders
- Cardiovascular: [[anemia]], [[angina]], [[hypertension]], [[congestive heart failure]]
- Pulmonary: [[asthma]], [[hyperventilation]], [[pulmonary embolism]]
- Neurological: [[epilepsy]], [[migraine]], [[multiple sclerosis]], [[Wilsons disease]], infection, tumor
- Endocrine: [[Addison Disease|Addisons disease]], [[carcinoid syndrome]], [[pheochromocytoma]], [[diabetes]], [[hyperthyroidism]], [[hypoglycemia]], [[hypoparathyroidism]]
- Drug Intoxication: [[amphetamine]], [[Anticholinergic Drugs|anticholinergics]], [[hallucinogens]], [[marijuana]], [[nicotine]], [[cocaine]], [[theophylline]]
- Drug Withdrawal: [[Alcohol Withdrawal]], [[antihypertensives]], [[Opioids]], [[sedative hypnotics]]
- Other: [[Anaphylaxis]], [[B12]] deficiency, [[systemic Lupus Erythematosus]], [[temporal arteritis]], [[uremia]], [[heavy metal poisoning]], electrolyte disturbances
