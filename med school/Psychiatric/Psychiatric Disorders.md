#### [[Anxiety Disorders]]
Characterized by excessive fear, worry, high physical arousal
- [[General Anxiety Disorder]]: persistent worry about aspects of daily life
- [[PTSD]]: anxiety after traumatic event
- [[OCD]]: intrusive thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors (compulsions)
- [[Panic Disorder]]: repeated, sudden episodes of high fear and physical symptoms
- [[Social Anxiety Disorder]]: intense fear of social situations and being judged by others
- [[Phobias]]: fear and avoidance of specific objects
#### [[Mood Disorders]]
Persistent changes in mood that interfere with daily life and relationships
- [[Major Depressive Disorder]]: ongoing sadness, hopelessness, lack of interest/pleasure
	- Persistent Depressive Disorder: continuous sadness but not to the level of depressive episode (≥ 2 years, ≥2 symptoms)
- [[Mania]]: periods of abnormal elevated mood
- Bipolar Affective Disorder: episodes of depression and mania
	- [[Bipolar I]]
	- [[Bipolar II]]: Hypomanic episodes but no full mania
	- Cyclothymic Disorder: Symptoms don’t reach mania or depression, must have for 2 yrs with 50% presence
- [[Disruptive Mood Dysregulation Disorder]]
#### [[Psychotic Disorders]]
Disturbance in thinking, perception, and ability to test reality
- [[Schizophrenia]]: chronic disorder with [[psychosis]], disorganized thinking, impaired functioning
- [[Brief Psychotic Episode]]: short term [[psychosis]] from stress or other factor
- [[Delusional Disorder]]: persistent delusions without other symptoms of psychosis
- [[Substance Induced Psychosis]]: [[psychosis]] from substance use or withdrawal
- [[Paraphrenia]]: late onset [[psychosis]] with prominent hallucinations in older patients
#### [[Eating Disorders]]
Distorted body image and dysfunctional eating behaviors
- [[Anorexia Nervosa]]: low food intake causing low body weight, intense fear of gaining weight
- [[Bulimia Nervosa]]: recurrent binge eating episodes followed by vomiting, ↑ exercise, or fasting
- [[Binge Eating Disorder]]: recurrent binge eating to point of discomfort
- [[PICA]]: persistent eating of non-food substances for 1 month
#### Addiction Disorders
Inability to resist harmful urges
- [[Pyromania]]: repeated fire setting
- [[Kleptomania]]: repeated urge to steal
- [[Substance Use Disorders]]: problamatic use of substances like [[alcohol]], drugs, or gambling
#### [[Personality Disorders]]
Enduring and inflexible patterns of thinking, behaving that cause problems socially
- [[Antisocial Personality Disorder]]: disregard for and violation of rights of others
- Borderline, Histrionic, Narcissistic Personality Disorders: mood, behavior, self-image instability, ↑ attention seeking, lack of empathy
- Avoidant, Paranoid, Schizoid Personality Disorders: social inhibition, distrust, suspicion of others
#### [[Adjustment Disorder]]
Emotional symptoms due to change in life
#### [[Dissociative Disorders]]
Disruption in identity, memory, consciousness
- [[DID]]: 2+ identities
- [[Depersonalization Disorder]]: recurrent feelings of detachment from own body
- [[Dissociative Amnesia]]: inability to remember personal info due to trauma
#### [[Somatoform Disorders]]
Physical symptoms not explained medically
- [[Somatic Symptom Disorder]]: physical symptoms + thoughts of them constantly
- [[Illness Anxiety Disorder]]: preoccupation with having serious illness but no symptoms
- [[Conversion Disorder]]: neuro symptoms inconsistent with biology
- [[Factitious Disorder]]: intentional faking of symptoms without external motivators
- Malingering: deliberate faking of symptoms for external gain
