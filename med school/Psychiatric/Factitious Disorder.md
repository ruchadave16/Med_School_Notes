Intentional faking of medical condition with goal of becoming sick
- NO secondary goal (monetary etc)
- Can be imposed on self or others

#### Presentation
- Reports symptoms without having them
- Produces **fake evidence**

**Warnings**
- Dramatic and inconsistent history
- Unclear symptoms not controllable, change once treatment started
- Predictable relapses once improvement
- Extensive knowledge of medicine
- Surgical scars presence
- New symptoms right after negative test results
- Symptoms only when pt is alone
- Eagerness to have tests
