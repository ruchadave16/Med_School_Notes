*Excessive worry and anxiety about general life*
### Etiology
- Primary causes
- Risk factors
### Pathophysiology
- Mechanism of disease
- Relevant pathways (e.g. immune, hormonal)
### Clinical Features
- At least 6 months
- Presents with headache and ↑ BP
- Worry WARTS
	- Wound-up
	- Absentminded
	- Restless
	- Touchy / irritable
	- Sleepless
### Diagnosis
DSM-V Criteria
- Excessive anxiety happening more days than not for ≥ 6 months
- Difficulty controlling worry
- Anxiety and worry + ≥3 symptoms
	- Restlessness
	- Easy fatigue
	- Difficulty concentrating
	- Irritability
	- Muscle tension
	- Sleep disturbance
*Only 1 symptom needed for children*

- Caveats: 
	- Symptom not associated to drug use or medical condition
	- Disturbance not better explained by other mental condition
### Treatment
First Line
- [[Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors|SSRIs]]

Second Line
- [[Benzodiazephines]] – ONLY for immediate and acute
- [[Buspirone]]
- [[Imipramine]]
- [[Pregabalin]]
