*Behavioral pattern where compulsive drug use despite consequences*

- Activation of [[reward pathway]] causing + feelings
- Over time, compulsive
- High risk of relapse

>[!Note]
>Nonaddictive [[drugs of abuse]] – thalamocortical pathway
>- Hallucinogens
>	- LSD → serotonin [[5-HT2A receptors]]
>- Dissociative Anesthetics
>	- Ketamine and PCP → [[NMDA receptors]]

>[!Note]
>Some have dual effect on mesocortical and thalamocortical pathways
>- [[Psychosis]] type effects
#### Triggers of Relapse
- Reexposure
- Stress
- Contextual Cues
