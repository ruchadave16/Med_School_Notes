### Etiology
- Common with patients with brain injury or antecedent brain disorder
	- Up to 66%

### Pathophysiology
- Mechanism of disease
- Relevant pathways (e.g. immune, hormonal)

### Clinical Features
- Presence of symptoms of altered motor/sensory but not in line with medical condition
- If complaint = pain, then diagnosis = somatic symptom disorder
- Symptoms generally right after stressor
	- Pt unconcerned with symptom
### Diagnosis
- Labs
- Imaging
- Criteria

### Treatment
- Reassurance with education abt stress 
- Psychotherapy
- Hypnosis
- Antiolytic meds

### Complications
- Short-term
- Long-term

### High-Yield Associations
- Women > men
- Common in pt from rural, uneducated
