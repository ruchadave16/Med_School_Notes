*[[Benzodiazephines]] receptor*

$\alpha1$ – sedation, hypnosis, amnesia, ataxia
$\alpha2$, $\alpha3$ – anxiolytic, muscle relaxing
$\alpha5$ – memory impairment
#### [[BDZ Agonists]]
- Enhance [[GABA]] activity

#### Antagonists
- Block BDZ and [[BDZ Agonists|Z drugs]]
- Don’t affect ethanol or barbiturates
- Ex: [[Flumazenil]]

#### Inverse Agonists
- Convulsive and anxiogenic effects
