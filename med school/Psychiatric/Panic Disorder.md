*Presence of [[Panic Attack|panic attacks]] and worry about having another one*
### Etiology
- Primary causes
- Risk factors
### Pathophysiology
- Mechanism of disease
- Relevant pathways (e.g. immune, hormonal)

### Clinical Features
- Worry must last for 1 month following attack

### Diagnosis
DSM-V Criteria
- Recurrent, unexpected [[Panic Attack|panic attacks]]
- 1 attach followed by ≥1 month of worry or maladaptive behavior
- No attribution to substance use
- Not better explained by other condition

### Treatment
First Line
- [[Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors|SSRIs]]
- [[Venlafaxine]] 

Second Line
- [[Clomipramine]] – TCA
- [[Alprazolam]], [[Clonazepham]] – Benzodiazephine
- [[Citalopham]]
- [[Imipramine]] – TCA
