*Presence of psychiatric distress from symptoms that resemble disease*
- Rule out medical conditions first

- [[Somatic Symptom Disorder]]: physical symptoms + thoughts of them constantly
- [[Illness Anxiety Disorder]]: preoccupation with having serious illness but no symptoms
- [[Conversion Disorder]]: neuro symptoms inconsistent with biology
- [[Factitious Disorder]]: intentional faking of symptoms without external motivators
- Malingering: deliberate faking of symptoms for external gain
	- Men >> women, common in prisoners and homeless
