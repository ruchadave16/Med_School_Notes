*Persistent disruptions in eating behaviors that impair health and functioning*
#### Etiology
- Individual factors
	- Perfectionism
	- Lack of control
	- History of sexual abuse
- Personality
	- Obsessive compulsive traits
	- Histrionic and borderline personality traits
- Family Environment
	- Family dysfunction
	- Emphasis on control + weight regulation
- Cultural
	- Industrialized societies
	- Media obsession w thinness
#### Risk Factors
- Physical
	- Obesity
	- Chronic illnesses
- Psychological
	- Career pressure to stay thin
	- Family history of [[mood disorders]], substance use, eating disorders
	- Other psychiatric disorders
		- Depression
		- [[OCD]]
		- Anxiety disorders, mainly panic disorder and agoraphobia
		- Substance use
#### Categorizations
- [[Anorexia Nervosa]]
- [[Bulimia Nervosa]]
- [[Binge Eating Disorder]]
- [[PICA]]
