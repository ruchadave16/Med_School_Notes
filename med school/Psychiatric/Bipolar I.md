### Clinical Features
- At least 1 [[manic episode]]
	- 90% of ppl with one episode will have recurrence
	- Rapid cycling = 4+ in 1 year
- DO NOT need depressive episode
- 1st manic, depressive episode at 18 yrs
### Diagnosis
- Labs
- Imaging
- Criteria

Misdiagnosis
- Co-morbid psychotic disorder with no mania/depression = [[schizoaffective disorder]]
### Treatment
- Pharmacologic
	- Acute Mania: 
		- [[Lithium]]
		- [[Valproate]]
		- [[Carbamazepine]]
		- [[Second Generation Antipsychotics]] 
	- Maintenance
		- [[Mood Stabilizing Drugs|Mood stabilizers]]
		- Depression: [[antidepressants]] can cause mania 
- Surgical
- Lifestyle

### Complications
- 15x higher risk for suicide
