1) [[G Protein Coupled Receptors|GPCR]] Activators
	- [[Opioids]]: mu-[[opioid receptors]] activate, euphoria and analgesia
	- [[Cannabinoids]] ([[THC]]): CB1 receptors
	- [[GHB]]: [[GABA B Receptors|GABA B receptors]], sedation and euphoria
2) Ionotrophic Receptors
	- [[Benzodiazephines]]: [[GABA A Receptors|GABA A receptor]] enhance, inhibitory signalling
	- [[Nicotine]]: [[Nicotinic Receptors|nicotinic receptor]] enhance dopamine release
	- [[PCP]] and [[Ketamine]]: block NMDA glutamate receptors, stop excitatory
3) Biogenic Amine Transporters
	- [[Cocaine]]: block dopamine reuptake
	- [[Amphetamine]]: increase dopamine and norepinephrine release
	- [[MDMA]]: increase serotonin release
