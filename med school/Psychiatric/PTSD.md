### Etiology
- Exposure to death, serious injury, sexual violence
	- Direct exposure
	- Repeated exposure (1st responder)
	- Witnessing event
	- Learning abt close event of family

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms last > 1 month
	- Intrusion: Intrusive repeated thoughts, memories, dreams, flashbacks
	- Avoidance: Avoiding reminders of event, place, people, objects
	- Alteration in mood: inability to remember details, negative thoughts and feelings
	- Alteration in arousal + reactivity: irritable, angry bursts, behaving recklessly, easily startled
