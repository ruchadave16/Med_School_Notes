**CAN BE LETHAL**
#### Stages
- Shakes
	- 6-18 hrs
	- Tremor, autonomic instability, nausea, vomiting, sweating, anxiety
	- ↑ HR and BP sometimes
- [[Seizures]]
	- 7-38 hrs
	- Peek at 24-48 hrs
	- 1-6 generalized seizures
- Hallucination
	- Within 48 hrs, can last 1 week
-  **Delirium Tremen** 
	- present in 5% of hospitalized pts, 1/3 of those with seizures
	- 2-5 days after drink
	- 5-15% mortality if untreated
#### Management
**Determine Severity**
![[Screenshot 2026-01-11 at 1.28.51 AM.png]]

- [[Benzodiazephines]]
	- Allosteric activators of [[GABA A Receptors|GABA A receptors]]
	- FIRST LINE to stop seizures
	- [[Chlordiazepoxide]] – long acting
	- [[Diazepam]] – quick for symptom relief or in DTs
	- [[Oxazepam]], [[temazepam]], [[lorazepam]] (“Outside The Liver”) for pt with liver damage

- Thiamine BEFORE glucose → for malnutrition
	- Watch for [[Wernickes Encephalopathy]]
- [[Beta blockers]] – improve vitals
- [[Alpha agonists]] – reduce symptoms
