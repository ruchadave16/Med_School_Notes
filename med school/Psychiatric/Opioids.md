Heroin
#### Action
- [[Opioid Receptors|Opioid receptors]], mainly $\mu$ (Gi)
	- ↓ cAMP, ↓ Ca2+ influx, ↑ K+ efflux
	- Neuronal **inhibition**
#### Signs
- Analgesia
- Euphoria
- **Respiratory depression**
- Sedation / coma
- **Miosis**
- Constipation
- Nausea / vomiting
#### Uses

#### Administration

#### Overdose Signs
- Miosis
- Respiratory depression
- Low consciousness

>[!Warning]
>Give [[Naloxone]]
#### Withdrawal
- Mydriasis
- Diarrhea
- Lacrimation
- Piloerection
- Muscle aches
- Stomach pain
- Rhinorrhea
#### Contraindication
