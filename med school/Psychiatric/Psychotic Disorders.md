#### Categories
-  [[Schizophrenia]]: chronic disorder with [[psychosis]], disorganized thinking, impaired functioning
- [[Brief Psychotic Episode]]: short term [[psychosis]] from stress or other factor
- [[Delusional Disorder]]: persistent delusions without other symptoms of psychosis
- [[Substance Induced Psychosis]]: [[psychosis]] from substance use or withdrawal
- [[Paraphrenia]]: late onset [[psychosis]] with prominent hallucinations in older patients
- Schizophreniform Disorder: Like schizophrenia but < 6 months
