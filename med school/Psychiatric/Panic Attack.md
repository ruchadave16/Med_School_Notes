Sudden, intense sense of fear that peaks in minutes
#### DSM-V Criteria 
- Sudden surge of intense fear that peaks in minutes with ≥ 4 symptoms
	- Palpitations, pounding heart
	- Sweating
	- Trembling
	- SOB
	- Choking
	- Chest pain
	- Nausea / abdominal stress
	- Dizzy, lightheadedness, faint
	- Chills / heat sensation
	- Paresthesias
	- Derealization or depersonalization
	- Fear of losing control
	- Fear of dying

>[!Note]
>Panic attacks can happen in many conditions but are NOT coded as [[Panic Disorder|panic disorder]] unless they met criteria for it
