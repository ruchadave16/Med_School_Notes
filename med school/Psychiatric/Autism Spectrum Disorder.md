### Etiology
- Strong genetic correlation
	- SHANK3 mutations are associated with synaptic dysfunction, strongly implicated

### Pathophysiology
- Alterations in brain development + circuits
- Altered GABAergic dysfunction

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Social development issues
	- Repetitive behaviors
	- MUST be present in early development
- Signs
- Variants

### Diagnosis
- Clinical
- Symptoms in early development + causing functional impairment
- Autism Diagnostic Observation Schedule (ADOS2)
- Autism Diagnostic Interview revised (ADI-R)

### Treatment
- Pharmacologic
- Surgical
- Lifestyle
