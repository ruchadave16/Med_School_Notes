#### Activation
- Stimulates [[Nicotinic Receptors|nicotinic receptors]] on dopaminergic neurons in VTA
	- $\alpha4\beta2$ have high affinity for nicotine, desensitized if chronic exposure and upregulation
	- $\alpha7$ have low affinity for nicotine, promote glutamate release
- Activates [[Reward Pathway]]
- Dopamine release into nucleus accumbens causes euphoria
#### Tolerance
- Constant receptor activation and desensitization
- Upregulation of $\alpha4\beta2$ 
#### Withdrawal
- Mild compared to other drugs
- Symptoms
	- Stress, irritability
	- ↑ appetite
	- Insomnia
	- Cognition disruptions
	- Poor concentration
	- Headache
