### Etiology

### Clinical Features
- Delusions with congruent affect for ≥ 1 month but absence of bizarre behavior
- Limited impairment, pt is employed but might be antisocial
- Most common type = persecutory
	- grandiosity, somatic, jealous, erotomanic
	- Capgras’ syndrome = ppl familiar to pt replaced by doubles
	- Fregoli syndrome = ppl familiar identified as strangers
	- Cotard’s syndrome = body parts have changed
- Onset late adult life
