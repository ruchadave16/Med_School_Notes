*Inflexible and maladaptive personality traits that deviate from culture and impair social functioning*
- Ego-syntonic nature (pt can’t tell its problamatic)
- Pervasiveness across cultures
- Onset before adulthood
- Diagnosis threshold < 18 yrs

![[Screenshot 2026-01-11 at 2.28.44 PM.png]]
#### Onset
- Identifiable before adulthood, relatively stable
- Features must stay for at least 1 year
	- Exception: Antisocial Personality Disorder pt must be >18 yrs
#### Complications
- Very high rate of comorbid diagnoses
- Higher rates of unemployment, homelessness, divorce, domestic violence, substance use, traumatic accidents
- Less symptomatic with age
#### Treatment
- NO FDA approved medications
- Psychotherapy
- [[Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors|SSRIs]] might help with impulsivity
#### Classifications
**Cluster A** (“weird”)
- Overlap with [[Psychotic Disorders|psychotic disorders]]
- High rate of [[Schizophrenia|schizophrenia]] in family

- Schizotypal Personality Disorder
	- Odd thought, speech – external events relate to self somehow
	- Eccentric behavior – talks to self, impoverished speech
	- Can eventually be diagnosed as schizophrenia
	- Family members with schizophrenia

- Schizoid Personality Disorder
	- Discomfort and disinterest in social relationships, loner
	- Restricted range of emotions, cannot express hostility
	- Few interests
	- Indifference to criticism
	- Self absorbed
	- NOT associated with schizophrenia

- Paranoid Personality Disorder
	- Unwarranted distrust in others
	- Persistently bears grudges, egocentric
	- Cannot take criticism
	- Argumentative

**Cluster B** (“Wild“)
- High rates of [[mood disorders]] + early [[alcoholism]]

- Borderline Personality Disorder
	- Mostly women
	- Unstable interpersonal relationships, frantic efforts to avoid abandonment
	- Impulsivity
	- Splitting – people are all bad or all good
		- Picks one member of the group who is “good” and rest are bad
	- Suicidal behavior, emptiness, inappropriate anger
	- Treatment: 
		- Antidepressants, especially SSRIs for impulsivity control
		- Antipsychotics can help

- Narcissistic Personality Disorder
	- Mainly men
	- Sense of self-importance
	- Should be recognized as superior
	- Arrogant, haughty
	- 6 E’s
		- Enamored with fantasies
		- Entitlement
		- Exploitative
		- Exhibitionist need for attention
		- Empathy-less
		- Envious / believes ppl are envious of him

- Antisocial Personality Disorder
	- Mainly men, sociopath, psychopath
	- Disregard for rights of others
	- Deceitful
	- Reckless and irresponsible
	- No remorse

- Histrionic Personality Disorder
	- Mainly women
	- Everything turned up to 11
	- Excessive attention seeking
	- Uncomfortable if not center of attention

**Cluster C** (“worried”)
- High rates of [[Major Depressive Disorder|depression]] and late onset [[alcoholism]]

- Dependent Personality Disorder
	- No self confidence so can’t function independently
	- Submissive, clinging behaviors
	- Extra reassurance and drive
	- Does unpleasant things to get people to like them

- Avoidant Personality Disorder
	- Avoids relationships or conflict to avoid rejection
	- Excessively shy
	- Hypersensitive to criticism
	- Doesn’t take risks
	- Can’t be easily intimate
	- Fear of rejection

- Obsessive Compulsive Personality Disorder
	- (early adolescence and childhood, unaware of impact of traits)
	- Preoccupied with perfection
	- Overconscientious
	- Does everyone’s work, doesn’t delegate
