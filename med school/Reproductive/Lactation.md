During [[Prenatal Period|pregnancy]]
- [[Estrogen]] and [[Progesterone]] block action of [[prolactin]] on breast so NO lactation

After [[birth]]
- ↓ [[Estrogen|estrogen]] and [[progesterone]] so ↑↑ lactation
- [[Ovulation]] suppressed until lactation done

#### Process
- Maintained by suckling – causes [[oxytocin]] and [[prolactin]] secretion
- Produced continuously by alveoli but doesn’t flow
- Must be ejected into ducts – neurogenic + hormonal response
	- [[Oxytocin]]
	- Contraction of myoepithelial cells of alveoli
