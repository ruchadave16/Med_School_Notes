Liver bud → 2/3 transverse colon
Distal duodenum, jejunum, ileum, cecum, appendix, ascending colon, proximal 2/3 of transverse colon

#### Blood Supply
**[[Superior mesenteric artery]]**
- U shaped loop extending out of abdomen
- Curve attached to [[vitelline duct]] connected to [[yolk sac]]

#### Innervation
- Parasympathetic:
	- Posterior vagal trunk
- Sympathetic
	- Lesser splanchnic n (T10-T11) – synapse on superior mesenteric ganglion
- Visceral Afferent
	- Run with sympathetic 

#### Rotation of Midgut

![[Attachments/Excalidraw/Drawing 2025-09-04 01.42.35.excalidraw.md]]
- 270 $\degree$ rotation around superior mesenteric artery
- Week 6 – physiological herniation into [[umbilical cord]]
	- Cranial loop (small intestine)
	- Caudal loop (distal ileum, cecum, appendix, ascending colon, 2/3 transverse colon)
	- 90 $\degree$ rotation counterclockwise

- Week 10 – retracts into abdomen and 180$\degree$ rotation 
	- Cecum in RUQ
	- Growth of ascending colon pulls cecum down to RLQ and pulls transverse colon across
