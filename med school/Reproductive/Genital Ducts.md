Indifferent Stage
- Both male and female have 2 ducts: [[mesonephric ducts]] (Wolffian) and [[paramesonephric ducts]] (Mullerian)

#### Male Ducts
- [[Testosterone]] stimulates development of mesonephric ducts into [[epididymis]], [[Ductus Deferens]], [[Seminal Gland|seminal vesicle]], and [[Ejaculatory Ducts|ejaculatory duct]]
- Mesonephric tubules make connection between rete testes (from cords) and epididymis to make **efferent ductules**
- [[AMH]] of [[Sertoli cells]] regresses [[Paramesonephric Ducts|paramesonephric ducts]]

**System**

>[!Info] Path of Sperm
[[seminiferous tubules]] → straight tubules → rete testis → efferent ductules → [[epididymis]] → [[Ductus Deferens|ductus deferens]] → [[Ejaculatory Ducts|ejaculatory duct]] → [[urethra]]
#### Female Ducts
- [[Paramesonephric Ducts|Paramesonephric ducts]] form
	- [[Uterine Tubes|Uterine tube]]
	- Uterine corpus
	- [[Uterine Cervix|Cervix]]
	- Upper part of [[vagina]]

>[!Clinical] Fusion failure of ducts can cause uterine congenital abnormalities

>[!Clinical] [[Mesonephric ducts]] degenerate and become
>- Epoophoron (analagous to epididymis)
>- Paraophoron
>- Gartner Cyst
