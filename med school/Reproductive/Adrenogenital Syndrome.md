### Pathophysiology
- Enzyme deficiency → overproduction of androgens
	- [[Adrenal System]] produce << [[cortisol]]
	- No negative feedback → [[anterior pituitary]] overproduces [[ACTH]] → ↑ secretion of androgens

### Clinical Features
- Signs
	- Ambiguous genitalia in females (enlargened [[clitoris]], fusion of [[labia majora]])
	- Precocious puberty in males
- Variants
