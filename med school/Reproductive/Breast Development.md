[[mammary gland|Mammary glands]] develop from mammary crest of [[Ectoderm]]
- Can have several glands
- At puberty, glandular tissue develops bcz of [[estrogen]]

Most of mammary crest regresses

>[!Clinical]
>[[Polymastia]]: Abnormal development of breast tissue along milk line
>[[Polythelia]]: Accessory nipples along milk line
>

>[!Clinical]
>[[Amastia]]: Lack of breast development or complete regression 
>[[Gynecomastia]]: Enlargenment of breast in men after puberty
