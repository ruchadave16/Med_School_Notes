*Spermatogenic cell before [[puberty]] found in [[seminiferous tubules]]*

- Poorly differentiated
- Round cells near basal lamina
- Round nuclues with patchy heterochromatin
- Diploid (46 [[chromosomes]], 2N [[DNA]])

Undergoes [[spermatogenesis]] to make [[primary spermatocytes]]
1) Type A spermatogonia
	- [[Stem cells]] – 1 replace, 1 progenitor cell
	- Progenitor cells – multiple [[mitosis]] to make Type B spermatogonia
2) Type B spermatogonia
	- Mitosis to make [[primary spermatocytes]]
