[[Umbilical Cord]]
- 2 umbilical arteries – deoxy blood, waste, CO2 to placenta
- 1 umbilical vein – oxy blod and nutrients from placenta

3 Shunts
- **Ductus venosus** – umbilical vein to IVC
- **Ductus arteriosus** – pulmonary artery/trunk → aorta
- **Foramen ovale** – opening between right and left atrium

#### Blood Pathway
- Umbilical vein → liver + IVC through **ductus venosus**
- IVC → RA → LA through **foramen ovale**
- LA → LV
- Ventricular systole
	- LV → aorta (head, heart, brain)
	- RV → pulm. arteries → **ductus arteriosus** → descending aorta → placenta through **umbilical arteries**

#### Post Birth Change
- Umbilical connection cut so less blood enters RA, more enters LA from lung
- Pressure in LA > RA so foramen ovale CLOSES (turns into fossa ovalis)
	- Now deoxygenated blood normally goes RA → RV
- Ductus arteriosus closes (becomes ligamentum arteriosum)
	- Blood from pulmonary trunk → lungs
- Ductus venosus not patent (becomes ligament venosum)
- Umbilical arteries → umbilical ligaments
- Umbilical vein → ligamentum teres
