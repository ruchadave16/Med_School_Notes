##### Mesoderm Differentiation

![[Screenshot 2025-08-11 at 4.22.48 PM.png]]

![[Screenshot 2025-08-12 at 1.29.52 PM.png]]

- Cardiogenic mesoderm
	- Cells that migrate cranially and laterally to oropharyngeal membrane 
	- Adult - formation of heart
- Paraxial mesoderm
	- Cells migrate laterally to notochord
	- Forms simultaneously on both sides of neural tube and notochord
	- Form **[[somites]]** → Form axial skeleton, skeletal musculature, dermis
	- Occipital somites → Face musculature
- Intermediate mesoderm
	- Located between paraxial and lateral plate mesoderm
	- Urinary and genital systems
- Lateral plate mesoderm
	- Appendicular skeleton, blood cells, blood vessels, lymphatic and GI systems, adrenal gland, body wall
	- Later divides horizontally into 
		- Dorsal somatic layer (adjacent to [[ectoderm]] → forms body wall + [[limb buds]], appendicular skeleton) 
		- Ventral splanchnic layer (adjacent to [[endoderm]] → forms CV, smooth muscles, connective tissues of digestive and respiratory systems)
		- Space between these layers = [[Intraembryonic Coelom]] (body cavity)

>[!Clinical]
>[[Caudal Regression Syndrome]]: Abnormal posterior mesoderm development
>Common with maternal diabetes
>Severity from mild → life threatening
>Impairs development of lower half body (limbs, lower back, genitourinary tract, GI tract)
