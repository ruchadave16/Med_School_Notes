Space that gives room for organ growth – turns into 3 cavities (pleura, pericardial, and peritoneal)

Splits [[mesoderm]] into somatic (contact with ectoderm) and splanchnic (contact with endoderm)

- Enclosed limb (surrounded by mesoderm)
- Open limb (splits mesoderm and communicates with [[extraembryonic coelom]])
