##### Internal Features
![[Pasted image 20250920000552.png]]
- Dartos muscle/fascia – comes from subcutaneous tissue (camper’s and scarpa’s fascia)
- External spermatic fascia – comes from external oblique m. aponeurosis
- [[Cremaster muscle]]– comes from internal oblique m.
- Cremasteric fascia – comes from internal oblique m. fascias
- Internal spermatic fascia – comes from transversalis fascia
- Tunica vaginalis – comes from processus vaginalis – [[peritoneum]]
##### Blood Supply
- Anterior scrotal a. ← external pudendal a. 
- Posterior scrotal a. ← perineal branch of internal pudendal a. 
- Cremasteric a. ← inferior epigastric a.
##### Innervation
- Genital branch of genitofemoral n. (L1-L2) – anterolateral
- Anterior scrotal n. ← ilioinguinal n. (L1) – anterior
- Posterior scrotal n. ← [[Pudendal Nerve|pudendal n.]] (S2-S4) – posterior
- Perineal branch of posterior femoral cutaneous n. (S1-S3) – posteroinferior
##### Lymphatic Drainage
Superficial inguinal nodes
##### Embryology
- Forms from genital swellings and fuses midline
