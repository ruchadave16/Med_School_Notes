*Supports, stores, maintains development of [[ova]] and also produces [[steroid hormones]]*

#### Structure
- Pseudostratified layer of [[peritoneal cells]] – [[Epithelial Tissue|epithelium]]
- Internally divided into [[ovary cortex]] and [[ovary medulla]] – boundary ill-defined…

#### Blood Supply
- Ovarian arteries from aorta → run in [[Suspensory Ligament of Ovary]]
- R ovarian v to IVC, L ovarian → L renal v.

>[!Clinical] [[Oophorectomy]] = removal of ovaries
#### Development
- Cortex of [[Gonads|gonad]] forms ovaries, medulla regresses
- Sex cords
	- Primary of gonad in medulla regress
	- Secondary sex cords form in cortex
	- Primordial germ cells enter mesenchyme of cortex and turn into [[oogonium]]
	- Sex cords break into clusters and surround oogonia forming [[follicular cells]]
	- Primordial [[Ovary Follicles|follicle]] = oogonium + follicular cells
