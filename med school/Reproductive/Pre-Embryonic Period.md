**Cleavage** – [[mitosis|mitotic]] divisions of [[zygote]] where cells become smaller

At 8 cell stage, [[compation]]
- Maximizing contact with outside cells through [[tight junctions]]
- Inner and outer cells segragated

**[[Morula]]** 16 cell (~3 days)
- Inner cell mass: forms [[embryo]]
- Outer cell mass: contributes to [[placenta]]
- Zona pellucida: outer shell

![[Screenshot 2025-08-06 at 1.57.03 PM.png]]

[[Blastocyst]] (~day 4)
- Fluid filled ball of cells
- Zona pellucida degrades, fluid fills space creating blastocyst cavity
- Inner mass called [[embryoblast]] – makes embryo
- Outer mass called [[trophoblast]] – makes placenta

![[Screenshot 2025-08-06 at 1.56.29 PM.png]]

[[Implantation]] (~day 6)
- Ideally superior part of [[uterus]]
- [[Trophoblast]] interacts with uterine lining
