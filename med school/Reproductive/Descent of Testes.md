- Gubernaculum connects [[testes]] to anterior body wall near [[Deep Inguinal Ring|deep inguinal ring]] location
	- Eventually becomes ligament of testes

![[Drawing 2025-09-20 00.02.43.excalidraw]]

- Processus vaginalis is outpouch that fuses at internal ring after descent
	- Eventually becomes tunica vaginalis
- Testes follow into canal  and carry fascia + muscles EXCEPT transversus abdominis m.

>[!Clinical] If this doesn’t fuse, indirect hernia risk increases
