*Organ that helps with nutrient and gas exchange between maternal and fetal parts*

1) Fetal = Chorion frondosum
2) Maternal = Decidua basalis
#### Contents
###### Fetal Components
- Come from [[trophoblasts]] and [[extraembryonic mesoderm]]
- [[Chorion]] - outer layer
	- Villi cover surface
	- Villi on embryonic pole form chorion frondosum
	- Villi on abembryonic pole degenerate (smoothen)

![[Drawing 2025-09-17 16.40.35.excalidraw]]
###### Maternal Components
- Comes from [[endometrium]]
- Decidua = functional part
	- Decidua basalis - decidua over chorion frondosum
	- Decidua capsularis - decidua over abembryonic pole (degenerates)
	- Decidua parietalis - uterine wall

#### External Features
[[Chorionic Plate]] – fetal border
[[Decidual Plate]] – maternal border
[[Junctional Zone]] – border between 2
- Intermingling of trophoblast + decidual cells
- Intervillous spaces – filled with *maternal blood*

[[Placental Membrane]]
#### Blood Supply
Fetus
- Umbilical arteries + veins pierce chorionic plate, enter placental villi (blood inside villi = fetal)
- Blood from umbilical arteries (↓ $O_2$) → villi → capillaries (gas exchange) → umbilical vein (↑ $O_2$)

![[Screenshot 2025-09-17 at 4.28.45 PM.png]]
Maternal
- Spiral arteries in endometrium turned into vessels to supply intervillous splaces (blood around villi = maternal)
- Blood from spiral arteries → intervillious space → fills surface → endometrial veins
