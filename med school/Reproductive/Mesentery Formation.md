#### Terminology
- Parietal [[peritoneum]]: lines abdominal cavity
- Visceral [[peritoneum]]: covers outside of abdominal organs
- Mesentery (double layer of peritoneum enclosing organ and suspending from posterior abdominal wall)
	- Intraperitoneal organs: Organs formed inside and connect to posterior wall by mesentery
	- Retroperitoneal organs: Organs formed posterior to parietal peritoneum, on abdominal wall (like kidney)
	- Secondarily retroperitoneal organs: Organs formed as intraperitoneal but become retroperitoneal during development
		- SAD PUCKER
		- Suprarenal, aorta/IVC, duodenum (2nd and 3rd), pancreas, ureters, colon (ascending and descending), kidneys, esophagus, rectum (distal 1/3)
- [[Foregut]]: distal esophagus, stomach, proximal duodenum, liver, gallbladder, pancreas, spleen
	- Supplied by celiac trunk
- [[Midgut]]: distal duodenum, jejunum, ileum, cecum, appendix, ascending colon, proximal 2/3 of transverse colon
- [[Hindgut]]: distal 1/3 of transverse colon, descending colon, sigmoid colon, rectum, proximal anal canal

#### Development
Comes from [[mesoderm]], broken into dorsal and ventral 

###### Ventral Mesentery
Portion of [[septum transversum]] developing under diaphragm
- Anterior to foregut
- Forms:
	- Visceral peritoneum of liver
	- Falciform lig. (liver → ventral body wall)
	- Lesser omentum (liver → stomach)
	- Coronary lig. of liver (peritoneal reflections from liver → diaphragm)
	- Triangular lig. of liver (liver → diaphragm)
- Liver grows into septum transversum 
- Lesser omentum
	- Lesser curvature of stomach → 1st part duodenum and liver
	- Hepatogastric lig. and hepatoduodenal lig. 
	- Hepatoduodenal lig. opening to omental foramen (contains [[portal triad]])


###### Dorsal Mesentery
- Posterior wall to organs connection

1) [[Dorsal mesogastrium]] (connects to stomach)
	- Greater omentum (not attached anymore)
	- Mesoduodenum (connects to duodenum)
2) Mesentery proper (connects to jejunum, ileum)
3) Transverse mesocolon (connects to large intestine)

OTHERS
1) Mesoappendix (appendix to mesentery of ileum)
