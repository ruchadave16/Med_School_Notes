*Fusion of [[sperm]] with secondary [[oocyte]] to form a [[zygote]]*

Location: ampulla of [[Uterine Tubes|uterine tube]] 
- Must go through [[capacitation]] and [[acrosomal reaction]]

#### Phases
1) Penetration of corona radiata
2) Penetration of zona pellucida
	- Initial binding causes [[acrosomal reaction]], which allows penetration
3) Fusion of oocyte and sperm
	- [[Integrins]] of oocyte bind to disintegrins of sperm
	- Now cell undergoes [[meiosis]] II (metaphase of [[oogenesis]])

#### Resulting Reactions
- Cortical oocyte granules released – makes membrane *impenetrable* by other spermatozoa
- Zona pellucida changes to stop other sperm binding
- [[Oocyte]] goes through 2nd [[meiosis]] division
	- One daughter cell = second [[polar body]]
	- Other = definitive oocyte
- Metabolic activation of egg
- Nuclei form to make [[zygote]]
