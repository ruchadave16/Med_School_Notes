- Lateral folding (open limbs ventrally)
	- [[Intraembryonic coelom]] completely closes
	- Endoderm + splanchnic layer pinch together, connected to [[yolk sac]]
	- Ectoderm + somatic layer (somatopleure) stretch to make anterior wall
	- Somatic mesoderm → parietal wall → parietal layer of peritoneum
	- Splanchnic mesoderm → visceral wall → visceral layer of [[peritoneum]] (layer surrounding GI tube)
	- 2 layers continuous at dorsal mesentery
	- Ventral mesentery erodes to form [[peritoneal cavity]]

![[Attachments/Excalidraw/Drawing 2025-09-03 16.48.43.excalidraw.md]]


>[!Clinical] Failure of somatopleure fusion
>[[Ectopia Cordis]] – heart outside of body – failure of sternum fuse or heart to be brought in
>[[Gastroschisis]] – splitting of belly
>[[Omphalocele]] – umbilic defect
