#### Indifferent Stage
- 3rd week – [[Cloaca|cloaca]] has folds form around membrane which form 
	- Genital tubercle – merged folds cranially
	- Urethral folds
	- Genital swellings – lateral to urethral folds
	- Anal folds

#### [[Male External Genitalia]]
Forms because of androgens from [[gonads]]
1) [[Penis]] – from elongated tubercle
2) [[Scrotum]] – from genital swellings
3) [[Spermatic Cord]]
4) [[Testes]]

#### [[Female External Genitalia]]
Forms in absence of androgens
1) [[Clitoris]] – from elongated tubercle
2) [[Labia minora]] – from urethral folds (DON’T FUSE)
3) [[Labia majora]] – from genital swellings (DON’T FUSE)
4) [[Vestibule]] – Urogenital groove open and turns into vestibule
