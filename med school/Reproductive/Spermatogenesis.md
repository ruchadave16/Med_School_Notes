*Mainly takes place in [[Testes|Testis]], process of producing and maturing [[spermatozoa]]*

1) Spermatocytogenesis: [[Spermatogonia]] make [[primary spermatocytes]] through [[mitosis]]
2) [[Meiosis]] I to make [[Secondary Spermatocytes|secondary spermatocyte]] and II to make [[spermatids]]
3) [[Spermiogenesis]]: Mature into final [[spermatozoa]]
