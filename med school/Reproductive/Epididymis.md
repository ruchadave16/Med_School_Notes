*Single tube where sperm move through – location of final maturation*

Epithelium: Psuedostratified columnar with [[stereocilia]]
#### Function
- Storage of spermatozoa
- Location of maturation
- Secretion of glycerophosphocholine
- [[Phagocytosis]] of residual bodies
- Peristaltic contractions move sperm → [[Ductus Deferens|ductus deferens]]

>[!Clinical]
>[[Acute Epididymitis]] – caused by [[STI]]; chronic inflammation and infection, common cause of [[male infertility]]
