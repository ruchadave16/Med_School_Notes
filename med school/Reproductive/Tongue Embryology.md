#### Muscles
- Occipital somites 2-4 migrate 
- Innervation: [[Hypoglossal Nerve|CN XII]]
#### Mucous Membrane
Endoderm of pharynx ([[pharyngeal arches]]) swells making mucous membrane of [[tongue]]

![[Drawing 2025-10-31 22.05.16.excalidraw]]

- Lateral and medial lingual swellings of 1st arch
	- Fuse together to make anterior 2/3 of [[tongue]]
	- Sensory = [[Mandibular Nerve|CN V3]]

>[!Clinical]
>Failure of fusion of lateral lingual swellings = Bifid tongue

- Copula of his from 2nd arch → mostly gets obliverated
- Hypopharyngeal eminence from 3rd and 4th arch
	- 3rd makes up posterior 1/3 of tongue ← [[Glossopharyngeal Nerve|CN IX]]
	- 4th forms [[epiglottis]] ← [[Vagus Nerve|CN X]]

>[!Clinical]
>[[Ankyloglossia]] – tongue-tie
