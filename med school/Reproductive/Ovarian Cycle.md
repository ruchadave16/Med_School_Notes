*Changes in [[Ovary Follicles|ovary follicles]]*
#### Follicular Phase (~Day 0-14)
- Development of 1 Graffian [[Ovary Follicles|ovarian follicle]] due to [[FSH]]
	- Undergoes [[oogenesis]] – Prophase I → Metaphase II (suspended)
	- 1 becomes dominant follicle and other regresses
- LH and FSH receptors upregulated
	- Granulosa have ONLY FSH receptors
	- Secrete [[estrogen]] through theca cells help
	- [[Estradiol]] ↑ → uterus proliferation
	- Negative feedback on [[anterior pituitary]] causes FSH and LH ↓ 
- LOW [[progesterone]]
- [[Menses]] → [[ovulation]] ~ 14 days
- [[Estrogen]] secreted by follicle

#### [[Ovulation]] (~Day 14)
- Cervical mucus ↑↑ during this phase
#### Luteal Phase (Last 14 days)
- Development of [[Corpus Luteum|corpus luteum]]
- 14 days exact
- [[Estradiol]] and [[progesterone]] secreted by corpus luteum for [[implantation]]
	- Both peak midway through phase
	- [[Endometrium]] changes to prepare for egg
	- Basal body temp ↑ ([[progesterone]] → [[hypothalamus]])
	- If no fertilization, corpus luteum breaks into [[Corpus Albicans|corpus albicans]]
	- Estradiol and progesterone ↓ 
	- [[Menses]] and cycle restarts

#### [[Menses]] (Day 0-4)
- [[Endometrium]] lost due to ↓↓ [[estradiol]] and [[progesterone]]
