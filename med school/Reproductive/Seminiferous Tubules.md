#### Structure
- Long tubules with 3 layers
	- Tunica propria (fibrous CT) with myoid cells
	- [[Basal lamina]]
	- Spermatogenic epithelium *(Stratified seminiferous epithelium)*
#### Origin
- Mesodermal epithelium → sex cordsf
#### Contents
- [[Sertoli cells]] – secrete [[AMH]] which makes paramesonephric duct stop developing
- Primordial germ cells ([[spermatogonia]]) – mature into [[spermatozoa]] during puberty through [[spermatogenesis]]
