#### Contents
- Distal 1/3 of transverse colon → proximal 2/3 anal canal
- Distal 1/3 of transverse colon, descending colon, sigmoid colon, rectum, proximal anal canal
- **[[Inferior mesenteric artery]]**
- Terminal end enters posterior cloaca

#### Innervation
- Sympathetic
	- Lumbar splanchnic (L1-L3) – to inferior mesenteric ganglion AS superior hypogastric plexus
- Parasympathetic
	- Pelvic splanchnic (S2-S4) (anterior rami) – through [[inferior hypogastric plexus]]
- Visceral affarent
	- L colic flexure to mid-sigmoid = pain retrograde with sympathetic, reflex with sympathetic
	- Distal - mid-sigmoid = both with parasympathetic 

#### [[Cloaca]]
- [[Endoderm]] lined pouch at end of gut tube – connected to [[allantois]]
- Gets waste from GI, urinary, reproductive
- Anterior and posterior separated by urorectal septum
	- Anterior: urogenital sinus – [[Urinary Bladder|bladder]], [[vagina]], [[urethra]]
	- Posterior: [[rectum]], [[anal canal]]
- Membrane is [[ectoderm]] derived, 3 part separation: 
	- Anal membrane – invaginates anal pit to meet rectum at pectinate line
		- Ruptures to open [[rectum]]
	- Perineal body
	- Urogenital Membrane

#### [[Allantois]]
- Extension of posterior wall of [[yolk sac]]
- Vessels in umbilical cord
- Drains fetal bladder into [[placenta]]
