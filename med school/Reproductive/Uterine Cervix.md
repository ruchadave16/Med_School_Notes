*Cylindrical part of [[uterus]] that extends into [[vagina]]*

#### Components
1) Endocervix
	- Internal → external cervical os
	- Simple columnar epithelium with mucous secreting
	- Thick lamina propria
**In between = transformation zone**
2) Ectocervix
	- Part beyond external os
	- Non-keratinized stratified squamous epithelium continuous with [[vagina]]

>[!Clinical]
>[[Cervical Cancer]] reduced because of [[Pap smear]] (cells scraped from cervix to identify epithelial changes
>- [[HPV]] is highly implicated in development of this
