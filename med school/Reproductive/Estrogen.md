*[[Steroid hormones]] secreted by [[Ovary|ovaries]]*

#### Production
- Follicular phase – Granulosa CANNOT, rely on theca cells
- Luteal phase – Granulosa → granulosa-lutein and CAN create estradiol

#### Functions
- Negative and [[positive feedback]] on [[FSH]] and [[LH surge]]
- Mature and maintain [[Uterine Tubes|fallopian tubes]], [[uterus]], [[Uterine Cervix|cervix]], [[vagina]]
- Female secondary sex characteristics at [[puberty]]
- Development of [[Breast|breasts]]
- Up-regulate estrogen, LH, progesterone receptors
- [[Proliferation]] and development of granulosa cells
- ↑ uterine contractility
- Build bone
- ↓ [[LDL]]
- During [[Prenatal Period|pregnancy]]
	- ↑ myometrium and ductal system growth in breast
	- [[prolactin]] secretion
