*Continuous belt of [[Sertoli cells]] separating [[Seminiferous Tubules|seminiferous tubule]] epithelium*

Separates into
1) Basal component
	- Between basal lamina + belt
	- Holds spermatogonia
	- Accessible to blood-borne substance
2) Adluminal component
	- Between belt + lumen
	- Inaccessible to blood-borne substance except those passed through

>[!Clinical] [[Testicular Cancer]] – 95% due to germ cell tumors
