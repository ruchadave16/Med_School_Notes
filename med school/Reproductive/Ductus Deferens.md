*Transmits [[sperm]] from [[testes]] to prostatic part of [[urethra]]*

Tail of [[epididymis]], runs inside [[spermatic cord]], crosses in front of [[ureters]] and forms ampulla of ductus deferens behind bladder
- Joins [[seminal gland]] duct to make [[Ejaculatory Ducts|ejaculatory duct]]

>[!Clinical] [[Vasectomy]] – Ligation of ductus by incision in scrotum

#### Histology
Epithelium: pseudostratified columnar with fewer [[stereocilia]], mucosa buckles in lumen folds
Muscle: 3 layers of smooth muscle (longitudinal, circular, longitudinal)
- Allows for peristalsis
