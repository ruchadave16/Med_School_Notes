[[Lung Buds Formation|Respiratory diverticulum]] to pancreatic buds

- [[Esophagus]], [[stomach]], proximal [[duodenum]]
- [[Liver]], [[gallbladder]], [[pancreas]], [[spleen]]
#### Vasculature
[[Celiac trunk]] off of aorta
#### Innervation
- Parasympathetic
	- Anterior vagal trunk – lesser curvature stomach, pylorus, biliary apparatus, gallbladder, liver, proximal duodenum
	- Posterior vagal trunk – posterior stomach
	- **Secretion ↑ and peristalsis**

- Sympathetic
	- Greater splanchnic (T5-T9) – through diaphragm to synapse at celiac ganglia (at roots of celiac trunk)
	- **Vasoconstriction and ↓ peristalsis**

- Visceral afferent pain
	- Along sympathetic of abdomen
