*Yellow structure with hemorrhagic central cavity that forms from [[ovary follicle]] after [[ovulation]]*

#### Function
- Makes [[progesterone]] under control of [[LH]]
	- Granulosa cells become Granulosa Lutein (make progesterone)
	- They become larger and have pale cytoplasm with lipid droplets
- Theca interna cells enlargen to become Theca Lutein – make progesterone and androgens
- Without [[fertilization]], corpus luteum shrinks to become [[corpus albicans]]

#### [[Prenatal Period|Pregnancy]] Version
- Much larger
- Makes [[progesterone]] until [[placenta]] makes enough, then turns into large [[corpus albicans]]
