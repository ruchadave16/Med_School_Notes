[[Hypothalamus]] – [[GNRH]] released for onset of [[puberty]]
- Causes anterior pituitary to release FSH and LH

[[Anterior Pituitary]] – [[FSH]] and [[LH]] released to stimulate production of [[estrogen]] and [[progesterone]] from [[Ovary Follicles|ovarian follicle]]
- [[FSH]] → granulosa cells
- [[LH]] → thecal cells

[[Gonads]]
- Granulosa cells → [[estrogen]]
- Thecal cells → [[testosterone]]
- [[Corpus Luteum|Corpus luteum]] → [[estrogen]] and [[progesterone]]
