Excess fluid in persistant processus vaginalis
- Common in boys

Presents: painless scrotal swelling

Testis
- Confined to scrotum
Spermatic cord
- Confined to spermatic cord

Diagnosis
- Light to side of scrotal enlargement – transmission of light means excess fluid
