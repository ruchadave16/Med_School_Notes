*Hormone released from [[lactotrophs]] by [[anterior pituitary]] for production of milk in [[Breast|breast]]*

[[Estrogen]] and [[Progesterone]] stimulate release from [[anterior pituitary]]
- [[TRH]] is also a stimulator
- Nursing and breast stimulation = stimulators
- [[PRH]] also from [[hypothalamus]] but less important

**Block effects on [[breast]] during [[Prenatal Period|pregnancy]]**

![[Screenshot 2025-10-21 at 3.15.40 PM.png]]
#### Effects
- [[Lactogenesis]] 
- Breast development during [[puberty]]
- **Inhibition** of [[ovulation]]
	- ↓ [[GnRH]] secretion
	- ↓ action of [[GnRH]] on [[anterior pituitary]], so ↓ [[LH]] and ↓ [[FSH]]
	- ↓ action of LH and FSH on [[Ovary|ovaries]]

>[!Clinical]
>High prolactin → [[galactorrhea]] – discharge of milk from patients not nursing

#### Inhibition
- [[Dopamine]]

[[Bromocriptine]] – dopamine agonist

>[!Clinical]
>Prolactin Deficiency
>- Destruction of [[anterior pituitary]] or destruction of [[lactotrophs]]
>  
>  Prolactin Excess
>- [[Galactorrhea]]
