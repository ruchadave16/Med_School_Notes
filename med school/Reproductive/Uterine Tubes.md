*Muscular tube that helps with transport of [[ovum]] from [[ovary]] to [[uterus]]*
#### Boundaries
- Anterior to ureters, found in [[Broad Ligament]]
#### Components
- Infundibulum – Funnel shaped opening with finger-like projections (fimbriae)
- Ampulla – long location where [[fertilization]] happens
- Isthmus – Narrow portion near uterus
- Fimbriae – finger

![[Screenshot 2025-09-14 at 9.28.06 PM.png]]

#### Layers
1) Mucosa: ↑↑ folds with projections into lumen
	- Simple columnar [[Epithelial Tissue|epithelium]] with ciliated cells to move to uterus and secretory [[Peg cells]] that secrete [[glycoproteins]] of mucus film 
	- Lamina Propria

2) Muscularis: Interwoven circular and longitudinal fibers
3) Serosa: Visceral peritoneum with mesothelial lining
