Formed from frontonasal prominence and parts of [[pharyngeal arches]]
- Frontonasal prominence (1) – cranial → stomodeum
	- Covered by ectoderm
- Maxillary prominences (2) – lateral to stomodeum ← 1st arch
- Mandibular prominences (2) – caudal to stomodeum ← 1st arch

![[Drawing 2025-10-31 23.25.20.excalidraw]]

#### Late 4th Week
- Nasal placodes form
- Mandibular prominences fuse

![[Drawing 2025-10-31 23.31.06.excalidraw]]

#### 5th Week
- Nasal pits form (burrow into mesoderm)
	- Medial and lateral nasal prominences on both sides
- Walls are still open towards 
- Nasolacrimal groove between frontonasal and maxillary

#### 7-10 Weeks
- Maxillary fuse to form border of nostril and upper lip

>[!Clinical] Failure of this fusion = [[cleft lip]] or cleft palate

- Medial nasal prominences fuse and make intermaxillary segment
	- Philtrum: indentation of upper lip
	- Primary palate
	- Medial maxilla: 4 incisor teeth

![[Screenshot 2025-10-31 at 11.41.49 PM.png]]

- Maxillary prominences fuse to make secondary palate
- [[Ectoderm]] of nasal prominences make **nasolacrimal duct**

>[!Clinical] Failure of fusion of lateral nasal prominence and maxillary prominence forms facial cleft
