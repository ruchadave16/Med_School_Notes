*Process by which groups of primordial follicles undergo maturation*

![[Screenshot 2025-09-14 at 9.25.03 PM.png]]
#### **Primordial Follicle**
- Distributed in superficial [[ovary cortex|cortex]]
- Have primary [[oocyte]] surrounded with 1 layer of flat [[follicular cells]] on basal lamina

![[Screenshot 2025-09-14 at 9.02.55 PM.png]]

**Primary Follicle**
- Unilaminar Stage – Enlargenment of [[oocyte]] and follicular cells go from flat to cuboidal
- Multilaminar Stage – Follicular cells proliferate and become granulosa cells
	- Granulosa cells react to [[FSH]] to make [[estrogen]] and [[inhibin]]
	- Stromal cells become layers of theca interna and externa cells – react to [[LH]] to release [[testosterone]]
	
![[Screenshot 2025-09-14 at 9.09.14 PM.png]]

#### **Secondary Follicle**
- Single fluid-filled cavity forms
- Distinct thecal layers form (well dis)

![[Screenshot 2025-09-14 at 9.11.31 PM.png]]![[Screenshot 2025-09-14 at 9.11.54 PM.png]]

- Theca interna (lightly stained cells w lipid droplets) and theca externa (fibroblasts and smooth muscle cells)
- [[Basement membrane]] separates interna and granulosa

#### **Graafian Follicle**
- [[Oocyte]] reaches size 
- Moves 1 pole where cells proliferated to make *cumulus oophorus*
- Cells surrounding oocytes make corona radiata

![[Screenshot 2025-09-14 at 9.14.24 PM.png]]

- [[Zona pellucida]] = [[Glycoproteins|glycoprotein]] coat outside oocyte

#### **[[Ovulation]]**
- Oocyte enters telophase I
- Follicule protrudes into peritoneal cavity
