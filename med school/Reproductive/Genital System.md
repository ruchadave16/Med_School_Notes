#### Components
- [[Gonads]] – [[testes]] and [[Ovary|ovaries]]
- [[Genital ducts]]
- [[External genitalia]]
#### Stages of Development
- Indifferent (First 6 weeks, no difference between male and female)
- Differentiated (6 weeks onwards)

Typically female development
- [[SRY]] gene on Y chromosome drives male development
- [[TDF]] = protein encoded by SRY gene → [[testis formation]] → [[testosterone]] production → external genitalia development
