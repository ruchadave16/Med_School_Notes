##### Boundaries
Starts at [[deep inguinal ring]]
Passes through [[inguinal canal]]
Exits through [[Superficial Inguinal Ring|superficial inguinal ring]]
Ends at posterior border of [[testes]]
##### Contents
Structures running to and from [[testes]]
- [[Ductus deferens]]
- Testicular a.
- Ductus deferens a.
- Cremasteric a. 
- [[Pampiniform venous plexus]]
- Sympathetic nerve fibers
- Genital branch of genitofemoral n. 
- Ilioinguinal n. (DOESN’T enter deep inguinal ring, enters canal and runs along spermatic cord)
##### External Features
1) Internal spermatic fascia – from transversalis fascia
2) Cremasteric fascia – from investing fascia of internal oblique m.
3) External spermatic fascia – from external oblique fascia
