##### Somite Formation
3rd embryonic week
**Paraxial** [[Mesoderm|mesoderm]] divides into paired blocks of cells (somites)
- These are locations where mesoderm cells migrate to new locations from 

![[Screenshot 2025-08-11 at 4.29.16 PM.png]]

4 occipital, 8 cervical, 12 thoracic, 5 lumbar, 5 sacral, 8-10 coccygeal pairs
- 1 occipital and 5-7 coccygeal disappear

During Week 4, medial [[mesenchymal]] cells are called [[sclerotomes]]
