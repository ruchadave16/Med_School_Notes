Normal Location: superior part of [[uterus]]

>[!Clinical] Abnormal Locations
>Lower part of [[uterus]] – near [[Uterine Cervix|cervix]]
>- [[Placenta previa]] → [[placenta]] closes over opening of cervix
>- Heavy bleeding during late [[Prenatal Period|pregnancy]] or [[birth]]
>
>Ectopic Sites (2% of all pregnancies, 9% of deaths)
>- [[Uterine Tube]] - generally ampulla (80%) or otherwise (12%)
>- Abdominal cavity (1%)
>- Ovary (1%)
