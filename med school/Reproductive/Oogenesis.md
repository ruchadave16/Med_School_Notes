1) [[Oogonia]] [[mitosis]]
2) [[Meiosis]] I (stops at prophase)
	- [[Primary oocytes]] stay here until [[puberty]]
	- [[LH surge]] drives some oocytes every month to undergo development, (before ovulation) finishing Meiosis I and giving [[secondary oocyte]] and [[polar body]]
3) [[Meiosis]] II
	- Every month, secondary oocyte stops at metaphase
	- Continue metaphase if [[fertilization]] occurs
	- Polar body degenerates

>[!Info]
>Final result = 1 [[oocyte]] and 3 haploid [[polar bodies]]
