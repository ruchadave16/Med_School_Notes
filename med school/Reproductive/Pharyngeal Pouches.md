*4 pairs of pouches formed right between [[pharyngeal arches]]*

![[Drawing 2025-10-31 20.54.37.excalidraw]]

1) 1st Pouch
	- Lateral part → [[Middle Ear|tympanic cavity]]
	- Medial part → [[Pharyngotympanic Tube|auditory tube]]
	- Membrane between 1st groove and pouch = [[tympanic membrane]]

2) 2nd Pouch
	- Palatine tonsil

3) 3rd Pouch
	- Dorsal → inferior parathyroid gland
	- Ventral → [[thymus]]

4) 4th Pouch
	- Dorsal → superior parathyroid gland
	- Ventral → Ultimopharyngeal body
