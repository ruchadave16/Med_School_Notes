*[[Steroid hormone]]s secreted by [[corpus luteum]] during luteal phase and [[placenta]]*

#### Functions
- [[FSH]] and [[LH surge]]
- [[Ovulation]] aid
- Development of lobules + alveoli in [[Breast|breast]]
- Negative feedback on FSH and LH during luteal phase
- Secretory activity of uterus during luteal phase
- Development of [[Breast|breasts]]
- During [[Prenatal Period|pregnancy]]
	- Endometrial lining
	- ↓ uterine excitability
