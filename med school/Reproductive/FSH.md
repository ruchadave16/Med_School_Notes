*Follicle Stimulating Hormone*

Increases during early follicular phase & late luteal phase
- Drives [[Ovary Follicles|ovarian follicle]] development
- As a result of ↓ [[negative feedback]] on [[hypothalamus]] by [[estrogen]] and [[progesterone]]
- Result of ↓ negative feedback on [[anterior pituitary]] by [[inhibin]] and [[estrogen]]

Decreases until [[ovulation]]
- Result of ↑ [[estrogen]] secretion by granulosa cells of follicle
- This helps **select dominant follicle**

↓ FSH during luteal phase – Don’t need to develop follicles
