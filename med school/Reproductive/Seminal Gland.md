Structure between [[Urinary Bladder|bladder]] and [[rectum]]
- Secretes seminal fluid – alkaline fluid that mixes with [[sperm]]
- Adds [[fructose]] for energy of spermatozoa

Innervation
- Parasympathetic: pelvic splanchnic n.

#### Histology
- Highly folded mucosa
- Pseudostratified columnar epithelium → produces fluid
- Smooth muscle under lamina propria which contracts during [[ejaculation]]
