*Form from [[neural crest cell]] migration into mesoderm*
#### Formation
- Made of neural crest, paraxial mesoderm, and lateral plate mesoderm

![[Drawing 2025-10-31 20.43.35.excalidraw]]

-  Neural crest cells migrate forward to form pharyngeal arches (brings paraxial mesoderm cells)

![[Drawing 2025-10-31 20.47.04.excalidraw]]
- In the endoderm inside, [[pharyngeal pouches]] are formed between each arch
	- Extend laterally
	
![[Drawing 2025-10-31 20.53.25.excalidraw]]

- Each arch contains artery (from aortic arch), cartilage (from neural cresty), muscle (from mesoderm) and cranial nerve (from neural crest)

![[Drawing 2025-10-31 20.54.37.excalidraw]]
#### Arches
1) 1st Pharyngeal Arch (contains oropharyngeal membrane, stomodeum)
	- Skeletal
		- Malleus, incus
		- Mandibular cartilage
		- Mandible and maxilla
		- Zygomatic, palatine, vomer bones
	- Muscular
		- [[Chewing|Mastication]] muscles
		- Mylohyoid
		- Digastric anterior belly
		- Tensor tympani
		- Tensor veli palatini
	- Nerve
		- [[Mandibular Nerve]] (CN V3)
	- Artery
		- Aortic Arch 1 ([[Maxillary Artery|maxillary a.]])
2) 2nd Pharyngeal Arch
	- Skeletal
		- Reichart’s cartilage
		- Stapes
		- Styloid process
		- Hyoid bone (lesser horn and superior body)
	- Muscular
		- Facial expression muscles
		- Stapedius
		- Stylohyoid
		- Digastric posterior belly
	- Nerve
		- [[Facial Nerve|Facial n.]] (CN VII)
	- Artery
		- Aortic Arch 2 (hyoid and stapedial a.)
3) 3rd Pharyngeal Arch
	- Skeletal
		- Hyoid bone (Greater horn and inferior body)
	- Muscular
		- Stylopharyngeus
	- Nerve
		- [[Glossopharyngeal Nerve|Glossopharyngeal n.]] (CN IX)
	- Artery
		- Aortic Arch III (internal carotid a. and common carotid a.)
4) 4th Pharyngeal Arch and 6th Pharyngeal Arch
	- Skeletal
		- Laryngeal cartilage
	- Muscular
		- 4th:
			- Pharynx constrictors
			- Levator veli palatini
			- Cricothyroid
		- 6th:
			- Intrinsic muscles of larynx
	- Nerve
		- 4th: [[Vagus Nerve|CN X]] superior laryngeal
		- 6th: [[Vagus Nerve|CN X]]: recurrent laryngeal
	- Artery
		- 4th: Aortic Arch IV (R subclavian a. and aorta)
		- 6th: Pulmonary a. and ductus arteriosus

*Note: 5th is not present in humans, regresses*
*Note: Eventually 4th and 6th merge*
