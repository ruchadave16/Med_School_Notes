Forms from 4 structures
- Septum transversum (4th week) – adult central tendon
- Esophageal [[Mesentery Formation|mesentery]] – crura of diaphragm
- Pleuroperitoneal folds – (from mesoderm, forms posterior-lateral wall)
	- Fuses with septum transversum and mesentery of esophagus
	- Scaffolding for myoblasts
- Muscle fibers (C3-C5) form lateral body wall

#### Descent of Diaphragm
- Initially anterior to C3-C5
- Descends because of overgrowth of cranial part
- At resting place by Week 8

>[!Clinical] [[Congenital Diaphragmatic Hernia]]
> Herniation of contents into thoracic cavity
