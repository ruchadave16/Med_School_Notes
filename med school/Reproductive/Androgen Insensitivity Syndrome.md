### Definition
[[Testes]] secrete androgens but don’t induce male genitalia
### Etiology
- Primary causes
- Risk factors
### Pathophysiology
1) Defect in androgen receptor → no response
2) External genitalia insensitive to androgens at cellular level

- Results in 46 XY [[chromosomes]], testicular tissue, but external genitalia of female
- MIS/AMH produced, NO internal female genitalia, [[Paramesonephric Ducts|Paramesonephric ducts]] degenerated

### Clinical Features
- Signs – generally seen after [[puberty]]
	- No [[menarche]]
- Variants
