*Cells clumped together in loose CT between [[Seminiferous Tubules|seminiferous tubules]]*

Nucleus: Large, pale, 1-2 nucleoli
Cytoplasm: many lipid droplets, obvious smooth [[Endoplasmic Reticulum|ER]], pale [[Golgi Apparatus]]

**Function**: Secrete [[testosterone]] when stimulated by [[LH]]
