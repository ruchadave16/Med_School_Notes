*Muscular canal from outside ([[vulva]]) to neck of uterus [[Uterine Cervix|cervix]]*
#### Layers
1) Mucosa
	- Has rugae
	- Stratified squamous epithelium (glycogenated, non-keratinized)
	- Loose lamina propria with [[Elastic Fibers|elastic fibers]]

2) Muscularis
	- Inner circular, outer longitudinal [[smooth muscle]]

3) Adventitia
	- Dense [[connective tissue]]
	- Cervical glands make mucus
