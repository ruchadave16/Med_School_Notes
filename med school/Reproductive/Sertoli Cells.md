*Cells in spermatogenic epithelium*

#### Structure
- Elongated columnar cells from basal lamina → lumen
- Deep cytoplasmic foldings cradling spermatogenic cells
- Large, pale nucleus, prominent nucleolus
- Tight margins with [[tight junctions]] – [[blood-testis barrier]]

#### Function
- Physical support to spermatogenic cells
- Regulate nutrition to [[spermatozoa]]
- Protect from autoimmune 
- [[Phagocytosis]] of residual bodies
- Secretion of
	- Fluid for transport of sperm
	- [[ADP]] (Androgen-binding protein) which combines with [[testosterone]]
