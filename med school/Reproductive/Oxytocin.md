Released from [[posterior pituitary]]

- Released from neurons in supraoptic and paraventricular nuclei of [[hypothalamus]]
- Made as preprophormones → granules → processed into active
- Transported down axons (neurophysins), accumulate in terminals in [[posterior pituitary]]
#### Stimulus
- Sucking of [[Mammary Glands|mammary gland]] → afferent nerve impulse to hypothalamus → oxytocin-secreting neurons
- Cervical dilation → stretch receptors in [[Uterine Cervix|cervix]] → oxytocin secretion ([[Positive Feedback|positive feedback]])
#### Functions
- Smooth muscle contractions (myoepithelial cells) in [[Breast|breast]] because of suckling
	- Milk EJECTION
- Contraction of smooth muscle in [[uterus]] during [[Birth|childbirth]]
- Increased during intercourse
