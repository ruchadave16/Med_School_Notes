*Largest spermatogenic cell*

- Closer to lumen
- Large round lumen with dark heterochromatin
- [[DNA Replication]]
- 46 [[Chromosomes]] (diploid) and tetraploid DNA (4N) to prep for [[meiosis]] I
- Undergoes [[meiosis]] I to make 2 [[secondary spermatocytes]]
