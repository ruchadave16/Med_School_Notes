[[Hemoglobin A]] = member of [[hemoglobin]] group
- Used to diagnose long term blood sugar

Glucose reacts with Hemoglobin A to make Hemoglobin A1C
- ↑ glucose = ↑ A1C in blood
- Can monitor to see if diet change or medication can control [[diabetes]]
