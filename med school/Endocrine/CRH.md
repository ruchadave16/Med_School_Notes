*Hormone secreted by [[hypothalamus]] that stimulates [[Anterior Pituitary|anterior pituitary]]*
- Causes it to release [[ACTH]]
- Neuronal centers regulate synthesis of CRH in response to stress + circadian rhythms

- Pro-inflammatory [[cytokines]] stimulate release of CRH
