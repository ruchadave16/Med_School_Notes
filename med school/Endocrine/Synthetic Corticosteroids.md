*Can minimize impact on mineralocorticoids while increasing therapeutic effect*

>[!Info]
>- [[Prednisone]] / [[Prednisolone]] = MAIN oral anti-inflammatory steroids
>- [[Methylprednisolone]] used when we want to avoid fluid retention
>- [[Dexamethasone]] = high potency, long lasting, no salt retention
>- [[Hydrocortisone]] and [[Fludrocortisone]] for adrenal insufficiency

- Drugs have delayed onset
- Plasma life << effects

#### Therapeutic Use
- Physiologic Dose (Replacement Therapy)
- Pharmacological Dose (Supraphysiologic Therapy)
	- Anti-Inflammatory 
		- Autoimmune, **bronchial asthma**, eczema, IBD
	- Anticancer
		- Hematologic malignancies so ↓ lymphocyte
	- <2 weeks is well tolerated
		- High dose may cause insomnia, behavior change, hyperglycemia, peptic ulcers
	- >2 weeks have adverse effects

**Guidelines**
- Diet ↑ protein and K+, ↓ Na+ and simple carbs

>[!Warning] For patients with
>- [[Peptic ulcers]] – ↑ GI bleeding risk, concurrent PPI use recommended
>- Heart disease / failure – Na and water retention could increase fluid overload
>- Infectious disease – Immune suppression can make worse
>- Psychoses – High dose can cause mood swing, mania
>- [[Diabetes]] – Monitor glucose and adjust antidiabetics
>- [[Osteoporosis]] – Increases bone loss, add Ca, Vitamin D, bisphosphonates
>- [[Glaucoma]] – Can increase intraocular pressure 

>[!Warning]
>After withdrawal, check for disease coming back
>- Chronic use suppresses ACTH levels so can cause acute adrenal insufficiency
>- Most common cause of [[Cushing Disease]]

>[!Info] To reduce toxicity
>- Use ones with ↓ mineralocorticoid activity 
>- Local therapies (inhaled / topical)
>- Intermediate as 1 morning dose to mimic normal cortisol
>- Gradually taper to prevent withdrawal
