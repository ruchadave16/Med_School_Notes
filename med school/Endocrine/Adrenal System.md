*Superomedial side of [[kidneys]]*

[[Adrenal cortex]]: Outer layer from [[mesoderm]], secretes [[hormones]]
[[Adrenal medulla]]: Inner layer from [[neural crest cells]]
- Preganglionic sympathetic (T10-L1) synapse **directly** onto [[chromaffin cells]]
- Release [[epinephrine]] and [[norepinephrine]] into adrenal v. 

Surrounded by connective tissue capsule
#### Blood Supply
- Superior adrenal a. (from inferior phrenic aa.)
- Middle adrenal a. (from abdominal [[aorta]])
- Inferior adrenal a. (from renal aa.)

- R suprarenal v. (Empties to [[Inferior Vena Cava|IVC]])
- L suprarenal v. (Joins inferior phrenic v. to IVC)
