*4 glands on posterior surface of [[thyroid gland]]*
#### Cells
1) Chief cells
	- Small, round, acidophilic
	- Make [[PTH]] when ↓ calcium in blood to increase Ca levels
	- Bone resorption
	- Calcium resorption in kidneys
2) Oxyphil cells
	- Function not understood well
