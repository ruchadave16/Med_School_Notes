*Comes from [[neural crest]], produces [[catecholamines]]*

- Made of anastomosing cords of [[chromaffin cells]] 
- Make and secrete [[catecholamines]]:
	- [[epinephrine]] and [[norepinephrine]]
	- some [[dopamine]]
	
>[!Clinical]
>[[Pheochromocytoma]] – tumor of [[chromaffin cells]], high release of [[Catecholamines|catecholamines]]
>- Can cause hypertension or hyperglycemia
#### Hormones
- [[Norepinephrine]] → [[epinephrine]] based on blood supply and if [[glucocorticoids]] present
- [[ACTH]] stimulates creation of norepinephrine and dopamine
#### Secretion: 
- [[Ach]] goes from sympathetic preganglionic neuron → greater splanchnic nerve → adrenal medulla
- Chromaffin cells have [[nicotinic receptors]] 
- Activation → [[Catecholamines|catecholamine]] release
