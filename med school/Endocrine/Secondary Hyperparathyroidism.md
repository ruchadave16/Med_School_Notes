### Etiology
Normal parathyroid glands but high secretion of [[PTH]] *secondary* to [[hypocalcemia]]
- Vitamin D deficiency, hypocalcemia, chronic kidney disease
### Pathophysiology
- Hypocalcemia causes parathyroid to secrete more PTH
- Relevant pathways (e.g. immune, hormonal)
