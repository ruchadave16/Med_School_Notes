*Located in [[liver]] and [[kidneys]], type of [[G Protein Coupled Receptors|GPCR]]*

1) Undergoes conformational change when [[glucagon]] binds
2) G-proteins are activated and alpha subunit activates adenylyl cyclase
3) cAMP production → PKA pathway
	- PKA phosphorylates phosphorylase kinase enzyme
	- Phosphorylase kinase phosphorylates and activates glycogen phosphorylase kinase ([[glycogenolysis]])
	- PKA also phosphorylates glycogen synthase and deactivates it
