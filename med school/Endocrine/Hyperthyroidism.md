### Definition
Hyperplasia of follicles (very high production of [[thyroid hormone]])

### Etiology
- [[Grave’s Disease]]: Most common cause

### Pathophysiology

### Clinical Features
Symptoms:
- Weight loss and high food intake
- ↑ Heat production and sweating
- Tachycardia
- Exertional SoB
- Tremor
- Nervousness
- Weakness

Signs
- Eye protrusion
- Enlarged [[thyroid gland]] ([[goiter]])
	- Can compress esophagus (difficulty [[swallowing]])
### Diagnosis
- Signs and Symptoms
- Measure T3 and T4 levels
- TSH can ↑ or ↓ based on cause

### Treatment
- Pharmacologic
	- [[Beta Blockers|Beta blockers]] can alleviate symptoms
	- [[Propranolol]] inhibits T4 → T3 conversion
- Surgical
- Lifestyle

- Short-term
