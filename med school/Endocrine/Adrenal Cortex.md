*Comes from [[mesoderm]], produces [[corticosteroids]]*

- Cuboid cells that synthesize and secrete but don’t store [[steroid hormones]]
- Main source of [[androgens]] in **women**
#### Zones
1) Zona glomerulosa
	- Right below capsule
	- Round clumps of cells that are dark staining
	- Make [[mineralocorticoids]] 
	- Increase sodium reabsorption in kidney
	- Expresses aldosterone synthase (**CYP11B2**)
	- Stimulated by angiotensin II and [[ACTH]]
	- Regulation = [[Renin-Angiotensin-Aldosterone System|RAAS system]]
		- No atrophy in absence of ACTH
2) Zona fasciculata
	- Thick layer
	- Cells with light, foamy cytoplasm
	- Make [[glucocorticoids]]
	- Expresses 17α-hydroxylase (**CYP17**) and 11β-hydroxylase
	- Stimulated by [[ACTH]], atrophy in absence
3) Zona reticularis
	- Inner layer
	- Small, acidophilic cells
	- Expresses **CYP17**
	- Atrophy in absence of ACTH
	- Make weak [[adrenal androgens]]
