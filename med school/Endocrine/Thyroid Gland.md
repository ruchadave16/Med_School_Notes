*2 lobes made of lobules of colloid-filled follicles connected by isthmus*

- Butterfly-shaped gland anterior to [[Trachea|trachea]] below [[larynx]], right under thyroid cartilage
- Deep to sternothyroid and sternohyoid m.
- C5-T1 vertebral levels
#### Thyroid Follicles
- Cyst-like compartments with parenchyma
- Follicular cells (simple cuboid epithelium)
	- Contain iodide transporters
		- Move iodide against gradient into thyroid **iodide trap**
	- Make [[thyroglobulin]]
		- Released into follicle lumen
		- Iodinated by enzymes and stored as colloid
	- When stimulated by [[TSH]] from [[anterior pituitary]], 
		- Endocytose colloid (iodinated thyroglobulin)
		- Thyroglobulin → [[Thyroid Hormones]] (T3 and T4)
		- These ↑ metabolism
#### Parafollicular Cells
- On outside attached to basement membrane
- Large cells, pale cytoplasm
- Contain [[calcitonin]] which is released when high [[Blood Calcium|blood calcium]] to decrease it
	- Stops bone reabsorption by [[osteoclasts]]

#### Vasculature
- Superior thyroid a. ← [[External Carotid Artery|external carotid a.]]
- Inferior thyroid a. ← thyrocervical trunk ← [[Subclavian Artery|subclavian a.]]

VENOUS
- Superior and middle thyroid vv. → internal jugular v.
- Inferior thyroid v. → brachiocephalic v.

#### Lymphatics
- Pretracheal LN
- Paratracheal LN
- Prelaryngeal LN

All go to inferior deep cervical LN
