#### Synthesis
- Delta cells of [[pancreatic islets]]
- Polypeptide of 14 AA (GI version has 28 AA)
#### Regulation
- Stimulation
	- Ingestion of nutrients
	- GI hormones
	- [[Glucagon]]
	- $\alpha$-adrenergic agonists
- Inhibition
	- [[Insulin]]
#### Function
- ↓ insulin and glucagon release
