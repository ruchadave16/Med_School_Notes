*Gland connected to [[hypothalamus]] by stalk (infundibulum) made of 2 glands*

Located in [[brain]] in sella turcica 
- Suspended by infundibulum from [[hypothalamus]]
#### Components
- [[Anterior Pituitary]] (Adenohypophysis) – glandular tissue
- [[Posterior Pituitary]] (Neurohypophysis) – nervous tissue

>[!Clinical]
>[[Growth Hormone|GH]]
>- High GH during tumor involving somatotrophs
>- [[Gigantism]]: tumor during childhood when epiphysial plates are acting
>- [[Acromegaly]]: tumor in adults after epiphysial plates stop growing
>- Low GH can cause [[dwarfism]]
>
>[[Diabetes Insipidus]]
>- ↓ [[Vasopressin|ADH]] causes water to not be reabsorbed by kidneys
>- High thirst and dilute urine
