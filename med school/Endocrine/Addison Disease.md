### Definition
Autoimmune destruction of [[adrenal cortex]]
### Etiology
Autoimmune destruction of [[adrenal cortex]]
### Pathophysiology
- ↓ synthesis of [[Mineralocorticoids|mineralocorticoids]], [[glucocorticoids]], [[adrenal androgens]]
- Increase of [[ACTH]]
### Clinical Features
High ACTH → **Hyperpigmentation** of skin

Loss of [[mineralocorticoids]]
- Hyperkalemia
- [[Metabolic acidosis]]
- Hypotension

Loss of [[glucocorticoids]]
- Hypoglycemia
- Anorexia
- Weight loss
- Nausea
- Vomiting
- Weakness

Loss of adrenal androgens (in women)
- ↓ pubic hair and axillary hair
- ↓ libido

- Labs

### Treatment
Pharmacologic
- Glucocorticoid and mineralocorticoid replacement
