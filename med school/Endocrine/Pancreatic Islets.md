*[[Endocrine System]] cells found in [[pancreas]]*

- Pale staining cells in clusters surrounded by intensely stained pancreatic acini
- Generally beta = 70% of space, more central
- Alpha cells = periphery
#### Cell Types
1) Alpha Cells
	- Periphery of islet
	- Secrete [[glucagon]] when ↓ blood glucose
	- Act on [[hepatocytes]] to breakdown glycogen to glucose
2) Beta Cells
	- In center of islet
	- Releases [[insulin]] when ↑ blood glucose
	- Cells intake glucose and [[hepatocytes]] make glycogen
3) Delta Cells
	- Releases [[pancreatic somatostatin]]
	- Inhibits other islet hormone release
	- Inhibits release of [[GH]] from [[anterior pituitary]]
4) PP Cells
	- Secretes pancreatic polypeptide
	- Inhibits pancreatic enzyme secretions

>[!Clinical]
>[[Diabetes Mellitus|Diabetes mellitus]]
>- Causes: ↓ insulin production or defective insulin receptors
>- Causes ↑ glucose levels
