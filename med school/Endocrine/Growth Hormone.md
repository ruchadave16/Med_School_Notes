- Comes from [[somatotrophs]] of [[anterior pituitary]]
- Released in response to [[GHRH]] from [[hypothalamus]]
	- Age 
	- Time of Day
#### Function
- Linear growth of body → protein synthesis, cell differentiation, cell multiplication
	- Requires [[Thyroid Hormones|thyroid hormone]] levels to be normal 
- ↑ Protein Synthesis and organ growth
- Anti-Insulin Effect
	- [[Insulin]] resistance
	- High [[gluconeogenesis]] in [[liver]]
	- ↓ glucose uptake, high blood glucose 
	- High [[lipolysis]] in [[Adipose|adipose tissue]]
*Indirectly stimulates release of somatomedins and insulin-like growth factor-I from liver*
