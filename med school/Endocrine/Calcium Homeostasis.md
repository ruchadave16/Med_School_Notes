[[PTH]] released in response to ↓ Ca2+

- Bone reabsorption, bringing Ca and phosphate to [[ECF]] (**PTH**)
	- [[PTH]] receptors on [[osteoblasts]] → Increase in bone formation by direct action on osteoblasts 
	- ↑ bone reabsorption on [[osteoclasts]] – indirect, mediated by [[RANKL]]
- GI absorbs vs excretes (**[[Vitamin D]]**)
- Kidneys absorb vs excrete (Vitamin D + PTH)
