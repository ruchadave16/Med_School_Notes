*Removal of [[thyroid gland]] surgically*

- Need to know arterial supply 
	- 10% chance of thyroid a. off of aorta
- Inferior thyroid a. is close to [[Recurrent Laryngeal Nerve|recurrent laryngeal n.]] 
	- Need to make sure not to damage

- Parathyroid is posterior to thyroid
	- Often can leave posterior thyroid intact
