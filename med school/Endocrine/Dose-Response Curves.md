*[[Hormones|Hormone]] effects graded to concentration of free hormone available*

![[Screenshot 2025-10-21 at 12.22.04 AM.png]]

- Generally basal activity without hormone
- Concentration increases to a **threshold** before effect is seen
- At high concentration, maximal response seen

**ED50** = dose for 50% of maximal response
- Shows sensitivity

#### Changes
Responsiveness
- Change in # functional cells
- Change in # number of receptors

Sensitivity (right or left shift)
- Change to receptor affinity
- Increase in hormone amount
