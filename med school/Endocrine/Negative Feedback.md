*Inhibition of [[Hormones|hormone]] secretion based on some feature of its action*

#### Simple Feedback
- Endocrine cell senses magnitude of effect of hormone → decreases secretion of hormone
- Not common
- Ex: [[Pancreatic Islets|Islets of Langerhans]]

#### Complex Feedback
- Multiple systems involved

>[!Example] [[Pituitary Gland]]
>Hypothalamus releases hormone → acts on anterior pituitary to release trophic hormones
>These cause target gland hormone release
>These actions are sensed by pituitary and hypothalamus → Negative feedback
