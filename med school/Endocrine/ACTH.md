*From corticotrophs cells of [[anterior pituitary]]*
- Derived from [[pro-opiomelanocortin]] (POMC)
- Higher affinity for MC2R than MC1R
	- Excess can bind to MC1R on [[melanocytes]] causing hyperpigmentation
#### Stimulus
- [[CRH]] of [[hypothalamus]] due to glucose, stress, time of day, and [[Vasopressin|ADH]]
- Pro-inflammatory [[cytokines]] stimulate release of ACTH
#### Function
- Synthesis and release of [[glucocorticoids]] and [[adrenal androgens]]
