Compound found in noradrenergic nerve terminals, liver, intestinal epithelium
- Metabolizes [[neurotransmitters]] within nerve terminal
- Inhibition causes ↑ monoamines in brain

**MAO A** – [[Norepinephrine|norepinephrine]], [[Serotonin|5 HT]], [[tyramine]]
**MAO B** – [[dopamine]]
