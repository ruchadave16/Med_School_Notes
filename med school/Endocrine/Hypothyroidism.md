### Etiology
- Primary causes
	- Autoimmune conditions
	- Secondary to pituitary or hypothalamic disease
- [[Hashimoto's disease]] = thyroid destruction autoimmune
- Iodine deficiency
	- Endemic colloid goiters – common in locations of low iodine in soil
### Pathophysiology
- Low iodine stops synthesis of [[thyroid hormones]]


### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Weight gain without increase in food
	- Low heat production and cold intolerance
	- Fatigue
	- Muscle slowness
	- ↓ heart rate
	- ↓ cardiac output
	- Mental lethargy
	- Constipation
	- Hair loss
	- Menstrual dysfunction
- Signs
	- Periorbital puffiness
	- Goiter ← If cause = defect in thyroid
- Variants

### Diagnosis
- Signs and Symptoms
- ↓ T3 and T4 levels
- TSH can be high or low

### Treatment
- Pharmacologic
	- Thyroid hormone replacement therapy
- Surgical
- Lifestyle

### Complications
- Short-term
- Long-term
	- Can cause [[cretinism]] if during perinatal period without treatment
