*From thyrotroph of [[anterior pituitary]] in response to TRH from [[hypothalamus]]*

- Turned off by [[somatostatin]]
#### Function
- Regulates [[Thyroid Hormones|thyroid hormone]] synthesis and secretion
- Maintains thyroid follicular cell size and functional integrity
