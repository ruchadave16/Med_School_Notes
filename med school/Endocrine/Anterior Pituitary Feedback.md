- Controlled by [[hypothalamus]] (releasing or release-inhibiting hormones) through [[hypophyseal portal circulation]] (pulsatile episodic secretion)
- Stimulating – (Growth hormone releasing) GRH, (Thyrotropin releasing) TRH, (Prolactin releasing) PRH, (Gonadotropin releasing) GnRH, (Corticotropin releasing) CRH
- Inhibitory – [[Dopamine]] (↓ PRL, ↑ [[Growth Hormone|GH]]), [[somatostatin]] (↓ GH and TSH)
