*Hydrophilic hormones*
#### Synthesis
Made on [[Ribosome|ribosomes]] and undergo post-synthesis processing
- **Preprohormones** = peptide with signal sequence that directs to [[Endoplasmic Reticulum|ER]]
- **Prohormones** = peptide in ER once signal sequence taken off
	- Processed through [[Golgi Apparatus]] into vesicles where they are cleaved to become active hormones
- Stored *in vesicles* and released through [[Membrane Transport|exocytosis]]
