*Low [[blood calcium]]*
#### Symptoms
- Hyperreflexia
- Twitching
- Muscle cramps
- Tingling
- Numbness

#### Signs
- **Chvostek sign** – twitching of face muscles by tapping on [[facial nerve]]
- **Trousseau sign** – carpopedal spasm when BP cuff inflated
