#### [[Hormones|Hormone]] Deficiency
- Most commonly, hormone is degraded in [[Glands|gland]] it is produced in
- Can also be genetic effects
#### Hormone Excess
- Need to differentiate between exogenous and endogenous
- Exogenous
	- High administration or backlog
	- Accidental or by design
- Endogenous
	- From hormone that produces, often tumors
	- Tumors from other, nonendocrine organs
	- Antireceptor antibodies stimulate receptor

#### Hormone Resistance
- Normal amount but action not seen
- Primary receptor deficit or post receptor mechanism problem
