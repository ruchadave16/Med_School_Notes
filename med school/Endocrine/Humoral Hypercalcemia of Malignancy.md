Malignant tumors can sometimes make [[PTH]]-like peptides

**Similar profile to [[Hyperparathyroidism|primary hyperparathyroidism]]** but LOW PTH levels

#### Treatment
- Tumor treatment
- Furosemide – inhibit renal Ca reabsorption and increase excretion
- Inhibitors of bone resorption (etidronate)
