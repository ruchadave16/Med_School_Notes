#### Synthesis
- Beta cells of [[pancreatic islets]]
- Peptide hormone with 2 straight chains, A chain, and B chain. 2 disulfide bridges between A and B chains and one more within A chain

![[Pasted image 20251027212607.png]]

- Made from [[proinsulin]] (single peptide) with connecting portion between A and B called C peptide
- Both insulin and C peptide are released together

#### Regulation
- [[Glucose]]
	- If glucose ↑, insulin ↑
- Dietary nutrients
	- Amino acids and fatty acids stimulate release
- Exercise inhibits
- [[Glucagon]] increases exocytosis

#### Functions
1) ↓ blood glucose
	- ↑ transport into cells by adding GLUT4 transporters into membranes
	- ↑ [[glycolysis]] especially in skeletal muscle
		- Activates [[Phosphofructokinase 1|PFK-1]]
	- ↑ glycogen formation in liver, inhibits glycogenolysis
	- Inhibits gluconeogenesis

2) ↓ [[Fatty Acids|fatty acid]] and ketoacid in blood
	- No mobilization or [[Beta Oxidation|fatty acid oxidation]]
	- ↑ storage of fatty acids, ↓ lipolysis

3) ↓ blood amino acid
	- Increased uptake by tissues
	- ↑ protein synthesis, no degradation

4) K+ into cells
5) Satiety signal to [[hypothalamus]]
