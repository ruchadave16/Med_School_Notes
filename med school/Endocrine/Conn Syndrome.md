### Etiology
[[Aldosterone]] secreting tumor

### Pathophysiology
- High aldosterone 
	- High Na reabsorption, K secretion, H secretion
	- ↑ ECF volume
- Relevant pathways (e.g. immune, hormonal)

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Hypertension
	- Hypokalemia
	- [[Metabolic alkalosis]]
- Signs
	- ↓ [[renin]] in circulation because of increasing ECF volume
- Variants

- Imaging

### Treatment
- Pharmacologic
	- [[Spironolactone]] – aldosterone antagonist

	- Removal of tumor
