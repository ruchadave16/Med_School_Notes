### Etiology
- [[Parathyroid gland]] adenomas 
### Pathophysiology
Parathyroid released high [[PTH]]
- Causes [[Hypercalcemia|hypercalcemia]] and [[hypophosphatemia]]

### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- High [[Blood Calcium|blood calcium]] → 
	- Bone weakness (↓ density)
	- Kidney stones
	- Constipation
	- Psych symptoms (memory, depression)
- Signs
	- ↑ secretion of phosphate, cAMP, and calcium in urine
- Variants
