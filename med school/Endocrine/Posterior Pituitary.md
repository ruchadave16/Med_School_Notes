*Extension of [[hypothalamus]]*

- Neural portion
- Pars nervosa + infundibular stalk
- Has unmyelinated hypothalamic axons and fenestrated capillary plexus

![[Screenshot 2025-10-21 at 2.48.07 PM.png]]
#### Components
- Pituicytes
	- Highly branched glial cells
	- Elongated nuclei
	- Stain for GFAP

![[Pasted image 20251108141426.png]]
#### Secretion
- [[Oxytocin]]
- [[Vasopressin]] (ADH)

These are produced in nuclei of [[hypothalamus]] but released by axon terminals (Herring bodies) in pars nervosa
- Released into fenestrated capillaries
#### Embryology
- Comes from neuroectoderm from floor of diencephalon
