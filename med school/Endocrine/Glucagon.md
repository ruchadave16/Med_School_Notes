#### Synthesis
- Alpha cells of [[pancreatic islets]]
- 29 AA straight chain [[Peptide Hormones|peptide hormone]]
- Made from preprohormone with other hormones

![[Pasted image 20251027215142.png]]

#### Regulation
- [[Glucose]] = regulator so ↓ glucose = ↑ glucagon
- [[Insulin]] inhibits release of glucagon
#### Function
- Action on [[liver]]
- ↑ blood glucose 
	- ↑ [[glycogenolysis]] and inhibits glycogenesis
	- ↑ [[gluconeogenesis]]
	- AA used up
	- ↓ [[glycolysis]]
- ↑ blood [[fatty acids]] and ketoacids
	- ↑ [[lipolysis]] and ↓ [[fatty acid synthesis]]
