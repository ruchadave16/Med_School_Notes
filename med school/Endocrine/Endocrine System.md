*Collection of ductless glands that synthesize and secrete [[hormones]] to maintain [[homeostasis]]*

- Highly vascularized 
- Hormones bind to target cell receptors at
	- Distant site = endocrine
	- Neighboring cells = paracrine
	- Itself = autocrine

#### Components
- Separate glands ([[pituitary gland]], [[thyroid gland]], [[parathyroid gland]], [[Adrenal System|adrenal system]], [[pineal gland]])
- Scattered masses of endocrine tissues ([[Pancreatic Islets|Islets of Langerhans]])
- Isolated endocrine cells ([[Diffuse Neuroendocrine System]])
