*32 AA peptide made and secreted by parafollicular cells of [[thyroid gland]]*
#### Function
- ↓ [[blood calcium]] and phosphate
- **Counters PTH for calcium, works with PTH for phosphate**

Bone:
- ↓ [[osteoclast]] action to break down bone

Kidney:
- ↓ tubular reabsorption of calcium and phosphate
#### Secretion
- Whenever ↑ blood calcium levels
