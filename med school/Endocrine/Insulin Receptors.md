*preformed dimerized [[RTKs|RTK]]*
#### Pathway
1) [[Insulin]] binds and beta subunits phosphorylate each others tyrosines
2) IRS (insulin receptor substrate) binds through SH2 and phosphate added to tyrosine at multiple locations
3) P-IRS provides binding sites for other proteins with SH2 domain
4) Grb2 activates [[Ras MAPK Pathway]]
5) PI3 kinase binds and makes more IP3
6) IP3 = docking for PDK1 and PKB serine protein kinases
	- PDK1 phosphorylates and activates PKB (AKT) on serines
	- AKT phosphorylates targets on serine residues → gene expression and [[glucose]] uptake
