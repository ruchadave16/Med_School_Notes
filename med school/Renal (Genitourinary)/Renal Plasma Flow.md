#### Measure

>[!Info] $RPF * A_X - RPF * RV_x = U_x * Y$ 
>RPF = renal plasma flow
>A_x = arterial plasma concentration
>RV_x = renal vein plasma concentration
>U_x = urine concentration
>V = urine flow

If substance completely extracted from plasma so RV = 0, $RPF = \frac{U_xV}{A_x} = Clearance$

**[[Paraaminohippurate]] (PAH)** and **[[Diodrast]]**
- Freely filtered, not reabsorbed, highly secreted

Can find [[renal blood flow]] by correcting for % absorption and hematocrit
$RBF = \frac{RPF}{1-Hct}$
