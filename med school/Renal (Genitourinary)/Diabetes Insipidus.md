### Pathophysiology
**Central**
- Failure of [[posterior pituitary]] to secrete [[Vasopressin|ADH]]
	- ADH low

**Nephrogenic**
- Cells in collecting duct don’t respond to ADH
	- ADH high

Water is NOT reabsorbed
- Urine not concentrated
- Serum osmolarity ↑
