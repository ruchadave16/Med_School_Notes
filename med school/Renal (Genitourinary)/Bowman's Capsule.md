Parietal layer – external capsule (simple squamous [[Epithelial Tissue|epithelium]])
Visceral layer – fenestrated capillary covering
- Made of [[Podocyte|podocytes]] – stellate cells with pedicels
- Pedicels wrap around glomerular capillaries, combine with other pedicles to form **filtration slits**
