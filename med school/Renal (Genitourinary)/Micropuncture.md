Inserting small, glass micropipettes into different parts of [[nephron]] to collect fluid

Tubular Fluid / Plasma (TF/P) ratio for each substance determined
- Need marker for reabsorption ([[inulin]] isn’t reabsorbed or secreted so it works)
>[!Example] Late proximal tubule TF/P inulin = 3 
>This means inulin concentrated 3x (2/3 filtered water is reabsorbed)
>Fraction filtered water remaining: 1 / TF/P inulin = 1/3 (33%)
>Fraction filtered water reabsorbed = 1 - fraction remaining = 67%
>Fraction filtered solute remaining = $\frac{TF/P solute}{TF/P inulin}$
>Fraction filtered solute reabsorbed = $1 - \frac{TF/P solute}{TF/P inulin}$
