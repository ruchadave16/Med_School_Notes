*Pouch from distal [[hindgut]] where GI tract and urinary system combine*

- Junction of ectoderm and endoderm forms cloacal membrane – degenerates
- Urorectal septum separates (splanchnic [[mesoderm]])

Urogenital Sinus:
- Allantois → [[urachus]] → turns into median umbilical ligament
- Upper part → [[Urinary Bladder|bladder]]
- Pelvic part → [[Urethra|urethra]], parts of [[vagina]]
- Phallic part → [[penis]]
