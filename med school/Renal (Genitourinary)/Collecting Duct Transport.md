Depends on [[Vasopressin|ADH]]
- [[Urinary Dilution]]
#### Presence of ADH
- Water permeable nephron so water reabsorbed until matches interstitial osmolarity
- Affects [[urea]] permeability in inner medullary collecting duct (urea concentration has increased because water left) – still has significant excretion since it isn’t transported actively, just diffused

**Mechanism**
- ADH binds to V2 receptors on basolateral (blood) side
- Increases luminal water permeability → cAMP mediated mechanism which adds [[aquaporins]] into luminal membrane
- Maximal osmolarity in this case = medullary interstitial osmolarity (1200 mOsm)
