*Alkalosis NOT due to change in $CO_2$ elimination*

**Causes** – alkali addition or acid loss: vomiting of HCl, overdose of alkaline drugs, higher renal excretion of $H^+$

1) [[Buffers]]: ECF $HCO_3^-$ ↑ because of $H^+$ loss or $HCO_3^-$ gain
2) [[Respiratory Regulation of Acid-Base|Lungs]]: pH ↑ decreases ventilation → $P_{CO2}$ ↑ → pH ↓ (limited response)
3) [[Renal Regulation of Acid-Base|Kidneys]]: ↑ excretion of $HCO_3^-$  and lower secretion of $H^+$ so less acidic urine, pH ↓ to normal so ventilation increases to normal
