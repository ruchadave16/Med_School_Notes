*Enzyme secreted by [[kidneys]] which starts [[Renin-Angiotensin-Aldosterone System|renin-angiotensin-aldosterone system]]*
#### Renin Secretion
 [[Renin]] secretion by [[juxtaglomerular cells]] (STIMULATION)
- ↑ renal sympathetic nerves → renin release
- ↓ arterial pressure → ↓ stretch of afferent arteriole → renin secretion
- ↓ filtration of Na → ↓ Na load on macula densa of [[Juxtaglomerular Apparatus|juxtaglomerular apparatus]] → renin secretion
#### Function
- Acts on [[angiotensinogen]] from [[liver]] to convert it to [[angiotensin I]]
