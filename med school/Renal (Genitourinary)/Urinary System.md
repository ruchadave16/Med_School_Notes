#### Functions
- Metabolic
	- Remove waste from blood + excrete
	- Regulate ion, salt, water balance
- Endocrine
	- [[Renin]] – ↑ BP by producing [[angiotensin II]] and [[aldosterone]]
	- [[Erythropoeitin]] – [[Erythrocytes|Erythrocyte]] production and differentiation
	- [[Vitamin D]] – Conversion of 25-OH vitamin D3 to active 1,25(OH)2 D3
