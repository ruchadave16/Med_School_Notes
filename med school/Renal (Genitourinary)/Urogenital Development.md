Formation of [[urogenital ridge]] from intermediate [[mesoderm]]
#### Kidney Development
- Development of 3 kidneys (cranial → caudal order)
- Pronephros made, regresses when mesonephros made, regresses when metanephros made
	- Pronephros eventually makes segments (nephrotomes) and regresses
	- [[Mesonephros]] – degenerates mainly when metanephros made
	- [[Metanephros]]
![[Drawing 2025-09-17 17.36.00.excalidraw]]

- Ascent of kidney upwards
	- Rotation so hilum faces medially
	- Urine production (Month 3)
	
>[!Clinical]
>Atypical locations
>- [[Pelvic kidney]]
>- [[Horseshoe kidney]]
>	- Lower poles of L and R kidneys fuse
>	- Position stays at lower lumbar

>- Accessory Renal Arteries

#### Urinary Development
[[Cloaca]] develops further
Ureteric buds that go into [[Kidneys|kidney]] form [[Ureters|ureter]] → distal part joins with urogenital sinus
- Urinary trigone on back wall of bladder
