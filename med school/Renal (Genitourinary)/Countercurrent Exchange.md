*Establishes osmolarity gradient in medulla*

- Blood flow arranged in loops (vasa recta) so it’s slow
- WITHOUT this, it would flow through medulla to renal pelvis fast, gain solute, and wash out gradient
- Now maintains gradient

![[Pasted image 20250918234049.png]]

- Different directions of diffusion of solute and water → flow is so slow and very little solute lost 
	- Increases by [[Ach]] → excess solute removal → lower osmolarity gradient
