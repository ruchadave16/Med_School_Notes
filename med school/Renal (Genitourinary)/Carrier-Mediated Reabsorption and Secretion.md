Absorption
- Maximal transport rate (Tm) – [[Glucose]], amino acids, phosphate, and sulfate
Secretion
- K+, H+, NH3, organic acids, foreign substances (penicillin, PAH, diodrast)
#### Glucose Reabsorption
- Rate high enough that no glucose excreted
- Glucose also isn’t secreted **
- If plasma glucose > Tm for all nephrons, reabsorption rate = Tm
- IF filtered load > Tm, [[glucosuria]] and [[osmotic diuresis]] (more urine flow because osmotically holds water in tubule)
