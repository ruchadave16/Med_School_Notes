Proximal epithelium – [[urothelium]]
- Changes to pseudostratified columnar → stratified squamous at external orifice
#### Male Urethra
**Histology**
- Longer
- Stellate lumen 
- Intramural
- Prostatic portion – proximal, exits bladder surrounded by prostate gland
	- Gets prostatic and ejaculatory ducts
- Membranous portion – short segment surrounded by [[skeletal muscle]] of urogenital diaphragm
	- Pseudostratified columnar epithelium
- Cavernous portion – through corpus spongiosum of penis
	- Many glands of Littre secrete mucous into lumen
	- Epithelium → stratified squamous

**Innervation*
- Prostatic plexus ← [[inferior hypogastric plexus]]
#### Female Urethra
- Shorter
- Similar epithelium $\Delta$

>[!Clinical] Shorter length means ↑ risk of [[UTIs]]
