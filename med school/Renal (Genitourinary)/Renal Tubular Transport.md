Technique to study renal tubular processes – [[micropuncture]]
#### Overview
###### Water Absorption
Adjusted based on hydration level
- 2/3 filtered fluid absorbed in [[Proximal Convoluted Tubule|proximal convoluted tubule]]
- 15% in descending [[Loop of Henle]]
- 8-17% in [[Distal Convoluted Tubules|distal convoluted tubule]] and [[collecting duct]]
###### Sodium Absorption
- 2/3 reabsorbed in [[Proximal Convoluted Tubule|proximal convoluted tubule]]
- 25% reabsorbed in ascending [[Loop of Henle|Loops of Henle]]
- 5% absorbed in [[collecting duct]]
