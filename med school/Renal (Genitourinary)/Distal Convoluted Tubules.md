Low cuboid cells with NO brush border
- Lateral boundaries indistinct

**Macula Densa**
- Epithelial cells at vascular pole of glomerulus **distal tubule**
- Contact afferent arteriole
- Sense ↓ salt and osmolarity and cause juxtaglomerular cells to release [[renin]] etc.
