[[Smooth muscle]] of afferent arteriole of [[Renal Corpuscle|glomerulus]] contracts or relaxes to change in transmural pressure
- Keeps blood flow and [[Glomerular Filtration|glomerular filtration]] constant

>[!Example]
>↑ perfusion pressure → ↑ transmural pressure across afferent arteriole → ↑ stretch of smooth muscle → vasoconstriction → ↑ afferent arteriole resistance → ↓ RBF and GFR to normal
