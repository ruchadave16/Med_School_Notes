*Not due to retention of $P_{CO2}$ – non-respiratory*

**Causes** – acid addition or alkaline loss: diarrhea, ingestion of acid, inability to break down acid, inability to excrete acid
#### Body Changes
- [[Buffers]]: ECF $HCO_3^-$ ↓ because of buffering $H^+$ or loss of $HCO_3^-$ 
- [[Respiratory Regulation of Acid-Base|Lungs]] (minutes): ↓ pH → ventilation → ↓ $P_{CO2}$ → pH rise
- [[Renal Regulation of Acid-Base|Kidneys]] (hours/days): ↑ excretion of $H^+$ and ↑ formation of $HCO_3^-$ to replenish buffers
