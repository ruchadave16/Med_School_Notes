- [[Renin]] released from [[Kidneys|kidney]] ([[Juxtaglomerular Apparatus|juxtaglomerular apparatus]]) as enzyme
- Renin acts on [[angiotensinogen]] from [[liver]] to make [[angiotensin I]] 
	- **Renin is RATE-LIMITING enzyme**
- This passes through lungs and [[angiotensin converting enzyme]] converts to [[angiotensin II]], which is active
	- ↑ Renin = ↑ angiotensin II in body 
- [[Angiotensin II]] stimulates release of [[aldosterone]]*

Activation: changes in blood volume or BP – detected by [[stretch receptors]] in aortic arc and carotid sinus and by [[blood volume receptors]] in atria
