Known amount of dye added to container of fluid
- Volume of distribution = amount dye / final dye concentration

>[!Example]
>50 mg dye added
>Final concentration = 0.2 mg/mL
>
>Dye distribution = 50 / 0.2 = 2500 mL

In body, kidneys excrete dye so collect [[urine]] and measure dye content – correction factor

>[!Info] Volume distribution = $\frac{injected - excreted}{final plasma conc.}$
