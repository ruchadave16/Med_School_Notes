*Color perception due to cones ([[photoreceptors]])*
- S cones (blue)
- M cones (green)
- L cones (red)
#### Opponens Color Theory
- Green vs red
- Blue vs yellow

>[!Clinical]
>Color Blindness – Absence of cone or abnormal function
>- 10% male population
>- M and L on X chromosome
>- [[Deuteranopia]] (defect of M cone)
>- [[Protanopia]] (defect of L cone)
