*Anterior 1/6 of external fibrous layer of eye*

- Transparent, avascular so nutrients from ambient air
#### Layers
- Corneal Epithelium
	- Nonkeratinized stratified squamous
	- Desmosomes
	- Free nerve endings of [[Ophthalmic Nerve|CN V1]]
- Bowman’s Membrane
	- Basement membrane of epithelium
	- Type 1 [[collagen]]
	- Infection barrier
- Stroma
	- Parallel Type 1 collagen fibers
	- “holes” are artifact
- Descemet’s Membrane
	- Basement membrane of endothelium
- Corneal Endothelium
	- Simple squamous
	- Tight junctions
	- Move ions and fluid in and out of stroma
