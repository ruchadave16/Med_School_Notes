*Anterior extension of uvea around lens*

- Ciliary Muscle
	- Most of stroma of ciliary body
	- [[Smooth muscle]]
	- Changes shape of lens, allows for visual accommodation
- Ciliary Processes
	- Ridges that make aqueous humor
- Ciliary Zonule
	- Radially oriented fibers of [[fibrillin]]
	- Attach to surface of lens to hold in place
