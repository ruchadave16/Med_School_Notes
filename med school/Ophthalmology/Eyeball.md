#### Inner Features
- Outer Layers
	- Fibrous Tunic
		- [[Sclera]] – tough, opaque posterior
			- White of [[Eye]]
			- Attachment of intrinsic and extrinsic muscles
		- [[Cornea]] – smaller, transparent anterior
			- Transparent layer covering anterior 1/6
			- NO vasculature
		- [[Limbus]] – point where cornea and sclera connect
	- Vascular Tunic (Uvea)
		- [[Choroid]] – vascular
		- [[Ciliary Body]]
			- Ring like thickening of vascular layer posterior to corneoscleral junction
			- Attachment of [[Lens]]
		- [[Iris]] – regulates light entering globe of eye through pupil
			- Contractile diaphragm on anterior surface of lens
	- [[Retina]]
		- Pigmented layer
		- Neural layer
