*Thin diaphragm between [[Cornea]] and [[Lens]] with central pupil*

- No epithelial covering
- Melanocytes and fibroblasts
- Posterior surface has pigmented epithelium – heavy [[melanin]]

1) Sphincter papillae muscle
	- [[Smooth muscle]] in circular bundle
	- [[Parasympathetic Nervous System|Parasympathetic]] innervation
	- Contraction ↓ pupillary diameter (miosis)
2) Dilator papillae muscle
	- [[Sympathetic Nervous System|Sympathetic]]
	- Contractions increase pupillary diameter
