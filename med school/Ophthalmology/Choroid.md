*Thin, pigmented sheet between [[Sclera]] and [[Retina]]*

- Posterior 2/3 of eye
- Contains [[melanocytes]] that make black layer, not allowing light into eye

#### Layers
1) Choroidocapillary Lamina
	- Vascular layer giving nutrients to [[Retina]]
	- Blood supply from short posterior ciliary aa
	- End at ora serrate
2) Brunch Membrane
	- Layer between choroid and retinal epithelium
	- Has collagen and elastic fibers between basal lamina
