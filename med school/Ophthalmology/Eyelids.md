*Protects [[Eyeball]] from injury and light, keeps cornea moist*

- Palpebral [[Conjunctiva]]: mucous membrane lining eyelid
- Tarsal glands: in palpebral tarsi producing lipid part of [[Tear Film]]
- Ciliary glands: eyelashes – make sebaceous part of [[Tear Film]]
- Medial and lateral palpebral commissures: junction of superior and inferior eyelids
