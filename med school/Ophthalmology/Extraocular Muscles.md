1) Levator palpebrae superioris
	- [[Ptosis]]: drooping of eyelid
	- [[Horner’s Syndrome]]
	- CN III

2) Rectus muscles (4)
	- Common tendinous ring: cuff around optic canal, origin of rectus muscles
	- Elevation, depression, adduction, abduction
	- Lateral rectus = CN VI, rest = CN III

3) Oblique muscles (2)
	- Inferior oblique: comes from anterior part of [[Eye|Orbit]]
	- Superior oblique: from apex (CN IV)

>[!Clinical] EOM palsy
>- Paralysis of muscle, seen by limited movement of eyeball
>- [[Oculomotor Nerve Palsy]]
>	- Most EOMs affected
>	- Superior eyelid droop
>	- Dilated, non-reactive pupil
>	- Abducted and depressed pupil
>- [[Abducent Nerve Palsy]]
>	- Pupil medial directed
>
