*Rods and cones*

- EXCEPTION: no receptors where [[optic nerve]] leaves eye
#### Structure
- Light absorbing photopigments in discs
- Inner and outer segments connected by cilium
- Inner segment = nucleus and outer = light-transducing apparatus

![[Screenshot 2025-10-28 at 8.17.16 PM.png]]

#### Rods
- Night vision, high sensitivity
- Photopigment to capture more light
- High amplification
- Saturate in daylight
- Low temporal resolution
- More sensitive to scattered light
- Low acuity, high convergent pathways
- No color, only one type
#### Cones
- Day vision, lower sensitivity
- Less photopigment
- Less amplification
- Faster response
- More sensitive to direct rays
- High acuity, low convergent pathways
- Color, 3 types
- *Fovea has no cones*
