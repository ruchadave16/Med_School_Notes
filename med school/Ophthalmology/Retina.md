#### Structure
- Outer pigmented epithelium
- Inner neural retina
	- Ends at ora serrata (junction of retina and ciliary body)
	
![[Screenshot 2025-10-28 at 3.44.36 PM.png]]

- Fovea centralis: depression in macula with only cones
	- Highest visual acuity region
- Macula lutea: yellow area in center of retina
	- Cone axons have carotenoids
	- Surrounds fovea
- Optic disc: Location of [[optic nerve]] leaving globe
	- No photoreceptors
	- “Blind spot”

#### Epithelium
Simple cuboid
- High pigmentation to absorb scattered light
- Blood-retina barrier
- Retinal regeneration
- [[Phagocytosis]]
