*New [[Blood Vessels|blood vessels]] made from existing vessels*

- Vascular Endothelial Growth Factors (VEGF) – promotes migration of endothelial cells 
	- Stimulates expression of Notch ligands
- Notch ligands bind to receptors on endothelial cells to regulate sprouting and branching of new vessels
- Platelet Derived Growth Factor (PDGF) – recruit smooth muscle cells
- ECM is scaffold for tissue growth
	- MMPs in ECM degrade parts of it to allow for extension of vascular tube
