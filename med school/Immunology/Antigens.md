#### T Cell Independent
- Trigger antibody response without [[T Lymphocytes|T cells]]
- TI-1: activate [[B Lymphocytes|B cells]] through membrane Ig (mIg) and B cell co-receptor
- TI-2: have repeating epitopes that crosslink to many Ig causing [[BCR]] clustering and [[B cell activation]]

#### T Cell Dependent
- Use [[T Lymphocytes|T cells]] (helper) to generate response
