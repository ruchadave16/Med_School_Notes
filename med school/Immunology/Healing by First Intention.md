*Thin injuries that don’t have a lot of tissue separation*

1) Clot formation (immediate)
2) Acute inflammation infiltrate – [[neutrophils]] (1 day)
3) Epithelial proliferation – (1-2 days)
4) Chronic inflammation infiltrate – [[Macrophage|macrophages]] (3 days)
5) [[Angiogenesis]] + granulation tissue (1 week)
6) [[Fibroblasts]] + collagen (2 weeks)
7) Scar over epithelium (1 month)

End result: **normal epithelial layer**
