#### Associations
- **HLA genes**
- non-MHC genes like PTPN22
- Infections
	- Molecular mimicry – microbes can express antigens that are similar to self-antigens, causing immune reactions against self
	- [[Rheumatic heart disease]] – antibodies against streptococcal proteins cross-react with myocardial proteins causing myocarditis
- UV Radiation
- Smoking
- Localized tissue injury
#### Features
- **More common in women**
- Chronic and progressive
