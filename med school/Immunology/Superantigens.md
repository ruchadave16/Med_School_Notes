[[Antigens]] that attract a LOT of [[Helper T Cells|helper T cells]]
- Can directly link [[MHC-II]] to T cell without being processed by [[Antigen Presenting Cells|APC]]
	- HUGE release of [[cytokines]] -- [[IL-2]] and [[IFN-y]]
	- Massive vasodilation and [[shock]]
### Symptoms
- Fever
- Rash
- Hypotension / shock
- Autoimmune
#### [[Toxic Shock Syndrome]]
- [[Staph aureus]] -- TSST-1
- [[Strep pyogenes]] -- Pyogenes exotoxin A or C
