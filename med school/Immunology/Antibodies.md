#### Structure
- 2 light chains, 2 heavy chains
- Variable region (antigen binding site) and constant region (1 for light, 3 for heavy)
	- **2nd constant region CH2: [[Complement System|complement]] fixed**
	- CH2 - CH3: macrophages, [[protein A]]
- Variable regions end in NH3 and constant in COOH
- Disulfide bridges to connect
- Variable region encoded by V gene, D gene, J gene
- Constant region encoded by C gene

#### Classes
Determined by Fc portion

![[Drawing 2026-01-21 11.12.28.excalidraw]]

- [[IgG]]: ‘G’eneric – Most common, found in all fluids, protects against infection 
- [[IgA]]: Secr’A’tory – high in mucous membranes, saliva, tears
- [[IgM]]: I’M’mediate – in [[blood]] and [[lymph]], first to fight infection
- [[IgE]]: All’E’rgy – allergic reactions
- [[IgD]]: ‘D’on’t worry about it – don’t know what this does
#### Functions
- [[Opsonization]]
	- Mark pathogens for phagocytosis
- Neutralization
	- Block adherence to structure
- [[Complement System|Complement]] activation – “Classical” pathway
#### Antibody Dependent Cellular Cytotoxicity
- Antibodies coat cell
- Destroyed by immune cells in non-phagocytic way
- [[Natural Killer Cells|Natural killer cell]]
	- IgG binds to infected cells
	- [[CD16]] on NK binds to Fc on IgG
- [[Eosinophils]]
	- IgE binds to pathogen
	- Eosinophils bind to Fc on IgE
