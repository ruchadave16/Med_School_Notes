*[[Lymph Nodes|lymph nodes]] of [[axilla]] region*

![[Pasted image 20251013000204.png]]

- Humeral (lateral)
	- Medial and posterior to axillary v. 
	- Drains [[Upper Extremity|upper extremity]]
- Pectoral (anterior)
	- Inferior border of pectoralis minor near lateral thoracic a. 
	- Drains anterior [[thoracic wall]], [[breast]], anterolateral abdominal wall above umbilicus
- Subscapular (posterior)
	- Near subscapular a./v., inferior margin behind axillary wall
	- Drains posterior [[Thoracic Wall|thoracic wall]] and [[scapula]] region
- Central LN
	- Near 2nd part of [[Axillary Artery|axillary a.]]
	- Drains pectoral, subscapular, and humeral LN
- Apical LN
	- Near 1st part of [[Axillary Artery|axillary a.]]
	- Drains central LN and nearby [[breast]] → subclavian trunk

>[!Info]
>Pectoral, subscapular, humeral LN → central LN → apical LN → subclavian trunk → jugular trunk + bronchiomediastinal trunk → R to R lymphatic duct, L to thoracic duct → [[venous angle]]
