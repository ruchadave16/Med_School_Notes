**2 Chains heterodimer: alpha and beta OR $\gamma$ and $\delta$**
- Surrounded by [[CD3]] complex (signaling into cell) and $\zeta$ homodimer
- Formed by process like antibody heavy chains – VDJC encoding to increase diversity
- Recognize antigen ONLY as foreign **peptide** bound to MHC

#### Structure
- Have Ig-like domains
- Each chain has 3 CDRs (hypervariable regions) for specificity
