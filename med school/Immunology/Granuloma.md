**[[Granuloma]]** = [[Macrophage|macrophages]], +/- lymphocytes, +/- tissue necrosis
- Macrophages are activated by IFN-y from [[Th1]] and gain cytoplasm and epithelial cells
- Can also fuse together into giant cells

![[Screenshot 2026-02-01 at 9.35.35 PM.png]]
#### Histology
- Rounded, well formed
- Aggregate of macrophages with cytoplasm = epithelial macrophages
- Lymphocytes on periphery
- Giant cells can help identify
- Can have [[caseous necrosis]] – caseating ([[tuberculosis]]) vs non-caseating ([[sarcoidosis]])
#### Foreign Body Granuloma
- Inert foreign body → no immune response – suture is most common cause
