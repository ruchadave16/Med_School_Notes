- 3 [[MHC-I]]: HLA-A, HLA-B, HLA-C
- 3 [[MHC-II]]: HLA-DR, HLA-DP, HLA-DQ

Located on **Chromosome 6**
- Determines tissue compatibility in [[transplantation]]
- **Polygeny**: individuals express different MHC genes/types → affects immune response strength
	- Different genotypes bind to different peptides based on AA sequence → activate different T-cell receptors
- **Polymorphism**: allelic variants that can change peptide specificity

>[!Info]
>This all ensures that pathogens cannot evade MHC and build immunity to it
