*Protein part of [[complement system]] – obsonization*

- Synthesized from C3, normally degraded unless it is stabilized
- Stable form ([[MAC]]) binds to **complement protein B**
	- Complement protein D clips B bound to C3b to form C3bBb (C3 convertase)
	- This C3 convertase can form more C3 into C3b
- By itself can act as [[obsonin]] for [[Macrophage|macrophages]] and draw them in
