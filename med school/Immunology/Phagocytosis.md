Method by which [[Macrophage|macrophages]] engulf pathogens and debris into themselves (phagosome)
- Phagosome merge with [[lysosome]]
- Lysosome breaks down through enzymes
	- [[ROS|ROS]]
	- reactive nitrogen intermediates
	- Protease
	- Nuclease 
	- [[Lysozymes]]
	
>[!Clinical]
> - Some pathogens can block this process
> - [[Tuberculosis]]
> - [[Chediak Higashi Syndrome]]
