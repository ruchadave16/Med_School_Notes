*Membrane bound [[IgM]] (and [[IgD]] receptor on [[B Lymphocytes|B cells]]*

- Signals through transmembrane signaling cascade: Ig$\alpha$ and Ig$\beta$
- Also uses co-receptor complex: CR2 ([[CD21]]), [[CD19]], [[CD81]]
- When antigen binds, BCRs can cluster
	- Triggers internalization and [[B Cell Activation|B cell activation]]
