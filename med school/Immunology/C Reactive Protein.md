*Acute phase reactant*

Made by [[liver]] in reaction to [[IL-6]] ([[Macrophage|macrophages]])
Binds to bacterial polysaccharides

- Activates classical pathway through [[C1 Protein|C1]] binding
	- Consumes C3, C4 and generate C3b
- Does not activate late pathway
	- Little consumption of C5-C9
