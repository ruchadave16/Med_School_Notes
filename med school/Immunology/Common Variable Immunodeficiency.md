### Etiology
- Unknown cause

### Pathophysiology
- Issue with B-cell differentiation, NOT maturation ← to differentiate from X-gamma globulinemia

### Clinical Features
- Upper GI and respiratory infections

### Diagnosis
- Labs
	- Low [[IgG]], [[IgA]], [[IgM]]
	- Normal B cell count
- Imaging
- Criteria

### Treatment
- Pharmacologic
	- Immune globulin treatment
- Surgical
- Lifestyle

### Complications
- Higher risk of autoimmune disease, granulomatous disease, malignancy

### High-Yield Associations
- **Presentation 20-40 yrs**
