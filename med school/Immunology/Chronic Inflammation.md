*Prolonged duration of inflammation due to persistent stimuli*

**Causes**
- Persistent infections
- [[Hypersensitivity]]
- Prolonged exposure to toxins

[[Macrophage|Macrophages]] dominate within **3-5 days**
#### Morphology
- Mononuclear cell infiltrates
	- **[[Macrophage|Macrophages]], lymphocytes, [[Plasma Cells]]**
- Tissue destruction
- Wound repair
	- Angiogenesis = proliferation of blood vessels
	- [[Fibrosis]]
