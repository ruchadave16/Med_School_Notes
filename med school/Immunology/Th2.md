 *Category of [[Helper T Cells|helper T cells]] that activates [[B Lymphocytes|B cells]]*
 
 - Can trigger [[Class Switching|class switching]]
- IFN-$\gamma$ inactivates Th2
- Activated by [[IL-4]]
- Releases: [[IL-4]], [[IL-5]], [[IL-10]], [[IL-13]]
	- Activates [[Eosinophils|eosinophils]] through IL-4 
- Important for **alternative pathway** of inflammation
