[[Type I Hypersensitivity|Type I hypersensitivity]] reaction
- Itching, diffused hives
- Bronchoconstriction → respiratory distress
- Hoarseness (laryngeal swelling)
- Vomiting, cramps, diarrhea
- Shock + death
#### Mediators
- ECF-A – [[Eosinophils|eosinophil]] chemotactic factor
#### Treatment
- [[Epinephrine]]
