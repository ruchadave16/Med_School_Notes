*Develop in waves during [[Embryonic Period|embryonic period]]*

- Migrate to GI, lungs, and reproductive tract
- ~5% of all T cells but MUCH faster response than $\alpha$ $\beta$ cells 
- Bind to self-MHC and MHC-like compounds such as [[CD1]]
- Recognize non-protein antigens
