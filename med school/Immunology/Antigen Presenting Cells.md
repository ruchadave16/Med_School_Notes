*[[Antigens]] must be brought to lymphocytes by antigen presenting cells*

These cells process + present antigens (peptide fragments)

**List**
- [[B Lymphocytes|B cells]]
- [[Macrophage|Macrophages]] 

Origin mainly connective tissue
#### Method
Facilitated by Major Histo-Compatibility locus molecules (MHC)

1) [[MHC-I]] – needs CD8 for interaction
	- receptor present on all nucleated cells
	- antigens processed internally by [[Proteosomes]]
2) [[MHC-II]] – needs CD4
	- **very few cells make these**
	- surface receptors only on antigen-presenting cells
	- present exogenous non-self peptides
