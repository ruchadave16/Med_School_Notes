*White blood cells that make [[Antibodies]] and help with immune memory*

Development in [[bone]] regulated by bone marrow stromal cells
- Self reactive cells eliminated in [[bone marrow]] through [[Apoptosis|apoptosis]]
#### Components
[[BCR]] receptor made of [[IgM]] antibodies – activates when binds to antigen
- When activated, makes more forms of this receptor in form of IgM antibodies
![[Drawing 2026-01-21 10.26.34.excalidraw]]

- Surface Proteins
	- CD40 – CD40L on T cells (induces [[Class Switching|class switching]])
		- Activates AID
	- [[MHC-II]]
	- B7 → CD28 on T cells
	- [[CD19]] – all B cells
	- CD20 – Most B cells, NOT plasma cells
	- C21 – complement

Round nucleus, Scant cytoplasm (Whole cell looks like nucleus)

#### VDJ Rearrangement 
 - Variable region encoded by V gene, D gene, J gene → many variations of each are present
 - As cell develops
	 - First 1 D and 1 J combined
	 - Then 1 V combined with DJ
	 - When mRNA made, pick one of many combinations of V, D, J
- Heavy chain variable region = Chromosome 14
	- Initially encodes IgM and IgD
	- Eventually this portion gets spliced out and rest are made ([[Class Switching|class switching]])
- Light chain variable region = V/J rearrangements

>[!Info] RAG Proteins
> RAG1 and RAG2 important for VDJ recombination 
> - Found in developing lymphocytes, deleting them can block development at this stage
