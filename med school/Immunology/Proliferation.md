1) Proliferation of differentiated cells
	- [[Growth factors]] from [[Macrophage|macrophages]]
	- **Depends on [[Extracellular Matrix|ECM]] integrity**
2) Development of mature cells from tissue [[stem cells]]
#### Signals
- Labile tissue – constantly growing like [[bone marrow]] and [[skin]] surface
	- Can exist as long as stem cell reserve exists
- Stabile tissue – in G0 stage generally but can proliferate to replace
	- [[Liver]], [[pancreas]], [[kidneys]]

>[!Note] Liver
>Can regenerate almost completely after loss

- Permanent tissue – terminally differentiated, injury = irreversible
	- [[Heart]], [[brain]], [[Muscle Tissue|muscles]]
	- Repair through scar formation
