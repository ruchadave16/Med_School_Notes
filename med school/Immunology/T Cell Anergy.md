#### Causes
- [[IL-2]] production deficiency → low proliferation
- Signal 1 without costimulation
- Inhibitor receptors signaling
	- CTLA-4 – upregulated on active TCRs and bind to B7 with more affinity than CD28
	- PDL-1 – binds [[Cytotoxic T Cells|CD8]] cells by binding to PD-1 receptor and turns off anti-tumor CD8 cells
		- Upregulated on surface of tumors

>[!Clinical] Checkpoint Inhibitor Therapy
>- Anti-PD-1 antibiotics let CD8 cells keep killing T cells

- Superantigen stimulation

**Reversible through [[IL-2]]**
