- Recognizes [[MHC-I]] (found on all but RBCs)
- Main goal: **Identify viral infected cells**

- Proliferation based on cytokines from [[Th1|Th1 cells]] – 
#### Function
- Secrete [[perforins]] and [[granzymes]] → kill target cell by [[apoptosis]]
- Insert granulysin
	- Kills bacteria
	- [[Apoptosis]]
- Makes Fas ligand
	- Binds to Fas (CD95) → extrinsic pathway of apoptosis
#### Steps
1) Ubiquitination of protein
2) Cut by proteasome
3) Transport to ER through TAP
4) Load onto MHC-I in ER
5) Express to surface
