Unique to **hematopoietic stem cell transplants**
- Treatment for hematological malignancies
- Bone marrow failure syndromes
- Inherited bone marrow diseases

Normally chemotherapy weakens immune system and transplanted HSC can grow
- In GVHD, transplanted HSC recognize alloantigens in host and attach host tissues
- Transplanted tissue attacks host
