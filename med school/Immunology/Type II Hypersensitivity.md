Antibodies against tissue antigens (IgG / IgM), bind to normal tissue
- Phagocytosis – Fc receptors or [[C3b]] on phagocytes
- Complement mediated lysis – IgM or IgG through classic pathway → formation of MAC
- Antibody based cytotoxicity – NK cells bind to Fc IgG
#### Examples
- [[Rheumatic Fever]]: Strep antibodies cross-react with cardiac myocytes
- Wrong [[Blood Typing|blood type]]: RBC lysis through IgG
	- [[Erythroblastosis fetalis]]
- Autoimmune hemolytic anemia
	- Methyldopa and [[penicillin]] bind to surface of RBCs
	- [[Mycoplasma pneumoniae]]: induces RBC antibodies
- [[Pemphigus Vulgaris]]: antibodies against [[desmosomes]] in epidermis
- [[Goodpasture Syndrome]]:
	- Type IV collagen antibodies
	- Nephritic syndrome + pulmonary hemorrhage
- [[Myasthenia Gravis]]
	- Antibodies against Ach receptors
