**Activated by** 
- [[Antigen Presenting Cells|Antigen presenting cells]]
	- [[Langerhans Cells|Dendritic cells]]
	- [[Macrophage|Macrophages]]
	- [[B Lymphocytes|B cells]]
- [[MHC-II]] (recognition)
- Require co-stimulation (green light) – B7 must bind to CD28 along with MCH-II → TCR and CD4
- Differentiates into categories based on cytokines released by APC


![[Screenshot 2026-01-24 at 11.40.03 PM.png]]

- Recognize presented antigens → release [[cytokines]]
#### Bonds Additional
- [[CD2]] → LFA3
- LFA1 (CD-18)→ ICAM-1, ICAM-2
- CTLA-4 = inhibitory that binds with B7 with more affinity than CD28, slows proliferation
	- PD-1 = co-inhibitor
- [[IL-2]] => promotes T cell proliferation and survival
#### Actions
- [[Th1]] cellular immune response → stimulate [[Macrophage|macrophages]] and [[Cytotoxic T Cells|cytotoxic T cells]]
	
>[!Note] IFN-$\gamma$ Deficiency
>- Severe [[salmonella]] and other myobacterial disease in childhood
>- IFN-$\gamma$ not affected, just don’t have receptors

	
>[!Example]
>- M. Tuberculosis
>	- Intracellular infection macrophages
>	- No antibodies
>	- Need [[Th1]]
>- [[Listeria Monocytogenes]]
>	- Intracellular organism

- [[Th2]] humoral immune response → stimulate [[B Lymphocytes|B cells]] → immunoglobin production
- [[Th17 Cells|Th17 cells]]
- Tfh cells – in [[germinal centers]] of lymphoid follicles
	- Helps B cells
	- Stimulated by IL-6, release [[IL-21]]
- [[Treg]] cells
