Use pattern recognition receptors (PRR) to identify pathogen associated molecular patterns (PAMP)
- PRR activation → activation of **Master Regulator** (NFkB) which is a transcription factor

Once they internalize pathogen as phagolysosome, destroy it
1) Oxygen-dependent = produce [[ROS]]
2) Oxygen-independent = [[lysozyme]] , [[lactoferrin]], [[defensins]]
3) Neutrophil Extracellular Traps
