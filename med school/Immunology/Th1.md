*Subset of [[Helper T Cells|helper T cells]] that stimulate [[Macrophage|macrophages]] and [[Cytotoxic T Cells|cytotoxic T cells]]*

- Activated by IFN-$\gamma$, [[IL-12]]
- Secrete IFN-$\gamma$ which activate [[Macrophage|macrophages]]
	- Macrophages also end up releasing [[IL-12]] which drives Th1 production
	- Important for **classic pathway** of inflammation through IFN-y
- Secrete [[IL-2]] → activates [[Cytotoxic T Cells]], also increases Th1
- Inhibited by [[IL-4]], [[IL-10]]
- Important for intracellular infection
- Important for [[granulomatous diseases]]
