*[[Gram negative]] rod*
- Oxidase positive
- Salt water, brackish water
- Risk for uncooked seafood
#### Mechanism
#### Symptoms
- Primary septicemia
	- Risk: Cirrhosis, Chronic liver disease, hereditary hemochromatosis
- Wound infections
	- Cellucitis, blisters, ulcers
- GI illness
#### Diagnosis
#### Treatment
- 3rd gen [[Cephalosporins|cephalosporin]] + [[doxycycline]] or [[ciprofloxacin]]
- Might need surgery / amputation
#### Prevention
