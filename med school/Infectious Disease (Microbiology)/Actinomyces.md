*Gram positive branching rod*
- Non acid-fast
- Anaerobic
- Slow growth on blood agar
- Common in mouth and female genital
- Yellow granules in tissues
#### Mechanism
#### Symptoms
- Endogenous infection after trauma, surgery, mucosal disruption
- Actinomycotic mycetoma

#### Treatment
