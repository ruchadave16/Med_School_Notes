#### Periplasm
- Periplasmic space between [[peptidoglycan]] and [[Bacterial Cell Membrane|bacterial cell membrane]]
- [[B-lactamase]] -> inactivates antibodies (hard to use antibiotics)
#### Outer Membrane
- Outer layer of [[LPS|lipopolysaccharide]] <- triggers immune system
- [[Lipid A]] -- virulence factor
- Porin channels that regulate antibiotic entry
