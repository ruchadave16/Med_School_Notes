- Dormant state of [[bacteria]] that can survive starvation
#### Exosporium
- Outermost layer
- "Keratin-like"
- Impermeable to chemicals, antibacterial
- **[[Virulence factor]] that helps with adhesion to host tissues**
#### Spore Coats
- Protein rich protective layers
#### Core Wall (Cortex)
- Innermost layer of [[Peptidoglycan|peptidoglycan]]
- Has **dipicolinic acid** combined with calcium
	- Large amounts found inside
	- Might help with heat resistance
#### Core
- bacterial DNA, ribosomes, cell membrane, peptidoglycan

>[!Info] Bacteria commonly [[gram positive]]
>- [[Bacillus anthracis]] -- central spore
>	- aerobic, gram (+) rod
>	- 
>- [[Bacillus cereus]]
>- [[Clostridium perfringens]]
>- [[Clostridium tetani]] -- terminal spore
>- [[Clostridium botulinum]]
>- [[C. Diff|Clostridium difficile]] -- anaerobic, gram (+) rod
>	- Causes pseudomembranous colitis, generally after antibiotic use
>- [[Coxiella burnetii]]
