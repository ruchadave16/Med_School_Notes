#### Early Phase
- During infection
- Recognize target cell, get taken in, and release genome to cell
#### Late Phase
- Genome replication -> assembly and release
#### Eclipse Period
- Time between infection and start of virus production
- Uncoated virus, not infectious
#### Latent Period
- Eclipse period + time till release of virus
