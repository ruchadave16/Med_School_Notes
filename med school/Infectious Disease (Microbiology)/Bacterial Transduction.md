Transfer of DNA through [[bacteriophage]] virus

**Generalized**
- Virus infects bacteria (during [[lytic cycle]])
- Randomly takes bacterial DNA (host DNA is fragmented)
- Moves to other bacteria

![[Screenshot 2026-02-17 at 12.10.49 AM.png]]

**Specialized**
- Viral DNA inserts into host DNA
- When bacteriophage DNA excised out, packaged into virus with specific host DNA
