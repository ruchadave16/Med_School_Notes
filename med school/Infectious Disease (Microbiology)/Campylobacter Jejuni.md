*[[Gram negative]] seagull shaped curved rod*
- Oxidase positive
- Grows at 42 C on Campy blood agar
#### Mechanism
- Cross contamination in food handling
#### Symptoms
- Watery diarrhea, bloody
- Fever
- Abdominal pain (RLQ)

- [[Guillain-Barre Syndrome]]
- [[IBS]]
- [[Reactive arthritis]]
- [[Hemolytic uremic syndrome]]
#### Diagnosis
#### Treatment
- Self limited
- Can give [[Azithromycin|azithromycin]] or [[Fluoroquinolones|fluoroquinolones]]
#### Prevention
