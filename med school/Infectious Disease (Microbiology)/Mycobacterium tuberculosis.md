*Slow growing non-spore intracellular aerobe*
- Acid fast staining
#### Mechanism
- Transmission = airborne, highly contagious
#### Symptoms
- Latent tuberculosis -- not contageous
	- Caseating [[Granuloma]]
- Active TB
	- Cough
	- low grade fever
	- blood in sputum
	- night sweats
	- ↓ appetite
	- weight loss
#### Diagnosis
- 6-8 weeks to grow on lab media
- [[Lowenstein-Jensen Agar]] -- selective

- Acid fast stain
- Fluorescent stain
- QunatiFERON Gold test / TB spot test
- PPD test
- TB PCR
