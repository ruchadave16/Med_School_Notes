*[[Gram negative]]*
- Found in water, soil, **hot springs**, hospital
- Survives extreme temperatures
- Aerobic
- Oxidase positive
- Non-lactose fermenting colonies
- Green pigment production

![[Screenshot 2026-02-17 at 9.06.55 PM.png]]
#### Mechanism
- Same as [[Corynebacterium Diphtheria|Diphtheria]] (ADP ribosylation stopping EF-2) so low protein synthesis
- Exotoxin A
- [[LPS]]
- Capsule / mucoid stains
#### Symptoms
- Hot tub folliculitis
- Cellulitis
- Burn wound infections **
- Malignant otitis externa 
- Ecthyma gangrenosum
#### Diagnosis
#### Treatment
- [[Ciprofloxacin]] (oral or IV)
• Other (all IV-formulation) [[Cefepime]], [[Ceftazidime]], [[Aztreonam]], [[Gentamicin]], [[Tobramycin]], [[Piperacillin]]/[[Tazobactam]]. [[Carbapenems]], except ertapenem and other newer antibiotics
