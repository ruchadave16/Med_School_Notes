*[[Gram positive]] cocci*
- CAMP test positive
- PYR negative
- Weak b-hemolysis on [[blood agar]]
- Hippurate hydrolysis test positive
- Common in female genital and GI tract
#### Mechanism
#### Symptoms
- Can cause [[neonatal sepsis]]
- Cause of asymptomatic bacteriuria, cystitis, pyelonephritis in [[Prenatal Period|pregnancy]]
#### Diagnosis
- Make sure to get urine culture in early pregnancy and vaginal swab in [[3rd trimester]]
#### Treatment
- [[Penicillins|Penicillin]] or [[ampicillin]]
#### Prevention
