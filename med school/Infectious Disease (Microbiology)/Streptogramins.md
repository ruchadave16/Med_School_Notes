#### Action
- Bind to 50S at same site as [[macrolides]] and [[clindamycin]]
#### Uses
- [[Gram positive]] cocci INCLUDING MRSA and VRE
	- Limited use since there are better tolerated alternatives like [[daptomycin]] and [[linezolid]]
#### Administration
- IV
#### Dosing
- Inhibits [[CYP3A4]]
#### Adverse Effects
- Pain locally 
- Arthalgias, myalgias
#### Contraindication
