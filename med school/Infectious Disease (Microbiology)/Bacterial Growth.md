1) Lag (Adaptation Phase)
	- Metabolically active, no division
	- Preparation
2) Growth (Log)
	- Exponential
	- Maximum, constant growth
	- Most susceptible to antibiotics here
3) Stationary
	- Rate of cell division = death
	- Nutrition depletes, waste accumulates
	- Endotoxin ([[Lipid A]]) released as gram (-) die
4) Death
	- ↓ viable cells
	- Toxic waste accumulates
		![[Screenshot 2026-02-13 at 7.59.02 PM.png]]
#### Generation Time
- Time to double during growth phase
- Depends on environmental factors

>[!Equation] $f = a(2^n)$ 
>- f = Final # bacteria
>- a = Initial # bacteria
>- n = # generations

>[!Info] Clinical Phases
>- Incubation Period = no signs or symptoms
>- Prodromal Period = bacteria multiplies, mild symptoms
>- Period of Illness = rapid division, pt is sick
>- Period of Decline = slowing down
