#### Action
- Reversible binding to 50S [[Bacterial Ribosome|bacterial ribosome]]
- Inhibits peptidyl transferase so no elongation
#### Uses
- Broad spectrum against (+) and (-)
#### Administration
- Oral, IV, topical
- Extensive distribution including CNS
- Conjugation with glucuronic acid in liver
#### Dosing

#### Adverse Effects
- GI: nausea, diarrhea, vomiting
- Dose-related reversible anemia because of mitochondrial protein synthesis prevention
- Grey baby syndrome because of low glucuronidation
- Inhibition of [[Cytochrome P450s|CYP]]
#### Contraindication
