- No catalase or SOD, often near mucosal surfaces
	- Common in gut and mouth flora
	- Found in abscesses
	- Aminoglycosides do NOT work
	- Fermentation -> CO2 and H2, short chain fatty acids
	- Ex: [[Actinomyces]], [[Bacteroides]], [[Clostridium Perfringens|Clostridium perfringens]]
#### Infections
- Abdominal abscesses
	- Gram (-) of GI tract
	- [[Bacteroides fragilis]] -- resistant to antibiotics
	- Treatment: [[Metronidazole]] and gram (-) agent
- Aspiration pneumonia
	- Mouth anaerobes in lungs
	- [[Peptostreptococcus]], [[Fusobacterium]], [[Prevotella]]
	- Treatment: [[Clindamycin]]
