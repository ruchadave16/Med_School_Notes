#### Categories
**[[Gram Positive]] vs [[Gram Negative]]**

- Both have inner phospholipid bilayer
- [[Gram Positive]] has thick [[peptidoglycan]] cell wall while - has thin
	- [[Gram Negative ]]has periplasmic space between peptidoglycan and cell membrane
	- Contains [[LPS]] (for endotoxic shock), outer membrane proteins, more porins, and a protein secretion method

**Shape**
- Coccus (sphere)
	- Cocci (1 sphere)
	- Diplococci (2 spheres)
		- Gram positive = [[streptococcus pneumonia]] / pneumococcus
		- Gram negative = meningococcus
	- [[Streptococci]] (chain)
	- Staphylococci (bunch)
	- Most are [[Gram Positive|gram positive]] (exception = [[Neisseria Gonorrhoea]], [[Moraxella catarrhalis]])
- Rod (bacilli)
	- Most are [[Gram Negative|gram negative]]
	- Exception
		- Gram positive in Chinese letter / angular arrangement: [[Corynebacterium diphtheria]])
		- [[Clostridium tetani]]
		- [[Listeria Monocytogenes]]
		- [[Bacillus Anthracis|Bacillus anthrax]] / [[bacillus cereus]]
- Coccobacillus
	- Most are [[Gram Negative|gram negative]] = moraxella, [[brucella]]
	- Weakly gram positive = hemophilus
- Seagull shaped = campylobacter
- Other
	- Branching / Filamentous like fungi
		- [[Actinomyces]]
		- [[Nocardia]]
	- Spirochetes
		- [[Treponema]] ([[syphilis]])
		- [[Borrelia Burgdorferi]] ([[Lyme disease]])
		- [[Leptospira]] ([[leptospirosis]])
	- Vibrio
		- [[Vibrio cholera]]
	- Pleomorphic
		- Can convert to anything 
		- [[Rickettsia]]
		- [[Chlamydia]]
