#### Categories
**Gram Positive vs Negative**
- Both have inner phospholipid bilayer
- Gram positive has thick [[peptidoglycan]] cell wall while - has thin
	- Negative has periplasmic space between peptidoglycan and cell membrane
	- Contains [[LPS]] (for endotoxic shock), outer membrane proteins, more porins, and a protein secretion method
- Gram positive also have teichoic acid and lipoteichoic acid
