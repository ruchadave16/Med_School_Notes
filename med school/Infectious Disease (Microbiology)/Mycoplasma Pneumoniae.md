- No cell wall so poor gram staining
	- Sterols in cell membrane for stability
- [[Eaton's Agar]] for growing -- needs cholesterol
	- "Fried egg colonies"
- Smallest bacteria
#### Mechanism
- Transmission through respiratory droplets
- No invasion of epithelium -- it inhibits ciliary movement
	- Production of cold agglutinins -- [[IgM]] autoantibodies cause [[hemolytic anemia]]
#### Presentation
- Walking pneumonia
- Erythema multiforme (target lesions)
#### Diagnosis
- Serology (antibody testing)
- PCR (bacterial DNA)
- Cold agglutinins (IM antibodies)
#### Treatment
- [[Azithromycin]]
