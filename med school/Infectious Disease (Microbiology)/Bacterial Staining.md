>[!Warning]
>[[Treponema pallidum]]-- too thin to see so use dark ground illumination microscopy
>[[Mycoplasma pneumoniae]] -- no cell wall so use PCR
>[[Legionella pneumophilia]] -- no uptake of red counterstain because of LPS -- can use silver stain but not routine
>[[Chlamydia]], [[Rickettsia]], [[Coxiella burnetii]] -- obligate intracellular so use inclusion body (Chlamydia) or Giemsa stain (Rickettsia)
