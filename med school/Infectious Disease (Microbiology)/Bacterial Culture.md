### Culture Media
*Provides nutrients to *
- Non-selective
	- General, grows many
	- Nutrient or blood agar
- Selective
	- Has toxic substances
	- [[Hemophilus Influenza|H. influenza]] = chocolate agar -- Factor V and Factor X
	- [[Thayer-Martin media]] = only [[Neisseria meningitidis]] and [[Neisseria Gonorrhoea]]
	- [[Bordetella Pertussis|B. pertussis]] = [[Bordet-Gengou Agar]], Regan-Lowe medium
	- [[Corynebacterium Diphtheria|C. diphtheria]] = [[Loeffler's Media|Tellurite plate]]
	- [[Mycoplasma Pneumoniae|M. pneumonia]] = [[Eaton's Agar]]
	- [[M. tuberculosis]] = [[Lowenstein-Jensen Agar]], Middlebrook media, 
	- Lactose fermenting = [[MacConkey's Agar]]
	- [[E. coli]] = [[EMB Agar]]
	- [[Brucella]], [[Francisella]], [[Legionella Pneumophilia]], [[Pasteurella]] = [[Buffered Charcoal Yeast Extract]]
	- [[Fungi]] = [[Sabouraud's Agar]]
	
- Enriched
	- Special nutrients so many bugs grow
	- [[Blood agar]] and chocolate agar
- Differential
	- Different bugs grow with different patterns
	- Blood agar -- alpha, beta hemolysis
### Colonies
- [[Bacillus anthracis]] = large, irregular medusa head colonies
- Proteus = swarming colonies on blood agar
- [[Clostridium prefringens]] = double zone hemolysis on blood agar
- [[Eikenella corrodans]] = pitting colonies on blood agar
- Smooth golden yellow = [[Staph aureus]]
- Streptococcus = small, beta hemolytic, alfa hemolytic, or no hemolytic on blood agar
- Mycoplasma = fried egg colonies on Eaton's agar

**Smell**
- Pseudomonas = fruity
- E. coli = fishy
- Eikenella = bleach

**Pigment**
- [[Pseudomonas aeruginosa]] -- Metallic shine on MacConkey's (blue-green)
- [[Serratia]] -- reddish pigment

**Mucoid**
- Suggests capsule production
- Klebsiella = big mucoid pink lactose fermenting on McConkey's agar
- [[E. coli]] = small non-mucoid pink lactose fermenting on McConkey's

### Categories
#### Routine Bacterial Culture
- 35-37 C
- Incubation time = 12-24 hrs

>[!Note] 
>[[Streptococcus Pneumonia|Pneumococcus]] = 10% CO2
>Campylobacter = Microaerophilic, 42 C
>Yersinia = colder 24-25 C
#### Mycobacterial Culture
- Needs to be specially requested
- [[Mycobacterium tuberculosis]] in negative pressure since **highly contagious**
- Lowenstein Jensen media has malachite green to stop other organism growth
	- Rapid growers = < 7 days
		- Mycobacterium chelonae
		- M. abscessus
		- M. fortuitum
	- Slow growers = 2-3 weeks
		- M. avium complex
		- M. kansasii
		- M. scrofulaceum
	- TB takes 6-8 weeks
