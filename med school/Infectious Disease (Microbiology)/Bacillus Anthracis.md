*[[Gram positive]] rod found in chains*

- Spore forming
- Forms b-hemolytic colonies on [[blood agar]]
- Capsule of D-glutamic acid
#### Mechanism
- Together, 3 toxins cause toxicity
	- Protective antigen (PA)
	- Lethal factor (LF)
	- Edema factor (EF)
- Edema toxin = EF + PA
- Mimics adenylate cyclase
	- Acts on skin, lungs, GI
#### Symptoms
- Lungs -- necrotizing pneumonia
	- Inhalation of spores
	- Hemorrhagic pleural effusion
	- Mediastinal lymphadenopathy -- widening of mediastinum
- GI -- ulcers

Cutaneous anthrax
- Skin lesions with edematous borders
- Painless

#### Treatment
- [[Ciprofloxacin]], [[doxycycline]], [[Penicillins|Penicillin]]
