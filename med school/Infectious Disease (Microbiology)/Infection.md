*Replication of microorganisms inside of host *
- Can cause immune response
**Subclinical Infection**
- No symptoms so no disease
- Transmissible
- Can be asymptomatic carriers
- Can see antibody titer increase

**Latent Infection**
- Dormant for years
- Cells do not actively multiply
- Commonly seen in [[TB]]
### Protection
- Physical
	- Skin
	- Mucous membranes
	- Mechanical clearance
- Chemical
	- ↓ pH in gastric, vaginal
	- Antimicrobial peptides -- [[defensins]], [[lysozymes]]
	- [[Bile salts]]
- Biological
	- Microbiome
	- [[IgA]] in secretions
