*Check if bacteria has cytochrome c oxidase (enzyme in [[Oxidative Phosphorylation|ETC]])*

- (+) when enzyme oxidizes reagent to purple-blue compound
	- Aerobic bacteria
	- Gram negative cocci: [[Neisseria Gonorrhoea]]
	- Gram positive rods: [[Pseudomonas]], [[Vibrio Cholera]], [[Campylobacter Jejuni]], [[Aeromonas]], [[Hemophilus]], [[Pasteurella]], [[Francisella]], [[Brucella]]
		- Campylobacter grows at 42 C, Vibrio does NOT
		- Vibrio grows on alkaline media
	- **Hacek group** causing [[endocarditis]]: [[Hemophilus]], [[Aggregatibacter]], [[Cardiobacterium]], [[Eikenella]], [[Kingella]]
- (-) does NOT mean only anaerobic, can be facultative
