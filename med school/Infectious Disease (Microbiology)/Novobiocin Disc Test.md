*Test to differentiate [[S. saprophyticus]] from [[Staph Epidermidis|S. epidermidis]]*
- Plate bacteria on agar with Novobiocin disc
- Measure clearance
	- [[Staph saprophyticus]] resistant (growth at edge of disc)
	- [[Staph epidermidis]] sensitive (large clearance)
