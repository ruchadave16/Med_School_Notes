*When [[bacteria]] penetrate tissues beyond colonization in [[infection]]*

- Breakdown of barriers
	- Hyaluronidase (streptococcus and staphylococcus) and collagenase ([[Clostridium Perfringens|clostridium perfringens]]) break down ECM
	- Streptokinase from [[Strep Pyogenes|strep pyogenes]] converts plasminogen -> [[plasmin]]
- Intracellular entry
	- **[[M cells]]** = antigen samplers + portals
		- Specialized epithelial cells in [[Peyer's Patches|Peyer patches]] and [[GALT]]
		- "Gatekeepers" that transport microbes into lymphoid
- Cytoskeleton manipulation
	- Actin rearrangements
	- [[Salmonella]]
