*[[Gram negative]] rod*
- Non-lactose fermenting colonies
- Produces H2S -- black colonies on TSI agar
#### Symptoms
- Salmonella typhi
	- [[Typhoid]] fever
	- [[Osteomyelitis]] in patients with [[sickle cell disease]]
- Non-typhoid
	- Gastroenteritis
	- Uncooked food transmission
#### Diagnosis
#### Treatment
- Salmonella typhi
	- [[Fluoroquinolones]] like ciprofloxacin
