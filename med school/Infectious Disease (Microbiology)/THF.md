Biologically active form of [[Vitamin B9|folic acid]]
- DHFR catalyzes conversion of DHF -> THF
- DHFR found in both prokaryotic and eukaryotic cells

*[[Sulfonamides]] block this by inhibiting upstream* in just bacteria
- DHFR Inhibitors block lower and affect both types of cells:
	- [[Trimethoprim]]
	- [[Pyrimethamine]]
