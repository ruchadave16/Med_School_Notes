*[[Gram positive]] part of [[Group D streptococcus]]*
- Found in pairs and short chains
- A hemolytic or not hemolytic
- **black colonies** on [[Bile Esculin Agar|bile esculin agar]]
- PYR positive
- tolerant to **6.5% NaCl**
#### Mechanism
- E. faecalis
- E. faecium (more penicillin resistant)
#### Symptoms
- UTI
- Bacteremia
- Intraabdominal sepsis
- Hospital acquired infection
