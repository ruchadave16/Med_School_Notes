*[[Gram negative]] small*
- Encapsulated
- Aerobe
- Oxidase positive
- Dew drop colonies on [[Bordet-Gengou Agar]]
#### Mechanism
- Pertussis toxin: Inhibition of Gi proteins through ADP ribosylation
	- Overactivation of adenylate cyclase so ↑ cAMP in [[neutrophils]]
	- Problematic recruitment of neutrophils
- FHA on pili to bind to respiratory cilated epithelium
	- Tracheal cytotoxin causes ↑ mucus secretion
- Intracellular survival
#### Symptoms
- Mucus hypersecretion
- Whooping cough

#### Treatment
- [[Macrolides]]
