#### Mechanism
- Oncogenic strains (16, 18)
- Direct contact -- sexual
#### Symptoms
- Common papilloma -- warts
- Condyloma acuminata
- Cervix carcinoma
	- Can cause oropharyngeal lesions
#### Diagnosis
- Koilocyte detection -- squamous epithelial cells
- Biopsy of lesions
- PCR along with cytology
#### Treatment
- Liquid nitrogen for skin lesions
- Podophyllin for genital lesions
- Salicylic acid for plantar lesions
#### Prevention
