*[[Gram negative]] enteric rod that is facultative anaerobic, short and stumpy*
- Capsulated
- Mucoid, lactose fermenting pink colonies on [[MacConkey's Agar]]
- Oxidase negative
- Indole negative
- String test positive
- **Opportunistic pathogen**

![[Screenshot 2026-02-17 at 8.49.39 PM.png]]
#### Mechanism
- Some strains are resistant to [[Carbapenems|carbapenem]] through carbapenemase enzymes
#### Symptoms
- Pneumonia in nursing homes
	- Bloody, thick sputum
- UTIs, intraabdominal infection
- Wound infection
- Sepsis
