- 30S and 50S subunits
- 16S rRNA sequence is highly conserved
- Translation happens in rRNA
- mRNA binds to 30S, start codon = AUG
- Elongation factors facilitate lengthening of growing peptide
### Inhibitors
- 50S Inhibitors
	- [[Macrolides]]
	- [[Lincosamides]]
	- [[Streptogramins]]
	- [[Chloramphenicol]]
	- [[Oxazolidinones]]
	- [[Pleuromutilins]]
- 30S Inhibitors
	- [[Tetracyclines]]
	- [[Aminoglycosides]] (bactericidal)

>[!Clinical]
>- [[Diphtheria]] inhibit EF-2 in eukaryotic ribosome stopping protein synthesis
>- [[Shiga toxins]] inactivate 28S rRNA in 60S
>- [[Tetracyclides]] bind to 30S subunit
>- [[Aminoglycosides]] interfere with 30S protein synthesis
