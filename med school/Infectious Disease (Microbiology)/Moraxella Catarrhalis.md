*[[Gram negative]] oxidase positive diplococci*
- Common in upper respiratory tract
- Forms biofilms
#### Mechanism
- Make [[B-Lactamase|b-lactamase]] so resistant to common ampicillin
#### Symptoms
- Leading cause of acute otitis media
- Sinusitis and rhinosinusitis in kids
- COPD exacerbation in adults
#### Diagnosis
#### Treatment
- [[Amoxicillin]]-clavulanate
- [[Cephalosporins]] (2nd or 3rd gen)
- [[Macrolides]], [[Fluoroquinolones|fluoroquinolones]], [[tetracyclins]]

#### Prevention
