#### Action
- Semisynthetic derivative of [[lincomycin]]
- Binds to 50S [[bacterial ribosome]], blocking peptide translocation
#### Uses
- [[Gram positive]] aerobes: [[Streptococci]], staphylococci, pneumococci
- **Anaerobes** 
- Alternative to [[b-Lactams]] in allergic patients

>[!Warning] Resistance
>- Mutation of binding site
>- Ribosomal methylation (MLS Type B)
>- Enzyme inactivation

#### Adverse Effects
- GI: nausea, diarrhea
	- HIGH [[C. diff]] infection risk
- Hypersensitivity -- rash
- Hepatic impact
- Neutropenia (rare)
