*[[Gram positive]] anaerobic rods*
#### Mechanism
- TcdA (Enterotoxin)
	- Brush border mucosa binding
	- Glucosylates Rho GTPase to cause fluid secretion and mucosal damage
- TcdB (Cytotoxin)
	- Actin filament breakdown
	- Glucosylates Rho GTPase to cause apoptosis
- Both bind to colon epithelial cells

Common after [[Fluoroquinolones|fluoroquinolones]] and [[Clindamycin|clindamycin]]
#### Symptoms
- C difficile colitis (Pseudomembranous colitis)
- Inflammation
- [[Toxic megacolon]] <- **fatal**
- Colonic perforation
#### Diagnosis
- Stool PCR
- Toxin detection assays
#### Treatment
- Oral [[vancomycin]], [[fidaxomicin]] <- this is actually first line but exam will ask vancomycin
	- Fecal microbiota transplantation
	- Monoclonal antibody for Toxin B to prevent recurrence
#### Prevention
