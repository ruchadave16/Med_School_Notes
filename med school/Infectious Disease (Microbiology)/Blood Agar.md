Made of mammalian blood, 5% sheep 
- Non-selective
- Enriched (blood)
- Differential (hemolytic patterns)

![[Screenshot 2026-02-12 at 11.47.06 PM.png]]

- Used to differentiate species of [[Streptococci|streptococcus]]
	- Alfa -- Partial hemolysis (greenish zone around colonies)
		- [[Streptococcus Pneumonia|Pneumococcus]]
		- [[S. viridans]]
	- Beta -- Complete hemolysis (clear around colonies)
		- [[Strep Pyogenes|Group A streptococcus]]
		- [[Bacillus anthracis]]
		- [[Staph aureus]]
		- [[Listeria Monocytogenes]]
		- [[Group B streptococcus]]
	- Gamma -- No hemolysis
		- [[Staph Epidermidis|Staphylococcus epidermidis]]
		- [[Staphylococcus saprophyticus]]
- Special feature of pseudonomas
	- Beta-hemolytic
	- Greenish-metallic colonies
- [[Staph aureus]]
	- Beta hemolytic
### Chocolate Agar
- Variation of blood agar
- [[Erythrocytes|RBCs]] that are lysed by heating
- Have NAD (Factor V) and hemin (Factor X)
	- NAD from inside RBCs
- [[Hemophilus Influenza|H. influenza]] will grow
- Classic scenario
	- Bacteria won't grow on blood agar unless S. aureus exist
