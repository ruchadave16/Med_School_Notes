*[[Gram negative]] comma shaped*
- Oxidase positive
- Found in marine water, contaminated seafood
- Grows on TCBS agar
- Highly mobile -- darting on wet mount
- Needs alkaline pH to grow
#### Mechanism
- Binds to GM1 ganglioside
- Permanently activates Gs -> ↑ cAMP in intestinal cells
	- ↑ loss of electrolytes and water
- Toxin found in [[Bacteriophages|bacteriophage]]
#### Symptoms
- Rice water diarrhea
- Vomiting
- [[Cholera]]

**Death**
- Dehydration
- Electrolyte loss
- Shock
#### Diagnosis
#### Treatment
- Aggressive volume replacement
- [[doxycycline]] or [[azithromycin]]
#### Prevention
- Common in places without clean water
