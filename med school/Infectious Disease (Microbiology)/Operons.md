*Genes ordered in clusters transcribed as **one mRNA***
### Lac Operon
- Lactose metabolism when glucose is scarce
	- Lac Z: b-galactosidase
	- Lac Y: lactose permease
	- Lac A: transacetylase
- Repressor: blocks transcription in absence of lactose
- Catabolite repression: glucose presence suppresses enzymes for alternative methods
