- Differentiates parts of bacteria, Gram + vs -
	- Pus can appear pink
	- Fungal can appear purple

>[!Warning] Gram + bacteria from old cultures can look - because damaged cell walls
#### Procedure
- Positive = purple, negative = pink
	- 
	![[Screenshot 2026-02-11 at 10.12.13 PM.png]]
- Fixation -> Crystal violet -> iodine -> decolorization -> safranin (counterstain)

	- ![[Screenshot 2026-02-11 at 10.14.34 PM.png]]
	
>[!Warning]
>- [[Treponema]] (syphilis) -- too thin to see
>- [[Mycobacterium tuberculosis]] -- mycolic acids in cell wall
>- [[Mycoplasma]] -- no cell wall
>- Intracellular
>	- [[Rickettsia]] 
>	- [[Chlamydia]] 
>	- [[Legionella Pneumophilia]]
