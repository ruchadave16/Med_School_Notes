Gram positive has thick [[peptidoglycan]] cell wall
- Easily accessible by [[antibiotics]]
- No outer membrane

Unique feature: LTA (lipo**teichoic acid**) -- [[Antigens|antigen]] for immune rxn
- Has induced arthritis, uveitis, meningial inflammation, cascades -> septic shock
- [[Cytokines|Cytokine]] release induced
- Binds to [[antibodies]] -> [[Complement System|complement]] activation
