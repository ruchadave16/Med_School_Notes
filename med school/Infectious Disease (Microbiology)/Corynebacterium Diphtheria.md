*[[Gram positive]] rod with club shaped appearance*
- Areobic
- Non-spore
- Have volutin granules at poles for energy store
- **Black colonies** on [[Loeffler's Media|tellurite plate]]
#### Mechanism
- Toxin: Inactivates EF-2 (elongation factor) stopping protein synthesis
	- Transfers ADP-ribose from NAD -> EF-2
	- Found in [[Bacteriophages|bacteriophage]]

![[Screenshot 2026-02-17 at 2.08.05 PM.png]]
#### Symptoms
- Sore throat with membranous, swollen nodes
	- "Bull's neck"
- Diphtheria

- Cardiotoxicity
- Neurotoxicity
- Pseudomembrane formation
#### Diagnosis
#### Treatment
- [[Erythromycin]], [[penicillin]] + antitoxin
#### Prevention
- DPT vaccine
