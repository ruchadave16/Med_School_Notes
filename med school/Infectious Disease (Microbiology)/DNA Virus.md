#### Characteristics
- Replication in nucleus generally
- DNA replicated by DNA polymerase
- Mainly icosahedron shape
- Mainly linear DNA
- **Latency**
#### Families (Largest to Smallest)
- Poxvirus -- [[Variola Virus|Variola]], vaccinia, monkeypox, canarypox, [[Molluscum Contagiosum Virus|molluscum contagiosum]]
	- ds, linear
	- brick shaped, enveloped
	- DNA polymerase
	- **EX: replication in cytoplasm**
- Herpes virus -- [[HSV1]], [[HSV2]], [[HHV3]], [[EBV]], [[Cytomegalovirus|CMV]], [[HHV6]], 7, [[HHV8]]
	- ds, linear
	- icosadeltahedral, enveloped
		- tegment between nucleocapsid and envelope
		- envelope from host membrane
	- Own DNA polymerase
- Adenovirus -- [[adenovirus]]
	- ds, linear
	- icosadeltahedral
	- DNA polymerase
- Hepadnavirus -- [[hepatitis B virus]]
	- ds, circular
	- spherical, enveloped
	- DNA polymerase
- Papillomavirus -- papilloma virus ([[HPV]])
	- ds, circular
	- icosadeltahedral
- Polyomavirus -- [[JC virus]], [[BK virus]], SV40
	- ds, circular
	- icosadeltahedral
- Parvovirus -- [[parvovirus B19]], adeno-associated virus
	- ss, linear
	- icosahedral
	
**Latency** -- persistent infection
- Herpes (sensory nerve ganglion)
- Hepatitis B (hepatocyte)
- EBV (B lymphocytes)
