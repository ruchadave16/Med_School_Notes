*[[Gram positive]] rod*
- NON spore forming
- Facultative anaerobe
- Found in soil, animals, plants, female genital tract
- Grows in cold temp
- Tumbling motility at 25 C but NOT in host
#### Mechanism
- [[Actin]] mediated intracellular motility for spreading
- Transmission through unpasteurized dairy
- [[Complement System|Complement]] based phagocytosis (C3b)
#### Symptoms
**High risk**
- Immunocompromised
- Pregnant
- Infants

- Can cause meninigitis
- Gastroenteritis
#### Diagnosis
#### Treatment
- Ampicillin
- [[Trimethoprim]]-sulfamethoxazole
#### Prevention
