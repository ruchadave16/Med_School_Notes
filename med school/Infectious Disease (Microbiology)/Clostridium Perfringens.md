*[[Gram positive]] rod, short thick*
- [[Endospores|Endospore]] forming
- **[[Obligate Anaerobes|obligate anaerobe]]**
- Double zone hemolysis on [[blood agar]] (alpha and theta)
#### Mechanism
- **Alpha toxin**
- [[Phospholipase C]]enzyme  -- degrades phosphatidylcholine and sphingomyelin
	- Infection generally after injury (deep or crushing)
#### Symptoms
- Muscle breakdown (myonecrosis)
	- ↓ muscle blood flow
	- Foul gas production
	- Subcutaneous gas -- crepitus upon palpation
- Occlusive plugs: platelets, leukocytes, fibrin
- Low O2 environment -> ↑ bacteria
- Can cause food poisoning
	- Reheated chinese food
