*[[Gram negative]] diplococci*
- Intracelluar
- Non-capsulated
- Cultured on [[Thayer-Martin Media]]
#### Mechanism
- Phage and antigenic variation of Type IV pilin protein
	- Resistance to killing by serum antibody
#### Symptoms
- Gonorrhoea
- Gonococcal disease
- Septic arthritis
- Neonatal conjectivitis
#### Diagnosis
- PCR testing
#### Treatment
- [[Ceftriaxone]]
