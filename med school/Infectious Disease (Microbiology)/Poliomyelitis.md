#### Definition
Infectious disease due to [[poliovirus]]
#### Etiology

#### Pathophysiology
- Virus replicates in and destroys motor nerve fibers in [[Spinal Cord|spinal cord]] or [[brainstem]]
- Results in [[Wallerian degeneration]] of nerves and atrophy of affected muscle
	- During recovery phase, remaining nerve fibers will send terminal axon sprouts to reinnervate other muscle fibers → loss of motor units with remaining units innervating more fibers than originally
	- Larger than normal units called macromotor units → loss of fine montor control, but this allows muscle strength regain and paralysis recovery

#### Clinical Features
- No sensory defects
- Motor weakness + paralysis
