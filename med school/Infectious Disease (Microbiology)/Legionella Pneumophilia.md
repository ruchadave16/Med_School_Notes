*Weak gram staining rods*
- Grow on [[Buffered Charcoal Yeast Extract|BCYE]]
#### Mechanism
- Transmission through inhalation of contaminated water
- Survives in [[Macrophage|macrophages]]
#### Symptoms
- Atypical pneumonia
- Pontiac fever
- Legionnaires' disease
#### Diagnosis
- Urine antigen testing for serogroup 1
#### Treatment
- [[Azithromycin]]
#### Prevention
