*[[Gram negative]] diplococci*
- Grows on chocolate media
- Oxidase positive
- Maltose fermenter
#### Mechanism
- Thick polysaccharide capsules
- [[Lipooligosaccharide|LOS]] as endotoxin
#### Symptoms
- Bacterial meningitis
- Waterhouse-Friedrichsen syndrome
	- septic shock and adrenal failure
#### Diagnosis
#### Treatment
- [[Ceftriaxone]]
- Penicillin
#### Prevention
