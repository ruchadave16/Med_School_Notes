#### Action
- Selectively binds bacterial DHFR and stops conversion of DHF -> [[THF]]
- Low affinity to animal DHFR

*Resistance*
- ↓ drug permeability
- ↑ production of DHFR
- Altered DHFR with low affinity 
#### Uses
- Combined with sulfamethoxazole
- UTIs
- PCP prophylaxis
- [[Nocardia]] infections
- MRSA skin infections
