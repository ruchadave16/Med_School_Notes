#### Mechanism
- STD
- Birth canal to child

- Entry through mucosal surfaces and replicate in epithelial cells
- Spread to sensory nerve endings -- retrograde to sacral dorsal root
- Latency in sensory neurons
- Reactivation due to stress, trauma, immune issues
	- Anterograde back to skin
#### Symptoms
- Herpes genitalis
- Aseptic meningitis
- Neonatal infection
#### Diagnosis
- Distinguish from [[HSV1]] by monoclonal antibody against **glycoprotein G**
	- HSV1 = gG-1
	- HSV2 = gG-2
- PCR
#### Treatment
- [[Acyclovir]]
#### Prevention
- No vaccine
- Protection during sex
- C-section during birth if mother has active lesions
