*[[Gram positive]] cocci in clusters*
	- Coagulase positve
	- Catalase positive
	- Grows in [[Mannitol Salt Agar|mannitol salt agar]] (ferments mannitol)
#### Mechanism
- [[Protein A]]
- Enterotoxin
- Hyaluronidase
- Coagulase
- Staphylokinase
- **TSS-1 -> [[toxic shock syndrome]]**
#### Symptoms
- Skin infections, soft tissue
- Serious: [[septic shock]], endocarditis, osteomyelitis, septic arthritis, pneumonia, [[toxic shock syndrome]] 
- Food poisoning -- enterotoxin
#### Diagnosis
#### Treatment
- Check if [[B-Lactamase]] being produced or not
- Methicillin resistant (MRSA)
	- [[Cephalosporins]] / [[Penicillins]]
	- Oral: [[Doxycycline]], [[Clindamycin|clindamycin]], [[linezolid]]
	- IV: [[Vancomycin]], [[daptomycin]], [[ceftaroline]], [[dalbavancin]]
#### Prevention
