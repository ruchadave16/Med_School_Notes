- Selective for [[Gram Negative|gram negative]] bacteria
	- Inhibits gram + (Made of crystal violet and bile salt)
- Differential for lactose fermenters
	- Produces acid and turns agar pink
	- Non-lactose fermenters = colorless
		- [[Citrobacter]] and [[Serratia]] appear like this initially since its slow growing
- pH indicator = neutral red
