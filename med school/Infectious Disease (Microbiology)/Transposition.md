Transposons = DNA segments that are in bacterial DNA that can be removed and reintegrated
- Mechanism of transfer for antibiotic resistance genes
### Types
- Simple -- has inverted terminal repeats, only encodes transposase
- Composite -- 2 IS elements around other genes
### Others
- Gene Cassettes
	- Small mobile elements with 1+ gene recombination sites
	- No movement machinery, depend on integrons
- Integrons
	- Integrase + recombination site + promoter
