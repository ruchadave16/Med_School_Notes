### Mechanism
- Bind to 50S [[bacterial ribosome]] by blocking acetylamino translocation -- no elongation

![[Screenshot 2026-02-16 at 10.47.01 PM.png]]
### Resistance
- ↓ Drug accumulation
	- Efflux pumps
	- ↓ cell permeability
- Enzymatic inactivation
- Modification of target site -- MLS Type B resistance

### Drugs
- [[Erythromycin]]
- [[Clarithromycin]]
- [[Azithromycin]] -- no CYP3A4 inhibition***
- Fidaxomicin -- non-systematically absorbed
	- Use for [[C. diff colitis]]
	- Inhibits RNA polymerase (highly selective for [[C. Diff]])
