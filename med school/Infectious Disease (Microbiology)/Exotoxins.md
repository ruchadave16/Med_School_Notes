*Proteins secreted by [[bacteria]]*

- 2 component A-B polypeptide
	- A = toxic
	- B binds to cell surface
### Effects
- ↓ protein synthesis
	- [[Corynebacterium diphtheria]] -- ADP ribosylation of proteins
	- [[Pseudomonas aeruginosa]] -- Exotoxin A (ADP ribosylation of proteins)
	- [[Shigella]]
	- [[EHEC]]
- ↑ fluid secretion
	- [[ETEC]]
	- [[Bacillus Anthracis|Bacillus anthrax]]
	- [[Vibrio cholera]]
- ↓ phagocytosis
	- [[Bordetella Pertussis|B. pertussis]]
- ↓ neurotransmitter release
	- [[Clostridium tetani]]
	- [[Clostridium botulinum]]
- lyse cell membrane
- Superantigens
