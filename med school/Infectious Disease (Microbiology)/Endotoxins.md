Found on [[Gram Negative]] bacteria
- Component of cell wall ([[LPS]])
- Binds to LPS-binding protein in plasma
- Released upon death
### Structure
- [[Lipid A]] core
- O antigen -- terminal sugars that vary based on bacteria
- Heat stable
### Function
- Bind to **[[CD14]] on [[macrophage]]** -> 
	- Triggers TLR4
	- [[Cytokines|Cytokine]] production
		- [[IL-1]] (fever) [[IL-6]]  
		- [[IL-8]] (neutrophil migration)
		- [[TNF]] (fever, ↓ BP)
		- NO (↓ BP)
	- Fever, shock
	- Weak antibody response
- [[Complement System|Complement]] -- C3a / C5a 
	- ↓ BP, edema
	- neutrophil chemotaxis
- [[Tissue Factor]]
	- Coagulation
### Reaction Examples
- [[Meningococcemia]]
- Gram (-) sepsis
