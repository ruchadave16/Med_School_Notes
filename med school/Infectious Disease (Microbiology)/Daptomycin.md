#### Action
- Binds bacterial membrane, makes pores -> K+ efflux and cell death
- Stops DNA, RNA, protein synthesis
#### Uses
- Against vancomycin-resistant enterococci (VRE) and MRSA
- Complicated skin infection, bacteremia, endocarditis

#### Dosing

#### Adverse Effects
- Myopathy and rhabdomyolysis (MONITOR CPK LEVELS)
- Inactivated by lung surfactant 
#### Contraindication
- Do NOT use for pneumonia
