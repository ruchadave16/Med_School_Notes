*[[Gram positive]] cocci in pairs*
- Capsule strains are pathogenic
- alpha-hemolytic on [[blood agar]]
- Bile soluble colonies
- [[Optochin]] sensitive
- Common in nasopharynx
#### Mechanism
- Pneumolysin -- pore forming toxin
	- Can directly bind complement in ECM and start classical pathway
- Capsule
- IgA protease
#### Symptoms
- Community acquired pneumonia
- Sinusitis
- Otitis media
- Meningitis
#### Diagnosis
#### Treatment
- [[Penicillins]]
- [[Ceftriaxone]] in hospital for CAP
	- For resistant, use [[Fluoroquinolones]], [[macrolides]]
#### Prevention
- High risk = skull fractures (meningitis), asplenia
- [[PCV23]] vaccine and conjugate vaccines
