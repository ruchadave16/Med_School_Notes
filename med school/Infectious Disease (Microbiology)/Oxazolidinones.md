*Linezolid and Tedizolid*
#### Action
- Binds to 23S rRNA of 50S
- Inhibition of initiation of bacterial protein synthesis
#### Uses
- [[Gram positive]] 
- [[Mycobacterium tuberculosis]]

#### Dosing

#### Adverse Effects
- Reversible thrombocytopenia, neutropenia, anemia
- Prolonged use
	- Peripheral neuropathy
	- Lactic acidosis (mitochondrial issues)
- Interactions
	- Linezolid: Weak [[MAO Inhibitors|MAO inhibitors]] -- [[serotonin syndrome]] risk
	- Tedizolid
