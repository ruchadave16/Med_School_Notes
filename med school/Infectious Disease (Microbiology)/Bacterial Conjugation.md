Transfer of DNA from one bacteria to another through [[pili]] in [[plasmids]]
**Example: [[Shigella]]**

HFr Cell (High Frequency Recombination)
- Donor: F+ has genes for sex pili
- Recipient: F- 
- After conjugation recipient is F+

F' (F-primed)
- Incorrect excision so plasmid has part of bacterial chromosome in it
