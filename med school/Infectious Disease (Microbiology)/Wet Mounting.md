### KOH prepration
- Amine Whiff test
	- Wet amount of vaginal fluid and add KOH
	- Fishy smell suggests [[bacterial vaginosis]]
- [[Clue cells]]
	- Vaginal epithelial cells studded with bacteria
	- Suggestive of [[bacterial vaginosis]] from [[Gardnerella vaginosis]] -- gram - bacteria
### Motility Study
- Identifies bacteria
- **Darting mobility** -- [[vibrio cholera]]
- **Umbrella motility** -- [[Listeria Monocytogenes]] at 25 C
- Common mobile = [[Shigella]] and [[Klebsiella Granulomatis]]
- Non-mobile = [[E. coli]] and [[pseudomonas]]
