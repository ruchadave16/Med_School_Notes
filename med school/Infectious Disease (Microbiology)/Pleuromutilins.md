*Lefamulin and Retapamulin*
#### Action
- Bind to 50s [[bacterial ribosome]] near A/P site
- Inhibits peptide bond formation
#### Uses
- [[Gram positive]]
#### Administration
- Lefamulin = Oral + IV
- Retapamulin = topical
#### Dosing

#### Adverse Effects
Lefamulin = Oral + IV
- IV: site infection
- Oral: GI
- Systematic: QT prolongation, teratogenic


#### Contraindication
