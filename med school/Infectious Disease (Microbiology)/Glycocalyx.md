- Slime layer = loose, unorganized layer of polysaccharides
	- Capsule + slime layer = glycocalyx
	- Protection, adhesion, resistance to phagocytosis
- Biofilm = structured community of microorganisms in matrix of extracellular polymer substances
	- ↑ adhesion to surfaces
	- Microorganisms can share nutrients and exchange genetic material
	- Protection from desiccation, antibiotics, immune response

>[!Clinical]
>Coagulase negative staphylococci
>Candida
>E. coli
>[[Pseudomonas aeruginosa]]

>[!Note] These are big contributors to device-related infections
