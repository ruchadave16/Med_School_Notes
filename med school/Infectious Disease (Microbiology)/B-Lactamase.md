Enzymes mainly found in [[gram negative]] bacteria that hydrolyze ring in [[b-lactams]] and reduce the antibiotic's efficacy

- ESBLs -- hydrolyze [[penicillins]], [[cephalosporins]], and [[monobactams]]
- Carbapenemases -- hydrolyze almost all b-lactams and infection is difficult to treat
