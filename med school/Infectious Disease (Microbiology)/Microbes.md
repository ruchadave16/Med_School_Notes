#### [[Virus|Viruses]]
- No cell wall
- No nucleus
- No ribosomes or mitochondria
- No motility
- Outer capsid / envelope
- Replicate based on host
#### [[Bacteria]]
- Cell wall, 70S ribosomes
- No mitochondria
- Outer peptidoglycan layer
- Some have motility
- Binary fission
#### [[Fungi]]
- Cell wall, membrane bound nucleus, mitochondria
- 80S ribosome
- Chitin wall
- No motility
- Mitosis / budding
#### [[Parasites]]
- Variable cell wall
- Membrane bound nucleus, 80S ribosome, flexible cell membrane
- Mitosis
- Most have motility
