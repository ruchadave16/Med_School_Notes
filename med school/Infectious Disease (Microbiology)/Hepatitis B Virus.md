#### Mechanism
- DNA polymerase acts as reverse transcriptase since DNA is incomplete circular dsDNA
- Antigens
	- HbsAg (surface)
	- Core antigen
	- E antigen
- HBx inactivates [[p53]] -> hepatocellular carcinoma
- Transmission: blood, birth, sexual
#### Symptoms
- [[Hepatitis B]]
- Hepatocellular carcinoma
#### Diagnosis
- Serology: surface antigen (HBsAg), surface antibody (HBsAb), core antibody (HBcAb)
- If HBsAg for > 6 months, chronic carrier
- If neither but only HBcAb, in "window" phase
#### Treatment
#### Prevention
- Vaccine with HBsAg  immunogen
- Hyperimmune serum globulins
- Education of chronic carriers
