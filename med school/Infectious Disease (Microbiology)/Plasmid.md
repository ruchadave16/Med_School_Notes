Gives selective advantage to bacteria
- Has CCC (covalently closed circular) DNA
- Found in gram + and -
- Self-replicating, can integrate with bacterial DNA
#### Function
- Transfer gene from 1 bacteria to another through [[bacterial conjugation]] (F plasmids)
- Antibiotic resistance genes (R plasmids)
- Virulence factors (Virulence plasmids)
