*Molecular machines that are made of proteins found in [[Bacterial Cell Membrane|bacterial cell membrane]]*
1) T1SS
	- 1 step directly to cytoplasm
	- Ex: E. coli hemolysis
2) T2SS
	- inside -> Periplasm (Sec/Tat) -> out
	- Ex: cholera
3) T3SS
	- needle-like injection of effectors
	- Ex: shigella, [[salmonella]]
4) T4SS
	- protein/DNA transfer into bacteria
	- Ex: [[H. Pylori]] CagA , [[Legionella Pneumophilia]]
5) T5SS
	- proteins transport themselves out across membrane
