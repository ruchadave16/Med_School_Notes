*Lowest concentration of antibiotic that inhibits visible growth*
#### Microbroth Dilution Technique
- Serial dilutions of antibiotic in liquid culture media
- First well with no bacterial growth = MIC value

![[Screenshot 2026-02-14 at 12.19.19 PM.png]]

#### Interpretation
- **Susceptible (S)**: antibiotic expected to be effective at standard dose
- **Intermediate (I)**: may be effective but high doses required
- **Resistant (R)**: unlikely to be effective even at high doses
- S-DD: Success possible at high dose or extended infusion time

These breakpoints are individual to each microbe-drug pair
