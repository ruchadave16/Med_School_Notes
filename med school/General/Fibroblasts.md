Most common type in connective tissue proper

- Produce [[Collagen|collagen]] and components of ground substance: [[glycosaminoglycans]] (GAGs), [[proteoglycans]], and [[glycoproteins]]
- Wound healing: myo-fibroblasts have contractile function and enriched w actin to close edge of wound

Can differentiate into [[Adipocytes|adipocytes]], [[chondroblasts]], [[osteoblasts]]
##### Appearance
Oval to spindled (taper at end)
**Eusinophilic** cytoplasm
Borders are indistinct
