20% body weight
Measured using [[inulin]] or radiolabeled sulfate

1) [[Interstitial Fluid]]
2) [[Plasma Volume]]

#### ECF Volume
Highly linked to $Na^+$

Na gain → ↑ Posm (pressure for water to leave cells) *Hypertonic Expansion*
- H2O from ICF to ECF
- ↑ [[Vasopressin|ADH]] so ↑ renal H2O absorption
- [[thirst]] if threshold reached
- ↑ ECF volume

Na loss → ↓ Posm (pressure for cells to enter cells) *Hypotonic Contraction*
- H2O from ECF to ICF
- ↓ [[Vasopressin|ADH]] so ↓ renal H2O absorption
- ↓ [[thirst]] – ↓ water intake
- ↓ ECF volume
