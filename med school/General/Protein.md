#### Structure
1) Primary: Sequence of [[Amino Acids]]
2) Secondary: [[Hydrogen Bonds|Hydrogen bonds]] between carboxyl and amide 
	- $\alpha$-helix – Center is empty, rest in spiral around
	- $\beta$-sheet – Flat structure
3) Tertiary: Side chain interactions
	- Disulfide bonds
	- Van der Waals – NOT affected by pH
	- Hydrogen and ionic bonds – ARE affected by pH
4) Quaternary
	- Multiple protein interactions
#### Folding
- Chaperone proteins provide control of folding
