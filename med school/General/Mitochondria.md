2 membranes (outer and inner)
- Cristae in inner membrane (folds) → number corresponds to energy needs
- Intermembrane space
- Intercristae space 

$Ca^{2+}$ present in matrix as granules

Contains [[RNA]] and [[mitochondrial DNA]]
#### Functions
1) ATP synthesis through [[Oxidative Phosphorylation|ETC]]
2) [[Citric acid cycle]]
3) Buffering of $Ca^{2+}$
4) Induction of [[necrosis]]
#### [[Protein Transport]]
- Outer Membrane
	- TOM – General Import Pore
	- [[SAM]] – β-barrel proteins
	- Mim – α-helical proteins
- Inner Membrane
	- TIM23 – Matrix and membrane proteins
	- TIM22 – Carrier proteins
	- OXA – Insertion of matrix proteins

>[!Clinical]
>- Deficiency of TIM8 → [[Deafness-Dystonia Syndrome]]
>	- X-linked recessive neurodegenerative
>	- sensorineural deafness, cortical blindness, dystonia, dysphagia and paranoia
####  Damage
- Can happen due to ↑ cytosol Ca2+, [[ROS]], [[hypoxia]]
- Consequence: ↓ ATP → low plasma membrane energy-dependent pump → **cell swelling** because water enters cell
- [[Necrosis]] or [[apoptosis]]
