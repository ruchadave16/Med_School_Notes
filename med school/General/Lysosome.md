Contains hydrolytic enzymes (acid hydrolases)

Very abundant in [[Phagocytes]]
- Interior is acidic, inactive enzymes at pH 7

**Primary Lysosyme**
- Recently made by [[Golgi Apparatus]], unused

**Secondary Lysosyme**
- Activated after fusing with vesicles containing phagocytic material

**Residual Bodies**
- Debris from [[lysosyme]] activity → contains [[lipofuscin]]

>[!Clinical Consideration]
>Hereditary disorders caused by deficiency in lysosyme acid hydrolases
>→ Now lysosymes cannot degrade compounds
