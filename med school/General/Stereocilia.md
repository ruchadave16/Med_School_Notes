*Function: Increase SA for absorption*

Appears MUCH longer than cilia or microvilli
Wavy appearance
Only found in sensory cells of ear or [[epididymis]]

Similar composition to microvilli
- **Actin core**
- **Ezrin** links actin to plasma membrane
