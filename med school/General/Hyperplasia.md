*Increase in number of cells due to increased [[proliferation]]*

Physiological or pathological
- Hormonal hyperplasia
- Compensatory hyperplasia (tissue regrowth)
- Excessive hormone or GF

Tightly controlled – **risk factor for [[cancer]]**

Controls
- Hormone or GF driven proliferation of mature cells
- ↑ output of new cells from [[stem cells]]
