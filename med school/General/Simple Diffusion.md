Movement of molecule down gradient, without using energy or carriers
Semipermeable membrane allows movement of solvent but not solutes

**Reflection Coefficient ($\sigma$)** 
- 1.0 means impermeable membrane, maintains solute concentration across membrane
- 0.0 means no reflection of solute

**Osmolarity** = total solute concentration of solution
- Osmotic pressure = pressure to prevent [[osmosis]], based on concentration on both sides
- Water moves because of osmotic pressure difference when reflection coefficient > 0
- If 0, no osmosis occurs

**[[Tonicity]]** → action of solution to body
- Isotonic: no movement of water into or out of cell
- Hypotonic: net movement into cell
- Hypertonic: net movement out of cell
