Misfolded [[General/Protein|proteins]] moved from [[Endoplasmic Reticulum|ER]] to be degraded by [[proteasomes]]
- Ubiquinated, retro-translocated, and digested

>[!Clinical]
>In [[Cystic Fibrosis]], deletion of F508 (most common) causes CFTR protein to be unfolded and degraded by ERAD pathway
