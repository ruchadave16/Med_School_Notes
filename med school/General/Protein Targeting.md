All proteins start on free [[Ribosome|ribosomes]]
- [[SRP]] (Signal Recognition Particle) bind to signal sequence (at N termini) and moves to rough [[Endoplasmic Reticulum|ER]]
	- Hydrophobic residues
	- 15-30 AA long
- SRP and SRP receptor use GTP during this transfer
- [[Translation|Protein translation]] continues here on rER

Peptide removed by **signal peptidase**, protein enters lumen of rER
- Transferred to [[Golgi Apparatus|Golgi body]]
