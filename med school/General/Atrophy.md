*Reduction in cell size because of loss of cell structure*

- ↓ protein synthesis and ↑ protein degradation
	- Low trophic signals
	- Ubiquitin-Proteasome Pathway
		- Atrophic signals activate ubiquitin ligases
		- Ubiquitin attached to cellular proteins
		- Proteins with ubiquitin are targeted for degradation within proteasomes

- Physiological is a part of normal development
- Pathological
	- Low workload
	- Loss of innervation
	- Low blood supply
	- Inadequate nutrition – [[marasmus]], [[cachexia]]
	- Loss of endocrine stimulation
	- Pressure
