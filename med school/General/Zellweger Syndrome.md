### Etiology
- Genetic: [[Mutations|Mutation]] in PEX10 (membrane protein for import of proteins into peroxisome)
### Pathophysiology
- [[Peroxisome]] absent
	- Enzymes may have been dislodged to cytoplasm
- Relevant pathways (e.g. immune, hormonal)
### Clinical Features
- Symptoms
	- Neurological, liver, kidney problems
- Signs
	- Electron micrograph biopsy shows empty peroxisomes
- Variants
