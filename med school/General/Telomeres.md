- Normally removal of RNA primer means DNA shortens every round
- Noncoding, repeating sequence that protects chromosomal ends from degradation
- **Telomerase** adds repeating sequence to end (3’ end) – telomere

>[!Note]
>Somatic cells = no telomerase
>[[Stem cells]] = high telomerase
>Cancer cells = high telomerase

[[Telomeropathy]] = disease due to abnormal telomere maintenance
