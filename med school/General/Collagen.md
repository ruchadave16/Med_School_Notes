*Flexible, very high tensile strength*
*Most abundant [[General/Protein]] made of either 3 peptide strands wrapped around central axis or like mesh*
##### Appearance
Wavy, variable width and length
**Eosinophilic** staining on H&E, blue on Mallory trichrome, green on Maisson trichrome
- ![[Screenshot 2025-08-04 at 11.01.36 AM.png]]

EM has uniform fiber diameter in given location
##### Synthesis 
Produced by [[fibroblasts]] 
- Made of 3 procollagen-$\alpha$ chains in triple helix arrangement
- Regular Format: Gly – X – Hyp (X is any AA, Hyp is [[hydroxypoline]])

>[!Clinical]
>[[Scurvy]] can weaken collagen (deficiency of [[Vitamin C]]) because hydroxyproline isn’t made

Secreted by exosynthesis as **procollagen** molecules (incapable of arrangement)
Processed by peptidases to trim non-helical terminal ends to create collagen molecule
→ Now can self-assemble

##### Degradation
Matrix Metalloproteinases ([[MMPs]])
- Synthesized and secreted into [[Extracellular Matrix|ECM]] by many connective tissue cells
- Secreted by invasive cancer cells as step in [[metastasis]]
	- Can be inhibited by tissue inhibitors of metalloproteinases (TIMPs)
