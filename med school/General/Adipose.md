Collection of fat cells ([[Adipocytes]])

Types:
1) White adipose tissue
	- Adult humans
	- Energy storage, insulation, cushion organs, hormone secretion
2) Brown adipose tissue
	- Fetal life
	- Offsets heat loss to prevent hypothermia
