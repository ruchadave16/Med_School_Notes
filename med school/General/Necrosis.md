*Rapid and uncontrollable cell death*
#### Cellular Morphology
- Eosinophilia in cytoplasm
- Nucleus
	- Pyknosis – nuclear shrinking and basophilia
	- Karyorrhexis – nuclear fragmentation
	- Karyolysis – nuclear digestion
