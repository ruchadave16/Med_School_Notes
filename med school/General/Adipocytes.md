Found in connective tissue proper

Specialize in cytoplasmic storage of lipid for energy

1) White adipocyte
	- Primary in adults
	- Very large with one large lipid droplet and nucleus squished into side
	- Look white, cleared out 
	![[Screenshot 2025-08-04 at 12.25.30 AM.png]]
2) Brown adipocyte
	- Found in fetal but small pockets in adult
	- Can **transdifferenciate** from white to brown and back
	- Smaller than white, polygonal, multilocular (many lipid drops)
		- Numerous [[mitochondria]] ← Gives color
	- Specialized for **generating heat**
