*Energy that travels in waves or high speed particles with enough energy to remove electrons from atoms*
#### Mechanism
- [[DNA Damage]]
	- Double strand breaks to [[DNA]]
	- Repair through homologous recombination or non-homologous end joining
- Indirect Damage
	- [[ROS]] generation

**Determinants**
- Dose
- Rate of delivery – more harm when given over short time
- Field size – smaller field and higher dose is better than large filed and low dose
- Cell proliferation – more problems if irradiated cells divide
- Oxygen
