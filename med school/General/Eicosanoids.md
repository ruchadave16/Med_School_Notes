#### [[Type I Hypersensitivity]]
- PGE2 – redness (vasodilation), edema (permeability), fever (hypothalamus), pain (nerves)
- PGD2 – bronchoconstriction, [[Eosinophils|eosinophil]] infiltration
- LTC4 / LTD4 – vasoconstriction, bronchoconstriction
- [[LTB4]] – neutrophil, eosinophil chemotaxis
