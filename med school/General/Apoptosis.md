*Programmed Cell Death*
- Occurs normally to eliminate harmful cells

**Physiologic**
- Embryogenesis
- Proliferative tissue turnover
- Involution of hormone dependent tisssue
- Elimination of harmful lymphocytes

**Pathological**
- DNA damage
- Misfolded proteins 
- Viral infections

- DNA fragmentation → phagocyted

	- Pyknotic [[nucleus|nuclei]]
- [[Mitochondria|Mitochondrial]] function loss
- Membrane blebbing
- Apoptotic bodies formation

#### Morphology
- Dark and condensed nucleus that fragments (karyorrhexis)
- Cell shrinks and fragments

#### Regulation
- [[BCL-2]] = antiapoptotic protein in mitochondrial membrane
	- Limits leakage of cytochrome C
	- Implicated in [[neoplasia]]
