Made of **Type III [[Collagen]]**

More delicate than collagen, branching patterns rather than bundles

##### Appearance
Positive for PAS
Not easily seen on H&E
Looks black on silver stains 

![[Screenshot 2025-08-04 at 11.30.01 AM.png]]

##### Locations
Many lymphoid organs
Surrounding [[Adipocytes|adipocytes]], smooth muscle, nerve fibers, small blood vessels
Stroma of [[bone marrow]] (hematopoietic tissue)
