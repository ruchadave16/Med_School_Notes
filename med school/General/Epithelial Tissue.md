*Tissue that covers exterior body surfaces and lines cavities and tubes*

##### Characteristics
- Avascular
- Low [[connective tissue]]
- On [[basement membrane]], which forms boundary between epithelial and connective
- Continuously renewed
- Generally demonstrate **polarity** → apical vs basal domains with different functions
- Bound together by [[Intercellular Junctions|cell junctions]]

##### Apical Domain Features
1) [[Microvilli]]: Increase apical surface area for absorption
2) [[Cilia]]: Move current of fluid in 1 direction
3) [[Stereocilia]]: Also increases SA for absorption
