**Passive** $Cl^-$ transporter in [[lungs]] and [[pancreas]] allowing chloride movement across [[Epithelial Tissue|epithelial]] membranes
- Sodium ions and water follow ensuring secretion is liquid and mucous doesn’t form

![[Screenshot 2026-02-17 at 2.28.08 PM.png]]

*[[Mutations]]* cause decreased ion and water transport, so thick mucous in lungs causes respiratory issues and blocks pancreatic ducts
