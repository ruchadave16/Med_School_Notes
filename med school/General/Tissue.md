*Group of cells organized to do a certain function*

| Tissue                | Cell                           | [[Extracellular Matrix\|ECM]] | Functions                                |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------ | ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| [[Epithelial Tissue]] | Aggregated polyhedral          | Small amount                  | Lining of surface, cavities              |
| [[Connective Tissue]] | Fixed and wandering cells      | Abundant amount               | Surface and protection of tissues/organs |
| [[Muscle Tissue]]     | Long contractile cells         | Moderate amount               | Contraction, body movements              |
| [[Nervous Tissue]]    | Long cells with fine processes | Very small amount             | Nerve impulse transmission               |
