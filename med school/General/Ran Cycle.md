- GTPase that provides directionality to transfer across [[nuclear envelope]]
	- GTP/GDP gradient

**Nucleus**: RanGTP >>
**Cytoplasm**: RanGDP >>

RanGAP (Ran GTPase Activating Protein) activates GTP hydrolysis
- Found in cytoplasm (RanGTP → RanGDP)
- RanGTP → RanGDP causes dissociation of export complex
	- Export complex = Exportin + cargo + RanGTP

RanGEF (Ran Guanine-nucleoside Exchange Factor) encourages GDP GTP exchange
- Found in [[nucleus]] only
- Bound to chromatin
