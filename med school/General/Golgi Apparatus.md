Stacked cisternae with vesicles budding off
- Cis and trans sides (one way movement)
##### Functions
1) Receives  proteins and lipids from rough [[Endoplasmic Reticulum|ER]] to **cis side**
2) Protein glycosylation, sulfation, phosphorylation, etc continues
	- [[Glycosylation]] of enzymes for [[lysosome]] results in mannose 6 phosphate attaching to oligosaccharide
	- Causes [[clathrin]] coat vesicle → endosome → lysosome
	- Clathrin coat can also go to cell membrane
3) Protein packaging and dispatch ([[protein transport]])
	- Proteins with sequence: Lys–Asp–Glu–Leu (KDEL) returned to ER in **retrograde transport*
