*Primary producer of [[Antibodies]] against specific antigen*
- Come from [[B Lymphocytes|B-Lymphocytes]]
- Transform when they meet their [[antigen]] and are activated
	- Travel to [[spleen]] or [[bone marrow]] to secrete antibodies

##### Appearance
Large, squarish cells
**Basophilic** cytoplasm
Round, eccentrically placed nucleus with clear cytoplasm next to it
- Alternating hetero and euchromatin (**clockface**)
