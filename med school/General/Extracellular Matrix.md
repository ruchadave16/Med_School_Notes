##### Ground Substance
*Highly hydrated, transparent mixture of [[glycosaminoglycans]] (GAGs), [[proteoglycans]], [[glycoproteins]]*

- Fills space between cells and fibers in connective tissue
- Allows small molecule diffusion
- Viscous → Lubricant and barrier to invaders

**Appearance**
[[Microscopy|EM]]: electron-dense filaments
[[Microscopy|LM]]: If freeze drying, amorphous dark staining
- H&E ground substance lost because it is extracted during fixation

**Composition**
- [[GAGs]]: Long polymers of disaccharide units
	- Negatively charged, stain w basic dyes
	- Attracts water so hydrated gel
	- Rigid → gives structured framework for cells
	- Largest and most ubiquitous = [[hyaluronic acid]]
- [[Proteoglycans]]: Core protein with 1+ GAG chains
- [[Glycoproteins]]: Large molecules w branched oligosaccharide chains
	- Stabilizes ECM
