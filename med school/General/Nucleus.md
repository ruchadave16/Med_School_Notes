1) Nuclear Envelope
	- 2 membranes, interrupted by nuclear pores for macromolecule transport
	- Connects directly into rough [[Endoplasmic Reticulum|ER]]

2) [[Chromatin]]
	- Nucleosome: smallest unit of chromatin
	
3) Nuclear Matrix
	- Structural proteins, [[transcription factors]], ions
	- Nuclear lamina: internal to inner nuclear membrane where chromatin attached
	
4) Nucleolus
	- Has [[DNA]], [[rRNA]], [[General/Protein|proteins]]
	- Assembles [[Ribosome|ribosomes]] from ribosomal proteins (made in cytoplasm) and [[rRNA]]
	- Large and small units moved to cytoplasm for final assembly
	- Disappears during [[Cell Cycle|prophase]]
