Buds off of the [[Endoplasmic Reticulum|ER]]

- Contains oxidative enzymes
- Contains catalase to get rid of hydrogen peroxide
##### Functions
- Production and breakdown of hydrogen peroxide
- Lipid metabolism
- Bile salt synthesis
- Detoxification

-
