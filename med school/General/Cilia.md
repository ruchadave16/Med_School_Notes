*Function: Move current of fluid in 1 direction along epithelium *

Hair-like extensions
Longer than microvilli, more variable
Can see individual strands

##### Composition
Microtubules in 9+2 arrangement
- Core is called **axoneme**
Basal Body: insertion point of axoneme at base of cilium
**[[Dynein]]**: motor protein that produces motion
